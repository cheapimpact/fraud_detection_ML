raw materials like corn and soybeans. Additionally, competition

local poultry goods.  Furthermore, the potential risk of animal

disrupt the production and distribution of poultry products.

Perseroan yang terus bertambah.
Malindo aims to serve both domestic and export markets. To 
enhance its presence in the domestic market, Malindo intends 
to expand its production facilities by constructing new cages 
to optimize the production process. Simultaneously, in foreign 
markets, Malindo is focusing on the development of processed 
food products with the intention of selling them overseas.
Malindo has extended its sales strategy to include the online 
market, aiming to provide easier access to its products for 
consumers as one of Indonesia’s livestock agribusiness 
companies. The Company remains committed to upholding 
product quality and expanding its customer base through 
continuous innovation, offering diverse products accessible to 
all segments of community. Malindo is increasingly prioritizing 
downstream products, particularly ready-to-eat food items 
and food processing, which can be observed in the ongoing 
growth of the Company’s food processing customers and 
outlets.
