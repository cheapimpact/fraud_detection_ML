TINJAUAN INDUSTRI
INDUSTRY OVERVIEW
Pada 2024, ekonomi global melambat akibat penurunan 
pertumbuhan 
ekonomi, 
ketegangan 
geopolitik, 
dan 
ketidakpastian perdagangan internasional, dengan proyeksi 
Bank Indonesia untuk pertumbuhan global hanya 3,2%, 
yang diperkirakan berlanjut hingga 2026. Sementara itu, 
perekonomian Indonesia tetap menguat, didukung oleh 
permintaan domestik, investasi, dan Proyek Strategis Nasional 
(PSN). Bank Indonesia memperkirakan pertumbuhan 
Indonesia di kisaran 4,7% hingga 5,5% pada 2024 dan 2025, 
dengan potensi sedikit lebih tinggi pada 2026.
Pertumbuhan ekonomi Indonesia juga didorong oleh 
sektor-sektor utama seperti transportasi dan pergudangan 
(8,64%), akomodasi dan makan minum (8,33%), serta 
industri pengolahan (4,72%). Dari sisi pengeluaran, 
konsumsi Lembaga Non-Profit yang Melayani Rumah 
Tangga (LNPRT) mencatatkan kenaikan tertinggi (11,69%), 
diikuti oleh impor (11,47%) dan ekspor (9,09%). Dengan 
tren ini, Indonesia diperkirakan akan terus menunjukkan 
pertumbuhan yang stabil meskipun tantangan global 
tetap terus berlanjut.
Industri 
pengolahan 
terus 
memberikan 
kontribusi 
signifikan terhadap perekonomian Indonesia dengan 
pertumbuhan sebesar 4,72%. Pada 2024, industri kemasan 
menghasilkan nilai produksi Rp100 triliun, meningkat 
dari Rp93,2 triliun di tahun sebelumnya. Peningkatan ini 
didorong oleh tingginya konsumsi masyarakat, kemajuan 
teknologi pengemasan, serta pertumbuhan sektor 
farmasi dan perdagangan digital. Selain itu, preferensi 
konsumen terhadap kemasan yang aman dan tahan lama 
turut memperkuat kinerja industri. Meski begitu, industri 
ini menghadapi tantangan, seperti rendahnya tingkat 
utilisasi kapasitas produksi yang hanya 40-60%, akibat 
perubahan pola permintaan pasar yang mengharuskan 
fleksibilitas lebih tinggi. Meskipun demikian, Perseroan 
optimis sektor ini akan terus tumbuh dengan prospek 
berkelanjutan, didukung teknologi yang lebih baik dan 
ramah lingkungan.
In 2024, the global economy slowed down due to a decline in 
economic growth, geopolitical tensions, and uncertainties 
in international trade, with Bank Indonesia forecasting 
global growth at only 3.2%, expected to continue until 
2026. Meanwhile, Indonesia’s economy remained strong, 
supported by domestic demand, investment, and the 
ongoing implementation of the National Strategic Projects 
(PSN). Bank Indonesia projects Indonesia’s growth to range 
between 4.7% and 5.5% in 2024 and 2025, with potential 
for slightly higher growth in 2026.
Indonesia’s economic growth is also driven by key 
sectors 
such 
as 
transportation 
and 
warehousing 
(8.64%), accommodation and food services (8.33%), 
and manufacturing (4.72%). On the expenditure side, 
consumption by Non-Profit Institutions Serving Households 
(LNPRT) recorded the highest increase (11.69%), followed 
by imports (11.47%) and exports (9.09%). With this trend, 
Indonesia is expected to continue showing stable growth 
despite the ongoing global challenges.
The manufacturing industry continues to make a 
significant contribution to Indonesia’s economy, with a 
growth rate of 4.72%. In 2024, the packaging industry 
generated a production value of Rp100 trillion, up from 
Rp93.2 trillion in the previous year. This growth is driven 
by high consumer demand, advancements in packaging 
technology, and the growth of the pharmaceutical 
and digital commerce sectors. Additionally, consumer 
preference for safe and durable packaging has further 
strengthened the industry’s performance. However, 
the industry still faces challenges, such as low-capacity 
utilization rates, ranging from 40-60%, due to shifts in 
market demand that require greater flexibility. Despite 
this, the Company remains optimistic that this sector will 
continue to grow sustainably, supported by improved and 
environmentally friendly technology.

KEUNGGULAN KOMPETITIF
COMPETITIVE ADVANTAGES
Perseroan menjalankan kegiatan usaha di bidang 
perdagangan sebagai resmi dan penyewaan barang-
barang industri pengemasan termasuk suku cadang 
dan servis seperti coding, marking, labelling, dan product 
inspection system 
serta 
shrink-packaging, 
protective 
packaging, food packaging dan pharmaceutical (blister) 
packaging. Aktivitas operasional Perseroan berada di Pulau 
Jawa, dengan wilayah pemasaran dan penjualan berada di 
Jakarta, Semarang, dan Sidoarjo. Berikut adalah informasi 
terkait segmen usaha Perseroan.
Perseroan menjual produk dengan layanan unik mulai 
dari pre-sales sampai dengan trial produk sehingga ini 
menjadi ciri khas yang membuat berbeda dari yang lain. 
Perseroan akan memberikan saran yang cocok kepada 
para pelanggan berupa custom mesin, fitur khusus, atau 
consumables yang akan digunakan. Selain itu, Perseroan 
berpengalaman di bidang pemasaran packaging dan 
material. Dengan pengalaman di pemasaran packaging 
dan material maka Perseroan mampu memberikan solusi 
pelengkap kepada sebagian besar pelanggan yang 
membutuhkan solusi pengemasan akhir.
Di samping itu, Perseroan berfokus pada segmen pasar 
Business to Business (B2B) dan mencoba menjadi pemimpin 
dalam segmentasi ini dengan layanan purna jual yang 
kuat dan hubungan jangka panjang dengan mitra dan 
pelanggan Perseroan, sehingga memberikan nilai lebih 
bagi mitra yang bekerja sama dengan Perseroan. Tak hanya 
itu, Perseroan melayani berbagai industri, yang membantu 
mendiversifikasi portofolio produk dan solusi Perseroan.
The Company operates in the trade and leasing of 
packaging industry goods, including spare parts and 
services such as coding, marking, labelling, and product 
inspection systems, as well as shrink-packaging, protective 
packaging, food packaging, and pharmaceutical (blister) 
packaging. The Company’s operational activities are 
located on the island of Java, with marketing and sales 
areas in Jakarta, Semarang, and Sidoarjo. The following is 
information related to the Company’s business segments.
The Company sells products with unique services ranging 
from pre-sales to product trials, making it a distinctive 
feature that sets it apart from others. The Company 
provides suitable recommendations to customers in the 
form of custom machines, special features, or consumables 
to be used. In addition, the Company has extensive 
experience in the marketing of packaging and materials. 
With this experience, the Company is able to offer 
comprehensive solutions to most customers who need 
end-of-line packaging solutions.
Furthermore, the Company focuses on the Business-to-
Business (B2B) market segment and aims to become 
a leader in this segment through strong after-sales 
service and long-term relationships with its partners and 
customers, thereby providing added value to the partners 
working with the Company. Additionally, the Company 
serves various industries, which helps diversify its product 
and solution portfolio.

Rp22.83 billion, an increase of 1.47% or Rp331 million

2024, a decrease of 27.61% or Rp23.03 billion compared 
to the previous year, which stood at Rp83.43 billion. This 
decline was due to a reduction in both current.
Current Liabilities

at Rp49.97 billion, a decrease of 33.95% or Rp25.68 billion

Non-Current Liabilities

Perseroan mencatatkan penjualan neto sebesar Rp128,82 
miliar di tahun 2024, meningkat sebesar 30,93% atau 
setara Rp30,43 miliar, dibandingkan tahun 2023 yang 
tercatat sebesar Rp98,39 miliar. Kondisi ini disebabkan oleh 
meningkatnya penjualan consumabel sebesar 31,87%.
Beban Pokok Penjualan 
Pada tahun 2024, beban pokok penjualan tercatat sebesar 
Rp92,51 miliar. Jumlah tersebut meningkat sebesar 37,27% 
atau setara Rp25,12 miliar, dibandingkan tahun sebelumnya 
yang tercatat sebesar Rp67,40 miliar. Kondisi ini disebabkan 
seiring dengan meningkatnya penjualan neto.

tahun 2024, meningkat sebesar 17,15% atau setara Rp5,32 
miliar, dibandingkan tahun 2023 yang tercatat sebesar 
Rp30,99 miliar. Hal ini disebabkan oleh meningkatnya 
penjualan neto dan beban pokok penjualan.
Laba Operasi
Pada tahun 2024, laba operasi Perseroan tercatat sebesar 
Rp11,14 miliar. Jumlah tersebut menurun sebesar 15,47% 
atau setara Rp2,04 miliar, dibandingkan tahun 2023 yang 
tercatat sebesar Rp13,18 miliar. Kondisi ini dipengaruhi oleh 
meningkatnya beban umum dan administrasi sebesar 36,43%.

Net Sales
The Company recorded net sales of Rp128.82 billion in 
2024, an increase of 30.93% or Rp30.43 billion compared 
to Rp98.39 billion in 2023. This growth was driven by a 
31.87% increase in consumables sales.
Cost of Goods Sold 
In 2024, the cost of goods sold was recorded at Rp92.51 
billion, an increase of 37.27% or Rp25.12 billion compared 
to Rp67.40 billion in the previous year. This increase was in 
line with the growth in net sales.
Gross Profit
The Company’s gross profit was recorded at Rp36.10 billion 
in 2024, an increase of 17.15% or Rp5.32 billion compared 
to Rp30.99 billion in 2023. This growth was driven by the 
increase in net sales and the cost of goods sold.
Operating Profit
In 2024, the Company’s operating profit was recorded 
at Rp11.14 billion, a decrease of 15.47% or Rp2.04 
billion compared to Rp13.18 billion in 2023. This decline 
was influenced by a 36.43% increase in general and 
administrative expenses.
Net Profit for the Year After Proforma Adjustment 
Impact
The net profit for the year after the proforma adjustment 
impact in 2024 was recorded at Rp6.89 billion, a decrease 
of 27.76% or Rp2.65 billion compared to Rp9.53 billion in 
2023. This decline was driven by an 18.87% decrease in 
profit before income tax.
Total Comprehensive Income for the Period/Year
In 2024, the total comprehensive income for the period/
year was recorded at Rp8.16 billion, an increase of 34.51% 
or Rp2.09 billion compared to Rp6.07 billion in 2023. This 
growth was driven by a 34.84% increase in net profit for 
the year before the proforma adjustment impact.
Basic Earnings per Share
The Company’s basic earnings per share decreased by 
89.85% from Rp226.80 in 2023 to Rp23.03 in 2024.

activities was recorded at Rp6.64 billion, a decrease of

increase in receipts from (payments to) others.

recorded at Rp1.60 billion in 2024, an increase of 10.23% or

fixed assets.
Net Cash Obtained from (Used for) Financing Activities

activities in 2024 was recorded at Rp7.72 billion. This

was recorded at Rp547 million. This change was driven by 
an increase in bank loan repayments as well as receipts 
(payments) of other payables from related parties.

PERUBAHAN STANDAR KEBIJAKAN AKUNTANSI
CHANGES TO THE STATEMENTS OF FINANCIAL 
ACCOUNTING STANDARDS
Tidak terdapat perubahan peraturan maupun perundang-
undangan yang berdampak signifikan bagi kegiatan 
operasional Perseroan dan Entitas Anak selama tahun 
2024.
Dalam tahun berjalan, Perseroan telah menerapkan 
Standar Akuntansi Keuangan (“SAK”) dan Intrepretasi 
Standar Akuntansi Keuangan (“ISAK”) baru dan revisi 
termasuk pengesahan amandemen dan penyesuaian 
tahunan yang dikeluarkan oleh Dewan Standar Akuntansi 
Keuangan Ikatan Akuntan Indonesia yang dianggap 
relevan dengan kegiatan operasinya dan mempengaruhi 
Laporan Keuangan berlaku efektif untuk periode tahun 
buku yang dimulai pada atau setelah 1 Januari 2024.
SAK dan ISAK baru dan revisi termasuk pengesahan 
amandemen dan penyesuaian tahunan yang berlaku 
efektif dalam tahun berjalan adalah sebagai berikut.
• Amendemen PSAK 116: Sewa. Amendemen tentang 
liabilitas sewa dalam jual dan sewa balik. Amendemen 
ini mengatur pengukuran selanjutnya atas transaksi jual 
dan sewa balik.
• Amendemen PSAK 201: Penyajian Laporan Keuangan. 
Amendemen tentang liabilitas jangka panjang dengan 
kovenan. Amendemen ini mengatur bahwa hanya 
kovenan yang harus dipatuhi entitas pada atau sebelum 
tanggal pelaporan yang akan memengaruhi klasifikasi 
liabilitas jangka pendek atau jangka panjang serta 
pengungkapannya pelaporan.
• Amendemen PSAK 207: Laporan Arus Kas dan PSAK 107: 
Instrumen Keuangan: Pengungkapan. Amendemen 
tentang 
pengaturan 
pembiayaan 
pemasok. 
Amendemen ini memperjelas pengungkapan terkait 
pengaturan pembiayaan pemasok.
Beberapa dari SAK dan ISAK termasuk amendemen dan 
peyesuaian tahunan yang berlaku dalam tahun berjalan 
dan relevan dengan kegiatan Entitas telah diterapkan 
sebagaimana 
dijelaskan 
dalam “Ikhtisar 
Kebijakan 
Akuntansi Material.”
Beberapa SAK dan ISAK lainnya yang tidak relevan dengan 
kegiatan Perseroan atau mungkin akan mempengaruhi 
kebijakan akuntansinya di masa depan sedang dievaluasi 
oleh manajemen potensi dampak yang mungkin timbul 
dari penerapan standar-standar ini terhadap Laporan 
Keuangan.
There were no changes in regulations or legislation that 
had a significant impact on the operational activities of the 
Company and its Subsidiaries during 2024.
In the current year, the Company has adopted all of the 
new and revised Financial Accounting Standards (SAK) 
and Interpretation to Financial Accounting Standards 
(ISAK) including amendments and annual improvements 
issued by the Board of Financial Accounting Standards of 
the Indonesian Institute of Accountants that are relevant 
to its operations and affected to the financial statements 
effective for accounting period beginning on or after 
January 1, 2024.
The new and revised SAKs and ISAKs including 
amendments and annual improvements effective in the 
current year are as follows.
• Amendment to PSAK 116: Leases. Amendments on 
lease liabilities in sale and leaseback. This amendment 
regulates subsequent measurement of sale and 
leaseback transactions.
• Amendment to PSAK 201: Presentation of Financial 
Statements. Amendments on long-term liabilities 
with covenants. This amendment stipulates that only 
covenants that an entity must comply with on or before 
the reporting date will affect the classification of short-
term or long-term liabilities and their disclosure.
• Amendments to PSAK 207: Cash Flow Statement 
and PSAK 107: Financial Instruments: Disclosures. 
Amendments on supplier financing arrangements. 
These amendments clarify disclosures related to supplier 
financing arrangements.
Several SAKs and ISAKs including amendments and 
annual improvements that became effective in the current 
year and are relevant to the Entity’s operation have 
been adopted as disclosed in the “Summary of Material 
Accounting Polices”.
Other SAKs and ISAKs that are not relevant to the Company’s 
operation or might affect the accounting policies in 
the future are being evaluated by the management the 
potential impact that might arise from the adoption of 
these standards to the financial statements.
