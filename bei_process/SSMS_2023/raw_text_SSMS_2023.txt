Analisis Kondisi Perekonomian Global dan Nasional
Status pandemi Covid-19 telah resmi dicabut oleh WHO, namun 
pemulihan ekonomi global terhadap dampak yang ditimbulkannya 
masih terhambat. Berdasarkan data International Monetary Fund 
(IMF) yang dimuat dalam World Economic Outlook pertumbuhan 
perekonomian global tahun 2023 diestimasikan sebesar 3,1% lebih 
rendah dibandingkan tahun 2022 sebesar 3,5%. Pertumbuhan 
ekonomi negara maju hanya mencapai 1,6% lebih rendah dari 
tahun 2022 sebesar 2,6%, sedangkan pertumbuhan perekonomian 
negara berkembang stabil sebesar 4,1%. 
Keterbatasan pasokan energi dan pangan global masih berlanjut 
sebagai dampak dari perang Rusia di Ukraina serta permasalahan 
perubahan iklim. Kawasan Eropa mengalami cuaca dingin ekstrem 
dan Kawasan Asia mengalami Elnino sehingga mengganggu 
pasokan energi dan ketahanan pangan. Penawaran yang terbatas 
mendorong kenaikan harga energi dan pangan dunia serta 
keketatan pasar tenaga kerja di sejumlah negara maju. Inflasi yang 
tinggi dapat menurunkan daya beli masyarakat baik di negara 
maju maupun negara berkembang, sehingga Bank-bank sentral 
berupaya menurunkan tekanan inflasi, salah satunya dengan 
menaikkan suku bunga. Inflasi global diestimasikan mencapai 
6,8% pada tahun 2023. 
Ketegangan geopolitik dunia semakin meningkat seiring terjadinya 
konflik antar negara yang terus bertambah. Perang Rusia di Ukraina 
yang masih berlangsung ditambah konflik Israel dan Palestina. 
Perang dagang antara Amerika Serikat dan Tiongkok juga masih 
belum usai sehingga turut menghambat pertumbuhan volume 
perdagangan global. Negara-negara di dunia perlu bekerja 
sama untuk meredakan ketegangan geopolitik dan mengurangi 
dampaknya terhadap perekonomian global.

Analysis of Global and National Economic Conditions
Despite the Covid-19 pandemic status having been officially lifted 
by WHO, the global economic recovery from its impacts has 
been hampered still. Based on International Monetary Fund (IMF) 
data published in the World Economic Outlook, global economic 
growth in 2023 was estimated at 3.1%, lower than it was in 2022 
which reached 3.5%. Economic growth in developed countries 
only reached 1.6%, lower than it was in 2022 of 2.6%, whereas 
economic growth in developing countries was stable at 4.1%.
Limited global energy and food supplies have continued as a 
result of Russian war in Ukraine and climate change problems. The 
European region has been experiencing extreme cold weather 
and the Asian region has been experiencing Elnino, disrupting 
energy supplies and food security. Limited supply has driven 
up world energy and food prices as well as tight labor markets 
in a number of developed countries. High inflation has reduced 
people’s purchasing power in both developed and developing 
countries, so central banks are striving to reduce inflationary 
pressure, one of which is by raising interest rates. Global inflation 
was estimated to reach 6.8% in 2023.
World geopolitical tensions have been increasing as conflicts 
among countries have continued to increase, including the 
ongoing Russian war in Ukraine as well as the Israeli and Palestinian 
conflict. Furthermore, the trade war between the United States 
and China is not over yet, which is also hampering the growth of 
global trade volume. It is necessary for the countries in the world 
to collaborate to defuse geopolitical tensions and reduce their 
impact on the global economy.

Analisis Industri Kelapa Sawit dan Posisi Perseroan di 
Industri 
Kelapa sawit merupakan salah satu komoditas hasil perkebunan 
yang memiliki peran cukup penting bagi perekonomian Indonesia. 
Minyak nabati yang dihasilkan dari pengolahan kelapa sawit 
banyak dibutuhkan oleh sektor industri pengolahan. Industri 
kelapa sawit juga memiliki potensi besar dalam mendorong 
pertumbuhan ekonomi di daerah melalui penyerapan tenaga 
kerja dan pembangunan infrastruktur. Badan Pengelola Dana 
Perkebunan Kelapa Sawit (BPDPKS) mencatat kinerja sektor 
kelapa sawit dalam negeri melibatkan 2,4 juta petani swadaya 
dan 16 juta tenaga kerja. Hal ini memberikan pengaruh positif 
terhadap peningkatan kesejahteraan rakyat dan penurunan angka 
pengangguran. 
Perusahaan kelapa sawit melakukan pembangunan infrastruktur 
di daerah pedesaan tidak hanya untuk mendukung kegiatan 
operasionalnya, tetapi juga dapat dimanfaatkan oleh masyarakat 
pedesaan. Jalan, jembatan, dan sekolah di wilayah pedesaan 
yang semula terisolir dan terbelakang dibangun sehingga dapat 
meningkatkan aksesibilitas dan konektivitas masyarakat terhadap 
layanan publik yang lebih baik.
Industri kelapa sawit juga menjadi salah satu penghasil devisa 
negara yang besar. Devisa dari ekspor minyak kelapa sawit dan 
produk turunannya memiliki kontribusi penting dalam neraca 
perdagangan non-migas Indonesia. Namun, perkembangan 
industri kelapa sawit tahun 2023 menghadapi berbagai tantangan. 
Berdasarkan data Gabungan Pengusaha Kelapa Sawit Indonesia 
(GAPKI) sampai dengan Oktober 2023, devisa ekspor CPO hanya 
mencapai USD23,54 miliar dan diperkirakan hingga akhir tahun 
tidak akan melampaui capaian tahun 2022 sebesar USD39,07 
miliar. 
Analysis of the Palm Oil Industry and the Company’s 
Position in the Industry
Palm oil is one of the plantation commodities which plays an 
important role in Indonesia’s economy.  Vegetable oil produced 
from palm oil processing is much needed by the processing 
industry sector.  The palm oil industry also has great potential 
in encouraging regional economic growth through employment 
and infrastructure development.  The Palm Oil Plantation Fund 
Management Agency (BPDPKS) has noted that the performance 
of the domestic palm oil sector involves 2.4 million independent 
farmers and 16 million workers.  This has a positive impact on 
improving people’s welfare and reducing unemployment rates.
Palm oil companies carry out infrastructure development in rural 
areas not only to support their operational activities, but also to 
facilitate the rural communities.  Roads, bridges and schools in 
rural areas that were previously isolated and underdeveloped 
were built to increase community accessibility and connectivity 
to better public services.
The palm oil industry is also one of the country’s top foreign 
exchange earners.  Foreign exchange from exports of palm oil 
and its derivative products has greatly contributed to Indonesia’s 
non-oil and gas trade balance.  However, the development of the 
palm oil industry in 2023 encountered various challenges.  Based 
on data from the Indonesian Palm Oil Entrepreneurs Association 
(GAPKI) as of October 2023, CPO export foreign exchange only 
reached USD23.54 billion and it is estimated that by the end of the 
year it would not exceed the  achievement in 2022 of USD 39.07 
billion.

STRATEGI BISNIS
BUSINESS STRATEGY
Sebagai upaya untuk mengembangkan usaha, di tahun 2023 
Perseroan melakukan serangkaian strategi untuk meningkatkan 
produktivitas dan pengendalian biaya operasional, diantaranya 
mengakuisisi anak usaha melalui penyelesaian sebagian utang 
PT Citra Borneo Indah (“CBI”) dengan mengalihkan kepemilikan 
saham milik CBI pada PT Citra Borneo Utama Tbk (“CBUT”) kepada 
Perseroan, memberi peluang baru dalam hilirisasi produk sawit 
dan diversifikasi produk. Dengan demikian, secara keseluruhan 
saham Perseroan di CBUT adalah sebesar 78,22% dimana 70,22% 
saham dimiliki langsung oleh Perseroan, 4% saham dimiliki tidak 
langsung melalui KSA, dan 4% saham dimiliki tidak langsung 
melalui MMS.
Strategi tersebut dilakuan untuk mengoptimalisasi hilirisasi bisnis 
Perseroan yang berkelanjutan dengan menyediakan produk 
kelapa sawit yang menyeluruh dari hulu ke hilir.
Selain itu, Perseroan juga terus berupaya memperkuat inovasi 
dan riset pengembangan bersama Sulung Research Station 
(SRS) yang menjadi ujung tombak keberlanjutan produk kelapa 
sawit. Hingga akhir tahun 2023, SRS telah menghasilkan sejumlah 
produk inovasi yang menjadi solusi untuk menangani berbagai 
permasalahan dan kendala pada perkebunan kelapa sawit.
Strategi lain yang dijalankan Perseroan di tahun 2023 adalah 
dengan merampungkan berbagai sertifikasi seperti sertifikasi 
Rountable Susutainable Palm Oil (RSPO), Indonesia Sustainable 
Palm Oil (ISPO), International Sustainability and Carbon Certification 
(ISCC), dan Business and Human Rights International Standards for 
Certification 2011 (BHRISC) untuk hak asasi manusia (HAM) untuk 
seluruh unit bisnis Perseroan. Dengan demikian, keberlanjutan 
produk hulu Perseroan telah 100% tersertifikasi RSPO dan ISPO.
As an effort to develop the business, in 2023 the Company carried 
out a series of strategies to increase productivity and control 
operational costs, including acquiring a subsidiary by carrying out 
debt settlement of PT Citra Borneo Indah (“CBI”) by transferring 
CBI’s share ownership to PT Citra Borneo Utama Tbk  (“CBUT”) 
to the Company, as well as providing new opportunities in the 
downstreaming of palm oil products and product diversification. 
Thus, overall the Company’s shares in CBUT are 78.22%, and 
70.22% of which are owned directly by the Company, 4% of which 
are owned indirectly through KSA, and 4% of which are owned 
indirectly through MMS.
This strategy was carried out to optimize the Company’s 
sustainable downstream business by providing comprehensive 
palm oil products from upstream to downstream.
In addition, the Company has continuously strived to strengthen 
development innovation and research with Sulung Research 
Station (SRS), which is the key of the sustainability of palm oil 
products.  As of the end of 2023, SRS has produced a number of 
innovative products which are solutions to various problems and 
obstacles in oil palm plantations.
Another strategy implemented by the Company in 2023 was 
to complete various certifications such as the Roundtable 
Sustainable Palm Oil (RSPO), Indonesia Sustainable Palm Oil 
(ISPO), International Sustainability and Carbon Certification 
(ISCC), and Business and Human Rights International Standards 
for Certification 2011 (BHRISC) for human rights (HAM) for all of 
the Company’s business units.  Thus, the sustainability of the 
Company’s upstream products is RSPO and ISPO certified.

ASPEK PEMASARAN
MARKETING ASPECT
Industri sawit secara umum mengalami pertumbuhan yang baik, 
bahkan pada saat pandemi industri ini dikenal sebagai bisnis yang 
tahan krisis. Selain menjadi penghasil devisa, industri sawit mampu 
menyerap banyak tenaga kerja. Saat ini terdapat 22 provinsi dari 34 
provinsi di Indonesia yang berhasil mengembangkan perkebunan 
kelapa sawit, dimana sekitar 90% berada di pulau Sumatera 
dan Kalimantan dengan kontribusi sebesar 95% terhadap total 
produksi minyak sawit mentah/Crude Palm Oil (CPO).
Perkembangan pasar sawit semakin bertumbuh diiringi dengan 
konsumsi minyak sawit dalam negeri yang mengalami tren 
kenaikan selama selama enam tahun terakhir. Kenaikan konsumsi 
sawit dalam negeri dipicu oleh hilirisasi produk sawit yang memiliki 
ceruk pasar yang luas. Hilirisasi tidak hanya digunakan untuk 
memenuhi kebutuhan pangan dan non pangan dalam negeri, 
tetapi juga bermanfaat untuk menghasilkan produk substitusi 
impor dalam negeri. Berdasarkan data GAPKI, sepanjang 2023 
total konsumsi minyak sawit dalam negeri mencapai 23,21 juta ton, 
bertambah sekitar 2,07 juta ton atau tumbuh 9,8% dibandingkan 
tahun 2022 (yoy). Konsumsi ini paling banyak digunakan untuk 
pembuatan biodiesel yang mencapai 10,65 juta ton atau 45,9% 
dari total konsumsi nasional. Sementara konsumsi minyak sawit 
pangan sekitar 10,3 juta ton atau 44,4% dari total konsumsi 
nasional.

Oleh karena itu, pilihan mendorong hilirisasi turunan CPO 
merupakan pilihan yang harus diambil dan dijalankan oleh 
Perseroan. Strategi Perseroan mengakuisisi anak usaha PT Citra

The palm oil industry in general experienced good growth, even 
during the pandemic this industry was known as a crisis-resistant 
business.  Apart from being a foreign exchange earner, the palm 
oil industry is able to absorb a lot of workers.  Currently there are 22 
provinces out of 34 provinces in Indonesia that have successfully 
developed crude palm oil plantations, of which around 90% are on 
the islands of Sumatera and Kalimantan with a contribution of 95% 
to the total production of crude palm oil (CPO).
The development of the palm oil market is increasingly growing in 
line with domestic palm oil consumption which has experienced 
an upward trend over the last six years.  The increase in domestic 
palm oil consumption has been triggered by the downstreaming of 
palm oil products which have a wide market niche.  Downstreaming 
is not only used to meet domestic food and non-food needs, 
but also used to produce domestic import substitute products. 
Based on GAPKI data, throughout 2023 total domestic palm oil 
consumption reached 23.21 million tons, increasing approximately 
2.07 million tons or 9.8% growth compared to 2022 (yoy).  This 
consumption was mostly used for making biodiesel, which 
reached 10.65 million tons or 45.9% of total national consumption. 
Meanwhile, food palm oil consumption was estimated to have 
reached 10.3 million tons or 44.4% of total national consumption.
For that reason, encouraging the downstreaming of CPO 
derivatives is an initiative that must be taken and implemented 
by the Company.  The Company’s strategy to acquire PT Citra 
Boerneo Indah’s subsidiary, namely PT Citra Borneo Utama Tbk 
(“CBUT”) is the right strategy to develop the Company’s market.

Indonesian Economic Outlook for 2024
Indonesia’s economic growth projection for 2024 is expected to be 
maintained at 5.2%. National stability is expected to be maintained

Crude Palm Oil Performance
The Company’s main product is Crude Palm Oil (CPO).  The 
Company is one of the largest private CPO producers in Central 
Kalimantan province.  CPO is produced from fresh fruit bunches 
(FFB) provided by the Company’s oil palm plantations.  CPO is

production is carried out in the Company’s 8 (eight) Palm Oil Mills 
(PKS) located within oil palm plantations.

reached 22.12%, slightly decreasing 0.81% from the OER in 2022 
which was recorded at 22.30%.

Biogas Performance
The Company is developing a Biomass Power Plant (PLTBM) or 
Biogas Plant which produces environmental alternative fuels. 
The Biogas Plant produces electricity with a capacity of 1.5 Mega 
Watts and 900 Mega Watts from processing palm oil waste.  This 
environmentally friendly electricity is then distributed to homes 
in employee residential areas around the Company’s operational 
areas and utilized to support the Company’s operational activities.

Expeller (PKE).

Kemampuan Membayar Utang
Dalam memenuhi seluruh kewajiban Perseroan, baik jangka 
pendek maupun jangka panjang dapat diukur melalui rasio 
likuiditas dan rasio solvabilitas. Rasio likuiditas digunakan untuk 
mengukur kemampuan Perseroan dalam melunasi kewajiban 
jangka pendek, sedangkan rasio solvabilitas digunakan untuk 
mengukur kemampuan Perseroan dalam memenuhi seluruh 
kewajibannya.
Rasio Likuiditas
Perseroan menggunakan rasio likuiditas dalam memenuhi 
kewajiban jangka pendek yang akan jatuh tempo yang diukur 
menggunakan rasio lancar (current ratio), rasio kas (cash ratio), dan 
rasio cepat (quick ratio). Rasio lancar merupakan perbandingan 
antara aset lancar dengan liabilitas jangka pendek, rasio kas 
dihitung melalui perbandingan antara kas dan setara kas dengan 
liabilitas jangka pendek, dan rasio cepat digunakan untuk melihat 
kekuatan atau kelemahan finansial Perseroan dalam memenuhi 
kewajiban jangka pendeknya yang dihitung dengan mengurangi 
persediaan dari aset lancar, dan sisanya dibagi dengan liabilitas 
lancar atau liabilitas jangka pendek.
Ability to Pay Debt
In fulfilling all of the Company’s obligations, both short term and 
long term can be measured through the liquidity ratio and solvency 
ratio. The liquidity ratio is used to measure the Company’s ability 
to pay off current liabilities, while the solvency ratio is used to 
measure the Company’s ability to fulfill all of its obligations.
Liquidity Ratio
The Company uses the liquidity ratio to meet maturing short-term 
obligations, which are measured using the current ratio, cash ratio, 
and quick ratio. The current ratio is a comparison between current 
assets and short-term liabilities, the cash ratio is calculated by 
comparing cash and cash equivalents with current liabilities, and 
the quick ratio is used to see the Company’s financial strength 
or weakness in fulfilling its short-term obligations which is 
calculated by subtracting the inventory from current assets, and 
the remainder is divided by current liabilities or current liabilities.
