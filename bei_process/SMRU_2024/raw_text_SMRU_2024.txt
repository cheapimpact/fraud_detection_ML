Tinjauan Ekonomi Makro
Fokus utama pada perekonomian global di ta­
hun 2024 adalah tinggi nya tingkat inflasi, konf­
lik geopolitic di timur tengah, kebijakan ting­
kat suku bunga the Fed AS dan perlambatan 
ekonomi Tiongkok.
Eskalasi geopolitik di Timur Tengah berdampak 
pada meningkatnya risiko ketidakpastian di 
pasar global terutama terkait dengan rantai 
pasok energi dan peningkatan biaya logistik. 
Dampak langsung yang dirasakan oleh Indo­
nesia adalah pelemahan nilai expor Indonesia 
ke Timur Tengah dan potensi meningkatkan 
inflasi.
Dalam merespon tingginya inflasi, beberapa 
negara melalui Bank Sentral nya melakukan 
beberapa tindakan intervensi salah satunya 
adalah memangkas tingkat suku bunga ac­
uan. Di AS, selama tahun 2024 The Fed telah 
memotong sebanyak 3 kali tingkat suku bunga 
acuan. Di tahun 2024, Bank Indonesia secara 
proaktif turut menurunkan tingkat suku bunga 
di September 2024.
Perlambatan ekonomi Tiongkok dari 5,4% pada 
tahun 2023 menjadi 5,0% di tahun 2024 dise­
babkan krisis sektor properti, masih lemahnya 
pembangunan infrastruktur serta isu tingginya 
angka pengangguran di kelompok usia muda 
yang berimbas pada menurunnya kinerja per­
dagangan luar negeri Tiongkok. 
Berdasarkan laporan Badan Pusat Statistik 
(BPS), nilai total ekspor Indonesia sepanjang 
tahun 2024 mencapai US$ 287,17 miliar den­
gan menunjukkan tren yang beragam, dengan 
peningkatan di beberapa bulan dan penurunan 
di bulan lainnya. Secara kumulatif, nilai ekspor 
masih menunjukkan pertumbuhan yang sig­
nifikan di bandingkan tahun 2023.
Macroeconomic Overview
The main focus on the global economy in 2024 
is the high inflation rate, geopolitical conflicts 
in the Middle East, the US Fed’s interest rate 
policy and China’s economic slowdown.
Geopolitical escalation in the Middle East has 
resulted in increased risk of uncertainty in the 
global market, especially related to the energy 
supply chain and increased logistics costs. The 
direct impact felt by Indonesia is the weaken­
ing value of Indonesian exports to the Middle 
East and the potential to increase inflation.
In response to high inflation, several countries 
through their Central Banks have taken sever­
al intervention actions, one of which is cutting 
the interest rate. In the US, during 2024 the 
Fed has cut the interest rate 3 times. In 2024, 
Bank Indonesia proactively cut interest rates in 
September 2024.
China’s economic slowdown from 5.4% in 
2023 to 5.0% in 2024 is due to the crisis in the 
property sector, weak infrastructure develop­
ment and the issue of high unemployment in 
the young age group which has an impact on 
China’s foreign trade performance. 
Based on a report by the Central Statistics 
Agency (BPS), the total value of Indonesia’s 
exports throughout 2024 reached US$ 287.17 
billion and showed a mixed trend, with increas­
es in some months and decreases in others. 
Cumulatively, the value of exports still shows 
significant growth compared to 2023.

Tinjauan Kinerja Operasional
Perseroan mengkategorikan kegiatan usahan­
ya menjadi 3 (tiga) bagian yakni jasa penam­
bangan, jasa sewa dan dan jasa pengangku­
tan. Dimana kegiatan usaha penambangan 
meliputi jasa penambangan mulai dari pen­
gupasan lapisan penutup tanah atas, pengga­
lian batubara, jasa pengeboran dan peledakan 
lapisan penutup tanah. Kegiatan usaha sewa 
meliputi jasa penyewaan alat berat, sedangkan 
jasa pengangkutan adalah jasa pengangkutan 
penutup tanah ke area dumping dan batubara 
room, stockpile dan pelabuhan . 
In addition, 2024 is the year of the General 
Election (Pemilu) to elect the President and 
Vice President as well as members of the cen­
tral and regional legislatures. This directly or 
indirectly affects the domestic economy and in­
dustrial sector, which is triggered by economic 
actors’ concerns about policy changes by the 
new government, which has an impact on the 
postponement of investment plans and busi­
ness development by capital owners.
Operational Performance Review
The Company categorizes its business activ­
ities into 3 (three) parts, namely mining ser­
vices, rental services and transportation ser­
vices. Where mining business activities include 
mining services starting from stripping the top­
soil and overburden, coal excavation, drilling 
and blasting services. Rental business activi­
ties include heavy equipment rental services, 
while transportation services are transport top 
soil and overburden also coal transportation 
services to room, stockpile and port.

ed asset value of Rp702 billion, consisting of 
current assets of Rp55 billion and non-current 
assets of Rp647 billion. The decrease in the 
amount of consolidated current assets in 2024 
was mainly due to the realization of 2023 VAT 
restitution and 2022 corporate income tax 
both of which were received in 2024. As well 
as the realization of advances to vendors and 
a decrease in prepaid expenses for heavy 
equipment insurance.
 
Meanwhile, the decrease in the number of 
consolidated non-current assets in 2024 was 
mainly due to depreciation of heavy equip­
ment and the disposal of several heavy equip­
ment that were no longer used.

KEBIJAKAN DIVIDEN 
	
Dividends Policy
Mengingat kondisi keuangan Perseroan yang 
belum memperoleh laba, maka untuk tahun 
buku 2024 Perseroan tidak membagikan div­
iden kepada pemegang saham Perseroan 
dikarenakan kondisi keuangan Perseroan 
yang belum memungkinkan untuk memberi­
kan dividen kepada pemegang saham meng­
ingat kondisi pasar dan regulasi yang ber­
dampak terhadap kinerja Perseroan.
Considering the financial condition of the 
Company that has not gained a profit for 
the fiscal year 2024, the Company does not 
pay dividends to the shareholders due to the 
Company’s unfeasible financial condition to 
provide dividends to shareholders in view of 
market and regulatory conditions affecting the 
Company’s performance.
