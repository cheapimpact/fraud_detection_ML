- Tahun 2023 kemungkinan merupakan periode ekspansi atau restrukturisasi

- Tahun 2024 menunjukkan penurunan kebutuhan pendanaan karena 
kegiatan operasional dan investasi telah berjalan lebih stabil atau telah 
didukung oleh pendanaan internal;
- Perseroan mulai menunjukkan tingkat kemandirian pembiayaan yang lebih

managing the Company’s capital structure and liquidity.

Relevant Tax Policy Changes
In 2024, several notable changes in Indo-
nesia's tax regulations impacted the cor-
porate tax planning and compliance 
strategies of PT Era Media Sejahtera Tbk.
Firstly, under Law No. 7 of 2021 concern-
ing the Harmonization of Tax Regulations 
(UU HPP), the previously planned reduc-
tion in the corporate income tax rate to 
20% was officially revoked. As a result, 
the corporate income tax rate has 
remained at 22% starting from the 2022 
fiscal year and continues to apply in 
2024. This development has affected 
corporate tax planning and post-tax 
profit projections.
Secondly, the government issued Minister 
of Finance Regulation No. 131 of 2024, 
which revised the calculation method for 
Value Added Tax (VAT). Effective January 
1, 2025, the VAT will be calculated using a 
12% rate applied to 11/12 of the selling price. 
This amendment will have implications 
for pricing mechanisms and tax adminis-
tration processes, necessitating prepara-
tory adjustments by the Company.
PT Era Media Sejahtera Tbk remains 
committed to complying with prevailing 
tax regulations and is proactively adapt-
ing its accounting and financial reporting 
systems to address the implications of 
upcoming tax policy changes.
