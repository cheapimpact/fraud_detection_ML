TINJAUAN EKONOMI 
ECONOMIC OVERVIEW 
TINJAUAN OPERASI PER SEGMEN USAHA 
REVIEW OF OPERATIONS PER BUSINESS SEGMENT
Di sepanjang  tahun 2023, ekonomi global terus mengalami 
perlambatan yang dipengaruhi oleh gejolak geopolitik 
dan suku bunga kebijakan negara maju yang bertahan 
tinggi dalam waktu yang cukup lama. Bank Indonesia 
memproyeksikan pertumbuhan ekonomi global di tahun 
2023 sebesar 3,0% dan melambat menjadi 2,8% di tahun 
2024. Namun, pertumbuhan ekonomi nasional terus 
bertumbuh seiring dengan meningkatnya keyakinan 
konsumen dan berlanjutnya Proyek Strategis Nasional 
(PSN). Ekonomi nasional diproyeksikan tumbuh dalam 
kisaran 4,5-5,3% di tahun 2023 dan diperkirakan akan 
terus meningkat di tahun 2024 dalam kisaran 4,7-5,5% 
yang diimbangi dengan berbagai kebijakan pemerintah 
dan Bank Indonesia.
Sumber: Laporan Kebijakan Moneter Triwulan IV 2023 yang dipublikasikan 
oleh Bank Indonesia.
Dalam upaya memberikan solusi logistik yang terintegrasi 
dan inovatif, Perseroan terus meningkatkan keunggulan 
operasional dengan memaksimalkan utilisasi aset kapal 
Perseroan. Per Desember 2023, Perseroan mempunyai 
67 unit armada yang terdiri dari 31 unit Kapal Tunda, 26 
unit Tongkang, 3 unit Tongkang Derek Apung (Floating 
Crane), 2 unit Fasilitas Muatan Apung (FLF), dan 5 unit 
Kapal Kargo Curah (Mother Vessel/MV).
Dalam 
rangka 
memperkuat 
strategi 
diversifikasi, 
Perseroan 
memberikan 
layanan 
unggulan 
kepada 
perusahaan di beberapa sektor industri terutama 
pertambangan batubara, mineral, smelter, pembangkit 
listrik, dan pengangkutan multi kargo. Untuk diversifikasi 
pengangkutan multi kargo, Perseroan merambah ke 
pengangkutan komoditas nikel pada segmen Kapal Tunda 
dan Tongkang (TNB) dan segmen Kapal Karogo Curah 
(MV) dengan multi kargo pengangkutan nikel, alumina, 
tembaga, semen klinker, pasir besi, besi baja (steel 
billet), bijih besi, dan pupuk. Perseroan juga memperluas 
pemasaran ke skala internasional untuk mencapai target 
keuntungan dan pendapatan yang maksimal. 
Throughout 2023, the global economy continued to 
experience a slowdown influenced by geopolitical turmoil 
and policy rates in developing countries that remained 
high for a long time. Bank Indonesia projects global 
economic growth in 2023 at 3.0% and slowing to 2.8% in 
2024. However, national economic growth continues to 
grow in line with increasing consumer confidence and the 
continuation of the National Strategic Project (PSN). The 
national economy was projected to grow in the range of 
4.5-5.3% in 2023 and is expected to continue to increase 
in 2024 in the range of 4.7-5.5%, balanced with various 
government policies and Bank Indonesia.
Source: Monetary Policy Report for the Fourth Quarter of 2023 published by 
Bank Indonesia.
In an effort to provide integrated and innovative logistics 
solutions, the Company continues to improve operational 
excellence by maximizing the utilization of the Company’s 
ship assets. As of December 2023, the Company has 67 
units of fleet consisting of 31 units of Tugboats, 26 units 
of Barges, 3 units of Floating Cranes, 2 units of Floating 
Loading Facilities (FLF), and 5 units of Bulk Carriers (MV)
In order to strengthen its diversification strategy, the 
Company provides superior services to companies in 
several industrial sectors, especially coal mining, minerals, 
smelters, power plants, and multi-cargo transportation. 
To diversify multi-cargo transportation, the Company 
penetrated into the transportation of nickel commodities 
in the Tugboat and Barge (TNB) segment and Bulk 
Carriers (MV) segment with multi-cargo transportation of 
nickel, alumina, copper, clinker cement, iron sand, steel 
billet, iron ore, and fertilizer. The Company also expanded 
marketing to an international scale to achieve maximum 
profit and revenue targets.

Company maintains the following competitive advantages.
1. 	The timeliness of the loading and unloading process 
of cargo such as coal, nickel, and other commodities 
in accordance with the schedule agreed in the 
contract will result in a guarantee of trust from 
customers to cooperate with the company in the 
long term.
2. Responsive and solutive communication between 
customers and internal teams is also the main 
factor that supports the work process can run well 
according to the work plan.
Business strategy is determined as an effort or approach 
aimed at improving or maintaining the Company’s 
competitiveness and achieving satisfactory performance 
according to predetermined objectives.
Business Services
1.	Transshipment 
	
Commodity transportation activities collectively 
using Barges pulled by Tugboats from one place 
to the dock or to Bulk Carriers which will then be 
transported to other places.  The Company has 
several variations in the size of Barges and Tugboats 
to accommodate various customer needs.
2.	Inter-Island Transportation
	
Transportation of commodities from the port of 
origin to the destination port between domestic 
islands. The modes of transportation used in this 
transportation activity are Tugboats and Barges, 
and Bulk Carriers.  The main commodities served 
by the Company for inter-island transportation 
are coal, nickel, alumina, clinker cement, copper 
concentrate, and iron sand.

The types of integrated fleets and logistics owned by the

Kapal Tunda
Tugboats
Armada yang digunakan untuk menarik Tongkang bermuatan yang membawa kargo pelanggan dari 
satu tempat ke tempat lain. Hingga akhir 2023, Perseroan memiliki 31 unit Kapal Tunda dengan daya 
kekuatan antara 1.300 HP sampai dengan 2.400 HP. Armada tersebut dioperasikan oleh awak kapal 
berpengalaman dengan dilengkapi perangkat tracking berbasis satelit untuk memantau kinerja sistem 
dari segi kecepatan, jarak, dan posisi kapal.
The fleet is used to pull loaded Barges that carry customer cargo from one point to another. Until the 
end of 2023, the Company has 31 units of Tugboats with the capacity between 1,300 HP to 2,400 HP. 
The fleet is operated by experienced crew equipped with satellite-based tracking devices to monitor 
system performance in terms of speed, distance, and ship position.
Tongkang
Barges
Armada yang digunakan untuk membawa kargo muatan pelanggan. Kapal tongkang milik Perseroan 
adalah jenis tongkang tidak bermesin. Untuk memobilisasi muatan, diperlukan Kapal Tunda sebagai 
alat penariknya dari satu tempat ke tempat lainnya. Kapal ini dapat membawa muatan di beberapa 
jalur perairan seperti sungai, kanal, antar-pulau di dalam maupun luar negeri. Per 31 Desember 2023, 
Perseroan memiliki 26 unit Kapal Tongkang.
Selain itu, selama tahun 2023, Perseroan masih berfokus untuk mengoperasikan Kapal Tunda dan 
Tongkang (TNB) untuk jasa transshipment maupun angkutan antar pulau di dalam negeri dengan 
komoditas muatan utama, yaitu batubara dan nikel dengan permintaan yang tinggi dan memiliki 
prospek jangka panjang yang baik serta pengelolaan risiko terukur, di samping mengangkut muatan 
lainnya guna melayani kebutuhan pelanggan Perseroan.
This fleet is used to carry customer cargo. The Company’s barges are of non-motorized barge type. To 
mobilize cargo, a tugboat is needed as a means of towing from one point to another. This ship can carry 
cargo in several waterways such as rivers, canals, inter-islands at home and abroad. As of December 
31, 2023, the Company owns 26 units of Barges.
In addition, during 2023, the Company still focused on operating Tugboats and Barges (TNB) for 
transshipment services and inter-island transportation in the country with the main cargo commodities, 
namely coal and nickel with high demand and good long-term prospects and measurable risk 
management, in addition to transporting other cargo to serve the needs of the Company’s customers.
Fasilitas Muatan Apung 
(FLF)
Floating Load Facility 
(FLF)
Armada yang digunakan untuk memindahkan muatan angkut dari Tongkang ke Kapal Curah Besar yang 
berlokasi di perairan lepas dan dioperasikan oleh 38–40 awak kapal termasuk operator.
Perseroan saat ini memiliki 2 unit FLF dengan kapasitas muatan 25.000 MT per hari. Seluruh FLF 
yang dioperasikan dilengkapi dengan sistem pengecekan otomatis sampler batubara (automatic coal 
sampler) dan sistem pemisah besi (metal catcher) yang menjadi salah satu keunggulan kompetitif aset 
Perseroan dibandingkan dengan kompetitor, selain mempunyai kemampuan untuk blending batubara.
This fleet is used to transfer cargo from Barges to Bulk Carriers located in high waters and operated by 
38–40 crew members including operators.
The Company currently has 2 FLF units with a load capacity of 25,000 MT per day. All FLFs operated 
are equipped with automatic coal sampler checking system and metal catcher system which is one of 
the competitive advantages of the Company’s assets compared to competitors, in addition to having 
the ability to blend coal.
Tongkang Derek Apung
Floating Crane Barge
Perseroan membeli 3 unit Tongkang Derek Apung dengan kapasitas muatan 18.000 MT per hari. 
The Company purchased 3 units of Floating Crane Barges with a load capacity of 18,000 MT per day.
Kapal Kargo Curah (MV)
Bulk Carriers (MV)
Kapal angkutan dengan muatan besar yang digunakan untuk mengangkut dan memindahkan batubara, 
mineral, maupun komoditas lainnya untuk rute antar-pulau dalam negeri atau pun antar-negara. Kapal 
dilengkapi dengan 4 cargo crane yang mampu mengangkat sampai dengan 30 ton (Safety Working 
Load), mempunyai 4 set kubik meter grab dan 5 ruang muat. Kecepatan jelajah kapal sampai dengan 
14 knot. 
Terdiri dari 5 unit Kapal Kargo Curah (MV), yaitu 2 unit kelas Supramax dengan kapasitas masing-
masing 56 ribu metrik ton dan 3 unit kelas Handysize dengan kapasitas masing-masing 32 ribu metrik 
ton.  Hal ini merupakan salah satu strategi diversifikasi bisnis yang memperluas pangsa pasar dengan 
tidak hanya mengandalkan komoditas angkut batubara.

Diversification of Transport Commodity Products

the risk of the Company’s sales being concentrated in one

type of cargo transported, which is then expanded to 
include loads of iron ore and fertilizers other than nickel, 
alumina, copper, clinker cement, iron sand, and steel.
Diversify Customers with a Good Reputation
The Company always maintains the implementation of the 
principles of Good Corporate Governance and “Knowing 
Your Customer”, as a result, the Company is able to 
collaborate with various large companies in the coal, nickel, 
mineral, smelter, SOE, cement industry, and power plant 
miners in Indonesia with a good reputation, cooperation 
in long-term contacts, and provide measurable and 
sustainable income. The Company’s business partners

PT Multi Harapan Utama, PT Anugerah Bara Kaltim, 
Toba Bara Group, PT Marunda Graha Mineral, PT Aserra 
Logistik Indonesia, PT Asian Bulk Logistics, and PT Maritim 
Barito Perkasa.
Reliable Fleet
The Company always maintains its fleet so that it supports 
operational activities to run effectively and efficiently. As 
of December 31, 2023, the Company has 67 fleet units 
operating not only in Indonesian waters, but also serving 
interstate transportation.
The Company also always maintains the trust and 
satisfaction of customers by providing the best service by 
rejuvenating the older fleet, by changing to a new fleet or 
a younger fleet.

Pemeliharaan Kapal

diuraikan sebagai berikut. 
•	 Pemeliharaan Periodik

Pasar Batubara Internasional

pasar internasional di tengah masifnya inisiatif terkait net 
zero emission pada tahun 2060. Hal tersebut didukung 
kebutuhan batubara dari India ataupun Cina, dan

menjadikan persaingan bisnis batubara semakin ketat di 
antar negara. Menurut Badan Energi Internasional (IEA),

permintaan batubara di tahun 2023 didominasi Cina 
dan India yang akan terus bertumbuh, diimbangi dengan 
kebijakan, investasi, dan hubungan kerja sama yang 
menguntungkan kedua belah pihak.  Sementara di 
negara wilayah Eropa mengalami penurunan permintaan 
batubara karena mulai beralih pada penggunaan energi 
terbarukan.

conditions that has not improved, the Company is 
optimistic that it can achieve better performance in the 
following years. This is supported by the Company’s various 
strategic plans, especially in the business expansion 
strategy through diversification of transportation in 
coal and non-coal commodities. With its reliable fleet, 
operational efficiency, and reliable information technology, 
the Company is confident that it can achieve sustainable 
business growth.
International Coal Market
Coal remains a promising business in the international 
market amid massive initiatives related to net zero 
emissions by 2060. This is supported by the need for coal 
from India or China, and the existence of uncompetitive 
price competition, thus making coal business competition 
tighter between countries. According to the International 
Energy Agency (IEA), coal consumption in 2023 almost 
equaled that of 2022, at 8.3 MT. In 2023 and 2024 there 
will be a small decline in coal-fired power plants. That 
will most likely be offset by increased coal use by various 
industries. IAE also said that coal demand in 2023 was 
dominated by China and India which will continue to 
grow, balanced with policies, investments and cooperative 
relations that benefit both parties.  Meanwhile, European 
countries experienced a decrease in coal demand as they 
began to switch to renewable energy.

Pangsa Pasar Nikel
Sepanjang  tahun  2023,  harga  nikel  di  Indonesia 
mengalami 
penurunan 
yang 
dilatarbelakangi 
oleh 
menurunnya permintaan dari Cina, adanya sentimen 
geopolitik Rusia dan Ukraina, serta perlambatan ekonomi 
dunia.  Kementerian ESDM mencatatkan harga acuan 
nikel di Indonesia per Desember 2023 tercatat sebesar 
AS$17.653/Dry metric ton (dmt) yang menjadi harga 
terendah dari awal tahun. Akan tetapi, pemerintah 
menyakini pangsa pasar nikel akan membaik seiring 
dengan pulihnya ekonomi dunia dan adanya penyesuaian 
kebijakan, salah satunya kebijakan larangan ekspor nikel, 
yang  mendukung berjalannya hilirisasi nikel di dalam 
negeri. 
Selain itu, peluang pasar nikel diproyeksikan akan membaik 
seiring gencarnya transformasi energi terbarukan, yaitu 
peralihan penggunaan kendaraan konvesional menjadi 
kendaraan listrik. Nikel menjadi bahan baku utama 
dalam pembuatan baterai kendaraan listrik, yang akan 
menjadi peluang besar bagi perusahaan nikel di Indonesia 
ke depannya. Indonesia juga merupakan negara yang 
memiliki cadangan nikel terbesar  mencapai 17 miliar ton. 
Peluang pasar yang besar tersebut dapat dimanfaatkan 
dengan didukung oleh kebijakan ekspor yang selaras, agar 
Indonesia dapat menguasai pasar kendaraan listrik, baik 
skala nasional ataupun internasional.
Nickel Market Share
Throughout 2023, nickel prices in Indonesia had decreased 
due to declining demand from China, geopolitical sentiment 
in Russia and Ukraine, and the slowdown in the world 
economy.  The Ministry of Energy and Mineral Resources 
recorded that the reference price of nickel in Indonesia as 
of December 2023 was recorded at USD17,653/Dry metric 
ton (dmt), which was the lowest price from the beginning 
of the year. However, the government believes that nickel 
market share will improve in line with the recovery of the 
world economy and policy adjustments, one of which is 
the nickel export ban policy which is seen as beneficial 
for Indonesia, thus supporting the running of nickel 
downstream in the country. 
In addition, nickel market opportunities are projected 
to improve in line with the vigorous transformation of 
renewable energy, namely the transition of conventional 
vehicles to electric vehicles. Nickel is the main raw material 
in the manufacture of electric vehicle batteries, which will 
be a great opportunity for nickel companies in Indonesia in 
the future. Indonesia is also a country that has the largest 
nickel reserves reaching 17 billion tons. This large market 
opportunity can be utilized supported by aligned export 
policies, so that Indonesia can dominate the electric 
vehicle market, both on a national and international scale.
