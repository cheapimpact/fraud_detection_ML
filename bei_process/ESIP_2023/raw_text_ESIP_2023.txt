resistant, odorless, heat resistant and impact resistant. 
Indonesians know the term HDPE plastic bags as 
HD bags, plastic bags, asoy bags, HD plastic bags, or 
shopping bags. HDPE (High Density Polyethylene) is 
the raw material for this type of HDPE plastic, generally 
produced in the form of plastic bags, plastic roll, sheet 
plastic with various colors.
HDPE consists of several types:
-

for hot liquid packaging, hot food, or drinks.
-	
HDPE (HD) bags are generally used for bags of 
various daily groceries.
-	
HDPE Roll bags (HD Roll) are generally used as bags 
to fill fruits, vegetables or other goods and are also 
used as paper wrappers.
-	
HDPE Sheet (HD Sheet) is generally used as a 
base/coating of warm food containers or as food 
wrappers.
