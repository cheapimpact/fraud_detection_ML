The risk profile of finance companies is 
still maintained as can be seen from the non-
performing financing (NPF) net rate which was 
recorded at 0.72% in November 2023. The 
gearing ratio of finance companies remain 
positive and was recorded at 2.21x, which is still 
below the minimum limit of 10x.

company has a positive profit balance. Dividend 
payment must be approved by the shareholders at

performance of the Company in that period, 
without neglecting the financial condition of the 
Company and without reducing the right of the
