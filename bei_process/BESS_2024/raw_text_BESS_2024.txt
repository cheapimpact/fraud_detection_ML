Gambaran umum permintaan batu bara

Overview of global coal demand in 2024
Tren harga batu bara tahun 2024 umumnya 
mengalami penurunan. Hal ini terjadi karena 
produksi batu bara negara China menurun sebesar 
30%. Selain itu, melemahnya harga batu bara juga 
terjadi karena tingginya curah hujan di China yang 
menyebabkan produksi energi hydro meningkat 
sehingga mengurangi kebutuhan batu bara untuk 
pembangkit listrik. Mayoritas konsumsi batu bara di 
China yang dipakai untuk pembangkit listrik 
mencapai 53% dari seluruh total konsumsi, maka 
peralihan ke sumber energi terbarukan seperti 
tenaga hydro maupun tenaga air saat produksinya 
tinggi bisa membuat konsumsi batu bara menurun. 
Dengan China dan India menjadi negara utama 
yang mempengaruhi permintaan batu bara global, 
penurunan harga batu bara akan terjadi ketika 
salah satu dari dua negara ini mengurangi 
konsumsi atau meningkatkan produksinya.
Di sisi lain, perang Rusia-Ukraina yang masih 
berdampak signifikan pada industri batu bara 
Indonesia, 
terutama 
harga 
batu 
bara 
global. Kenaikan harga batu bara di negara Eropa 
terjadi karena gangguan pasokan dari Rusia, salah 
satu eksportir batu bara terbesar dunia. Sebagai 
negara penghasil batu bara ke 3 di dunia, tentunya 
Indonesia mendapatkan manfaat dari kenaikan 
harga ini, tetapi juga menghadapi tantangan terkait 
permintaan dan potensi lonjakan harga di masa 
depan.
The trend of coal prices in 2024 generally 
experienced a decline. This occurred as China's coal 
production decreased by 30%. In addition, the 
weakening of coal prices also occurred due to the 
high rainfall in China which caused hydro energy 
production to increase, reducing the need of coal for 
power plants. The majority of coal consumption in 
China used for power plants reaches 53% of total 
consumption, thus the shifting to renewable energy 
resources such as hydro power when the production 
is high could lead to reduce in coal consumption. 
With China and India being the main countries 
influencing global coal demand, coal prices will 
decline when one of these two countries reduces 
consumption or increases production.
On the other hand, the Russia-Ukraine war still 
brought significant impact on the Indonesian coal 
industry, especially global coal prices. The increase in 
coal prices in European countries occurred due to 
supply disruptions from Russia, one of the world's 
largest coal exporters. As the world's 3rd largest coal 
producing country, Indonesia certainly benefits from 
this price increase, but also faces challenges related 
to demand and potential price spikes in the future.

repayment ratio. The Company's liabilities to equity 
ratio (DER) in 2024 is 0.11 : 1, which is a decrease

Department conducts cash forecasts and maintains 
flexibility in funding by maintaining adequate credit 
facilities.
