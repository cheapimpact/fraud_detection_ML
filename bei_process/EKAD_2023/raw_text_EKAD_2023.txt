Production
OPP Production Process
The OPP Production Process consists of 3 series of processes, 
namely printing, coating and sliting. These three activities 
processed  at the Tangerang Pasar Kemis Factory, while the 
Surabaya Factory is only the Sliting Process.
But the Main Production Process is the Coating Process and 
The Zipper Process while the Printing Process is only carried 
out for special orders that are "Private Labels".
Printing Process is the process of coating OPP Film with Ink 
to print Logos, images or letters.

Proses Coating adalah proses melapisi OPP Film polos atau 
OPP Film yang sudah melalui proses cetakan (Printing) 
dengan resin menjadi OPP Jumbo Rolls.
Proses Sliting adalah Proses memotong OPP ukuran besar 
atau Jumbo Roll menjadi bentuk roll yang lebih kecil.
Kapasitas Mesin Coating dan Sliting masing-masing bisa 
mencapai 376 juta m2 per tahun. Penjualan OPP Pita 
Perekat mencapai 145 juta m2 ditahun 2023.
Proses Produksi Non OPP
Proses Produksi Non OPP terdiri dari 2 rangkaian yaitu 
Proses Rewinding dan Cutting.
Proses Rewinding adalah Proses Menggulung Ulang dari 
Bentuk Jumbo Roll menjadi bentuk Log roll.
Proses Cutting adalah Proses Memotong Non OPP ukuran 
Log Roll menjadi bentuk roll yang lebih kecil sesuai ukuran 
barang jadi.
Kapasitas Mesin Rewinding dan Cutting masing-masing 
bisa mencapai 36 juta m2 pertahun. Penjualan Non OPP 
mencapai 10 juta m2 dtahun 2023.
Perusahaan senantiasa menganalisa dan merencanakan 
penambahan kapasitas mesin sesuai dengan perkembangan 
pasar, baik untuk OPP maupun untuk Non OPP.

Coating process is the process of coating plain OPP Film 
or OPP Film that has gone through the molding process 
(Printing) with resin into OPP Jumbo Rolls.
Sliting process is the process of cutting a large size OPP or 
Jumbo Roll into a smaller roll shape.
The capacity of Coating and Sliting Machines can each reach 
376 million m2 per year. Sales of OPP Adhesive Tape reached 
145 million m2 in 2023.
Non OPP Production Process
Non OPP Production Process consists of 2 series, namely 
Rewinding and Cutting Process.
Rewinding Process is the Process of Re-Rolling from Jumbo 
Roll Shape to Log roll shape.
Cutting Process is the Process of Cutting Non OPP Log Roll 
size into a smaller roll shape according to the size of the 
finished goods.
Rewinding and Cutting Machine capacity can each reach 36 
million m2 per year. Non OPP sales reached 10 million m2 
in 2023.
The company always analyzes and plans to increase machine 
capacity to adapt market developments, both for OPP and 
for Non OPP.

Untuk memelihara dan menyesuaikan struktur permodalan, 
Perseroan dapat menyesuaikan usulan pembayaran dividen 
kepada pemegang saham atau mengusahakan tambahan 
pendanaan melalui pinjaman.
Kebijakan Perseroan adalah mempertahankan struktur
