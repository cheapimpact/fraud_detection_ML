Management Discussion and Analysis
Sektor kelapa sawit merupakan salah satu komoditas utama 
penopang subsektor perkebunan Indonesia yang memiliki 
kontribusi signifikan terhadap pertumbuhan perekonomian 
nasional. Kelapa sawit menyerap 16,2 juta orang tenaga 
kerja langsung maupun tidak langsung dan menciptakan 
kemandirian energi baru terbarukan biodiesel dengan 
pemanfaatan untuk pasar domestik mencapai 12,2 juta 
kiloliter pada 2023. Untuk itu, pemerintah terus berupaya 
meningkatkan penyerapan produk kelapa sawit Indonesia di 
pasar global, salah satunya melalui tata kelola perkebunan 
kelapa sawit yang semakin ramah lingkungan. Komitmen 
ini juga mendasari penerbitan Instruksi Presiden Nomor 6 
Tahun 2019 tentang Rencana Aksi Perkebunan Kelapa Sawit 
Berkelanjutan Tahun 2019-2024.
Hal ini tertuang Dalam laporan Statistik Perkebunan 
Unggulan Nasional 2021-2023, Kementerian Pertanian 
(Kementan) mengestimasikan luas perkebunan kelapa sawit 
Indonesia mencapai 16,83 juta hektar pada 2023. Lahan-
lahan ini tentunya diharapkan dapat menghasilkan kinerja 
yang optimal. Oleh sebab itu, pemerintah terus mendorong 
peremajaan kelapa sawit untuk mendorong keberlanjutan 
industri sawit secara jangka panjang. 
Hasil riset Gabungan Pengusaha Kelapa Sawit Indonesia 
(GAPKI) menunjukkan bahwa konsumsi minyak sawit 
domestik meningkat sebesar 9,08% dari 23,28 juta ton pada 
2022 menjadi 25,4 juta ton pada 2023, dengan penyerapan 
konsumsi biodiesel mendominasi sebesar 11,6 juta ton. 
Jumlah ekspor tahun 2023 diprediksi hanya mencapai 32,9 
juta ton, seiring dengan meningkatnya konsumsi domestik.
Palm oil is one of the primary commodities supporting 
Indonesia’s plantation subsector, a significant contributor to 
the national economic growth. The palm oil industry absorbs 
16.2 million direct and indirect workers and creates new 
renewable energy independence for biodiesel, with utilization 
for the domestic market reaching 12.2 million kiloliters by 
2023. For this reason, the government continuously strives 
to increase the absorption of Indonesian palm oil products 
in the global market through environmentally friendly 
management of oil palm plantations. This commitment also 
underlies the issuance of Presidential Instruction No. 6 of 
2019 regarding the Action Plan for Sustainable Palm Oil 
Plantations for 2019-2024.
In the 2021-2023 National Leading Plantation Statistics 
Report, the Ministry of Agriculture estimates that Indonesia’s 
oil palm plantation area has reached 16.83 million hectares 
by 2023. These lands are indeed expected to demonstrate 
production performance. Therefore, the government 
continues to encourage oil palm replanting to drive the 
long-term sustainability of the palm oil industry. 
Research by the Indonesian Palm Oil Association (GAPKI) 
shows that domestic palm oil consumption increased by 
9.08%, from 23.28 million tons in 2022 to 25.4 million tons in 
2023, with biodiesel consumption dominating at 11.6 million 
tons. Total exports in 2023 were predicted to reach 32.9 
million tons, in line with increasing domestic consumption.

Production Flow
The main product processed from oil palm is known as the 
fruit bunches. The flesh of the fruit bunch produces crude 
palm oil, which is processed into the raw material for cooking 
oil. Numerous advantages of vegetable oil from palm oil 
include low price, low cholesterol, and high beta carotene. 
Furthermore, palm oil can be processed into raw material for 
margarine, while palm kernel oil can be processed for fatty 
alcohol and the cosmetics industry.

After being released from the bunch, oil palm fruit is 
processed into two main products: CPO, extracted from 
the mesocarp or fruit flesh, and PKO, extracted from the 
hard seed in the center. The first step is fruit pressing to 
squash CPO from the mesocarp. Subsequently, the palm 
oil is filtered and purified to prevent cross-contamination to 
meet CPO standard specifications. 
Furthermore, CPO is transferred to the refinery plant to 
be processed into RBDPO and PFAD as raw materials for 
vegetable oil processing companies (cooking oil, cream, 
and margarine), oleochemicals (used in detergents and 
lubricants), biodiesel (fuel), and lauric acid (used in cosmetics 
and soaps). 
As for the remaining oil palm kernels, the palm kernel 
shells are shattered and separated. Afterward, the PK shells 
are extracted to be sold as biofuels, while the kernels are 
crushed to produce PKO and PKE. 
PKO can be used as raw materials for companies that 
produce food products, such as non-dairy cream and ice 
cream, while the rest of the production from PKE is generally 
used to make animal feeds.
Segment Performance
Unfortunately, in 2023, the condition of the palm oil industry 
was not better than the previous year, as global uncertainty, 
geopolitics conditions, and tightening exports of palm oil 
and its derivatives due to the government’s DMO policy 
continued. This situation greatly impacted the purchase price 
of raw materials, remaining stock amount, and selling price of 
products, which resulted in an increase in production costs.
Overall, this segment’s operational performance showed a 
decline in production and sales compared to the previous 
year due to the large amount of remaining stock of products 
with high production costs and decreasing global product 
selling prices. Consequently, the Company will continue 
to maximize domestic sales by analyzing market needs 
and remaining stocks, including selling prices for products 
to be produced for marketing. Controlled purchase of 
raw materials, in terms of volume and cost, and improved 
operational efficiency and effectiveness will always be 
empowered to achieve better performance.

produk curah cair, seperti kimia cair, CPO dan lainnya.

kapasitas 2.400 ton sebanyak 8 (delapan) tangki.

products, such as liquid chemicals, CPO, etc. The rental 
services are contracted with a price per kg system adjusted 
to the type of products that will be stored and the size of 
the storage tank for the main customers and suppliers. The 
Company leased a total of 26 storage tanks consisting of 18 
tanks with a capacity of 3,150 tons and 8 (eight) tanks with a 
capacity of 2,400 tons.

Kinerja Segmen
Di tengah ketidakpastian global, sektor perkebunan sawit 
menghadapi berbagai tantangan termasuk penurunan 
produksi hingga anjloknya harga komoditas. Selain itu, 
fenomena iklim El Nino turut menimbulkan disrupsi atas 
produksi sawit Indonesia yang akhirnya memicu penundaan 
panen karena buah terlambat matang akibat tidak adanya 
hujan. Perseroan juga mengalami peningkatan biaya 
produksi terkait upah, pupuk, dan logistik.
Segment Performance
The logistics segment delivered a considerably improved 
performance in 2023 due to a substantial reduction in the 
group’s storage tank usage; as such, lease costs could be 
optimized for other customers. In addition, the adjustment 
to increase lease prices for customers and the large storage 
volume of Palm Acid Oil (PAO) products with high storage 
rental prices positively affected the segment’s overall 
performance. 
With the decrease in the group’s storage tank usage, the 
Company was able to actively market its leasing services to 
other customers to maintain its performance growth. Through 
experience, excellent service, and strategic location, the 
Company optimally implemented marketing strategies and 
provided competitive prices to other customers for liquid 
bulk products that required extra storage space.
Plantation Segment
The plantation segment has been developed in stages as an 
effort to meet and comply with the provisions of Law of the 
Republic of Indonesia No. 39 of 2014 regarding Plantations, 
especially in terms of the provision of palm oil raw materials 
from own plantation of at least 20% of the total raw 
materials needed. This is done to strengthen the upstream 
industry sector to provide raw materials for production to 
subsidiaries sustainably.
The Company’s group has planned to continuously increase 
the area of its plantations through Target Companies, i.e., 
in North Sumatra, Riau, and South Sumatra. The initial 
development phase takes place in South Sumatra with a 
total land area of 967.74 hectares, and by the end of 2023, 
the total planted area will reach 427.88 hectares.
Segment Performance
Amid global uncertainty, the palm oil plantation sector faces 
various challenges, including declining production and falling 
commodity prices. In addition, the El Nino phenomenon has 
contributed to the disruption in national palm oil production, 
triggering harvest delays as fruits ripened late due to lack of 
rain. The Company also experiences increased production 
costs related to wages, fertilizers, and logistics.

tekanan akibat dampak yang berkelanjutan dari tahun 2022,

kontribusi untuk total produksi sebesar 1.157.000 
ton dengan pendapatan sebesar Rp10,41 triliun dan 
net profit sebesar Rp153 miliar.

781,943 tons in 2022 to 673,540 tons in 2023. This drop was 
also reflected in the sales volume, which decreased by 14.7% 
from 2022, with 476,395 tons sold in 2023 compared to 
557,618 tons. The decrease in production and sales volumes 
was due to the Company’s efforts to control remaining 
product stock.
In 2023, the Company’s operating revenues were Rp5.42 
trillion, down from Rp7.50 trillion in 2022. The comprehensive 
loss incurred was Rp163.50 billion, an increase from Rp28.73 
billion in 2022. However, the Company’s total assets 
increased by 4.62%, from Rp2.56 trillion in 2022 to Rp2.68 
trillion in 2023.
The Company’s performance in 2023 was under pressure 
due to the lingering impact of 2022, with the Company still 
having inventory of production raw materials purchased 
at high prices. Additionally, the recent export restrictions 
and drop in global prices of CPO and its derivatives had a 
significant impact on the Company’s performance, making it 
difficult to achieve previously set targets.
Completion of various downstream projects in 
2024, with structured integration with the Go-Green 
concept, is projected to contribute 1,157,000 tons 
to total production. The revenue is expected to be 
Rp10.41 trillion with a net profit of Rp153 billion.
