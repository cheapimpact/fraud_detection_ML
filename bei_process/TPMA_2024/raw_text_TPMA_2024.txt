Tinjauan Ekonomi
Economic Performance
Perekonomian Indonesia menunjukkan kinerja yang 
kuat dan stabil sepanjang tahun 2024, sebagaimana 
tercermin dari pertumbuhan Produk Domestik Bruto 
(PDB) sebesar 5,1%. Stabilitas ekonomi dan politik, 
didukung oleh kebijakan fiskal dan moneter yang 
efektif, berhasil meningkatkan kepercayaan investor 
dan mendorong realisasi investasi di berbagai sektor.
Pertumbuhan ekonomi nasional pada tahun 2024 
didorong oleh beberapa sektor utama yaitu industri 
pengolahan sebagai kontributor terbesar dengan 
pertumbuhan sebesar 4,5% yang didukung oleh 
peningkatan produksi manufaktur dan ekspor produk 
olahan, perdagangan besar dan eceran bertumbuh 
sebesar 4,8% yang didukung oleh meningkatnya 
konsumsi domestik dan daya beli masyarakat, informasi 
dan komunikasi yang mencapai pertumbuhan tertinggi 
sebesar 9,3% seiring dengan digitalisasi dan penetrasi 
internet yang semakin luas; serta pengeluaran dan 
konsumsi.
Dari sisi pengeluaran, konsumsi rumah tangga tetap 
menjadi pendorong utama dengan pertumbuhan 
4,9%. Investasi atau Pembentukan Modal Tetap Bruto 
(PMTB) juga menunjukkan peningkatan sebesar 4,6%, 
mencerminkan kepercayaan investor terhadap prospek 
ekonomi Indonesia.
Di tengah tantangan global, kinerja ekspor barang 
dan jasa Indonesia tumbuh sebesar 3,2%, sementara 
impor meningkat 3,5%. Meskipun demikian, neraca 
perdagangan tetap terjaga dengan baik. Tingkat inflasi 
sepanjang tahun 2024 juga terjaga stabil sebesar 2,8%, 
berada dalam kisaran target yang ditetapkan oleh 
pemerintah dan Bank Indonesia. Hal ini menunjukkan 
kemampuan pemerintah dalam menjaga stabilitas 
harga dan daya beli masyarakat.
Hingga saat ini, industri pelayaran nasional masih 
memegang peran strategis sebagai tulang punggung 
kelancaran 
logistik 
nasional. 
Namun 
dalam 
perjalanannya, pelayaran nasional masih dihadapkan 
pada sejumlah tantangan yang perlu dinavigasi secara 
strategis, seperti sistem perpajakan yang membebani 
para pelaku usaha di sektor pelayaran nasional. Dalam 
pandangan INSA, angkutan laut memiliki potensi besar 
untuk dijadikan bagian dari pengoptimalan infrastruktur 
yang sudah ada sesuai kebijakan pemerintah dalam 
meningkatkan konektivitas dan pemerataan ekonomi. 
Untuk itu, perlu adanya dukungan pemerintah dalam 
memberikan fasilitas insentif pajak maupun suku bunga 
perbankan terhadap galangan dalam negeri, sehingga 
dapat lebih kompetitif.
The Indonesian economy demonstrated strong and 
stable performance throughout 2024, reflected in a 
Gross Domestic Product (GDP) growth rate of 5.1%. 
Economic and political stability, bolstered by effective 
fiscal and monetary policies, has successfully increased 
investor 
confidence 
and 
encouraged 
investment 
realization across various sectors.
National economic growth in 2024 was fueled by 
several key sectors. The processing industry stood 
as the largest contributor, achieving a growth rate of 
4.5% due to increased manufacturing production and 
exports of processed goods. Wholesale and retail trade 
also experienced growth, increasing by 4.8%, supported 
by 
rising 
domestic 
consumption 
and 
enhanced 
purchasing power. Furthermore, the information and 
communication sector has reached the highest growth 
rate at 9.3%, fueled by widespread digitalization and 
internet penetration, along with increased spending 
and consumption.
Household consumption continues to be the main 
driver of spending, increasing by 4.9%. Investment, or 
Gross Fixed Capital Formation (PMTB), also experienced 
a growth of 4.6%, reflecting investor confidence in 
Indonesia’s economy outlook.
Amid global challenges, Indonesia’s export performance 
for goods and services increased by 3.2%, while imports 
rose by 3.5%. Nevertheless, the trade balance remains 
well-maintained. Throughout 2024, the inflation rate 
is stable at 2.8%, within the target range set by the 
government and Bank Indonesia. This reflects the 
government’s ability to maintain price stability and 
protect people’s purchasing power.
To date, the national shipping industry continues to 
serve as a strategic backbone for efficient national 
logistics. However, the sector still faces several 
challenges that must be navigated strategically, 
including a taxation system that burdens businesses in 
this industry. According to INSA, sea transportation has 
significant potential to enhance existing infrastructure 
in line with government policies aimed at increasing 
connectivity and promoting economic equality. For 
this reason, government support is crucial in providing 
tax incentives and favorable bank interest rates for 
domestic shipyards, enabling them to compete more 
effectively.

Operational Overview of Each Business Segment 
Aktivitas operasional yang dijalankan Perseroan berupa 
pengangkutan yang ditunjang oleh kapal tunda dan 
kapal tongkang. Layanan pengangkutan terbagi menjadi 
2 (dua) jenis, yaitu:
Pengangkutan Transshipment
Jenis pengangkutan barang/komoditas ini menggunakan 
kapal tunda dan kapal tongkang dengan tujuan kapal 
induk (mother vessel) yang kemudian akan melanjutkan 
proses transportasi. Layanan transshipment disediakan 
untuk memfasilitasi keterbatasan kapal induk untuk 
merapat ke pelabuhan muat. 
Selain menggunakan kapal tunda dan kapal tongkang, 
transshipment 
juga 
menggunakan 
crane 
barge 
saat pemindahan barang. Cakupan layanan pada 
transshipment umumnya memiliki jarak pengangkutan 
tidak lebih dari 250 (dua ratus lima puluh) mil.
Pengangkutan Antar Pulau
Jenis 
pengangkutan 
ini 
menyediakan 
fasilitas 
pengangkutan barang/komoditas antar pulau dari 
pelabuhan ke pelabuhan bongkar baik dalam negeri 
atau regional di seluruh wilayah Indonesia.
Operasi transshipment dan pengangkutan antar pulau 
berimbang mendukung kinerja Perseroan. Perseroan 
senantiasa 
mengatur 
dengan 
saksama 
shipment 
planning agar skema kepatuhan pelanggan dapat 
terpenuhi dengan baik.
Terlepas dari jenis layanannya, Perseroan secara rutin 
memantau keselamatan dan keamanan pelayaran 
kapal dengan menggunakan radar serta informasi dari 
awak kapal tunda. Dengan berpegang pada peraturan 
Undang-Undang Republik Indonesia Nomor 17 Tahun 
2008 tentang Pelayaran, Perseroan senantiasa berupaya 
menjaga kepercayaan pelanggan melalui pengawasan 
aktivitas operasional yang intensif selama 24 jam.
Armada Kapal   
Per 31 Desember 2024, Perseroan anak perusahaan dan 
perusahaan asosiasi memiliki kapal yang terdiri dari:
The 
Company’s 
operational 
activities 
provide 
transportation services using tugs and barges. These 
services are categorized into 2 (two) types:
Transshipment
This service employs tugboats and barges to transport 
goods and commodities to a mother vessel, which then 
continues the transportation process. Transshipment 
services are provided to address the inability of mother 
vessels to dock at loading ports.
In addition to using tugs and barges, transshipment 
also utilizes a crane barge for transporting goods. The 
range of transshipment services typically extends to a 
maximum of 250 (two hundred fifty) miles.
Inter-Island Transportation
This service provides transportation of goods and 
commodities between islands, from ports to discharge 
ports, both domestically and regionally throughout 
Indonesia.
Balanced transshipment and inter-island transportation 
operations also enhance the Company’s performance. 
The Company consistently manages shipment planning 
with care to adequately meet the customer’s compliance 
requirements.
Regardless of the type of service, the safety and security 
of shipping vessels are constantly monitored by the 
Company through radar and real-time information 
from the ship crews. By adhering to Republic of 
Indonesia Number 17 of 2008 laws regarding shipping, 
the Company is dedicated to preserving its customers’ 
trust by providing intensive 24-hour monitoring of all 
operational activities.

Memanfaatkan peluang ini, Perseroan memfokuskan 
bisnisnya di bidang jasa pengangkutan dan merumuskan 
beberapa strategi usaha untuk menjaga kelangsungan 
bisnisnya. Fokus strategi Perseroan adalah sebagai 
berikut:
Diversifikasi Layanan
Perseroan terus berupaya melakukan diversifikasi 
jenis layanan selain pengangkutan batu bara. Saat ini, 
Perseroan telah menyediakan layanan pengangkutan 
untuk berbagai jenis komoditas, seperti biji nikel, 
iron sand, clinkers, wood pellets, dan woodchip, yang 
merupakan bahan baku utama industri kertas, dan 
sponge rotary kiln, yang merupakan pengganti scrap 
serta gypsum. 
Optimalisasi Pemanfaatan Armada
Ketersediaan dan kualitas armada memiliki peranan 
yang krusial untuk memenuhi permintaan pelanggan 
yang terus meningkat. Selain menambah armada secara 
bertahap, Perseroan juga memetakan jadwal pelayaran 
yang detail agar mampu memberikan layanan yang 
optimal bagi pelanggan. Perseroan juga bekerja sama 
dengan pihak ketiga dalam hal penyediaan kapal. 
Selain itu, Perseroan menyediakan spot charter sebagai 
bentuk pemanfaatan armada untuk keperluan jangka 
pendek pelanggan.

senantiasa 
menjalin 
hubungan 
yang

INSA, Perseroan dapat terlibat dan berkontribusi

By capitalizing on this opportunity, the Company 
prioritizes its operations in the transportation service 
sector and develops strategies to ensure business 
continuity. The Company implements several strategies 
as follows:
Service Diversification
The Company aims to broaden its services beyond coal 
transportation. It offers transportation services for 
various commodities, including nickel ore, iron sand, 
clinkers, wood pellets, woodchips, which serve as raw 
materials for the paper industry, and sponge rotary 
kilns, which act as substitutes for scrap and gypsum.
Fleet Optimization
Fleet availability and quality remain a priority for 
efficiently managing the increasing customer demand. 
In addition to expanding the number of fleets, the 
Company carefully plans shipping schedules to provide 
the best possible service to its customers. Furthermore, 
the Company maintains strategic partnerships with 
third parties to supply ships. Additionally, the Company 
offers spot charters to optimize fleet utilization for 
short-term customer needs.
Beneficial Partnership
The Company is committed to fostering harmonious 
and mutually beneficial partnerships with all parties, 
including regulators, business partners, customers, and 
stakeholders. To reflect this commitment, the Company 
maintains an active membership in the Indonesian 
National Shipowners Association (INSA).
INSA unites various industry components, both 
domestic and international, to share information 
and collaborate. Through INSA, the Company stays 
informed about developments in the shipping industry 
while also sharing insights and building constructive 
relationships with competitors and related associations.

Revenues
In 2024, the Company and its Subsidiary successfully 
recorded operating revenue of US$ 119,076,001, 
reflecting a 7.1% increase from US$ 111,227,912 in 2023. 
This growth was primarily driven by a rise in shipping 
volume, especially on transhipment.
Direct Costs
The Group’s direct costs amounted to US$ 76,525,216, 
reflecting a 13.1% increase from US$ 67,656,318 in 
2023. This rise was primarily attributed to higher vessel 
charter costs and depreciation of fixed assets.
Gross Profit
The Group’s gross profit fell by 2.3%, decreasing from 
US$ 43,571,594 in 2023 to US$ 42,550,785 in 2024. This 
decline resulted from higher vessel charter costs and 
depreciation of fixed assets.
General and Administrative Expenses
In 2024, the Company’s general and administrative 
expenses reached US$ 8,526,419, marking an 8.5% 
increase from US$ 7,860,229 in 2023. This rise was 
primarily due to higher salaries, work benefits, and 
taxes.
Financial Expenses
Meanwhile, in 2024, the Group’s financial expenses 
were recorded at US$ 4,260,735, reflecting an increase 
of 84.0% from US$ 2,315,519 in 2023. This increase 
resulted from higher bank loan interest payments, the 
addition of bank loans for purchasing new fleets, and 
the acquisition of subsidiaries.

Pelaksanaan tata kelola yang disiplin, konsisten, dan 
berkesinambungan menjadi fondasi bisnis yang fundamental 
bagi Perseroan untuk meningkatkan pertumbuhan kinerjanya 
secara berkelanjutan. Dengan struktur tata kelola yang jelas, 
Perseroan berkomitmen untuk mengelola kegiatan usahanya 
dengan akuntabel, adil, dan bertanggung jawab dengan tetap 
melindungi kepentingan seluruh pemangku kepentingan dan 
mematuhi peraturan yang berlaku.
The establishment of disciplined, consistent, and sustainable governance is a crucial 
foundation for the Company to enhance its growth in a sustainable manner. With a 
well-defined governance structure, the Company commits to managing its business 
activities in an accountable, fair, and responsible way while safeguarding the interests 
of all stakeholders and complying with relevant regulations.
