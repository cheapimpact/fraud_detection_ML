The global coal demand throughout 2023 increased for the 
first time, exceeding 8.5 billion tons, equivalent to an increase 
of 1.4%. Nevertheless, there was a shift in coal demand and 
production which was increasingly concentrated towards Asia. 
The volume of coal demand from India increased by 8% and 
China by 5% in line with the increasing electricity demand. On

reduce coal use as an energy source.

developments in various aspects such as production, regulations, 
and environmental impacts, thereby providing a significant 
contribution to the Indonesian economy.
Furthermore, the realization of Indonesian coal production in 
2023 reached 775.2 million tons and managed to meet domestic 
needs of 213 million tons, equivalent to 120% of the target of 177

increasing market demand. The increase in commodity production 
and sales has a positive impact on the community’s economy and 
encourages significant employment.

employment in mining sector is investment growth. Investment 
in mineral and coal (Minerba) sub-sector in 2023 was USD7.46 
billion or 96.8% of the 2023 target of USD7.7 billion. However, 
in 2024, the global coal market demand is expected to weaken 
due to the growth of renewable energy. Nevertheless, domestic 
demand will still grow due to the 35 GW projects that must be 
completed.

Perbandingan Target dan Kinerja 
Tahun 2023 
Pada tahun 2023, volume produksi Perseroan mencapai 46,12 juta 
ton. Sementara itu, volume penjualan tahun 2023 sejumlah 46,89 
juta ton, lebih tinggi daripada target yang ditetapkan, dengan 
pangsa pasar terbesar, yakni Indonesia, Tiongkok, dan India.
Prospek Usaha
International Energy Agency (IEA) mengamati bahwa perdagangan 
batu bara global tahun 2024 akan menurun karena adanya 
peningkatan pembangkit listrik tenaga nuklir di Tiongkok, India, 
dan Uni Eropa mendorong penurunan konsumsi batu bara. Selain 
itu, adanya komitmen global terhadap perubahan iklim melalui 
Perjanjian Paris telah membawa perubahan struktural, mendorong 
pertumbuhan energi terbarukan dan berkelanjutan.  
Meskipun demikian, Indonesia menargetkan produksi batu bara 
sebanyak 922 juta ton di tahun 2024, dengan target domestic 
market obligation (DMO) sebesar 220 juta ton. Target produksi 
tersebut disebutkan akan mendorong pemanfaatan batu bara 
untuk sektor kelistrikan dan industri dalam negeri. Selain itu, 
sebagai eksportir utama batu bara termal di dunia, permintaan 
batu bara ke sejumlah negara Asia diproyeksi akan meningkat. 
Proyeksi pengiriman untuk 2 bulan pertama tahun 2024 melonjak 
hampir 25% dibanding periode yang sama di tahun 2023. Kondisi 
ini sejalan dengan peningkatan permintaan untuk rantai pasokan 
bahan bangunan dan suku cadang di Tiongkok, serta permintaan 
untuk memenuhi aktivitas pabrik semen dan baja di Vietnam.
Proyeksi 2024 Perseroan 
Perseroan menargetkan volume produksi tahun 2024 berkisar 
antara 49-52 juta ton, sedangkan volume penjualan antara 50-53 
juta ton.

Business Prospects
The International Energy Agency (IEA) observes that global coal 
trade in 2024 will decline due to the increase in nuclear power 
plants in China, India, and the European Union that drives down 
coal consumption. In addition, the global commitment to climate 
change through the Paris Agreement has brought structural 
changes, encouraging the growth of renewable and sustainable 
energy.
Nevertheless, Indonesia is targeting coal production of 922 million 
tons in 2024, with a domestic market obligation (DMO) target of 
220 million tons. Such production target is said to encourage the 
utilization of coal for the electricity sector and domestic industry. 
Furthermore, as the main exporter of thermal coal in the world, 
coal demand from a number of Asian countries is projected to 
increase. Delivery for the first 2 months of 2024 is projected to 
jump almost 25% compared to the same period in 2023. This 
condition is in line with increasing demand for the supply chain of 
building materials and spare parts in China, as well as demand to 
meet cement and steel factory activities in Vietnam.

between 49-52 million tons, while sales volume between 50-53 
million tons.
