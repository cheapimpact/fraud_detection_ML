Indonesia’s economy in 2023, measured by Gross Domestic 
Product (GDP) at current market prices, reached Rp20,892.4 
trillion and GDP per capita reached Rp75.0 million, supported 
by solid domestic demand. Household consumption grew by 
4.47% YoY, along with continued mobility, stable purchasing 
power, and high consumer confidence. Meanwhile, 
government consumption grew by 2.81% YoY. Overall 
investment growth increased to 5.02% YoY, in line with the

Indonesia’s continued strong economic growth is also 
reflected in terms of Business Fields and spatially. In terms

Warehousing, Accommodation and Food Services, as well

as the main contributor to growth, also grew well alongside 
strong domestic and global demand. Meanwhile, spatially, 
economic growth in the fourth quarter of 2023 in most 
regions of Indonesia was higher than the growth in the 
previous quarter. The highest economic growth in the fourth 
quarter of 2023 was recorded in the Sulawesi-Maluku-Papua 
(Sulampua) region, followed by Kalimantan, Java, Bali-Nusa 
Tenggara (Balinusra), and Sumatra.

PROSPEK USAHA
Sebagai industri yang erat kaitannya dengan industri properti, 
industri peralatan pengecatan juga dapat dikategorikan 
sebagai industri yang siklikal dan oleh karenanya sangat 
bergantung oleh perkembangan ekonomi makro. Selain 
itu, perkembangan sektor infrastruktur juga secara tidak 
langsung akan memberikan dampak positif melalui sektor 
properti. Berdasarkan data yang diberikan oleh pemerintah 
Indonesia serta mengusung peningkatan produktivitas 
untuk transformasi ekonomi menuju visi Indonesia Maju 
2045, pembangunan infrastruktur menjadi salah satu fokus 
kebijakan pemerintah di tahun 2023 mendatang, Pemerintah 
RI mengalokasikan anggaran infrastruktur sebesar Rp422,7 
triliun dalam Rancangan Anggaran Pendapatan dan Belanja 
Negara (RAPBN) 2024.  Angka tersebut naik 5,8% dari 
anggaran infrastruktur 2023 yang dialokasikan sebesar 
Rp399,6 triliun. Anggaran infrastruktur 2024 mendatang 
tercatat jadi yang terbesar dalam masa pemerintahan Presiden 
Joko Widodo. Jumlah tersebut dicanangkan untuk biaya 
pembagunan jalan daerah, pembangunan Ibu Kota Negara 
(IKN), renovasi stadion, hingga sarana prasarana pendidikan 
dan kesehatan. 
Dengan positifnya outlook industri infrastruktur dan properti 
di tahun 2024 jika dibandingkan dengan tahun 2022, akan 
memberikan efek multiplier terhadap industri-industri lainnya 
yang terkait dengan sektor infrastruktur dan properti, salah 
satunya adalah industri home appliance seperti keramik, cat, 
dan perlengkapan pendukung pengecatan lainnya, sehingga 
Perseroan optimis akan menorehkan performa bisnisnya lebih 
baik di masa mendatang.

BUSINESS PROSPECTS
As an industry closely related to the property sector, the 
painting equipment industry can also be categorized as 
cyclical and therefore highly dependent on macroeconomic 
developments. In addition, the development of the 
infrastructure sector will indirectly have a positive impact 
through the property sector. Based on data provided by the 
Indonesian government and aiming to increase productivity 
for economic transformation towards the vision of Indonesia 
Maju 2045, infrastructure development becomes one of the 
government’s policy focuses in the upcoming year 2023. 
The Indonesian government has allocated an infrastructure 
budget of Rp422.7 trillion in the Draft State Revenue and 
Expenditure Budget (RAPBN) for 2024. This figure represents 
an increase of 5.8% from the infrastructure budget allocated 
in 2023, which amounted to Rp399.6 trillion. The upcoming 
infrastructure budget for 2024 is set to be the largest amount 
during President Joko Widodo’s administration. The amount 
is earmarked for regional road construction, the development 
of the new capital city (IKN), stadium renovations, as well as 
education and health facilities.
With the positive outlook for the infrastructure and property 
industries in 2024 compared to 2022, it will have a multiplier 
effect on other industries related to the infrastructure and 
property sectors, including the home appliance industry such 
as ceramics, paints, and other painting support equipment. 
Therefore, the Company is optimistic about achieving better 
business performance in the future.
