Economic Review
Pada tahun 2023, ekonomi global mengalami perlambatan 
pertumbuhan 
yang 
signifikan. 
Pertumbuhan 
Produk 
Domestik Bruto (PDB) global menurun dari 3,1% pada tahun

TINJAUAN SEKTOR DIGITAL
Digital Sector Review
Di tengah perlambatan ekonomi Indonesia, sektor digital 
juga terkena dampaknya, walau dengan efek yang beragam. 
Menurut laporan dari Google, Temasek dan Bain & Company, 
sektor digital Indonesia mengalami perlambatan dari tingkat 
pertumbuhan sebesar 20% di 2022 menjadi hanya 8% di 2023, 
terutama didorong oleh sektor e-commerce. Selain ekonomi 
global dan ketidakpastian kondisi politik domestik, faktor 
kunci lain atas perlambatan ini adalah karena terjadinya 
stabilisasi konsumsi umum seraya masyarakat melakukan 
penyesuaian terhadap pemulihan pasca pandemi.
Walaupun 
dengan 
tantangan-tantangan 
tersebut, 
beberapa segmen dari ekonomi digital masih menunjukan 
ketangguhannya. Sektor transportasi sebagai contoh, 
masih berhasil mempertahankan momentumnya, didorong 
oleh peningkatan permintaan dari layanan ride-hailing 
sejalan dengan dicabutnya larangan terkait COVID. Sama 
persisnya juga terjadi dengan media online yang mengalami 
pertumbuhan yang stabil, dimana GMV mengalami kenaikan 
5% pada 2023 vs 6% pada 2022, didorong oleh pertumbuhan 
di layanan periklanan dan streaming.
In 2023, the global economy witnessed a notable deceleration 
in growth. Global Gross Domestic Product (GDP) growth 
declined from 3.1% in 2022 to a modest 2.6% in 2023, 
according to the World Bank report. This downturn can be 
ascribed to several factors, including political instability 
and reduced commodity demand, with China’s economic 
slowdown exerting a particularly significant influence.
 
Indonesia, too, grappled with economic challenges alike 
to that of the global slowdown. According to the Indonesia 
Statistics (BPS) report, Indonesia’s GDP growth rate fell 
from 5.3% in 2022 to 5.1% in 2023. Meanwhile, the Rupiah 
witnessed a gradual weakening throughout 2023. Despite an 
initial strengthening in the first half of the year at Rp14,670/
USD in April 2023, the currency lost ground in the latter half to 
more than Rp15,900/USD, prompting interventions from the 
Bank Indonesia (BI) to stabilize the Rupiah value.   
Amid Indonesia’s economic slowdown, the digital sector felt 
its impact as well, albeit with varying effects. According to a 
report by Google, Temasek and Bain & Company, Indonesia’s 
digital sector slowed from a growth of 20% in 2022 to only 
8% in 2023, mostly driven by the e-commerce sector. Besides 
global economic and domestic political uncertainties, another 
key factor for this deceleration was the general consumption 
stabilizing as the society continued to adjust with the post 
pandemic recovery.
Despite the challenges, certain segments of the digital 
economy showed resilience. The transport sector, for 
instance, maintained its momentum, driven by increased 
demand for ride-hailing services as COVID-related restrictions 
were lifted. Similarly, online media witnessed stable growth, 
with a 5% increase in GMV in 2023 vs 6% in 2022, fueled by 
growth in advertising and streaming services.

supporting locally produced goods.

capital city. While major cities continue to dominate the digital

Iklan berbasis Cloud Digital
Perseroan bertujuan untuk memberdayakan bisnis kliennya 
dengan menyediakan infrastruktur digital yang mereka 
butuhkan, tidak hanya untuk meningkatkan jangkauan 
pelanggan mereka, tetapi juga melakukannya dengan 
lebih presisi. Solusi periklanan berbasis Cloud Digital 
Perseroan ini memungkinkan klien-klien mereka untuk dapat 
menyesuaikan kampanye pemasaran mereka dengan lebih 
lincah sesuai dengan minat dinamis pelanggan mereka. 
Melalui berbagai paket yang disesuaikan (tailor-made), 
Perseroan membantu klien dalam mengembangkan bisnis 
mereka secara efektif sambil berevolusi ke era digital.
Perseroan juga berupaya menerapkan prinsip keberlanjutan 
pada segmen Iklan berbasis Cloud Digital. Perseroan menilai 
segmen ini memiliki dampak positif terhadap lingkungan 
karena penggunaan teknologi cloud memungkinkan iklan 
untuk diakses dan didistribusikan secara online, mengurangi 
ketergantungan pada pencetakan bahan untuk iklan 
konvensional seperti brosur, pamflet atau materi iklan fisik 
lainnya. Dampak dari langkah ini sangat signifikan, terutama 
dalam hal penggunaan kertas dan pengelolaan limbah.
Kinerja positif juga terlihat pada segmen bisnis Iklan 
berbasis Cloud Digital, di mana Perseroan mencatatkan 
pertumbuhan jumlah layar iklan, dari 24.732 menjadi 27.596 
pada tahun 2023. Bekerjasama dengan ZKTeco, Perseroan 
berpotensi melebarkan sayap melalui layanan canggihnya 
ke 49 negara dengan memadukan solusi Infrastructure-as-
a-Service (IAAS), Content Management System (CMS) dan 
Managed Service.
Software-as-a-Service
Perseroan mengembangkan segmen bisnis Software-as-a-
Service (SaaS), salah satunya berupa platform Whatsapp 
for Business (WABA). Teknologi ini bukan hanya digunakan 
sebagai alat komunikasi efektif antara bisnis dan pelanggan, 
tetapi juga sebagai alat untuk mendukung keberlanjutan 
lingkungan. Dalam konteks pengembangan platform WABA 
terdapat beberapa aspek yang menunjukkan bagaimana 
Perseroan menerapkan keberlanjutan lingkungan. 
Penggunaan platform WABA ini secara efektif mengurangi 
kontak fisik antar manusia. Melalui interaksi online, baik untuk 
promosi produk atau jasa, komunikasi layanan pelanggan 
sampai dengan pemesanan, Perseroan mendorong adopsi 
praktik “nirkontak”. Dengan mengurangi kontak fisik, 
Perseroan secara langsung membantu memitigasi potensi 
produksi limbah yang terkait dengan interaksi manusia. 
Teknologi pada platform WABA pun berperan sebagai sarana 
efisien yang mendukung transisi dari media promosi cetak 
ke media digital dengan mengurangi dampak lingkungan 
yang dihasilkan dari pencetakan material promosi.
Digital Cloud Advertising 
The Company aims to empower its clients’ businesses by 
providing them with the digital infrastructure that they need 
not only to increase their customer reach, but also do so 
with more precision. The Company’s digital cloud advertising 
solutions allow its clients to adapt their marketing campaigns 
with better agility according to their customers’ dynamic 
interests. Through tailor-made packages, the Company 
assists clients in growing their businesses effectively while 
evolving into the digital age. 
The Company also endeavors to apply sustainability 
principles to the Digital Cloud-Based Advertising segment. 
The Company perceives this segment as having a positive 
environmental impact due to the use of cloud technology, 
which enables ads to be accessed and distributed online, 
reducing the reliance on printed materials for conventional 
advertising such as brochures, pamphlets, or other physical 
advertising materials. The implications of this step are highly 
significant, particularly in terms of paper consumption and 
waste management. 
Positive performance is also evident in the Cloud-Based Digital 
Advertising business segment, where the Company reported 
a growth in the number of advertising screens, from 24,732 
in 2022 to 27,596 in 2023. In collaboration with ZKTeco, the 
Company has the potential to expand its advanced services 
to 49 countries, integrating Infrastructure-as-a-Service (IAAS), 
Content Management System (CMS) and Managed Service 
solutions. 
Software-as-a-Service 
The Company is developing the Software-as-a-Service 
(SaaS) business segment, one of them in the form of the 
Whatsapp for Business (WABA) platform. This technology is 
not only used as an effective communication tool between 
businesses and customers but also as a tool to support 
environmental sustainability. In the context of developing the 
WABA platform, there are several aspects that demonstrate 
how the Company implements environmental sustainability. 
The use of the WABA platform effectively reduces physical 
contact between individuals. Through online interactions, 
whether for product or service promotions, customer service 
communication or ordering, the Company promotes the 
adoption of “contactless” practices. By reducing physical 
contact, the Company directly helps mitigate the potential 
production of waste associated with human interactions. The 
technology in the WABA platform also serves as an efficient 
means to support the transition from print promotion media 
to digital media, reducing the environmental impact resulting 
from the printing of promotional materials.

Clean Energy Products and Services 
Climate change with its detrimental impact on the 
environment and disruption of ecosystems has become a 
critical concern both nationally and globally. One of the leading 
causes of climate change is environmental pollution in the 
form of carbon emissions. Among the total carbon emissions 
released, the transportation sector holds a significant role, 
primarily due to greenhouse gas emissions from fossil 
fuel-powered vehicles. Hence, the use of alternative energy in 
the transportation sector is considered essential for reducing 
carbon emissions and supporting environmentally-friendly, 
sustainable energy resilience in the present and future. 
In response to the environmental pollution and climate 
change challenges arising from these emissions, the 
Company has set the reduction of carbon emissions in the 
transportation sector as a key pillar in its commitment to 
sustainability. This is manifested through concrete efforts 
to enhance the development of eco-friendly electric vehicles 
with cutting-edge technology, exemplified by Volta. Volta 
exemplifies the Company’s spirit, along with its Subsidiaries, 
to actively contribute to achieving a clean energy era by 
significantly reducing pollutant emissions and aiming for the 
government’s goal of Net Zero Emissions by 2060 through 
the use of environmentally friendly vehicles.

dan mengoptimalkan penggunaan sumber daya. Dengan 
mengintegrasikan teknologi yang ramah lingkungan, motor 
listrik Volta tidak hanya menjadi alternatif yang lebih bersih 
dan berkelanjutan, tetapi juga memberikan kontribusi nyata 
dalam memitigasi dampak negatif terhadap lingkungan.
Volta dapat dianggap sebagai salah satu pemain terdepan 
dalam industri kendaraan listrik di tingkat nasional. Hal 
ini sejalan dengan pertumbuhan produksi kendaraan 
listrik di Indonesia yang terus berkembang seiring dengan 
langkah-langkah 
percepatan 
penggunaan 
kendaraan 
listrik yang diambil oleh Pemerintah. Pada tahun 2023, 
Pemerintah memutuskan memberikan bantuan subsidi 
Kendaraan Bermotor Listrik Berbasis Baterai (KBLBB) roda 
dua kepada masyarakat. Keputusan ini diungkapkan dalam 
Peraturan Menteri Perindustrian Nomor 21 Tahun 2023 
yang mengubah Peraturan Menteri Perindustrian No. 6 
Tahun 2023, yang mengatur panduan pemberian bantuan 
pemerintah untuk pembelian Kendaraan Bermotor Listrik 
Berbasis Baterai (KBLBB) Roda Dua.
Langkah berani Grup MCASH untuk memberikan kontribusi 
nyata terhadap keberlanjutan lingkungan telah memberikan 
dampak yang signifikan. Volta bahkan ditetapkan sebagai 
model kendaraan yang menjadi peserta program bantuan 
pemerintah untuk pembelian Kendaraan Bermotor Listrik 
Berbasis Baterai Roda Dua. Dimana pemberian bantuan 
pembelian sepeda motor listrik baru sebesar Rp7.000.000 
per unit untuk 200.000 unit diberikan bagi masyarakat 
penerima subsidi. Volta mendapatkan subsidi ini berkat 
Tingkat Komponen Dalam Negeri (TKDN) yang mencapai 
lebih dari 40% untuk model motor listrik 401, Virgo dan 
Mandala. 
Dengan adanya bantuan subsidi dari Pemerintah, Perseroan 
berharap tingkat adopsi kendaraan listrik akan semakin 
massif sehingga mengakselerasi terciptanya kualitas 
udara yang lebih bersih.  Selain itu, penggunaan kendaraan 
listrik juga dapat mengurangi penggunaan sumber daya 
alam yang semakin terbatas, seperti bahan bakar fosil 
serta mengupayakan perbaikan kualitas udara dengan 
mengurangi emisi. 
As previously highlighted, the marketing and development 
of electric vehicles have been intensified by MCASH Group, 
by manufacturing eco-friendly vehicles, empowered by 
Internet of Things (IoT) technology, instant Battery Swapping 
Systems (SGB) and integrated applications. The development 
process of Volta electric vehicles not only prioritizes 
technological innovation, but also places special emphasis 
on environmental aspects. 
IoT technology in Volta electric motorcycles provides users 
with comprehensive information about their electric vehicle 
units through a single application, such as battery usage 
status, which aids in managing and optimizing resource 
utilization. By integrating environmentally friendly technology, 
Volta’s electric motorcycles not only offer a cleaner and more 
sustainable alternative but also make a tangible contribution 
to mitigating any negative environmental impacts. 
Volta can be considered one of the leading players in 
the electric vehicle industry at the national level, aligning 
with Indonesia’s growing electric vehicle production trend 
propelled by governmental acceleration initiatives. In 2023, the 
Government decided to provide subsidies for the purchase of 
Two-Wheeled Battery-Based Electric Motor Vehicles (KBLBB) 
to the public. This decision was announced in Minister of 
Industry Regulation No. 21 of 2023, amending Minister of 
Industry Regulation No. 6 of 2023, which outlines government 
assistance guidelines for the purchase of Two-Wheeled 
Battery-Based Electric Motor Vehicles (KBLBB). 
MCASH Group’s bold step to make a genuine contribution 
to environmental sustainability has yielded significant 
impacts. Volta has even been designated as a vehicle model 
participating in the government’s subsidy program for the 
purchase of Two-Wheeled Battery-Based Electric Motor 
Vehicles. This subsidy, amounting to Rp7,000,000 per unit 
for 200,000 units, was provided to eligible recipients. Volta 
obtained this subsidy thanks to the Domestic Component 
Level (TKDN) reaching over 40% for 401, Virgo, and Mandala 
electric motorcycle models. 
With governmental subsidies in place, the Company hopes 
that electric vehicle adoption rates will significantly increase, 
thereby accelerating the creation of cleaner air quality. 
Furthermore, the use of electric vehicles can reduce the 
consumption of increasingly limited natural resources such 
as fossil fuels, while striving to improve air quality by reducing 
emissions.

POSISI KEUANGAN
Financial Position
a.	 Aset 
Aset lancar turun 3,8% YoY, dari Rp1,33 triliun pada 2022 
menjadi Rp 1,28 triliun pada 2023, terutama disebabkan 
oleh penurunan kas dan setara kas.
Aset tidak lancar naik 22,0% YoY, dari Rp585,1 miliar 
pada 2022 menjadi Rp713,7 miliar pada 2023, terutama 
disebabkan oleh peningkatan pada aset tetap.
Jumlah aset naik 4,1% YoY, dari Rp1,9 triliun pada 2022 
menjadi Rp2,0 triliun pada 2023, terutama disebabkan 
oleh kenaikan aset tidak lancar.
b.	 Liabilitas
Liabilitas jangka pendek naik 13,4% YoY, dari Rp583,5 
miliar pada 2022 menjadi Rp661,5 miliar pada 2023, 
terutama disebabkan oleh kenaikan utang usaha.
Liabilitas jangka panjang naik 373,6% YoY dari Rp33,5 
miliar pada 2022 menjadi Rp158,6 miliar pada 2023, 
terutama disebabkan oleh kenaikan surat utang konversi.
Total liabilitas naik 32,9% YoY, dari Rp617,0 miliar pada 
2022 menjadi Rp820,0 miliar pada 2023, terutama 
disebabkan oleh kenaikan liabilitas jangka panjang.
c.	 Ekuitas
Jumlah ekuitas Perseroan menurun sebesar 9,6% YoY, 
dari Rp1,3 triliun per akhir 2022 menjadi Rp1,2 triliun per 
akhir 2023. Hal ini terutama disebabkan oleh penurunan 
akun ‘selisih transaksi dengan pihak non pengendali’, 
dari negatif Rp541,7 miliar pada 2022 menjadi negatif 
Rp659,4 miliar pada 2023. Faktor pendorong utama 
atas hal ini adalah peningkatan kepemilikan Perseroan di 
beberapa entitas anak, terutama pada PT NFC Indonesia 
Tbk (NFCX) dan PT Telefast Indonesia Tbk (TFAS).
a.	 Assets
Current assets decreased by 3.8% YoY, from Rp1.33 
trillion in 2022 to Rp1.28 trillion in 2023, mainly caused by 
the decrease in cash and equivalents.
Non-current assets increased by 22.0% YoY, from 
Rp585.1 billion in 2022 to Rp713.7 billion in 2023, mainly 
caused by the increase in fixed assets.
Total assets increased by 4.1% YoY, from Rp1.9 trillion 
in 2022 to Rp2.0 trillion in 2023, mainly caused by the 
increase in non-current assets.
b.	 Liabilities
Current liabilities increased by 13.4% YoY from Rp583.5 
billion in 2022 to Rp661.5 billion in 2023, mainly caused by 
the increase in trade payables.
Long term liabilities increased by 373.6% YoY, from 
Rp33.5 billion in 2022 to Rp158.6 billion in 2023, mainly 
caused by the increase in convertible notes.
Total liabilities increased by 32.9% YoY, from Rp617.0 
billion in 2022 to Rp820.0 billion in 2023, mainly caused 
by the increase in long term liabilities.
c.	 Equity
The Company’s total equity decreased by 9.6% YoY, from 
1.3 trillion in 2022 to Rp1.2 trillion in 2023. This was 
mainly caused by the decline in ‘differences in value from 
transactions with non controlling interests’ account, from 
negative Rp541.7 billion in 2022 to negative Rp659,4 
billion in 2023. Main driver of this is the increase in the 
Company’s stake in several subsidiaries, primarily in PT 
NFC Indonesia Tbk (NFCX) and PT Telefast Indonesia Tbk 
(TFAS).

The Company’s dividend policy is aligned with the provisions 
of the Articles of Association of PT M Cash Integrasi Tbk.

shareholders at least once a year, by taking into account the

The amount of dividend to be paid will be based on net income 
for the year, with due regard to the Company’s financial 
soundness and without diminishing the right of the General 
Meeting of Shareholders to decide otherwise.

Standards, Amendments/ Improvements and Interpretations 
to Standards issued but not yet adopted
The adoption of these new standards, amendments/
improvements and interpretations to standards that 
are effective beginning January 1, 2023 did not result in 
substantial changes to the Group’s accounting policies 
and had no material effect on the amounts reported for the 
current or prior financial years.
•	
Amendments to PSAK 1, “Presentation of Financial 
Statements”: Disclosure of Accounting Policies that 
change the term “Significant” to “Material” and provide 
explanations of Material Accounting Policies
This amendment provides guidance and examples to help 
entities apply materiality judgments to accounting policy 
disclosures. The amendment aims to help entities provide 
accounting policy disclosures that are more useful by 
replacing the requirement for entities to disclose their 
‘significant’ accounting policies with a requirement to 
disclose their ‘material’ accounting policies and adding 
guidance on how entities apply the concept of materiality 
in making decisions about accounting policy disclosures.
•	
Amendments to PSAK 16, “Property, Plant and Equipment”: 
Proceeds before Intended Use
The amendments prohibit an entity from deducting from 
the cost of a property, plant and equipment the proceeds 
received from selling items produced by the property, 
plant and equipment before it is ready for its intended use. 
The sales proceeds would have met the revenue definition 
and therefore should be recognized in profit or loss.
•	
Amendments to PSAK 25, “Accounting Policies, Changes 
in Accounting Estimates and Errors: Definition of 
Accounting Estimates
The amendment introduces a definition of ‘accounting 
estimates’ and clarifies the distinction between changes 
in accounting estimates and changes in accounting 
policies and the correction of errors. Also, they clarify 
how entities use measurement techniques and inputs to 
develop accounting estimates.
