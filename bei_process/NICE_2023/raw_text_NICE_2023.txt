TINJAUAN MAKROEKONOMI
Macroeconomic Overview
Situasi makroekonomi global menghadapi tantangan yang 
cukup berat sepanjang tahun 2023 yang merupakan tahun 
pertama selepas pandemi Covid-19. Sejak awal tahun, 
pertumbuhan ekonomi global dibayangi ketidakpastian 
dengan tingginya tingkat suku bunga dan inflasi global yang 
antara lain diakibatkan oleh memanasnya situasi geopolitik 
dunia seiring berlanjutnya Perang Rusia dan Ukraina, serta 
masih tingginya harga komoditas dunia. 
Dana Moneter Internasional (International Monetary 
Fund/IMF) dalam laporannya yang dirilis Oktober 2023 
memperkirakan laju ekonomi global akan melemah ke level 
3,0% di tahun 2023 dan ke 2,9% di tahun 2024, di mana 
negara-negara maju diperkirakan hanya akan tumbuh 
1,5% di 2023 dan 1,4% pada tahun 2024 seiring dengan 
penerapan kebijakan pengetatan moneter. 
Perlambatan ekonomi dunia tersebut berdampak pada 
penyerapan produk-produk ekspor tak terkecuali produk 
ekspor Indonesia. Pada kuartal III/2023, ekspor barang 
Indonesia terkontraksi sebesar 4,26%.
Namun demikian, pertumbuhan ekonomi Indonesia tetap 
kuat di tengah ketidakpastian kondisi perekonomian global. 
Data Badan Pusat Statistik (BPS) menunjukkan pertumbuhan 
ekonomi Indonesia triwulan III 2023 tetap tumbuh kuat 
sebesar 4,94% (year on year/yoy), meskipun sedikit 
melambat dari pertumbuhan pada triwulan sebelumnya 
yang sebesar 5,17% (yoy). Pertumbuhan ekonomi dalam 
negeri ini didukung oleh permintaan domestik yang cukup 
solid. Konsumsi rumah tangga tumbuh sebesar 5,06% (yoy), 
seiring dengan kenaikan mobilitas yang terus berlanjut, 
daya beli masyarakat yang stabil, serta keyakinan konsumen 
yang masih tinggi. Indonesia berhasil menutup tahun 2023 
dengan mencatatkan laju pertumbuhan sebesar 5,05%.
The global macroeconomic situation was dealing with 
significant challenges throughout 2023, which was the first 
year of non-Covid-19 pandemic. Since the beginning of the 
year, global economic growth has been overshadowed by 
uncertainties due to high interest rates and global inflation 
primarily attributable to, among others, the burning 
geopolitical situation following the continued Russia and 
Ukraine War, as well as high world commodity prices. 
The International Monetary Fund (IMF) in its report 
released in October 2023 estimated the global economy 
to weaken to 3.0% in 2023 and to 2.9% in 2024, where 
advanced countries were expected to grow only at 1.5% in 
2023 and 1.4% in 2024 in line with the implementation of 
monetary tightening policies. 
The global slowdown has an impact on the absorption of 
export products, including Indonesian export products. 
In the third quarter of 2023, Indonesia's goods exports 
contracted by 4.26%.
Nevertheless, Indonesia's economic growth remained 
strong amidst global uncertainties. Data from the Central 
Bureau of Statistics (BPS) shows that Indonesia's economy 
in the third quarter of 2023 continued to grow at a pace 
of 4.94% (year on year/yoy), although slightly slower than 
the pace of growth in the previous quarter at 5.17% (yoy). 
Domestic economic growth was supported by solid domestic 
consumption. Household consumption grew by 5.06% 
(yoy), in line with the increasing mobility, stable purchasing 
power and high consumer confidence. Indonesia closed the 
year 2023 by growing at 5.05%.

TINJAUAN INDUSTRI
Industry Overview
Mengacu pada analisa Investing News Network, saat ini 
Indonesia merupakan salah satu negara yang memiIiki 
posisi penting dalam persaingan nikel dunia. Hal ini terbukti 
dengan Indonesia dan Australia yang berbagi peringkat 1 
sebagai negara dengan cadangan nikel terbanyak di dunia, 
atau dengan nominal sebesar 21 juta MT. 
Berdasarkan Statista, Indonesia berkontribusi sebesar 37% 
produksi nikel dunia dan sudah meraih posisi ini semenjak 
2021. Dari seluruh cadangan nikel di Indonesia, sebagian 
besar berada di tiga provinsi di daerah timur Indonesia, 
yaitu Sulawesi Tenggara (1,86 miliar wet metrik ton (wmt), 
Maluku Utara (1,40 miliar wmt) dan Sulawesi Tengah (870 
juta wmt). 
Cadangan nikel yang besar ini memberikan Indonesia posisi 
tawar yang kuat di dunia internasional, terutama di tengah 
maraknya pengembangan industri baterai untuk kendaraan 
listrik di berbagai belahan dunia, penggunaan energi 
terbarukan dan sebagainya. Sebelum marak digunakan untuk
baterai kendaraan listrik, nikel telah lebih dulu digunakan 
dalam pembuatan berbagai jenis baterai perangkat 
elektronik antara lain ponsel, laptop, kamera, dan peralatan 
listrik lainnya. 
Nikel juga digunakan oleh industri baja karena kemampuannya
memberikan ketahanan korosi dan stabilitas dimensi pada
stainless steel yang membuatnya banyak dipakai dalam 
pembuatan peralatan dapur, perlengkapan medis, konstruksi
bangunan hingga industri petrokimia.
Meluasnya pemanfaatan komoditas nikel untuk berbagai 
kebutuhan diprediksi akan semakin menaikkan permintaan 
bagi komoditas nikel sehingga menciptakan prospek 
pertumbuhan yang kuat bagi perusahaan tambang nikel 
seperti Perseroan.
Perseroan merupakan kontributor utama dalam industri 
bijih nikel global. Indonesia diharapkan akan menyumbang 
sekitar setengah dari pertumbuhan produksi nikel global 
antara tahun 2021 dan 2025, dan keberhasilan proyek-
proyek HPAL di Indonesia dianggap sebagai kunci pasokan 
global nikel untuk baterai di masa depan, paling tidak dalam 
waktu dekat. 
Sumber daya nikel Indonesia sangat melimpah, dengan 
tambang-tambang tersebar di berbagai wilayah. Sebagian 
besar lokasi ekstraksi nikel yang paling signifikan, termasuk 
yang dioperasikan oleh Perseroan terletak di Sulawesi.
Pursuant to the analysis of Investing News Network, 
Indonesia is currently one of the countries that has an 
important position in the global nickel competition. 
Indonesia and Australia share 1st place as the country with 
the most nickel reserves in the world, or with a nominal 
value of 21 million MT. 
According to Statista, Indonesia contributes 37% of the 
world's nickel production and has been in this position 
since 2021. Of all nickel reserves in Indonesia, most of them 
are located in three provinces in eastern Indonesia, namely 
Southeast Sulawesi (1.86 billion wet metric tons (wmt), 
North Maluku (1.40 billion wmt) and Central Sulawesi (870 
million wmt).
 
These large nickel reserves give Indonesia a strong 
bargaining position internationally, especially amid the 
rampant development of the battery industry for electric 
vehicles in various parts of the world, the use of renewable 
energy and so on. Before being widely used for electric 
vehicle batteries, nickel has been used in the manufacture 
of various types of electronic device batteries including 
mobile phones, laptops, cameras, and other electrical 
equipment. 
Nickel is also used by the steel industry because of its 
ability to provide corrosion resistance and dimensional 
stability in stainless steel which makes it widely used in 
the manufacture of kitchen utensils, medical equipment, 
building construction to the petrochemical industry.
The wide use of nickel commodities for various needs is 
expected to boost demand for nickel, thus creating strong 
growth prospect for nickel mining companies, including our 
Company.
The Company is a major contributor to the global nickel 
ore industry. Indonesia is expected to account for about 
half of global nickel production growth between 2021 and 
2025, and the success of HPAL projects in Indonesia will be 
the key to the global supply of nickel for the future battery 
industries. 
Indonesia's nickel resources are abundant, and the mining 
sites are scattered across various regions. The most 
significant nickel extraction sites, including those operated 
by the Company, are largely located in Sulawesi.

Company runs a nickel mining business. The Company's

MINING PROJECTS
The mining project is wholly owned (100%) by the Company 
and consists of two adjacent areas: Blocks A and B. The 
projects sit on an area of 1,975 ha and is fully owned and 
operated by the Company. The Company also has a Clean 
and Clear certificate, ensuring no overlap with other mining 
permits and special terminal permit for shipping from the 
Ministry of Transportation.
The project briefly started production from 2013 to 2014, 
but was halted due to regulations banning the export of 
raw ore. Mining resumed in 2017 at the Block B deposit 
and then at the Block A deposit. The project extracts 
direct shipping ore ("DSO"), which is currently supplied 
to  domestic smelters in Southeast Sulawesi and Central 
Sulawesi, Indonesia.

●	 Block B
	
located east of the Langikima River, covering 643.92 ha.
●	 Block C
	
located in the eastern area and used for offices,
	
campgrounds, and laboratories, covering an area of 18 ha.
From the results of geological mapping, the Company's 
concession area can be divided into 2 (two) lithological 
units, namely ultramafic rocks and sedimentary rocks.

PROSPEK USAHA
Business Prospects
Nikel telah menjadi salah satu sumber daya mineral yang 
strategis di pasar global, karena bahan baku penting 
untuk industri baterai kendaraan listrik dan pembangkitan 
energi geotermal. Permintaannya pun diprediksi akan 
meningkat seiring tingginya tren energi baru-terbarukan 
(EBT). Berdasarkan International Nickel Study Group (INSG), 
lembaga analisis pasar nikel, permintaan nikel sendiri telah 
meningkat sebesar CAGR 7,00% dalam 4 (empat) tahun 
terakhir, yang didukung terutama pada permintaan di 
industri stainless steel. 
Berdasarkan World Atlas, Cina pada peringkat posisi nomor 
1 sebagai konsumen terbesar nikel dunia atau mengambil 
50,4% dari permintaan dunia. Mayoritas dari permintaan 
Cina adalah untuk mendukung permintaan produksi 
stainless steel, sebagaimana diungkapkan dalam data 
dari National Bureau of Statistics China, bahwa nilai hasil 
konstruksi di Cina sudah sampai USD 4,1 triliun (+6.6% YoY). 
Tak hanya itu, maraknya pengembangan industri kendaraan 
listrik (Electronic Vehicle/EV) juga telah mendorong 
peningkatan permintaan nikel yang luar biasa, karena 
perannya dalam produksi baterai kendaraan listrik. 
Permintaan nikel dalam baterai kendaraan listrik melonjak 
sebesar 17,0% YoY pada tahun 2021, sehingga mendorong 
peningkatan produksi sebesar 12,0% YoY pada tahun 
2022. Permintaan nikel olahan global diperkirakan akan 
meningkat menjadi 3,1 MT pada tahun 2023, yang akan 
ditopang oleh meningkatnya permintaan untuk produksi 
baterai kendaraan listrik. 
Dalam studi terbarunya, International Nickel Study Group 
(INSG), mencatat bahwa produksi nikel akan melampaui 
permintaan nikel di tahun 2023 dan 2024.  Produksi nikel 
global mencapai 3,06 juta ton tahun 2022, dan diprediksi 
menyentuh 3,42 juta ton di tahun 2023 dan 3,71 juta ton 
pada 2024. Sementara itu, konsumsi nikel dunia mencapai 
2,96 juta ton tahun 2022, dan diprediksi naik menjadi 3,20 
juta ton tahun 2023 dan 3,47 juta ton di tahun 2024.  
Nickel has become one of the strategic mineral resources 
in the global market, as it is an important raw material for 
the electric vehicle battery industry and geothermal energy 
generation. The demand is also predicted to increase in line 
with the high trend of renewable energy (EBT). Based on 
the International Nickel Study Group (INSG), a nickel market 
analysis agency, nickel demand alone has increased by a 
CAGR of 7.00% in the last 4 (four) years, which is supported 
mainly by demand in the stainless steel industry.  
According to World Atlas, China ranks as the world's No. 1 
largest consumer of nickel, taking 50.4% of world demand. 
The majority of China's demand is to support stainless 
steel  production demand, as revealed in data from  China's 
National Bureau of Statistics, that the value of construction 
output in China has reached USD 4.1 trillion (+6.6% YoY). 
 
Not only that, the vast development of the electric vehicle 
(EV) industry has also driven a considerable increase in 
demand for nickel, due to its role in the production of 
electric vehicle batteries. Demand for nickel in electric 
vehicle batteries surged by 17.0% YoY in 2021, driving a 
12.0% YoY increase in production in 2022. Global refined 
nickel demand is expected to increase to 3.1 MT in 2023, 
which will be supported by increasing demand for electric 
vehicle battery production. 
In its latest study, the International Nickel Study Group 
(INSG), noted that nickel production will surpass nickel 
demand in 2023 and 2024.  Global nickel production 
reached 3.06 million tons in 2022, and is predicted to  reach 
3.42 million tons in 2023 and 3.71 million tons in 2024. 
Meanwhile, world nickel consumption reached 2.96 million 
tons in 2022, and is predicted to increase to 3.20 million 
tons in 2023 and 3.47 million tons in 2024.

Berdasarkan prediksi International Energy Agency (IEA), 
permintaan nikel untuk teknologi bersih akan berkembang 
hingga 20 kali lipat sampai 2040. Diperkirakan, penjualan 
nikel dari Asia Tenggara pada 2030 mencapai 36,6 miliar 
dolar AS dan meningkat lagi hingga 40,8 miliar dolar AS 
pada 2050. 
Indonesia, sebagai salah satu penghasil nikel terbesar 
di dunia, akan menjadi negara yang paling diuntungkan 
dengan tingginya permintaan ini. sementara itu, pemerintah 
Indonesia juga tengah mendorong hilirisasi industri dalam 
rangka menstimulasi permintaan dari dalam negeri. Banyak 
fasilitas pengolahan dan pemurnian (smelter) kini tengah 
dibangun di dalam negeri. 
Akibatnya, konsumsi bijih nikel di dalam negeri akan 
semakin 
meningkat 
pada 
tahun-tahun 
mendatang. 
Besarnya konsumsi nikel di Indonesia diyakini akan terus 
membuka peluang dan memperkuat prospek pertumbuhan 
bisnis Perseroan dalam jangka panjang.
Popularitas kendaraan listrik di dalam negeri juga 
mendongkrak permintaan terhadap nikel. Dengan Indonesia 
sebagai produsen nikel terbesar di dunia, pemerintah terus 
berupaya mendorong pengembangan industri kendaraan 
listrik dalam negeri. Pelaksanaan Peraturan Presiden No. 
55 Tahun 2019 mengamanatkan pengembangan industri 
kendaraan listrik dalam negeri sebagai prioritas nasional. 
Kementerian ESDM memperkirakan pada tahun 2040, 
permintaan nikel untuk kebutuhan industri baja akan 
mencapai sebesar 1,9 juta ton, atau meningkat dari 1,65 juta 
ton yang tercatat pada 2019. Sementara itu, permintaan 
dari sektor industri baterai diperkirakan akan meningkat 
dari 163 ribu ton menjadi 1,22 juta ton pada 2040.
Based on outlook by the International Energy Agency 
(IEA), nickel demand for clean technology will grow 20 
times higher by 2040. It is estimated that nickel sales from 
Southeast Asia in 2030 will reach 36.6 billion US dollars and 
climb again to 40.8 billion US dollars in 2050. 
Indonesia, as one of the world's largest nickel producers, 
will be the country that will benefit the most from this high 
demand. Meanwhile, the Indonesian government is also 
encouraging downstream industry in order to stimulate 
domestic demand. Many processing and refining facilities 
(smelters) are now being built domestically. 
As a result, domestic consumption of nickel ore will further 
increase in the coming years. The large consumption 
of nickel in Indonesia is believed to continue to open 
opportunities and strengthen the Company's business 
growth prospects in the long term.
The popularity of electric vehicles in the country has also 
boosted demand for nickel. With Indonesia as the largest 
nickel producer in the world, the government continues to 
encourage the development of the domestic electric vehicle 
industry. The implementation of Presidential Regulation 
No. 55 of 2019 mandates the development of the domestic 
electric vehicle industry as a national priority. 
The Ministry of Energy and Mineral Resources estimates 
that in 2040, nickel demand for the needs of the steel 
industry will reach 1.9 million tons, or an increase from 1.65 
million tons recorded in 2019. Meanwhile, demand from 
the battery industry sector is expected to increase from 163 
thousand tons to 1.22 million tons in 2040.

kontrak perjanjian jual beli. Perseroan telah memiliki 
kontrak eksklusif dengan PT Agung Mineral Abadi (AMA) 
berdasarkan Perjanjian Jual Beli Bijih Nikel No. 012A/AKP-
AMA/II/2023 tanggal 13 Februari 2023.
The Company currently serves customers who already have 
signed a sale and purchase agreement. The Company has 
an exclusive contract with PT Agung Mineral Abadi (AMA) 
based on the Nickel Ore Sale and Purchase Agreement No. 
012A/AKP-AMA/II/2023 dated February 13, 2023 .
