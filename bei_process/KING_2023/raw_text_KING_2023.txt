Macroeconomy Overview
Proyeksi ekonomi Amerika Serikat diperkirakan stabil pada 
tahun 2023 sebesar 2,1%. Proyeksi tersebut di revisi naik 
0,3% poin dari proyeksi yang sudah dirilis sebelumnya, 
didorong oleh penguatan investasi pada triwulan II tahun 
2023 dan pertumbuhan konsumsi yang tangguh, hal ini masih 
mencerminkan masih ketatnya pasar tenaga kerja. Proyeksi 
Eropa tahun 2023, menurun 0,7% dari sebelumnya, penuruan 
diperkirakan terjadi adanya pelemahan beberapa sektor yang 
terkena dampak dari suku bunga dan perlambatan permintaan 
mitra dagang.
Dengan keadaan yang terjadi pada perekenomian global, 
pada triwulan I 2023 di Indonesia tercatat 5,06% (yoy), 
adanya sedikit peningkatan pada triwulan sebelumnya. 
Pertumbuhan perekenomian Indonesia, tercermin dari sisi 
lapangan usaha dan spasial. Secara lapangan usaha. Seluruh 
triwulan I 2023, mencatat pertumbuhan positif, pada sektor 
Industri, Perdagangan besar dan eceran, serta pertambangan 
dan penggalian. Hal ini juga dikarenakan mulai bergeraknya 
mobilitas 
masyarakan 
dan 
wisata 
mancanegara 
serta 
penyelenggaran acara nasional ataupun internasional, yang 
turut bergerak maju setelah masa-masa covid.
Perbandingan proyeksi ekonomi global dan nasional terlihat 
berbanding terbalik, dengan adanya beberapa pertumbuhan 
maju dari beberapa lapangan usaha dan juga mobilitas yang 
terus bergerak maju yang terus menambah peningkatan 
operasional dari tempat penginapan, pusat perbelanjaan 
dan fasilita publiknya lainnya. Sehingga aspek pemeliharaan 
kebersihan pada tempat-tempat tersebut membutuhkan jasa 
kebersihan professional menjadi hal yang semakin dibutuhkan.
The economic projection for the United States is expected to 
remain stable in 2023 at 2.1%. This projection has been revised 
upward by 0.3 percentage points from the previous release, 
driven by strengthened investment in the second quarter of 
2023 and robust consumption growth, though it still reflects 
tightness in the labor market. The 2023 projection for Europe 
has decreased by 0.7% from the previous projection, with the 
decline expected due to weakness in several sectors affected 
by interest rates and slowing demand from trading partners.
Given the global economic conditions, Indonesia recorded 
a 5.06% (yoy) growth in the first quarter of 2023, showing a 
slight increase from the previous quarter. Indonesia's economic 
growth is reflected both in terms of industry and spatial. 
In terms of industry, all quarters of 2023 recorded positive 
growth, particularly in the Industrial, Wholesale and Retail 
Trade, and Mining and Quarrying sectors. This is also due to the 
resumption of community mobility and international tourism, 
as well as the hosting of national or international events, which 
have progressed following the COVID period.
The comparison between global and national economic 
projections appears to be inversely related, with some sectors 
experiencing significant growth and continuous advancement 
in mobility, leading to increased operational activities in 
accommodation facilities, shopping centers, and other public 
facilities. Consequently, the aspect of cleanliness maintenance 
in these places requires professional cleaning services, making 
it increasingly essential.

In 2023, the Company’s revenue increased in every business 
segment. The most significant increase was in the labor supply 
business segment in 2023, which saw a 123.98% increase from 
the previous year, contributing revenue of Rp24.67 billion 
compared to Rp11.01 billion in 2022. Furthermore, the hygiene 
business sector achieved a revenue of Rp10.74 billion in 2023, 
compared to Rp6.84 billion in 2022, representing an increase 
of 56.87%. The security and hygiene business sector also saw 
an increase, with a 23.44% increase in revenue compared to 
Rp126.87 billion in 2022, reaching Rp156.63 billion in 2023. 
The security business sector achieved a revenue of Rp11.76 
billion in 2023, compared to Rp10.18 billion in 2022, marking 
a 15.58% increase. The Company's subsidiary also experienced 
an increase in parking management, with a 24.98% increase 
in revenue from Rp4.88 billion in 2022 to Rp6.1 billion in 
2023. There was also an increase in parking equipment rental 
revenue, from Rp1.5 billion in 2022 to Rp3.17 billion in 2023, 
marking a 102.95% increase. Parking attendant services did not 
contribute to revenue in 2023.

The Company manages its capital structure and makes 
adjustments based on changing economic conditions. To 
maintain and adjust its capital structure, the Company may 
adjust dividend payments to Shareholders, capital returns to 
Shareholders, or issue new shares.
The Company monitors capital by using a leverage ratio, 
dividing net debt by total equity.
Net debt is calculated as short-term debt, business debt to third 
parties, other debt, outstanding expenses, consumer financing 
debt, lease liabilities, and long-term bank debt minus cash and 
bank balances. Total equity is calculated as equity as presented 
in the consolidated financial statements.

Potensi Cleaning Service Berevolusi Digital
Perkembangan digital di Indonesia mengalami kemajuan, 
tidak hanya pada peralatan yang digunakan tetapi juga sistem 
pengelolaan dan layanan akses yang dapat dijangkau oleh

bermitra dengan penyedia layanan sewa dan/atau menciptakan

pembangunan ataupun pariwisata, meningkatkan kebutuhan

washroom hygiene ini. Namun, permintaan jasa usaha 
washroom hygiene juga dapat berdiri sendiri tanpa adanya 
permintaan jasa usaha kebersihan.

tenaga kerja.

Potential for Digital Evolution of Cleaning Services
Digital development in Indonesia has progressed, not only 
in the equipment used but also in management systems and 
accessible service access, offering opportunities for marketing 
expansion. Certainly, the cleaning service industry can partner 
with rental service providers and/or create technology tailored 
to the needs of potential service customers.
Washroom Hygiene Business Prospects
With the increased mobility of the population in both the 
construction and tourism sectors, the demand for hygiene 
services has increased, which also increases the demand 
for washroom hygiene services. However, the demand for 
washroom hygiene services can also stand alone without the 
need for cleaning services.
Labor Supply Business Prospects
In 2024, development will progress. Several new buildings 
will start operating in 2024, and with the start of several new 
venues, opportunities for labor supply businesses will emerge. 
Not only with development, but with several events planned, 
there are also opportunities for labor supply businesses.
Security Service Business Prospects
With the return of advancing mobility, the demand for these
