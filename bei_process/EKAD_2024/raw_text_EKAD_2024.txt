KWW Wroduction Wrocess
The OPP Production Process consists of 3 series of 
processes, namely printing, coating and sliting. These 
three activities processed  at the Tangerang Pasar 
Kemis Factory, while the Surabaya Factory is only the 
Sliting Process.
But the Main Production Process is the Coating Process 
and The Zipper Process while the Printing Process is 
only carried out for special orders that are "Private 
Labels".
Printing Process is the process of coating OPP Film 
with Ink to print Logos, images or letters.
Coating process is the process of coating plain OPP 
Film or OPP Film that has gone through the molding 
process (Printing) with resin into OPP Jumbo Rolls.
Sliting process is the process of cutting a large size OPP 
or Jumbo Roll into a smaller roll shape.
