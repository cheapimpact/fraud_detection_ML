ANALISIS EKONOMI
Perekonomian global di 2023 masih diliputi ketidakpastian. 
Volatilitas harga komoditas energi akibat konflik geopolitik di 
Rusia-Ukraina dan Timur Tengah telah menahan laju penurunan 
Inflasi global. Negara-negara maju seperti Amerika Serikat (AS), 
Inggris, dan Uni Eropa tengah dihadapkan pada fenomena 
higher for longer, yaitu kondisi di mana suku bunga tertahan 
pada level yang tinggi dikarenakan tingkat inflasi yang berada 
di atas target. 
Di akhir 2023, inflasi AS tercatat 3,40% atau turun dari 6,50% 
di periode yang sama tahun sebelumnya. Namun, penurunan 
inflasi tersebut belum diikuti oleh penurunan suku bunga acuan 
yang masih bertahan di level yang cukup tinggi. Suku bunga 
acuan AS (Fed Fund Rate, FFR) meningkat 100 bps di sepanjang 
2023 menjadi 5,50%. Hal yang sama terjadi di Inggris, di mana 
inflasi turun menjadi 4,00% di 2023 dari 10,50% di 2022. Namun, 
suku bunga Bank of England meningkat 175 bps di 2023 menjadi 
5,25%. 
Fenomena higher for longer memicu capital outflow dari 
Emerging Markets ke negara maju dan mendorong penguatan 
signifikan dolar AS terhadap berbagai mata uang dunia.
  
Perekonomian global tahun 2023 juga dipengaruhi oleh 
perlambatan pertumbuhan ekonomi China akibat kebijakan 
Zero COVID-19 dan krisis sektor properti yang terjadi di negeri 
tirai bambu tersebut. Sebagai mana diketahui, China merupakan 
salah satu pasar terbesar dalam perdagangan dunia.
Dalam laporan World Economic Outlook edisi Januari 2024, 
Lembaga Dana Moneter Internasional (IMF) memproyeksikan 
pertumbuhan ekonomi global pada 2023 mencapai 3,1%. 
Proyeksi tersebut meningkat dibandingkan laporan yang 
diterbitkan pada Oktober 2023 yang sebesar 2,9%. Menurut 
IMF, pertumbuhan ekonomi global tahun ini ditopang adanya 
perbaikan kondisi di China, Amerika Serikat, dan negara-negara 
berkembang. Sejalan dengan itu, IMF juga memproyeksikan 
ekonomi global 2024 akan tumbuh stabil di level 3,1%. 
IMF juga menggambarkan proyeksi ekonomi global 2024 
dengan istilah “resilient but slow” dengan asumsi bahwa harga 
komoditas bahan bakar dan non-bahan bakar akan turun pada 
2024-2025, dan tingkat suku bunga di negara-negara maju akan 
menurun. IMF memperkirakan rata-rata harga minyak bumi akan 
turun 2,3% pada 2024, dan harga komoditas non-bahan bakar 
turun 0,9%.
ECONOMIC ANALYSIS
The global economy in 2023 continued to be shrouded in 
uncertainty. Volatility in energy commodity prices due to 
geopolitical conflicts in Russia-Ukraine and the Middle East has 
restrained the decline in global inflation. Advanced countries 
such as the United States (US), the United Kingdom, and the 
European Union ware facing the phenomenon of “higher for 
longer,” where interest rates are held at high levels due to 
inflation rates above target.
At the end of 2023, US inflation stood at 3.40%, down from 6.50% 
in the same period the previous year. However, this decline in 
inflation has not been followed by a decrease in benchmark 
interest rates, which remain at relatively high levels. The US 
Federal Reserve’s benchmark interest rate (Fed Fund Rate, FFR) 
increased by 100 bps throughout 2023 to 5.50%. The same trend 
occurred in the UK, where inflation dropped to 4.00% in 2023 
from 10.50% in 2022. However, the Bank of England’s benchmark 
interest rate increased by 175 bps in 2023 to 5.25%.
The “higher for longer” phenomenon has triggered capital 
outflows from emerging markets to advanced countries 
and significantly strengthened the US dollar against various 
currencies worldwide.
The global economy in 2023 was also affected by a slowdown in 
China’s economic growth due to Zero COVID-19 policies and a 
crisis in the property sector in the bamboo curtain country. As is 
known, China is one of the largest markets in global trade.
In the World Economic Outlook report edition of January 2024, 
the International Monetary Fund (IMF) projected global economic 
growth in 2023 to reach 3.1%. This projection is an increase from 
the report published in October 2023, which was 2.9%. According 
to the IMF, global economic growth this year is supported by 
improvements in conditions in China, the United States, and 
developing countries. In line with this, the IMF also projected that 
the global economy in 2024 will grow steadily at 3.1%.
The IMF also described the 2024 global economic projection 
with the term “resilient but slow,” assuming that fuel and non-fuel 
commodity prices will decline in 2024-2025, and interest rates 
in advanced countries will decrease. The IMF predicted that the 
average price of crude oil will decrease by 2.3% in 2024, and non-
fuel commodity prices will decrease by 0.9%.

In line with the global economic slowdown, Indonesia’s economy 
also slowed down but still grew solidly by 5.05%, down from 
5.31% in 2022. This slowdown was mainly due to the decrease 
in exports to USD 258.82 billion from USD 291.90 billion in 2022, 
following the normalization of commodity prices throughout 
2023. On average, in 2023, coal and palm oil prices decreased 
by 55% and 25%, respectively, from the average prices in 2022. 
Nevertheless, Indonesia’s trade balance in 2023 still recorded 
a surplus of USD 36.93 billion. This achievement indicates 
Indonesia’s trade performance remained resilient amid the 
global economic slowdown.
In addition to the decline in exports, household consumption also 
slowed to 4.82%. This was driven by a decrease in transportation 
consumption due to the increase in subsidized fuel prices since 
the end of the third quarter of 2022 and a decrease in food and 
beverage consumption due to the increase in prices of some 
staple goods.
Inflation in Indonesia, in December 2023, was controlled and 
recorded at 2.61%, down from 5.51% in 2022. This inflation level 
fell within the Government’s target range of 3%±1%. The decrease 
in inflation was mainly attributed to administered prices because 
subsidized fuel prices remained relatively stable compared to 
prices at the end of 2022. Although domestic inflation is in line 
with the inflation target, the benchmark interest rate of Bank 
Indonesia increased again by 50 bps to 6.00% in 2023. This was 
done to maintain the stability of Indonesia’s financial markets 
by setting a higher benchmark interest rate compared to global 
interest rates.

INISIATIF STRATEGIS TAHUN 2023
Menghadapi tahun 2023, Kimia Farma telah menyiapkan Strategi 
Korporasi menjadi 3 besar Integrated Healthcare Company di 
Indonesia seperti tergambar dibawah ini:
BPS noted that the gross domestic product (GDP) at constant 
prices (ADHK) of chemical, pharmaceutical and traditional 
medicine industries amounted to Rp57.06 trillion in the second 
quarter of 2020. The value was corrected by 1.36% compared to 
the same period in previous year (year on year/yoy) of Rp57.85 
trillion. The declining performance of pharmaceutical industry 
continued the contraction trend that has occurred since the third 
quarter of 2022. 
Previously, the performance of pharmaceutical industry had 
grown positively in the fourth quarter of 2021 to the second 
quarter of 2022. Entering 2023, the pharmaceutical industry 
corrected in the first & second quarters, then grew positively 
in the third quarter of 2023 by 4.53% compared to the same 
period in 2022, and then entering the fourth quarter of 2023 
the pharmaceutical industry once again grew slightly by 0.89% 
and closed the year of 2023 by growing by 0.11% from 2022. The 
corrected performance of pharmaceutical industry cannot be 
separated from the lifting of the domestic Covid-19 pandemic 
in June 2023, the weakening of rupiah exchange rate in the 
year and the pressure to increase the price of imported drug 
raw materials. This resulted in the pharmaceutical industry only 
growing slightly in 2023, but such condition was still supported 
by the growth of pharmaceutical industry exports in 2023. Based 
on data from the Ministry of Industry (Kemenperin) in 2023, 
exports of chemical, pharmaceutical & traditional medicine 
industry grew 8.78% compared to 2022, with an export value of 
USD543.7 million.

Pabrik Banjaran, Bandung, Jawa Barat Merupakan pabrik 
yang memiliki kapasitas terbesar se-Asia Tenggara yang 
mengimplementasikan IoT. Mulai dilakukan peletakan batu 
pertama pada tahun 2015, saat ini telah mendapatkan 
sertifikat Produksi Industri farmasi dari Kementerian 
Kesehatan dan Sertifikat CPOB untuk seluruh fasilitas 
produksi di Gedung Pharma dari Badan Pengawas Obat dan 
Makanan Republik Indonesia. Plant Banjaran sudah mulai 
produksi di tahun 2020, dan pengiriman perdana di bulan 
Juni 2020. 
2.	
Pabrik Bahan Baku Obat, Cikarang, Jawa Barat Pabrik ini 
dibangun sebagai tindak lanjut dari kerja  sama melalui 
pendirian PT Kimia Farma Sungwun  Pharmacopia, 
perusahaan Joint Venture (JV) antara  Kimia Farma dengan 
PT Sungwun Pharmacopia Indonesia yang merupakan 
perwakilan dari Sungwun  Pharmacopia Co. Ltd dari Korea 
Selatan. 
3.	
Untuk saat ini, jenis Bahan Baku Obat yang akan diproduksi 
ada 8 (delapan) item yaitu: Simvastatin, Atorvastatin, 
Rosuvastatin, Pantoprazole, Esomeprazole, Rabeprazole, 
Clopidogrel dan Sarpogrelate dengan total kapasitas 
produksi 30 ton per tahun. Produksi Bahan Baku Obat ini 
untuk memenuhi 100% kebutuhan seluruh industri farmasi 
di Indonesia untuk ke delapan bahan baku tersebut dan 
selebihnya untuk pasar ekspor.
Selain memproduksi Bahan Baku Obat Aktif (API), Pabrik 
ini juga akan memproduksi High Function Chemical (HFC) 
yang dapat digunakan sebagai bahan baku kosmetika dan 
food supplement, yang seluruh hasil produk HFC ini akan 
di ekspor ke Korea, Jepang dan Amerika. Hal ini sejalan 
dengan Program Pemerintah yaitu Program Kemandirian 
Bahan Baku Obat Nasional yang tertuang dalam Roadmap 
Kementerian Kesehatan serta didukung dengan adanya 
Paket Kebijakan Ekonomi XI serta Instruksi Presiden RI No. 
6 Tahun 2016 tentang Percepatan Pengembangan Industri 
Farmasi dan Alat Kesehatan, maka KF optimis bahwa 
ketergantungan akan impor Bahan Baku Obat (API) akan 
mulai berkurang ke depannya dan pada akhirnya Indonesia 
mampu mandiri dalam produksi Bahan Baku Obat.  
In addition, the Company has developed its Production Facilities 
as follows:
1.	
Banjaran Plant, Bandung, West Java. It is a plant that has 
the largest capacity in Southeast Asia that implements IoT. 
Starting from the laying of the first stone in 2015, it has now 
received a Pharmaceutical Industry Production Certificate 
from the Ministry of Health and GMP Certificates for all 
production facilities in Pharma Building from the Food and 
Drug Supervisory Agency of the Republic of Indonesia. 
Banjaran Plant has started production in 2020, and the first 
shipment was done in June 2020. 
2.	
Active Pharmaceutical Ingredient Plant, Cikarang, West 
Java. This plant was built as a follow-up to the collaboration 
through the establishment of PT Kimia Farma Sungwun 
Pharmacopia, a Joint Venture (JV) company between Kimia 
Farma and PT Sungwun Pharmacopia Indonesia which is 
a representative of Sungwun Pharmacopia Co. Ltd. from 
South Korea. 
3.	
Currently, there are 8 (eight) types of active pharmaceutical 
ingredient to be produced, namely: Simvastatin, Atorvastatin, 
Rosuvastatin, Pantoprazole, Esomeprazole, Rabeprazole, 
Clopidogrel and Sarpogrelate with a total production 
capacity of 30 tons per year. The production of these active 
pharmaceutical ingredients is to fulfil 100% of the needs of the 
entire pharmaceutical industry in Indonesia for the eight active 
pharmaceutical ingredients and the rest for the export market. 
In addition to producing Active Pharmaceutical Ingredient 
(API), this plant will also produce High Function Chemicals 
(HFC) which can be used as raw materials for cosmetics and 
food supplements, all of which will be exported to Korea, 
Japan and America. This is in line with the Government’s 
Program, namely the National Pharmaceutical Ingredient 
Independence Program which is contained in the Roadmap 
of the Ministry of Health and is supported by the Economic 
Policy Package XI and the Indonesian Presidential 
Instruction No. 6 of 2016 concerning the Acceleration of the 
Development of the Pharmaceutical and Medical Devices 
Industry. KF is optimistic that the dependence on imported 
Active Pharmaceutical Ingredient will begin to decrease 
in the future and eventually Indonesia will be able to be 
independent in the production of Active Pharmaceutical 
Ingredient.

KEGIATAN DISTRIBUSI
Kimia Farma menerapkan strategi pengembangan usaha yang 
terintegrasi dari hulu ke hilir. Untuk menunjang kinerja segmen 
manufaktur, Perusahaan mengembangkan kegiatan usaha 
distribusi produk farmasi.  
Kegiatan usaha distribusi dijalankan oleh entitas anak 
Perusahaan, yaitu PT Kimia Farma Trading & Distribution 
(KFTD). Dalam menjalani kegiatan  usaha ini, Perusahaan telah 
mengantongi sertifikasi Cara  Distribusi Obat yang Baik (CDOB). 
Distribusi produk farmasi yang dilakukan Perusahaan tidak 
terbatas hanya pada pasar domestik, tapi juga telah merambah 
pasar internasional, antara lain Arab Saudi, Amerika Serikat, 
Belanda, Inggris, Tiongkok, Malaysia dan beberapa negara 
lainnya.
JALUR DISTRIBUSI 
Jalur distribusi Kimia Farma diawali dari Pabrik Kimia Farma yang 
kemudian disalurkan kepada Pedagang Besar Farmasi (PBF). 
Selanjutnya, PBF akan menyalurkan atau mendistribusikan obat 
tersebut kepada PBF cabang, Apotek, Instalasi Farmasi Rumah 
Sakit dan Fasilitas Kesehatan.
Khusus untuk sediaan farmasi berupa narkotika dan psikotropika 
memiliki jalur distribusi khusus. Untuk Narkotika hanya bisa 
disalurkan dari Industri Farmasi kepada Pedagang Besar 
Farmasi Tertentu, Apotek, Sarana Penyimpanan Sediaan Farmasi 
Pemerintah Tertentu, dan Rumah Sakit. Kemudian dilanjutkan 
dari PBF disalurkan kepada PBF tertentu lainnya, apotek, dan 
fasilitas kesehatan lainnya. Pendistribusian ini sesuai pada 
Undang-Undang No. 35 Tahun 2009 tentang Narkotika.

Health Products Distribution and Trading Segment
DISTRIBUTION ACTIVITIES 
Kimia Farma implements an integrated business development 
strategy from upstream to downstream. To support the 
manufacturing segment performance, the Company has 
developed the pharmaceutical product distribution business 
activities. 
Distribution business activities are carried out by the Company’s 
subsidiaries, PT Kimia Farma Trading & Distribution (KFTD). In 
carrying out these business activities, Company has obtained 
Good Drug Distribution Practices Certification. 
The distribution of pharmaceutical products is not limited to 
domestic market only, but also to the international market, 
including Saudi Arabia, the United States, Netherlands, the 
United Kingdom, China, Malaysia and several other countries.
 
DISTRIBUTION CHANNEL 
Kimia Farma’s distribution channel starts from Kimia Farma Plant, 
which is then distributed to the Pharmaceutical Wholesalers. 
After that, Pharmaceutical Wholesalers will distribute the drug 
to Pharmaceutical Wholesalers branches, Pharmacies, Hospital 
Pharmacy Installations and Medical Centers. 
Especially for pharmaceutical supplies in the form of narcotics 
and psychotropic drugs, they have special distribution channels. 
For Narcotics, the drugs can only be distributed from the 
Pharmaceutical Industry to Certain Pharmaceutical Wholesalers, 
Pharmacies, Certain Supplies Storage Facilities for Government, 
and Hospitals. Then from Pharmaceutical Wholesalers, the drugs 
are distributed to certain other Pharmaceutical Wholesalers, 
pharmacies, and other health facilities. This distribution is in 
accordance with Law No. 35 of 2009 for Narcotics.

Farma dan product private label di seluruh Apotek Kimia Farma 
dengan target penjualan >10% dan melakukan pengembangan 
digitalisasi layanan online dan e-commerce untuk meningkatkan 
aksesibiltas pelanggan dengan tetap mengutamakan layanan

PHARMACEUTICAL RETAIL SEGMENT
RETAIL OPERATIONAL AND BUSINESS 
ACTIVITIES 
Retail Segment is the sale of medicines and pharmaceutical 
products directly to customers. This business activity is carried 
out by a subsidiary, namely PT Kimia Farma Apotek (KFA), that 
manages pharmacy network that carries the brand Apotek Kimia 
Farma.
RETAIL SEGMENT CHALLENGES AND 
DEVELOPMENT STRATEGIES
People’s purchasing power that has not fully recovered and the level 
of competition that is getting stringent are the main challenges 
faced by the retail segment in 2022. In addition, the Covid-19 
Pandemic has not completely ended, so the number of customer 
visits to outlets has not returned to the pre-pandemic era. 
Facing these challenges, one of the Company’s strategies is 
to develop new pharmacies to maintain the continuity of 
pharmacy business growth through leasing, joint operation (JO) 
and acquisitions as well as self-ownership in accordance with 
the potential and target market segments, both community 
pharmacies, pharmacies at malls and airports, and hospital 
pharmacy. 
In addition, the Company prioritizes the sales of Kimia Farma 
products and private label products in all Kimia Farma Pharmacies 
with a sales target of >10% and develops digitalization of online 
and e-commerce services to increase customer accessibility 
while still prioritizing professional services.

RENCANA PENGEMBANGAN SEGMEN RITEL
Kebijakan Pengembangan Apotek 
1.	
Pengembangan apotek baru dilakukan untuk menjaga 
keberlangsungan pertumbuhan bisnis apotek dengan 
cara sewa, kerja sama operasi (KSO) dan akuisisi maupun 
kepemilikan sendiri sesuai dengan potensi dan segmen 
pasar yang dituju baik apotek komunitas, mall, bandara dan 
apotek di rumah sakit dengan memperhatikan: 
a.	
Penentuan lokasi yang optimal berbasis zipcode, 
analisis demografis, langkah kompetitor, serta strategi 
pengembangan apotek baru. 
b.	
Return on investment dan mitigasi risiko bisnis apotek 
dilakukan dengan mempertimbangkan: 
•	
Hasil kajian FS dari sisi revenue, HPP, Biaya dan 
Profit yang akan dicapai dalam perhitungan BEP 
dan keuntungan yang akan diperoleh di akhir 
masa sewa/KSO; 
•	
Persiapan operasional pembukaan apotek baru 
harus dilakukan sesuai timeline (dari izin prinsip 
sampai dengan grand opening) 
•	
Review perkembangan kinerja apotek baru 
dilakukan secara periodik dengan konsisten; 
•	
Continuous improvement terhadap apotek yang 
tidak sesuai dengan rencana FS-nya.

RETAIL SEGMENT DEVELOPMENT PLAN
Pharmacy Development Policy 
1.	
The development of new pharmacies is carried out to 
maintain the sustainability of pharmacy business growth 
by means of leases, joint operations (JO) and acquisitions as 
well as own ownership in accordance with the potential and 
target market segments of community pharmacies, malls, 
airports and pharmacies in hospitals with due regard: 
a.	
Optimal location determination based on zipcode, 
demographic analysis, competitor steps, and new 
pharmacy development strategies. 
b.	
Return on investment and mitigation of pharmacy 
business risks are carried out by considering: 
•	
The results of FS study in terms of revenue, COGS, 
costs and profits to be achieved in the calculation 
of BEP and profits to be obtained at the end of the 
lease / PSO period; 
•	
Operational preparation for the opening of a new 
pharmacy must be carried out according to the 
timeline (from the principle permit to the grand 
opening) 
•	
Review of the progress of new pharmacy 
performance is carried out periodically and 
consistently; 
•	
Continuous improvement of pharmacies that are 
not in accordance with the FS plan.

OTHER SEGMENT ACTIVITIES 
Another segment is derived from the business activities carried 
out by a subsidiary, PT Kimia Farma Diagnostika (“KFD”), 
which provides clinical laboratory services including routine, 
referral, and research-supporting laboratory examinations, 
non-laboratory examinations (complete physical, spirometry, 
audiometry, X-rays, panoramic X-rays, electrocardiograms or

occupational health consultations and nutritional consultations.

Test, Immunology, Serology, Urinalysis, Hematology, Clinical 
Chemistry (including Kidney Function Test, Liver Function Test, 
Diabetes Mellitus), Biomolecular Viruses & Oncology and others.

The Company manages capital to maintain business continuity 
with a view to maximize shareholders’ wealth and benefits to 
other parties concerned with the Company and its subsidiaries

maintenance of healthy capital ratios to support the business and 
maximize shareholder value. The Company and its subsidiaries 
are not required to meet certain capital requirements.

the total equity attributable to owners of the Parent Entity. The 
Company and Subsidiaries’ policy is to maintain the gearing ratios 
within the range of similar industrial companies in Indonesia to 
secure access to funding at a rational cost.

that allows employee participation in owning company shares.

the Company became a public company by conducting an 
Initial Public Offering (IPO) and being listed on the Jakarta and

Stock Ownership (KSKM), facilitating the Company’s Management

Company.

traded (locked) for 2 (two) years from July 4, 2001. All activities 
related to management and employee share ownership have 
been realized.

nasional. 
Untuk mencapai sasaran dan target yang diinginkan dari setiap 
strategi, PT Kimia Farma Tbk dalam pelaksanaan melakukan 
kolaborasi, sinergi dan optimalisasi dari seluruh elemen baik 
di internal Grup Kimia Farma, Holding BUMN Farmasi dan 
stakeholder lainnya yang sejalan dengan 5 (lima) prioritas 
Kementerian BUMN.
In accordance with the Letter of the Minister of State-Owned 
Enterprises (SOE) of the Republic of Indonesia Number S–787/
MBU/10/2021 dated October 8, 2021 concerning Aspirations of 
Shareholder/Capital Owner, Kimia Farma, which is a member of 
the SOE Pharmaceutical Holding, implements the formation of 
strategies and strategic initiatives for the Health Industry Cluster 
as follows: 
1.	
Pharmacy Sub-Cluster
To become a globally competitive healthcare company 
that is sustainable to improve the quality of life through 
increasing product dominance in focus areas, maximizing 
efficiency and productivity, strengthening the overall value 
chain, reducing imports and increasing exports, advancing 
innovation and science through R&D, synergizing with the 
healthcare sector ecosystem nationwide, and business 
diversification into the lifestyle segment. 
2.	
Health Sub-Cluster
Making SOEs the pillars of national health security through 
scale, world-class service, and operational excellence with 
elements of: guaranteed access to health services, supply 
chain sovereignty, market control, and medical data 
sovereignty through the provision of quality health services, 
network and scale improvements, capability development 
and innovation, and integration and synergy of the national 
health sector ecosystem. 
In order to achieve the desired goals and targets from each 
strategy, PT Kimia Farma Tbk is carrying out collaboration, 
synergy and optimization of all elements both internally 
within Kimia Farma Group, SOE Pharmaceutical Holding and 
other stakeholders

Adapun sebagai penjabaran dari 5 (lima) prioritas Kementerian 
BUMN tersebut, PT Kimia Farma Tbk menyusun inisiatif strategis 
sebagai berikut:
1.	
Nilai Ekonomi dan Sosial Untuk Indonesia PT Kimia Farma 
Tbk memiliki fundamental yang kuat di industri layanan 
kesehatan yang perlu terus dilakukan penguatan agar tetap 
menjadi yang terdepan di antara kompetitor antara lain 
dengan: 
a.	
Brand Apotek Kimia Farma yang memiliki reputasi 
baik dan terpercaya di masyarakat, sehingga ke depan 
PT Kimia Farma Tbk akan terus memperkuat jaringan 
layanan kesehatan ini melalui peningkatan layanan 
kesehatan yang terintegrasi dengan mengedepankan 
kualitas layanan dan lebih mendekatkan kepada 
masyarakat melalui perluasan jangkauan layanan 
kesehatan. 
b.	
PT Kimia Farma Tbk memiliki akses yang kuat di seluruh 
channel distribusi produk farmasi dan alat kesehatan, 
sehingga ke depan akan diperkuat dengan perluasan 
channel distribusi melalui penambahan prinsipal 
potensial dan pembukaan channel baru. 
c.	
Untuk terus dapat meningkatkan daya saing terhadap 
kompetitor, PT Kimia Farma Tbk akan melakukan 
kerja sama dengan mitra strategis dalam rangka 
pengembangan produk unggulan dengan melakukan 
transfer teknologi. 
d.	
PT Kimia Farma Tbk juga akan terus melakukan ekspansi 
usaha dengan pengembangan bisnis yang berkaitan 
dengan life style yaitu dengan pengembangan bisnis 
kosmetika dan healthy food.
As for the elaboration of the 5 (five) priorities of the SOE Ministry, 
PT Kimia Farma Tbk has prepared the following strategic 
initiatives:
1.	
Economic and Social Value for Indonesia 
PT Kimia Farma Tbk has strong fundamentals in the 
healthcare industry which need to be continuously 
strengthened with a view to remain ahead of competitors, 
including by: 
a.	
Kimia Farma Pharmacy brand has a good reputation 
and is trusted in the community, so that in the future 
PT Kimia Farma Tbk will continue to strengthen this 
health service network through improving integrated 
health services by prioritizing service quality and 
getting closer to the community through expanding 
the reach of health services. 
b.	
PT Kimia Farma Tbk has strong access to all distribution 
channels for pharmaceutical products and medical 
devices, so that in the future it will be strengthened by 
expanding distribution channels by adding potential 
principals and opening new channels. 
c.	
In order to continue to improve competitiveness 
against competitors, PT Kimia Farma Tbk will cooperate 
with strategic partners in the context of developing 
superior products by transferring technology. 
d.	
PT Kimia Farma Tbk will also continue to expand its 
business by developing businesses related to life style, 
namely by developing the cosmetics and healthy food 
business.

also be carried out, especially in health services, namely 
by integrating IT at PT Kimia Farma Diagnostika to improve 
services, both clinical services and clinical laboratory 
services.

Pengembangan Talenta

dijalankan sesuai dengan aspek Tata Kelola Perusahaan yang Baik

akan mengimplementasikan seluruh program dan kegiatan 
operasional sesuai dengan aspek Tata Kelola Perusahaan yang 
Baik (GCG) dan core values AKHLAK serta terus melakukan 
internalisasi GCG dan AKHLAK tersebut kepada seluruh insan 
Kimia Farma.
Farma Tbk already has an API production facility that

domestic pharmaceutical industry, which is expected 
to reduce dependence on imports API gradually.

Increase in Export Sale
For the development of market coverage, PT Kimia 
Farma Tbk will not only play in domestic sales but will 
also expand the export market for pharmaceutical, 
non-pharma and aromatherapy products, so that it will 
increase sales growth through this export market. 
d.	
Asset Optimization
PT Kimia Farma Tbk has assets in various regions and 
is located in a strategic location, so it is necessary to 
optimize assets, both to meet the internal needs of 
Kimia Farma Group and to cooperate with strategic 
partners, after conducting a study of the highest 
and best used of all these assets, so that optimal and 
maximum results will be obtained from the efforts to 
optimize the Company’s assets.

Talent Development

continue to develop HC through various training and 
development programs which in the future will become 
potential talents, especially for young talents and female 
talents to become the next leaders both in the Company, 
Holding Pharmaceutical SOE and other SOEs.
