Secara segmentasi, melanjutkan tahun sebelumnya, 
segmen Freight Charter perseroan pada tahun 2023 
kembali memiliki kontribusi lebih dari separuh total

peningkatan secara nominal.

Perseroan pada tahun 2023 mengalami kenaikan dari

Revenue

466.99% or Rp285.61 billion, the Company’s revenue 
in 2023 recorded a slight correction. The company’s

billion from the previous year at Rp346.77 billion.

Revenue from the Freight Charter segment weakened

an increase of 46.27% or Rp24.72 billion from the previous 
year at Rp53.42 billion. From year to year, the Time Charter 
segment has consistently increased in nominal terms. 
 
Despite the decline in revenue, the Company’s EBITDA

Nickel Downstreaming Policy will Increase Demand 
for Transportation Needs
In line with coal, the national nickel sector also continues 
to increase from year to year. Based on ESDM data, 
national nickel ore production in 2023 will almost touch 
the 200 million ton mark, namely 193.50 million tons. This 
figure increased by 106.44% or 99.77 million tons from the 
previous year which was recorded at 93.73 million tons. 
The increase was due to the nickel downstreaming policy 
that has been in effect since January 1, 2020, starting with 
a ban on exporting nickel ore, and followed by an export 
ban for all minerals in ore form, in accordance with Law 
Number 3 of 2020 concerning Mineral and Coal Mining 
(Minerba Law). By 2024, nickel ore production is expected 
to continue to increase, along with the completion of 
several nickel smelters.
The nickel downstreaming policy aims to obtain added 
value from the downstreaming of nickel ore into 
derivative products, so as to increase the export value of 
nickel. Based on the report of the Coordinating Minister 
for Maritime Affairs and Investment, the policy has 
increased the value of nickel exports, where in 2017 when 
Indonesia was still exporting nickel ore, the value of 
export proceeds was only USD 4 billion. This figure then 
increased significantly to USD 33.81 billion or equivalent 
to IDR 504.2 trillion, an increase of around 745% in 
2022, after Indonesia no longer exports nickel ore, but 
processed nickel.

Realisasi Penggunaan Dana Hasil Penawaran Umum | 
Realization of Initial Public Offering Proceeds
Perseroan memperoleh Pernyataan Efektif dari Otoritas 
Jasa Keuangan (OJK) melalui Surat No. S-232/D.04/2021, 
tertanggal 
8 
Desember 
2021, 
untuk 
melakukan 
Penawaran Umum Perdana Saham kepada masyarakat 
sejumlah 370.045.000 lembar saham dengan nilai 
nominal Rp25 per saham, dan dengan harga penawaran 
sebesar Rp117 per saham. Total dana yang diperoleh dari 
Penawaran Umum adalah sebanyak Rp43.295.265.000 
setelah dikurangi dengan biaya emisi adalah sebesar 
Rp38.963.527.226 (Dana Bersih). 
Hingga 31 Desember 2022, seluruh Dana Bersih telah 
direalisasikan Perseroan sesuai dengan peruntukkan 
sebagaimana tercantum dalam Prospektus yang telah 
disampaikan dan dipublikasikan oleh Perseroan. Sesuai 
dengan ketentuan POJK 30/POJK.04/2015 Pasal 6, 
Perseroan telah mempertanggungjawabkan Laporan 
penggunaan hasil penawaran umum periode 30 Juni 
2022 pada Rapat Umum Pemegang Saham Tahunan 
2022 tanggal 14 Juli 2022. Laporan Realisasi Penggunaan 
Dana telah dilaporkan oleh Perseroan kepada OJK 
melalui surat No. 004/DIR-BSML/I/2023 tanggal 14 
Januari 2023 dan telah diunggah pada Sistem Pelaporan 
Elektronik (SPE) OJK.
The Company obtained an Effective Statement from the 
Financial Services Authority (OJK) through Letter No. 
S-232/D.04/2021, dated December 8, 2021, to conduct 
an Initial Public Offering of 370,045,000 shares with a 
nominal value of Rp25 per share, and at an offering price of 
Rp117 per share. The total funds obtained from the Public 
Offering amounted to Rp43,295,265,000 after deducting 
the issuance costs amounted to Rp38,963,527,226 (Net 
Funds). 
As of December 31, 2022, all Net Funds have been realized 
by the Company in accordance with the allocation as 
stated in the Prospectus that has been submitted and 
published by the Company. In accordance with the 
provisions of POJK 30/POJK.04/2015 Article 6, the 
Company has accounted for the Report on the use of 
proceeds from the public offering period June 30, 2022 at 
the 2022 Annual General Meeting of Shareholders on July 
14, 2022. The Realization of Use of Funds Report has been 
reported by the Company to OJK through letter No. 004/
DIR-BSML/I/2023 dated January 14, 2023 and has been 
uploaded to the OJK Electronic Reporting System (SPE).
