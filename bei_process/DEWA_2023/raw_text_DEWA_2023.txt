Kemampuan DEWA dalam membayar hutang, baik jangka

likuiditas Perseroan. Rasio likuiditas yang dapat digunakan 
sebagai acuan Perseroan dalam menjaga likuiditas untuk 
memenuhi kewajibannya adalah; rasio utang terhadap ekuitas, 
rasio utang terhadap ekuitas dan rasio utang terhadap aset.
Rasio Utang terhadap Ekuitas
Rasio utang terhadap ekuitas Perseroan pada tahun 2023 
adalah sebesar 42,89%, turun 17,89% dari tahun 2022 yang 
sebesar 60,78%. Penurunan rasio ini mengindikasi bahwa 
kemampuan Perseroan dalam menyelesaikan utang-utangnya 
semakin lebih baik. Penurunan rasio ini karena penurunan 
jumlah utang yang lebih besar dibandingkan kenaikan jumlah 
ekuitas. 
Rasio Utang terhadap Aset
Rasio utang terhadap aset Perseroan pada tahun 2023 adalah 
sebesar 17,34%, turun 8,45% dari tahun 2022 yang sebesar 
25,79%. Penurunan rasio ini mengindikasi bahwa Perseroan 
mampu menyelesaikan utang-utangnya menggunakan aset 
semakin lebih baik. Penurunan rasio ini disebabkan penurunan 
jumlah utang yang cukup besar kerena pembayaran sesuai 
jadwal.

in financing activities, namely Bank Loan receipts which 
increased by 3.35% or an increase of Rp33.95 billion to

Other Payable - Third Parties amounting to Rp46.84 billion, a 
decrease of 91.87% from Rp576.34 billion in the previous year.

the total increase in funding expenditure for other activities

Overall, the Company recorded an increase in the use of cash 
to support financing activities, meaning that the Company 
made more payments to pay off maturing liabilities than 
receiving funds from financing activities, with a total cash 
outflow of Rp773.09 billion.

billion, an increase of 27.30% from Rp303.90 billion in the 
previous year.

Dividend Policy
In accordance with the prevailing laws and regulations, 
dividend payments must be approved by the shareholders at 
the Annual General Meeting of Shareholders. The Company’s 
Articles of Association state that dividends can only be paid 
under the Company’s financial capacity based on decisions 
made at the General Meeting of Shareholders.
All of the Company’s issued and fully paid shares have the same 
and equal rights, including the right to dividend distribution.
The Company’s dividend policy is regulated in the Company’s 
Articles of Association, namely Article 19 concerning the Use 
of Profit and Dividend Distribution as described below:
•	
Article 19 paragraph 1: The net profit of the Company in a 
fiscal year is as stated in the balance sheet and profit and 
loss calculation which has been approved by the Annual 
GMS and is a positive balance of profit divided according 
to the method of use determined by the GMS.
•	
Article 19 paragraph 2: Dividends can only be paid in 
accordance with the financial capacity of the Company 
based on the decisions made at the GMS, in which payment 
and the form of dividends must also be determined by 
taking into account the applicable provisions on the Stock 
Exchange in the Republic of Indonesia where shares of the 
Company are listed.
•	
Article 19 paragraph 4: If the profit and loss calculation in a 
fiscal year shows a loss that cannot be covered by a reserve 
fund, then the loss will still be recorded and included in the 
profit and loss calculation and in the next fiscal year and 
the Company is deemed not to have made a profit as long 
as the loss recorded and included in the profit and loss 
calculation has not been completely covered.
•	
Article 19 paragraph 5: The Company may distribute 
interim dividends based on the decision of the Board 
of Directors meeting with the approval of the Board of 
Commissioners and the decision must be reported at the 
next Annual GMS.

Kebijakan Transaksi Berelasi
•	
Transaksi berelasi terjadi semata-mata karena adanya 
kepentingan bisnis yang sama dan dapat saling 
mendukung diantara para pihak. Melalui transaksi 
tersebut, para pihak dapat bersinergi dan bersama-sama 
mendapatkan manfaat optimal dari transaksi yang terjadi 
karena menyangkut kesamaan tujuan dan adanya saling 
pengertian.
•	
Kebijakan yang berlaku dalam proses maupun harga 
transaksi pada dasarnya sama dengan transaksi dengan 
pihak ketiga yang lain, yakni mendapatkan/membayar 
dengan harga yang wajar atau harga terbaik dibandingkan 
harga pasar yang berlaku untuk memperoleh barang/jasa 
dengan kualitas yang sama.
