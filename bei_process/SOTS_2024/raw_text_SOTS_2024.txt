ANALISA PEREKONOMIAN DAN INDUSTRI
ANALYSIS OF ECONOMY AND INDUSTRY
Tahun 2024 membawa berbagai tren dan pola global yang 
memengaruhi perkembangan bisnis di berbagai sektor. Beberapa 
aspek utama yang menjadi fokus adalah antara lain Transformasi 
Digital & Integrasi AI. Perusahaan semakin mempercepat adopsi 
kecerdasan buatan (AI) dan otomatisasi. AI digunakan dalam 
analitik data, chatbot, dan otomatisasi proses bisnis untuk 
meningkatkan efisiensi, menekan biaya, serta meningkatkan 
pengalaman pelanggan. Disamping itu, Keberlanjutan & Bisnis 
Ramah Lingkungan juga turut memengaruhi perkembangan bisnis 
di berbagai sektor. Faktor Lingkungan, Sosial, dan Tata Kelola (ESG) 
tetap menjadi prioritas utama. Perusahaan mulai berinvestasi 
dalam praktik bisnis berkelanjutan, seperti pengurangan emisi 
karbon, ekonomi sirkular, dan produk ramah lingkungan guna 
memenuhi regulasi dan harapan konsumen.
Faktor lain yang tidak kalah penting adalah ketidakpastian ekonomi, 
termasuk inflasi, fluktuasi suku bunga, dan ketegangan geopolitik, 
mendorong Perusahaan untuk fokus pada ketahanan finansial. 
Diversifikasi bisnis, strategi manajemen risiko, dan optimalisasi 
biaya menjadi kunci dalam menghadapi tantangan ini. Dari sisi 
digital, Pertumbuhan Ekonomi Digital seperti E-commerce, 
fintech, dan sistem pembayaran digital terus berkembang secara 
global. Munculnya keuangan terdesentralisasi (DeFi), aplikasi 
berbasis blockchain, dan aset digital semakin mengubah lanskap 
industri keuangan dan perilaku konsumen.
Sepanjang tahun 2024, beberapa sektor bisnis nasional di 
Indonesia mengalami perkembangan signifikan yang berkontribusi 
pada pertumbuhan ekonomi negara. Berikut adalah sektor-sektor 
utama yang menunjukkan kemajuan tersebut:
•	 Industri Pengolahan (Manufaktur)	
	
	
	
Industri pengolahan tetap menjadi pilar utama perekonomian 
Indonesia. Pada triwulan II-2024, sektor ini menyumbang 
18,52% terhadap Produk Domestik Bruto (PDB), meningkat 
dari 18,26% pada periode yang sama tahun sebelumnya. 
Data dari Prompt Manufacturing Index (PMI) Bank Indonesia 
menunjukkan ekspansi sebesar 51,54% pada triwulan III-
2024, dengan kapasitas produksi terpakai mencapai 73,13%, 
menandakan operasi yang optimal dan kesiapan memenuhi 
permintaan pasar yang meningkat. 
•	 Transportasi dan Pariwisata	
	
	
	
	
Sektor transportasi mencatat pertumbuhan sebesar 7,92% 
pada kuartal IV dan mencapai 8,69% sepanjang tahun 2024. 
Sejalan dengan itu, sektor akomodasi dan makanan minuman 
tumbuh 6,61% pada kuartal IV dan 8,56% sepanjang tahun 
2024, mencerminkan peningkatan mobilitas masyarakat dan 
aktivitas pariwisata domestik. 
•	 Ekonomi Digital dan E-commerce	
	
	
	
Sektor 
e-commerce 
terus 
menunjukkan 
pertumbuhan 
signifikan. Nilai transaksi perbankan digital diproyeksikan 
meningkat sebesar 23,2% pada tahun 2024, mencapai 
Rp71.584 triliun, didukung oleh sistem pembayaran yang aman 
dan andal. 
•	 Energi dan Sumber Daya Alam	
	
	
	
Pemerintah Indonesia berencana membangun kilang minyak 
dengan total kapasitas 1 juta barel per hari di beberapa pulau, 
termasuk Kalimantan dan Sulawesi, sebagai upaya mengurangi 
ketergantungan pada impor minyak mentah dan bahan bakar. 
Selain itu, Indonesia menargetkan peningkatan produksi gas 
petroleum cair (LPG) sekitar 1 juta metrik ton per tahun untuk 
mengurangi impor dan meningkatkan ketahanan energi 
nasional. 
The year 2024 brings various global trends and patterns that 
affect business development in various sectors. Some of the key 
aspects to focus on include Digital Transformation & AI Integration. 
Companies are accelerating their adoption of artificial intelligence 
(AI) and automation. AI is used in data analytics, chatbots, and 
business process automation to increase efficiency, reduce costs, 
and improve customer experience. In addition, Sustainability 
& Green Business is also influencing business development in 
various sectors. Environmental, Social and Governance (ESG) 
factors remain a top priority. Companies are starting to invest 
in sustainable business practices, such as carbon emission 
reduction, circular economy, and eco-friendly products to meet 
regulations and consumer expectations.
Another important factor is economic uncertainty, including 
inflation, interest rate fluctuations, and geopolitical tensions, 
prompting companies to focus on financial resilience. Business 
diversification, risk management strategies, and cost optimization 
are key in facing these challenges. On the digital front, the growth 
of the Digital Economy such as E-commerce, fintech, and digital 
payment systems continues to expand globally.The rise of 
decentralized finance (DeFi), blockchain-based applications, and 
digital assets is further changing the landscape of the financial 
industry and consumer behavior.
Throughout 2024, several national business sectors in Indonesia 
experienced significant developments that contributed to the 
country’s economic growth. Here are the key sectors that showed 
such progress:
•	 Processing Industry (Manufacturing)	
	
	
	
The manufacturing industry remains the main pillar of the 
Indonesian economy. In Q2-2024, the sector contributed 
18.52% to the Gross Domestic Product (GDP), up from 18.26% in 
the same period the previous year. Data from Bank Indonesia’s 
Prompt Manufacturing Index (PMI) showed an expansion of 
51.54% in Q3-2024, with utilized production capacity reaching 
73.13%, signaling optimal operations and readiness to meet 
increasing market demand.	 	
	
	
	
	
	
	
•	 Transportation and Tourism	
	
	
	
	
The transportation sector recorded growth of 7.92% in the fourth 
quarter and reached 8.69% throughout 2024. Correspondingly, 
the accommodation and food and beverage sector grew 
by 6.61% in the fourth quarter and 8.56% throughout 2024, 
reflecting increased public mobility and domestic tourism 
activities.
•	 Digital Economy and E-commerce	
	
	
	
The e-commerce sector continues to show significant growth. 
The value of digital banking transactions is projected to increase 
by 23.2% by 2024, reaching IDR71,584 trillion, supported by a 
secure and reliable payment system. 	
	
	
	
	
•	 Energy and Natural Resources	
	
	
	
	
The Indonesian government plans to build oil refineries with 
a total capacity of 1 million barrels per day on several islands, 
including Kalimantan and Sulawesi, in an effort to reduce 
dependence on crude oil and fuel imports. In addition, 
Indonesia targets to increase liquefied petroleum gas (LPG) 
production by around 1 million metric tons per year to reduce 
imports and improve national energy security.

business prospects in the Property, Real Estate and tourism

for the Company in terms of increasing business income and 
profits both short and long term.

North Jakarta, which is planned to include a harbor, theme park, 
and international racing circuit, show great potential in attracting 
investment and boosting the tourism sector.

and long term can continue to show optimism. Due to the positive
