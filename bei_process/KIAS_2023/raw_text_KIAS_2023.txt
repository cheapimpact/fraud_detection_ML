Tinjauan Operasi Segmen Usaha
Review of Operational Business Segments
Perseroan bergerak di bidang usaha “Industri bahan bangunan 
dari keramik” dengan aktivitas operasional meliputi produksi 
keramik dinding serta keramik lantai beserta aksesorisnya. 
Untuk keramik genteng, secara khusus diproduksi oleh entitas 
anak Perseroan.
KERAMIK DINDING
Produksi
Di tahun 2023, Perseroan mencatat adanya penurunan 
sebesar 30,5% pada produksi keramik dinding menjadi 
2,84 juta m2 dari sebelumnya 4,09 juta m2 di tahun 2022. 
Menurunnya jumlah produksi ini dipicu oleh faktor seperti 
penurunan permintaan pasar akibat ketidakstabilan kinerja 
industri keramik nasional.

KERAMIK LANTAI
Produksi
Segmen keramik lantai membukukan jumlah produksi 
untuk tahun 2023 sebanyak 8,60 juta m2, lebih rendah 1,9% 
dari produksi tahun 2022 sebanyak 8,76 juta m2. Hal ini

ketidakstabilan kinerja industri keramik nasional.
The Company is engaged in the business of “Building 
Material Industry from Ceramics” with operational activities 
including the production of wall tile and floor tiles and their 
accessories. For roof tile, it is specifically produced by the 
Company’s subsidiaries.
WALL TILE
Production
In 2023, the Company recorded a 30.5% decrease in wall 
tile production to 2.84 million m2 from 4.09 million m2 in 
2022. This decline in production was primarily influenced by 
factors such as a decrease in market demand, which resulted 
from the national ceramic industry's performance instability.
REVENUE
Revenue generated by wall ceramic products 2023 decreased 
(33.77%) to Rp121.43 billion from 2022 revenue of Rp183.34 
billion. The cause of this decline in revenue was the decline in 
market demand due to weakening purchasing power, rising 
inflation rates, and market competition with imported ceramic 
products from China.
FLOOR TILE
Production
The floor tiles segment booked a total production for 2023 
of 8.60 million m2, 1.9% lower than the 2022 production of 
8.76 million m2. This is due to the decline in market demand 
due to the instability of the performance of the national 
ceramic industry.

BEBAN POKOK PENDAPATAN

penjualan, akan tetapi terdapat kenaikan pada harga bahan

berjumlah Rp6,57 miliar. Penyebab terjadinya perolehan rugi 
komprehensif ini adalah peningkatan pada rasio beban pokok 
pendapatan terhadap pendapatan dan beban administrasi.
REVENUE
In 2023, the Company had a revenue of Rp 562.62 billion. This 
amount decreased by 13.17% from the revenue in 2022, which 
amounted to Rp647.95 billion. This decline is due to decreased 
market demand due to weakening purchasing power, rising 
inflation rates, and market competition with imported ceramic 
products from China.
COST OF REVENUE
In 2023, the Company had a revenue cost of Rp577.94 billion. 
This amount decreased by 6.14% from the cost of revenue 
in 2022, which amounted to Rp615.75 billion. The decrease 
in the cost of revenue was considered to be in line with the 
decline in sales, but there was an increase in the price of raw 
materials for production, which resulted in a decrease in costs 
that were not as large as the decrease in sales.
Total Comprehensive Profit (Loss)
In 2023, the Company recorded a comprehensive loss 
of Rp33.17 billion. This amount has changed from the 
previous comprehensive income in 2022, which amounted 
to Rp6.57 billion. The cause of the comprehensive loss was 
an increase in the ratio of cost of revenue to revenue and 
administrative expenses.

LIQUIDITY RATIO
The Company's ability to fulfill its financial obligations through 
debt repayment demonstrates the Company's reputation 
in carrying out its responsibilities as a business entity with 
integrity. The Company's capability to fulfill these obligations 
is measured by liquidity ratios, including current, quick, and 
cash ratios. 
In 2023, the Company demonstrated its commitment to fulfill 
its obligations with the results obtained from calculating the 
liquidity ratio. From the current ratio, the result recorded 
in 2023 reached 214.61%, higher than the 2022 result of 
189.15%. For the quick ratio, there was an increase in 2023 
to 152.68% from 149.43% in 2022. Meanwhile, the cash ratio 
recorded a value of 24.99%, an increase compared to the 
2022 value of 5.12%.
SOLVABILITY RATIO
Two solvency ratios, the Liability to Total Assets Ratio (DAR) and 
the Liability to Total Equity Ratio (DER), are used to measure 
the level of solvency. These two ratios measure the Company's 
ability to fulfill its obligations while maintaining stable financial 
performance. 
The Company can maintain a financial balance in 2023, 
as reflected in the solvency ratio calculation. The ratio of 
Liabilities to Total Assets (DAR) in 2023 is lower than the 
previous year's ratio by recording a ratio result of 15.34% 
for 2023. The Liability to Total Equity Ratio (DER) for 2023 is 
18.12%, which is lower than the 2022 ratio.

Dilansir dari laporan World Economic Outlook edisi Januari

pertumbuhan ekonomi global untuk tahun 2024 diproyeksikan 
hanya sebesar 3,1%. Dibandingkan proyeksi yang disampaikan 
IMF di laporan sebelumnya, proyeksi dalam laporan edisi 
Januari 2024 tersebut naik 0,2 poin namun masih cenderung 
stagnan dibandingkan tahun 2023. Sementara dari World 
Bank, prediksi perekonomian global hanya mengalami 
pertumbuhan 2,4%.
Di tahun 2024, negara-negara diproyeksikan oleh IMF 
mengalami penurunan pada pertumbuhannya sebelum 
mengalami kenaikan di tahun 2025 dengan pertumbuhan 
di zona euro dan moderasi pertumbuhan AS. Sementara 
pertumbuhan stabil diperkirakan akan terjadi pada negara-
negara emerging market dan negara-negara berkembang 
hingga tahun 2024 dan 2025, dengan variasi yang terjadi di 
setiap wilayah. IMF juga memperkirakan laju inflasi global 
akan mengalami penurunan menjadi 5,8% pada tahun 2024 
dan 4,4% pada tahun 2025 dengan proyeksi tahun 2025 telah 
direvisi menjadi lebih rendah dari estimasi sebelumnya. 
Dari sisi ekonomi dalam negeri, Bank Indonesia masih 
memiliki optimisme akan pertumbuhan ekonomi Indonesia 
untuk tahun 2024. Gubernur BI menyampaikan bahwa 
proyeksi untuk pertumbuhan ekonomi Indonesia tahun 
2024 dipertahankan di angka 4,7% sampai 5,5% secara 
tahunan. Adanya keyakinan positif dari para pelaku ekonomi 
dalam negeri memperkuat keyakinan bahwa pertumbuhan 
Indonesia akan tetap baik. 
Aspek performa industri keramik nasional menurut Asosiasi 
Aneka Industri Keramik Indonesia (ASAKI) diperkirakan 
mengalami pertumbuhan positif di tahun 2024 dengan 
estimasi tingkat utilisasi kapasitas produksi nasional 
berada di persentase mencapai 75%. Sisi produksi keramik 
ditargetkan sejumlah 445 juta meter persegi (m2) untuk 
tahun 2024. Dibandingkan produksi yang terealisasi di tahun 
2023 sebanyak 418 juta meter persegi (m2), target jumlah 
tahun 2024 tersebut lebih tinggi 6,4%. Pertumbuhan positif 
ini didorong salah satunya oleh adanya peningkatan yang 
terus ada pada kapasitas produksi terpasang sejak tahun 
2020. Di tahun 2020 tersebut, kapasitas produksi keramik 
nasional mencapai 537 juta per m2 dan terus meningkat 
hingga di tahun 2023 mencapai 598 juta unit per m2.
According to the January 2024 edition of the World Economic 
Outlook report issued by the International Monetary Fund 
(IMF), global economic growth for 2024 is projected at only 
3.1%. Compared to the IMF's projection in the previous 
report, the projection in the January 2024 edition increased 
by 0.2 points but still tends to stagnate compared to 
2023. Meanwhile, the World Bank predicts that the global 
economy will only experience growth of 2.4%.
In 2024, countries are projected by the IMF to experience a 
decline in growth before picking up in 2025 with growth in the 
Eurozone and moderation in US growth. Meanwhile, stable 
growth is expected in emerging markets and developing 
countries until 2024 and 2025, with variations across regions. 
The IMF also expects the global inflation rate to decline to 5.8% 
in 2024 and 4.4% in 2025, with the 2025 projection revised to 
be lower than the previous estimate. 
Regarding the domestic economy, Bank Indonesia is still 
optimistic about Indonesia's economic growth for 2024. The 
BI Governor said that the projection for Indonesia's economic 
growth in 2024 is maintained at 4.7% to 5.5% annually. The 
positive confidence of domestic economic players reinforces 
the belief that Indonesia's growth will remain favorable.
According to the Indonesian Ceramic Industry Association 
(ASAKI), the performance aspect of the national ceramic 
industry is expected to experience favorable growth in 2024, 
with an estimated national production capacity utilization 
rate at a percentage of 75%. The ceramic production side is 
targeted at 445 million square meters (m2) for 2024. Compared 
to the production realized in 2023 of 418 million square meters 
(m2), the 2024 target amount is 6.4% higher. This positive 
growth was partly driven by the increased installed production 
capacity since 2020. In 2020, the national ceramic production 
capacity reached 537 million per m2 and continued to increase 
until 2023, when it reached 598 million units per m2.

Bagi Perseroan, aspek pemasaran masih menjadi bagian

display yang berisi informasi produk untuk menarik pelanggan.

pemasaran dari tahun-tahun sebelumnya.  Strategi pemasaran 
tersebut antara lain mencakup:

High Value Added (HVA) khususnya KIA dan KIA HD dengan 
target segmen pasar menengah ke atas untuk menjaga 
daya saing Perseroan di industri keramik;
2.	 Fokus pada pengembangan desain produk yang indah dan 
fokus menjaga kualitas produk-produk yang dihasilkan

cara; dan promosi ukuran produk yang lebih besar guna 
mengikuti tren pasar sehingga pelanggan memiliki 
berbagai pilihan ukuran yang sesuai dengan kebutuhan;
4.	 Implementasi program pengurangan biaya melalui 
rekondisi mesin untuk meningkatkan efisiensi produksi 
dan laba operasi;
5.	 Penerapan proses produksi terbaik dan peningkatan 
investasi pada teknologi produksi, serta pelaksanaan 
penelitian dan pengembangan teknologi tersebut untuk 
menunjang Perseroan dalam memberikan produk terbaik 
dan ramah lingkungan bagi pelanggan; dan
6.	 Pengembangan kompetensi karyawan serta penerapan 
Total Quality Management (TQM) dan pendekatan 
pemecahan masalah di seluruh fungsi Perseroan.

that supports the Company's steps in increasing brand 
awareness, communicating the unique value of products to 
customers, maintaining business continuity, and achieving 
better business growth from year to year. In improving this

several strategic steps such as innovation with new products, 
managing communication media facilities with customers

1.	 Focus on developing new product designs with High-Value

market segment being the upper-middle class to maintain

technologies to support the Company in providing the best

problem-solving approach in all functions of the Company.

Persaingan yang semakin ketat di industri keramik mendorong Perseroan untuk lebih fokus 
meningkatkan efisiensi di segala bidang, terutama produksi, operasional dan tidak terkecuali

dilakukannya Transaksi Afiliasi ini dengan tujuan untuk digunakan sebagai modal kerja Perseroan.
Increasing competition in the ceramic industry encourage the Company to be more focus on 
increasing efficiency in all fields, especially production, operational and funding as well. To be 
able to stay competitive and continuously follow the update trend and technology in ceramic 
industry, Company needs adequate investment and funding. In order to execute the plan 
according to the set target period, and considering financial condition of KKM can support the 
plan, therefore this Transaction Affiliation is carried out with the purpose to be used as working 
capital of the Company.
