Economic Review
Pada tahun 2024, perekonomian Indonesia menunjukkan 
kinerja yang solid meskipun menghadapi berbagai 
tantangan domestik dan global. Badan Pusat Statistik 
menyampaikan pada 5 Februari 2025 bahwa perekonomian 
Indonesia tumbuh sebesar 5,03% pada tahun 2024, sedikit 
melambat dibandingkan dengan pertumbuhan 5,05% 
pada tahun sebelumnya. 
Meskipun demikian, angka ini tetap menunjukkan 
pertumbuhan yang stabil. Faktor-faktor yang mendorong 
pertumbuhan tersebut antara lain peningkatan belanja 
politik, investasi yang meningkat, dan percepatan proyek 
infrastruktur.  
Beberapa faktor utama yang mendukung ketahanan dan 
pertumbuhan ekonomi Indonesia selama tahun tersebut 
antara lain adalah:
1.	Konsumsi rumah tangga, yang menyumbang sekitar 
setengah dari PDB, tumbuh sebesar 4,91% secara 
tahunan pada kuartal ketiga 2024. Meskipun ada 
penurunan kecil dibandingkan kuartal sebelumnya, 
konsumsi

In 2024, the Indonesian economy showed solid 
performance despite facing various domestic and global 
challenges. The Central Statistics Agency reported on 
February 5, 2025 that the Indonesian economy grew by 
5.03% in 2024, slightly slower than the 5.05% growth in 
the previous year. 
Nevertheless, this figure still shows stable growth. Factors 
driving this growth include increased political spending, 
increased investment, and accelerated infrastructure 
projects.
Some of the main factors that supported Indonesia’s 
economic resilience and growth during the year included:
1.	Household consumption, which accounts for about half 
of GDP, grew by 4.91% year-on-year in the third quarter 
of 2024. Despite a slight decline compared to the 
previous quarter, consumption remains the main driver 
of economic growth;

Industri kelapa sawit Indonesia memainkan peran 
penting dalam perekonomian nasional dan pasar global.

terhadap perekonomian negara. Pada tahun 2024, ekspor

Indonesia’s palm oil industry plays a vital role in the 
national economy and the global market. As the world’s 
largest producer of palm oil, Indonesia accounts for around 
58% of global production, equivalent to 23% of the world’s 
total vegetable oil production. 
The Indonesian palm oil industry contributes significantly 
to the country’s economy. In 2024, Indonesia’s palm oil

a sustainable, efficient, and competitive palm oil industry.

The B40 biodiesel program, which requires a 40% palm 
oil blend in biodiesel, is planned to be fully implemented 
by March 2025. This step aims to reduce dependence on 
imported diesel and increase domestic demand for palm 
oil.
Monthly data shows a decline in export volume. According 
to the Indonesian Palm Oil Entrepreneurs Association 
(GAPKI), in December 2024, Indonesia’s palm oil exports 
are estimated to reach 24.8 million tons, 10% lower than 
the previous year. The decline in export volume in 2024 is 
influenced by several factors, including increased domestic 
consumption due to the B40 biodiesel program which 
increases domestic demand for palm oil. GAPKI estimates 
that domestic palm oil consumption will increase to 25.4 
million tons in 2024, with biodiesel consumption reaching 
11.6 million tons. 
Despite the decline in export volume, Indonesia’s palm 
oil sector remains a major contributor to the national 
economy. However, challenges such as price fluctuations, 
international regulations, and changes in global market 
demand require effective adaptation strategies to maintain 
the competitiveness of the Indonesian palm oil industry in 
the international market.

Business Segment Review
Perseroan yang didirikan pada tanggal, 28 Aprll 1995, 
merupakan suatu Perusahaan Terbatas di Indonesia yang 
berfokus pada perkebunan kelapa sawit. Kegiatan usaha 
utama Perseroan yaitu penanaman tanaman kelapa sawit, 
pemanenan dan pengolahan TBS yang menghasilkan CPO 
serta Palm Kernel/”PK”. 
Kebun dan fasilitas pengolahan TBS Perseroan berada 
di Kabupaten Banyuasin, Provinsi Sumatera Selatan, 
sedangkan kantor pusatnya terletak di Jakarta.
Berdasarkan Pasal 3 ayat (2) Anggaran Dasar, Perseroan 
dapat melakukan kegiatan usaha sebagai berikut: 
1.	Perkebunan buah kelapa sawit, mencakup usaha 
perkebunan mulai dari kegiatan pengolahan lahan, 
penyemaian, pembibitan, penanaman, pemeliharaan 
dan pemanenan buah kelapa sawit, termasuk kegiatan 
pembibitan dan pembenihan tanaman buah kelapa 
sawit.
2.	Industri minyak mentah kelapa sawit (Crude Palm 
Oil/CPO), mencakup usaha pengolahan kelapa sawit 
menjadi minyak mentah (CPO) yang masih perlu diolah 
lebih lanjut dan biasanya produk ini dipakai oleh industri 
lain.
3.	Industri minyak mentah inti kelapa sawit (Crude Palm 
Kernel Oil/CPKO), mencakup usaha pengolahan inti 
kelapa sawit menjadi minyak mentah inti (CPKO) yang 
masih perlu diolah lebih lanjut dan biasanya produk ini 
dipakai oleh industri lain; dan 
4.	Industri pemisahan/fraksinasi minyak mentah kelapa 
sawit dan minyak mentah inti kelapa sawit, mencakup 
pemisahan fraksi padat dan fraksi cair dari minyak 
mentah kelapa sawit menjadi minyak mentah kelapa 
sawit olein (Crude Palm Olein) dan minyak mentah kelapa 
sawit stearin (Crude Palm Stearin) atau dari minyak 
mentah inti kelapa sawit menjadi minyak mentah inti 
kelapa sawit olein (Crude Palm Kernel Olein) dan minyak 
mentah inti kelapa sawit stearin (Crude Palm Kernel 
Stearin) yang masih perlu diolah lebih lanjut.
Proses Pengolahan dan Pemasaran Kelapa 
Sawit 
Minyak kelapa sawit adalah salah satu sumber minyak 
nabati di Indonesia dan dunia. Minyak kelapa sawit (CPO) 
dan inti kelapa sawit (PK) dapat diekstraksi dari tanaman 
kelapa sawit. Proses pengolahan CPO lebih lanjut dapat 
dipergunakan sebagai bahan untuk membuat sabun, 
deterjen, minyak goreng, dan biodiesel. Sedangkan PK 
dapat diolah lebih lanjut menjadi bahan baku sabun dan 
produk perawatan tubuh.
The Company, which was established on April 28, 1995, is a 
Limited Liability Company in Indonesia that focuses on oil 
palm plantations. The Company’s main business activities 
are planting oil palm plants, harvesting and processing 
FFB which produces CPO and Palm Kernel/”PK”. 
The Company’s FFB plantations and processing facilities 
are located in Banyuasin Regency, South Sumatra Province, 
while its head office is located in Jakarta. 
Based on Article 3 paragraph (2) of the Articles of 
Association, the Company may carry out the following 
business activities:
1.	Oil palm plantations, including plantation businesses 
starting from land processing, sowing, nursery, planting, 
maintenance and harvesting of oil palm fruit, including 
nursery and seeding activities of oil palm fruit plants. 
2.	Crude Palm Oil (CPO) industry, including palm oil 
processing businesses into crude oil (CPO) which still 
needs to be processed further and usually this product 
is used by other industries. 
3.	Crude Palm Kernel Oil (CPKO) industry, including palm 
kernel processing businesses into crude oil kernels 
(CPKO) which still needs to be processed further and 
usually this product is used by other industries; and 
4.	The industry of separation/fractionation of crude palm 
oil and crude palm kernel oil, includes the separation 
of solid fractions and liquid fractions from crude palm 
oil into crude palm olein (Crude Palm Olein) and crude 
palm stearin (Crude Palm Stearin) or from crude palm 
kernel oil into crude palm kernel olein (Crude Palm 
Kernel Olein) and crude palm kernel stearin (Crude Palm 
Kernel Stearin) which still need to be processed further.
Palm Oil Processing and Marketing Process 
Palm oil is one of the sources of vegetable oil in Indonesia 
and the world. Crude palm oil (CPO) and palm kernel 
(PK) can be extracted from oil palm plants. Further 
processing of CPO can be used as a material for making 
soap, detergent, cooking oil, and biodiesel. While PK can 
be further processed into raw materials for soap and body 
care products.

Hutang
Rasio likuiditas merupakan rasio yang menunjukkan 
kemampuan Perseroan dalam memenuhi kewajiban 
keuangan yang kurang dari 1 tahun dengan aset Perseroan 
yang juga dibawah 1 tahun, yang ditunjukkan dengan 
rasio lancar dan rasio kas. 
Pada akhir tahun 2024, Perseroan membukukan Rasio 
Lancar sebesar 0,768 kali atau membaik dibandingkan 
tahun sebelumnya sebesar 0,578 kali. Ini bisa menjadi 
indikator risiko keuangan, karena Perseroan mungkin 
memiliki kesulitan dalam memenuhi kewajiban jangka 
pendeknya dengan menggunakan aset yang dimilikinya. 
Rasio lancar di bawah 1 juga dianggap sebagai tanda 
peringatan, karena menunjukkan bahwa Perseroan 
mungkin memiliki keterbatasan likuiditas.
Sementara, rasio solvabilitas merupakan perbandingan 
dana yang disediakan oleh Perseroan dengan dana yang 
dipinjam dari kreditur. Rasio ini dimaksudkan untuk 
mengukur sampai seberapa jauh aset Perseroan dibiayai 
oleh utang, dan merupakan indikasi tingkat keamanan 
dari para kreditur.
Solvency
To measure the Company’s ability to pay long-term and 
short-term obligations, the Company has calculated the 
following liquidity ratios and solvency ratios:
Liquidity and Solvency 
The liquidity ratio is a ratio that shows the Company’s 
ability to meet financial obligations of less than 1 year 
with the Company’s assets which are also below 1 year, as 
indicated by the current ratio and cash ratio. 
At the end of 2024, the Company posted a Current Ratio 
of 0.768 times or improved compared to the previous year 
of 0.578 times. This could be an indicator of financial risk, 
because the Company may have difficulty in meeting its 
short-term obligations using its assets. A current ratio 
below 1 is also considered a warning sign, as it indicates 
that the Company may have limited liquidity. 
Meanwhile, the solvency ratio is a comparison of funds 
provided by the Company with funds borrowed from 
creditors. This ratio is intended to measure the extent to 
which the Company’s assets are financed by debt, and is an 
indication of the level of security of the creditors.

Kebijakan Dividen
Dividend Policy
Pembagian dividen di dalam suatu perseroan diatur dalam 
Undang-Undang No. 40 Tahun 2007 tentang Perseroan 
Terbatas dan anggaran dasar Perseroan. Seluruh laba bersih 
dikurangi penyisihan untuk cadangan dibagikan kepada 
pemegang saham sebagai dividen, kecuali ditentukan 
lain dalam Rapat Umum Pemegang Saham. Dividen hanya 
boleh dibagikan apabila Perseroan mempunyai saldo laba 
yang positif.
Sesuai dengan keputusan Rapat Umum Pemegang Saham 
Tahunan (RUPST) Tahun 2024 dan 2023, Perseroan tidak 
membagikan dividen untuk kinerja pada tahun buku 2023 
dan 2022.
