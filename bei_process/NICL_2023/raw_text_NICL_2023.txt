Struktur Modal
Capital Structure
Perseroan berkomitmen untuk menjaga permodalan yang 
baik dan sehat dengan menjaga struktur modal melalui 
penyesuaian pembayaran dividen kepada Pemegang 
Saham, menerbitkan saham baru, serta pendanaan melalui

struktur modal menggunakan rasio utang terhadap ekuitas, 
yang merupakan nilai utang dibagi dengan jumlah ekuitas.
The Company is committed to maintaining a healthy 
capital structure by adjusting dividend payments 
to Shareholders, issuing new shares, and funding 
through loans if necessary. Additionally, the Company 
monitors the capital structure using the debt-to-
equity ratio, which is the debt value divided by the 
total equity.

industries in Indonesia, downstreaming and development 
initiatives in the nickel industry implemented by the 
government, as well as climate change conferences, and 
the transition to environmentally friendly vehicles.

Ketidakpastian Kondisi Ekonomi
Economic Conditions Uncertainties
Ketidakpastian kondisi ekonomi global akan terus 
berlanjut di sepanjang tahun 2024, dengan proyeksi 
perlambatan Bank Indonesia sebesar 2,8%. Perlambatan 
tersebut dipengaruhi oleh eskalasi geopolitik di Timur 
Tengah dan Benua Eropa. Namun, Bank Indonesia dan 
pemerintah akan saling bersinergi dan berkoordinasi untuk 
mengefektifkan kebijakan dalam menjaga ketahanan 
ekonomi nasional, sehingga pertumbuhan tetap terjaga 
dengan baik, terutama kondisi inflasi yag terkendali.
Global economic uncertainty is expected to persist 
throughout 2024, with Bank Indonesia projecting 
a slowdown of 2.8%. This slowdown is influenced 
by geopolitical escalations in the Middle East and 
Europe. However, Bank Indonesia and the government 
will synergize and coordinate efforts to effectively 
implement policies to maintain national economic 
resilience, ensuring that growth remains robust, 
particularly with controlled inflation conditions.
