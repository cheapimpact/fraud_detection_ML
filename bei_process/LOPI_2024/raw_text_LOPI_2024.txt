Tinjauan Ekonomi
Economic Overview
Prospek pertumbuhan ekonomi global hingga 
akhir tahun 2024 masih dihadapkan pada berbagai 
tantangan. 
Faktor 
utama 
yang

bunga yang masih tinggi.
The global economic growth outlook through the 
end of 2024 remains challenged by various factors. 
Key contributors to this situation include rising 
geopolitical instability in several regions, economic 
slowdown in major economies such as the United 
States and China, as well as persistently high 
interest rate policies.
Bank 
Indonesia 
memperkirakan

Indonesia diprediksi tetap tumbuh stabil, dengan 
perkiraan pertumbuhan sebesar 5,0% pada tahun 
2024 dan meningkat menjadi 5,1% pada tahun 2025. 
Pertumbuhan ini didorong oleh kuatnya permintaan 
domestik, tingginya tingkat kepercayaan konsumen, 
serta kelanjutan implementasi Proyek Strategis 
Nasional (PSN).
Bank Indonesia estimates that global economic 
growth in 2024 will reach 3.2% before slowing to 
2.7% in 2025. Meanwhile, Indonesia's economy is 
expected to remain stable, with a projected growth 
rate of 5.0% in 2024 and increasing to 5.1% in 2025. 
This growth is driven by strong domestic demand, 
high consumer confidence, and the continued 
implementation of National Strategic Projects (PSN).
Meski demikian, berbagai tantangan eksternal 
seperti

stabilitas serta mendorong pertumbuhan ekonomi 
Indonesia di tengah ketidakpastian global.
Nevertheless, various external challenges, such as 
geopolitical tensions and global monetary policy 
dynamics, may impact future economic prospects. 
Therefore, a flexible and responsive policy strategy 
at the domestic level is essential to maintain stability 
and drive Indonesia's economic growth amid global 
uncertainties.

2.
DELIVERY PLANNING
2.
DELIVERY PLANNING
SPV melakukan koordinasi proses delivery planning 
untuk menentukan jenis truk berdasarkan (kapasitas, 
kondisi jalan dan ketersediaan truk), pengemudi, 
dan menentukan biaya perjanjian berdasarkan 
(jarak, banyaknya muatan, daerah pengiriman, berat 
muatan, dan metode pembayaran dilakukan secara 
lump sum atau bulanan).
SPV coordinates the delivery planning process to 
determine the type of truck based on factors such 
as capacity, road conditions, and truck availability, 
assigns drivers, and establishes the agreement cost 
based on distance, cargo volume, delivery area, cargo 
weight, and the selected payment method, whether 
lump sum or monthly.
3.
DELIVERY SCHEDULE
3.
DELIVERY SCHEDULE
SPV melakukan penjadwalan pengiriman sesuai 
dengan order pelanggan.
The Supervisor (SPV) schedules the delivery in 
accordance with the customer's order.
4.
DELIVERY
4.
DELIVERY
Sales Admin mengeluarkan Surat Perintah Muat (SPM) 
untuk pengemudi. Kemudian, kasir menerbitkan 
estimasi voucher (slip uang jalan), setelah mendapat 
slip uang jalan pengemudi mengirimkan muatan 
ke pelanggan. Setelah pengiriman, pengemudi 
menyerahkan surat-surat (SPM, MRC, DO, dan POD) 
kepada SPV, lalu SPV melakukan validasi surat-
surat tersebut. Kasir memverifikasi slip uang jalan 
dan sisa uang jalan sebagai laporan operation close 
report (OCR) serta menyerahkan lock transaksi OCR 
ke AR Accounting untuk dilakukan approval. Setelah 
dilakukan approval, kasir menerbitkan voucher 
payment. AR Accounting memproses bukti transaksi 
pembayaran 
dan 
menerbitkan 
invoice. 
Proses 
pencatatan MRC ini dilakukan semua via aplikasi 
menggunakan 
sistem 
Transport 
Management 
System (TMS) yang dibentuk oleh Perseroan.
Sales Admin issues an Order Load (SPM) for driver. 
Then, cashier publish estimated voucher (travel 
money slip), after got a travel money slip driver send 
load to customer. After delivery, the driver delivers 
letters (SPM, MRC, DO, and POD) to the SPV, and then 
the SPV carries out validation letters. The Cashier 
verify travel money slips and remaining travel money 
as operation close report (OCR) report as well submit 
the OCR transaction lock to AR Accounting for 
approval is carried out. After approval, the cashier 
issue payment vouchers. AR Accounting processes 
proof transaction payment and issuing invoices. This 
MRC recording process done all via the application use 
Transport Management System (TMS) established by 
the Company.
