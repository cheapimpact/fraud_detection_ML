Explanation and Productivity of Business 
Segment
PT Citra Borneo Utama Tbk is one of the companies operating 
in the downstream palm oil industry by producing and selling 
palm oil derivative products such as Refined Bleached 
Deodorized Palm Oil (RBDPO), Palm Fatty Acid Distillate 
(PFAD), Olein, Stearin , Crude Palm Kernel Oil (CPKO) and 
Palm Kernel Expeller (PKE).
In the production process, the Company needs main raw 
materials in the form of Crude Palm Oil (CPO). CPO is a 
product extracted from the mesocarp (fruit flesh) of oil palm 
and then used by the Company as raw material in the Refinery 
process in the Company. While PK is the kernel or seed of oil 
palm fruit which is then used by the Company in the Kernel 
Crushing process in the Company. The Company obtains 
Crude Palm Oil (CPO) and Palm Kernel (PK) supplies from a 
number of companies. The followings are five companies that 
are the Company’s main CPO suppliers: 
1.	
PT Sawit Sumbermas Sarana Tbk; 
2.	
PT Sawit Mandiri Lestari;
3.	
PT Sawit Multi Utama;
4.

prices affect the selling prices of the Company's products 
and ultimately affect the sales performance and overall 
business results of the Company.

dan hasil usaha Perseroan.

karenanya kenaikan maupun penurunan biaya logistik

diiringi inflasi yang masih tinggi.

tumbuh dengan perolehan pasar yang lebih luas. Perseroan 
optimis dalam industri sawit memiliki prospek usaha yang

dengan teknologi saat ini nilai usahanya di tahun 2023 mencapai 
USD38,4 miliar.

strategic route for global trade.
