2023, Europe’s economic outlook for 2024 is expe-
riencing a gradual recovery.  As prices decline and 
as wages rise, Europe’s consumers are starting to 
recoup purchasing power, and that will lift domestic 
demand. European Commission projected a modest-
growth outlook at 0.5% in 2023 and 0.9% in 2024.
China’s economy grew by 5.2 percent in 2023, hitting 
the government’s official target, but concerns about 
growth momentum remain amid a protracted proper-
ty crisis, sluggish consumer and business confidence, 
and weak global growth.

perhaps 3.0-3.5% growth in 2024 as property bot-

remain the dominant story for years to come.

disruption throughout 2023, both in terms of supply

economy is relatively resilient. This achievement is 
the foundation for the growth rate in 2024.

the first quarter of 2022 experienced growth of 5.03%

shows that Indonesia’s economic growth strength-

compared to the first quarter of 2022 which experi-
enced growth of 5.04% YoY. Economic growth in the 
following quarter also showed a positive trend. BPS 
data for quarter III-2023 recorded economic growth 
of 4.94% YoY.

main contributor to the economy, will grow 4.82%

has soared, growing 9.83% throughout 2023. This is 
closely related to the holding of the 2024 elections.
Government consumption throughout 2023 will 
grow by 2.95%. The Government’s policy package 
and optimal absorption of state spending were 
able to encourage Government consumption in the 
fourth quarter and provide direct benefits to society. 
Optimal absorption of state spending also contrib-
utes to encouraging regional economic activity.

karena kebutuhan listrik khususnya yang bersumber 
dari EBT akan terus meningkat dan menjadi fokus 
pemerintah Indonesia maupun global.

to increase electricity supply from EBT sources in 
national energy policy, renewable electricity gener-
ation capacity is projected to continue to grow. For 
2024, the Ministry of Energy and Mineral Resources 
projects the installed capacity of EBT power plants to 
be 13,886 MW or growth of 5.56% compared to the 
realization in 2023 of 13,155 MW.
In 2024, the Government is targeting a national 
energy mix of 19.49% in 2024 and is optimistic that 
it will be able to reach 23% in 2025. To achieve this 
target, the government will continue to encourage 
the development of EBT, both in terms of installed 
capacity, production and consumption.
Going forward, we believe that the prospects of the 
Company’s business will grow even better because 
the need for electricity, especially that sourced from 
EBT, will continue to increase and become the focus 
of the Indonesian and global governments.

usaha Pembangkit Listrik Tenaga Air (PLTA) melalui

sa (Proyek Pakkat), PT Bangun Tirta Lestari (Proyek 
Air Putih), dan entitas anak tidak langsung PT Naga-
ta Dinamika Hidro Madong (Proyek Ma’dong). 
Melalui entitas anak Perseroan yaitu PT Sumatera 
Energi Lestari, Perseroan juga tengah membangun 
proyek PLTM Ordi Hulu yang ditargetkan akan sele-
sai dan mulai beroperasional secara komersil pada 
kuartal pertama 2024.  
Pada tanggal 21 Desember 2023 melalui entitas 
anak tidak langsung Perseroan yaitu PT Tiara Tirta 
Sentosa, Perseroan juga telah berhasil menanda-
tangani Perjanjian Jual Beli Tenaga Listrik dengan 
PT PLN (Persero) untuk PLTM Salu Noling yang 
pembangunannya ditragetkan akan dimulai pada 
kuartal kedua 2024.
Kelima anak perusahaan memproduksi energi baru 
terbarukan yang ditujukan untuk pemenuhan kebu-
tuhan saat ini dan akan datang.
Adapun rata-rata tarif listrik dari sumber EBT yang 
dihasilkan Perseroan adalah Rp1.244,91 per kWh.
As of December 31, 2023, the Company runs the 
hydroelectric power plant business through its sub-
sidiaries PT Energy Sakti Sentosa (Pakkat Project), 
PT Bangun Tirta Lestari (Air Putih Project) and indi-
rect subsidiary PT Nagata Dinamika Hidro Madong 
(Ma’dong Project). 
Through the Company’s subsidiary, PT Sumatera 
Energi Lestari, the Company is also building the Ordi 
Hulu PLTM project which is targeted to be completed 
and start commercial operations in the first quarter 
of 2024. 
On December 21, 2023, through the Company’s 
indirect subsidiary, namely PT Tiara Tirta Sentosa, 
the Company has also successfully signed a Power 
Purchase Agreement with PT PLN (Persero) for the 
Salu Noling PLTM, construction of which is targeted 
to begin in the second quarter of 2024.
The five subsidiaries produce new and renewable 
energy aimed at meeting current and future needs.

revenue. The net profit margin in 2022 was recorded 
at 34.62%.The decrease was due to the decrease of 
electricity production because of El-Nino.

Indicators for measuring profitability include two 
ratios, namely the ratio of profit to assets (Return on 
Assets/ROA) and the ratio of profit to equity (Return

4.58%. Furthermore, as of December 31, 2023, the 
Company recorded an ROE of 7.29% compared to the 
achievement of 8.27% in 2022.
