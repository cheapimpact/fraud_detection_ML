STRUKTUR MODAL
Manajemen
mempertimbangkan
pengelolaan
struktur
modal
yang
baik,
sehingga
memiliki
keleluasaan dalam melakukan kegiatan operasional
dan pada sisi lain memaksimalkan nilai pemegang
saham. Proporsi permodalan Perseroan semakin
meningkat sejalan dengan meningkatnya jumlah
saldo laba dan komponen ekuitas lainnya. Struktur
permodalan Perseroan pada akhir tahun 2023 adalah
dengan posisi tahun 2023 ekuitas membiayai 25%

liabilitas. Tingkat struktur modal yang lebih baik
akan memberikan keleluasaan bagi Perseroan untuk
bertumbuh dan meraih peluang pasar baru.

akan memenuhi ketentuan mengenai pembagian
dividen sebagai komitmen bagi pemegang saham.
Accounts Receivable
The Company has a level of trade receivables of
IDR 79.5 billion in 2023 compared to IDR 82.5
billion in 2022. In 2023 the Company recorded a
receivables turnover of 2.30 times, compared to
2022 which was recorded at 2.56 times, and strive
to increase the level of collectibility of receivables
in the future. Better macro conditions are expected
to increase the collectibility of the Company's
receivables to a better level.
CAPITAL STRUCTURE
Management
considers
good
capital
structure
management, so that it has freedom in carrying out
operational
activities
and
on
the
other hand
maximizes shareholder value. The proportion of the
Company's capital is increasing in line with the
increase in retained earnings and other equity
components. The Company's capital structure at the
end of 2023 is in the position that in 2023 equity
will finance 25% of total assets and the remaining
75% will be covered by liabilities. Meanwhile, in
2022 equity will finance 78.9% of total assets and
the remaining 21.1% will be covered by liabilities.
A better level of capital structure will provide
flexibility for the Company to grow and seize new
market opportunities.
