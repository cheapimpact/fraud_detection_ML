Tinjauan Perekonomian dan Industri
Economic and Industry Overview
Ekonomi global tahun 2024 menunjukkan pertumbuhan 
yang stabil, tetapi masih dihadapkan pada tantangan 
yang signifikan. Berdasarkan laporan World Economic 
Outlook (WEO) yang dirilis oleh International Monetary 
Fund (IMF) pada Januari 2025, pertumbuhan ekonomi 
dunia diperkirakan mencapai 3,2%. Meskipun angka ini 
menunjukkan adanya perbaikan dibandingkan tahun-
tahun sebelumnya, pertumbuhan tersebut masih berada 
di bawah rata-rata historis sebesar 3,7% yang dicatat 
pada periode beberapa tahun terakhir. Perlambatan 
ekonomi global ini masih dipengaruhi oleh faktor-
faktor eksternal seperti konflik geopolitik, kebijakan 
moneter yang ketat, serta perubahan dalam struktur 
perdagangan internasional. Walaupun beberapa negara 
telah mulai melonggarkan kebijakan suku bunga guna 
mendorong investasi dan konsumsi domestik, pemulihan 
masih berlangsung secara bertahap dan tidak merata di 
berbagai negara.
Berdasarkan data yang dirilis oleh Badan Pusat Statistik 
(BPS), perekonomian Indonesia pada tahun 2024 
mencatat pertumbuhan sebesar 5,03% dibandingkan 
tahun 
sebelumnya. 
Meskipun 
mengalami 
sedikit 
perlambatan dibandingkan pertumbuhan tahun 2023 
yang mencapai 5,05%, perekonomian nasional tetap 
menunjukkan stabilitas yang solid.
Dari sisi produksi, sektor dengan pertumbuhan tertinggi 
adalah Jasa Lainnya, yang mencatat kenaikan sebesar 
9,80%, diikuti oleh Transportasi dan Pergudangan dengan 
pertumbuhan 8,69%, serta Penyediaan Akomodasi dan 
Makan Minum yang tumbuh sebesar 8,56%. Sementara 
itu, sektor industri pengolahan, yang memiliki peran 
dominan dalam struktur Produk Domestik Bruto (PDB) 
nasional, mengalami pertumbuhan sebesar 4,43%.
The global economy in 2024 is experiencing steady 
growth, though it continues to face significant 
challenges. According to the World Economic Outlook 
(WEO) report released by the International Monetary 
Fund (IMF) in January 2025, global economic growth is 
projected to reach 3.2%. While this figure represents an 
improvement compared to previous years, the growth 
rate remains below the historical average of 3.7% 
observed in recent years. The ongoing global economic 
slowdown is influenced by external factors, including 
geopolitical conflicts, tight monetary policies, and shifts 
in international trade structures. Although some countries 
have started to ease interest rate policies to stimulate 
investment and domestic consumption, the recovery 
remains gradual and uneven across different regions.
According to data released by the Statistics Indonesia 
(BPS), Indonesia’s economy in 2024 grew by 5.03% 
compared to the previous year. While this marks a slight 
slowdown from the 5.05% growth recorded in 2023, 
the national economy continues to demonstrate solid 
stability.
In terms of production, the sector with the highest 
growth was Other Services, which saw an increase of 
9.80%, followed by Transportation and Warehousing at 
8.69%, and Accommodation and Food and Beverage 
Services with a growth rate of 8.56%. Meanwhile, the 
manufacturing sector, which plays a dominant role in the 
national Gross Domestic Product (GDP) structure, grew 
by 4.43%.

Pada tahun 2024, industri perunggasan Indonesia 
menghadapi berbagai dinamika yang memengaruhi 
kinerjanya. Salah satu isu utama adalah adanya 
keseimbangan 
antara 
supply 
dan 
demand 
yang 
mengakibatkan harga relatif stabil khususnya di DOC 
dan broiler.
Selain itu, penurunan ketersediaan jagung lokal turut 
memicu kenaikan harga pakan, yang berdampak pada 
meningkatnya biaya produksi di berbagai subsistem, 
mulai dari pakan hingga budidaya, serta harga di tingkat 
konsumen. Kondisi ini semakin menekan peternak dalam 
menjaga margin keuntungan mereka di tengah kompetisi 
pasar.
Namun 
demikian, 
terdapat 
optimisme 
terhadap 
perkembangan industri ini. Berdasarkan Laporan Global 
Animal Protein Outlook 2024 dari Rabobank, produksi 
daging unggas diprediksi mengalami peningkatan. Hal ini 
mencerminkan prospek positif bagi industri perunggasan, 
baik secara global maupun di Indonesia.
Pemerintah Indonesia, melalui Kementerian Pertanian 
(Kementan), turut berperan aktif dalam menjaga stabilitas 
sektor perunggasan. Langkah strategis stabilisasi yang 
dilakukan oleh Kementan telah membuahkan hasil positif. 
Harga ayam hidup di tingkat peternak kini berada di atas 
Harga Pokok Produksi (HPP), sehingga peternak dapat 
menikmati margin keuntungan yang wajar. Tantangan 
utama seperti fluktuasi harga menjadi perhatian serius 
pemerintah, yang diatasi dengan mengkonsolidasikan 
pelaku 
usaha 
dan 
asosiasi 
perunggasan 
untuk 
menciptakan stabilitas harga dan keberlanjutan usaha.
Secara keseluruhan, meskipun menghadapi tantangan 
dan kenaikan biaya produksi, industri perunggasan 
Indonesia 
pada 
tahun 
2024 
tetap 
menunjukkan 
pertumbuhan yang positif. Hal ini didukung oleh 
peningkatan permintaan pasar serta harga yang stabil.
In 2024, Indonesia’s poultry industry faces various 
dynamics that affect its performance. One of the main 
issues is the balance between supply and demand, which 
has resulted in relatively stable prices, especially in DOC 
and broiler.
Additionally, the decline in local corn availability has 
driven up feed prices, contributing to increased 
production costs across multiple subsystems, from feed 
to cultivation, ultimately affecting consumer prices. This 
escalating cost structure places additional pressure on 
farmers to maintain their profit margins amidst market 
competition.
Despite these challenges, the industry remains optimistic 
about future growth. According to Rabobank’s Global 
Animal Protein Outlook 2024, the poultry meat production 
is expected to rise, signaling a positive outlook for the 
sector, both globally and in Indonesia.
The Indonesian government, through the Ministry of 
Agriculture (MOA), continues to play a proactive role in 
stabilizing the poultry industry. Strategic interventions 
by the MOA have yielded positive results, with live bird 
prices at the farm level now surpassing production 
costs, enabling farmers to achieve fair profit margins. 
Addressing key concerns such as price volatility, the 
government has consolidated the industry players and 
poultry associations to create price stability and business 
sustainability.
Overall, despite ongoing challenges and rising production 
costs, Indonesia’s poultry industry in 2024 continues to 
demonstrate positive growth, supported by increasing 
market demand and stable pricing.

Realisasi Rencana Kerja Strategis 2024
Realization of 2024 Strategic Work Plan
Beberapa rencana kerja dan program strategis yang 
dirancang dan dilaksanakan pada tahun 2024 adalah 
sebagai berikut:
1. 	Diversifikasi Produk dan Ekspansi Ekspor
	
Ekspor Ayam Beku dan Produk Olahan: Malindo 
melanjutkan ekspor ayam beku dan produk olahan 
ke Singapura dan Jepang melalui anak perusahaan 
PT Malindo Food Delight. Pada Juni 2024, mereka 
mengirimkan dua kontainer dengan nilai sekitar 
USD65.000, 
menunjukkan 
keberhasilan 
dalam 
memenuhi standar ketat dari Singapore Food Agency 
(SFA) dan pasar Jepang. 
2. 	Ketahanan Bahan Baku Pakan
	
Kemitraan dengan Petani Jagung Lokal: Untuk 
mengatasi fluktuasi harga bahan baku pakan, Malindo 
menjalin kerja sama dengan petani jagung lokal 
dan membangun fasilitas pengeringan (corn dryer) 
serta silo di berbagai wilayah Indonesia. Langkah 
ini bertujuan untuk menekan biaya produksi dan 
memastikan ketersediaan bahan baku. ​
3. 	Inisiatif Energi Terbarukan
	
Pemasangan Panel Surya: Malindo memasang panel 
surya di fasilitas produksi mereka, termasuk pabrik 
di Cikarang dan hatchery di Purwakarta. Inisiatif ini 
diproyeksikan menghasilkan lebih dari 1,9 juta kWh 
listrik per tahun dan mengurangi emisi karbon hingga 
1.310 ton CO₂, sejalan dengan komitmen perusahaan 
terhadap keberlanjutan lingkungan. 
 4. Penguatan Hilirisasi Produk
	
Pengembangan 
Produk 
Olahan: 
Melalui 
merek 
SunnyGold, 
Malindo 
terus 
mengembangkan 
produk olahan seperti nugget dan sosis. Mereka 
juga mengadakan acara promosi seperti “Bestie 
Challenge” untuk meningkatkan brand awareness dan 
memperluas pangsa pasar di segmen ritel dan 
konsumen akhir. 
Several strategic work plans and programs designed and 
implemented in 2024 are as follows:
1. 	Product Diversification and Export Expansion
	
Export of Frozen Chicken and Processed Products: 
Malindo continues to export frozen chicken and 
processed products to Singapore and Japan through 
its subsidiary, PT Malindo Food Delight. In June 2024, 
the Company successfully shipped two containers 
valued at approximately USD65,000, demonstrating 
its ability to meet the stringent standards set by the 
Singapore Food Agency (SFA) and the Japanese 
market.
2. 	Feed Raw Material Resilience
	
Partnership with Local Corn Farmers: To mitigate 
fluctuations in feed raw material prices, Malindo has 
formed partnerships with local corn farmers and 
established drying facilities (corn dryers) and silos in 
various regions across Indonesia. This initiative aims to 
reduce production costs while ensuring the consistent 
availability of raw materials.
3.	 Renewable Energy Initiatives
	
Solar Panel Installation: Malindo has implemented solar 
panel installations at its production facilities, including 
the plant in Cikarang and the hatchery in Purwakarta. 
This initiative is expected to generate over 1.9 million 
kWh of electricity annually, while also reducing 
carbon emissions by 1,310 tons of CO₂, supporting 
the 
Company’s 
commitment 
to 
environmental 
sustainability.
4.	 Strengthening Product Downstreaming
	
Processed Product Development: Under its SunnyGold 
brand, Malindo continues to expand its range of 
processed products, including nuggets and sausages. 
The Company also organizes promotional events such 
as the “Bestie Challenge” to enhance brand awareness 
and increase market share in the retail and end-
consumer segments.

Kebijakan Struktur Modal
Capital Structure Policy
Malindo secara aktif dan rutin menelaah dan mengelola 
permodalannya untuk memastikan struktur modal dan 
pengembalian yang optimal bagi pemegang saham, 
dengan mempertimbangkan efisiensi penggunaan modal 
berdasarkan arus kas operasi dan belanja modal, serta 
mempertimbangkan kebutuhan modal di masa yang akan 
datang.
Malindo 
senantiasa 
memonitor 
permodalan 
berdasarkan rasio gearing. Rasio ini dihitung dengan 
membagi jumlah utang neto dengan jumlah modal. 
Utang neto dihitung dari pinjaman (pinjaman bank 
jangka pendek, bagian jangka pendek dari pinjaman 
bank jangka panjang, utang pihak berelasi dan pinjaman 
bank jangka panjang dikurangi bagian yang jatuh 
tempo dalam waktu satu tahun) dikurangi kas dan setara 
kas. Jumlah modal dihitung dari ekuitas seperti yang ada 
pada laporan keuangan konsolidasian.
Malindo actively and regularly reviews and manages 
its capital to ensure the optimal capital structure and 
return to the shareholders, taking into consideration the 
efficiency of capital usage based on operating cash flow 
and capital expenditures and also consideration of capital 
needs in the future.
Malindo consistently monitors capital on the basis of the 
gearing ratio. This ratio is calculated as net debt divided 
by total capital. Net debt is calculated as borrowings 
(short-term bank borrowings, current maturities of long-
term bank borrowings, due to related party and long-term 
bank borrowings, net of current maturities) less cash and 
cash equivalents. Total capital is calculated as equity as 
shown in the condolidated financial statement

pembagian dividen untuk tahun buku 2021, dimana 
seluruh laba bersih dibukukan sebagai laba ditahan 
Perseroan.

the Annual General Meeting of Shareholders (AGMS).

the AGMS Resolutions for the last few years:

2024, under the second Agenda item regarding the 
Allocation of the Company’s Profit for the 2023 Fiscal 
Year, amounting to Rp63,373,085,000, it was decided

entire net profit was allocated as retained earnings.
2.	 Based on the AGMS Resolutions held on 27 June

earnings.

Company’s Profit for the 2021 Fiscal Year, totaling 
Rp60,376,485,000, it was resolved that no dividends 
would be distributed, and the full net profit would be 
retained as earnings.

Malindo maintains a positive business outlook for 2025, 
supported by strategic initiatives and favorable market 
conditions. A key driver of this growth is the government’s 
planned Free Nutritious Meal Program, which is expected 
to significantly increase demand for chickens and eggs. 
This initiative is anticipated to have a direct impact on 
boosting sales of Malindo’s products, both in live and 
processed chickens. Additionally, Malindo’s financial 
performance in 2024 demonstrated improvement and 
steady growth, reflecting operational efficiency and price 
improvements in the poultry sector.
Beyond domestic expansion, Malindo continues to 
strengthen its presence in international markets by 
increasing exports of processed poultry products to 
countries such as Japan and Singapore. This success 
underscores the Company’s global competitiveness and 
highlights opportunities for further market expansion. 
Moreover, the national poultry industry is projected to 
experience a 24% increase in demand by 2025, further 
reinforcing Malindo’s positive growth trajectory. With a 
combination of market expansion strategies, operational 
efficiency, and government policy support, Malindo 
is well-positioned to enhance its growth and solidify 
its status as a leading player in Indonesia’s poultry 
agribusiness industry.

Aspek Pemasaran
Marketing Aspect
Malindo menargetkan 2 (dua) segmen utama dalam 
strategi pemasarannya, yaitu pasar domestik dan 
ekspor. Di pasar domestik, Malindo berkomitmen untuk 
memperkuat kapasitas produksi dengan menyelesaikan 
pembangunan kandang-kandang baru guna memastikan 
efisiensi 
operasional 
dan 
optimalisasi 
produksi. 
Sementara itu, untuk pasar internasional, Malindo secara 
aktif mengembangkan produk makanan olahan guna 
meningkatkan daya saing di pasar global.
Sejalan 
dengan 
tren 
digitalisasi, 
Malindo 
mulai 
memperluas strategi penjualannya melalui platform 
daring. Langkah ini bertujuan untuk meningkatkan 
aksesibilitas produk bagi konsumen serta memperkuat 
posisi perusahaan sebagai salah satu pelaku utama dalam 
industri agribisnis peternakan di Indonesia.
Selain itu, Malindo terus berinovasi dalam diversifikasi 
produk guna memenuhi preferensi konsumen dari 
berbagai segmen pasar. Fokus utama pengembangan 
diarahkan pada produk hilir, khususnya makanan siap 
saji atau food processing. Pertumbuhan signifikan dalam 
jumlah pelanggan dan ekspansi outlet food processing 
menjadi bukti nyata dari keberhasilan strategi ini.
Dengan komitmen terhadap kualitas, inovasi, dan ekspansi 
pasar, Malindo terus memperkuat posisinya sebagai 
pemimpin industri yang adaptif dan berorientasi pada 
pertumbuhan berkelanjutan.
Malindo’s marketing strategy targets two key segments: 
the domestic and export markets. In the domestic market, 
the Company is focused on increasing production 
capacity by completing the construction of new poultry 
housing facilities to improve operational efficiency and 
optimize production. Meanwhile, in the international 
market, Malindo is actively developing processed food 
products to enhance its competitiveness on a global 
scale.
Aligned with the growing trend of digitalization, Malindo 
has begun expanding its sales strategy through online 
platforms. This initiative aims to improve product 
accessibility for consumers while reinforcing the 
Company’s position as a leading player in Indonesia’s 
livestock agribusiness industry.
Furthermore, Malindo continues to drive innovation in 
product diversification to cater to the preferences of 
various market segments. The primary development 
focus is on downstream products, particularly ready-
to-eat and processed foods. The significant growth in 
customer base and expansion of food processing outlets 
serve as strong indicators of this strategy’s success.
With a steadfast commitment to quality, innovation, 
and market expansion, Malindo continues to strengthen 
its position as an adaptable industry leader focused on 
sustainable growth.
