Kondisi Industri Transportasi Truk 
 
Industri transportasi khususnya angkutan truk 
merupakan sektor strategis dalam mendukung 
berbagai aktivitas perekonomian dalam suatu 
mata 
rantai 
pembangunan 
nasional. 
Transportasi truk  memiliki peranan yang sangat 
penting sebagai sarana distribusi barang dari 
pabrikan ke konsumen khususnya untuk 
transportasi 
lokal. 
Pengangkutan 
barang 
semakin didominasi oleh moda transportasi truk 
seperti yang terjadi selama ini. Industri ini 
diprediksi akan terus berkembang seiring 
dengan pertumbuhan ekonomi nasional.  
 
Tantangan 
yang 
dihadapi 
oleh 
industri 
transportasi truk adalah semakin ketatnya 
persaingan antar perusahaan transportasi.  
Selain itu, tingginya beban biaya operasional 
angkutan akibat kenaikan BBM yang tidak 
diimbangi dengan kenakan  tarif angkutan yang 
yang proporsional menjadi tantangan  bagi 
pengusaha truk. Dalam menghadapi tantangan 
yang demikian, pengusaha truk dituntut untuk 
mengelola biaya operasional dengan efektif dan 
efisien 
agar 
mampu 
bertahan 
dalam 
menghadapi persaingan. 
 
 
 
 
Truck Transportation Industry Conditions 
 
The 
transportation 
industry, 
especially 
trucking, is a strategic sector in supporting 
various economic activities in a chain of 
national development. Truck transportation 
has a very important role as a means of 
distributing goods from manufacturers to 
consumers, 
especially 
for 
local 
transportation. The transportation of goods 
is increasingly dominated by the truck mode 
of transportation as has been the case so far. 
This industry is predicted to continue to grow 
in line with national economic growth. 
 
The challenge faced by the trucking industry 
is the increasing competition between 
transportation companies.  In addition, the 
high burden of transportation operational 
costs due to fuel increases that are not offset 
by imposing proportional transportation 
rates 
is 
a 
challenge 
for 
trucking 
entrepreneurs. In facing such challenges, 
truck entrepreneurs are required to manage 
operational costs effectively and efficiently in 
order to survive in the face of competition.

Business Prospect

means of transportation is still the main 
means of transportation compared to other 
means of transportation considering its wide
