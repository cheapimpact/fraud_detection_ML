geopolitical tensions, worsen economic prospects and increase uncertainty in the 
global financial markets. This is characterized by the emergence of 5 (five) challenges 
that have generally dampened the prospects of the world economy, increased 
investment and trade divergence between developed and developing countries, and 
further raised the level of uncertainty in the global financial markets.

PROSPECTS FOR ECONOMIC REVIVAL TOWARDS AN ADVANCED INDONESIA 
Prospects for the improvement of the national economy will continue with maintained stability, supporting the national economic 
revival towards an Advanced Indonesia. Indonesia's improvement will persist, buoyed by increasing private consumption in line 
with sustained purchasing power, fiscal and monetary policy support, and the growing role of the millennial generation. 
Investments will rise alongside export growth and the ongoing National Strategic Projects (PSN), Export performance will also 
improve with the global economic outlook and the proliferation of downstreaming programs across various sectors. Consistent 
implementation of structural reforms, including infrastructure development, the implementation of the Omnibus Law, various 
downstreaming programs, digitalization acceleration, and human resource strengthening, will enhance economic capacity, 
allowing for higher growth without significant pressure on stability. Several risks from the dynamics of high global uncertainty need 
to be monitored and addressed with appropriate national policy responses to mitigate their impact on the domestic economy.
