J.  The Issuer's Business Prospects
    are related to Industrial
    Conditions, General Economy
    and International Markets 
 
In 2024, Indonesia's economy grew by 
5.03% (year-on-year), slightly lower than the 
2023 achievement of 5.05%. Despite the 
slight difference, this figure shows that the 
Indonesian economy continues to grow 
steadily amidst global and domestic 
challenges.In the fourth quarter of 2024, the 
Indonesian economy was recorded to grow 
by 5.02% yoy, slightly lower than the growth 
in the same period the previous year, 
which was 5.04% yoy.
 
Indonesia continues its commitment to 
a clean energy transition by 2024 through 
the development of palm-based biodiesel. 
After the success of the B30 Program that 
started in 2020, the government increased 
the biodiesel blending rate to 35% (B35) 
from February 2023 and maintained it 
throughout 2024. To support the 
implementation of this program, the 
government set the biodiesel distribution 
allocation at 13.41 million kiloliters (kL) in 
2024, which is an increase of about 1.9% 
compared to the previous year's allocation 
of 13.15 million kL.
 
During 2024, Indonesia's biodiesel 
production was recorded at 13.15 million kL, 
exceeding the set target of 11.3 million kL. 
This success contributed to foreign 
exchange savings of around USD 9.33 billion 
from reduced diesel imports. In addition, the 
B35 program also has a positive impact on 
the national economy by reducing the 
volume of diesel imports and strengthening 
the domestic palm oil industry. This program 
is in line with the government's strategy to 
promote energy independence and reduce 
dependence on fossil fuels.

    dengan Kondisi Industri, Ekonomi
    Secara Umum dan Pasar
    International

tumbuh sebesar 5,03% (year-on-year), 
sedikit lebih rendah dibandingkan dengan 
capaian tahun 2023 yang mencapai 5,05%. 
Meskipun terdapat perbedaan yang tipis, 
angka ini menunjukkan bahwa perekonomian 
Indonesia tetap tumbuh stabil di tengah 
tantangan global dan domestik.Pada 
Triwulan IV 2024, ekonomi Indonesia tercatat 
tumbuh sebesar 5,02% yoy, sedikit lebih 
rendah dibandingkan dengan pertumbuhan 
pada periode yang sama tahun sebelumnya, 
yaitu 5,04% yoy.
 
I n d o n e s i a  t e r u s  m e l a n j u t k a n  
komitmennya dalam transisi energi bersih 
pada tahun 2024 melalui pengembangan 
biodiesel berbasis kelapa sawit. Setelah 
sukses dengan Program B30 yang dimulai

estimated to reach 36.48 million kL. With a 
projected 3% growth in demand, the distribution 
for 2023 is estimated to increase to 37.57 million 
kL. To support this projection, the government 
set a biodiesel allocation of 13.15 million kL in 
2023, an increase of about 19% compared to the 
2022 allocation of 11.03 million kL.
 
Although the biodiesel production target in 
2023 was set at 13 million kL, the realization only 
reached 12.7 million kL, due to several factors, 
including infrastructure adjustments related to 
the implementation of the B35 program that 
began on February 1, 2023. However, the actual 
biodiesel distribution for the B35 program in 
2023 reached 12.15 million kL, indicating that the 
implementation of the program went well 
without significant obstacles.
 
The B35 program has had a significant 
impact on the Indonesian economy. Foreign 
exchange savings were recorded at USD 10.75 
billion or equivalent to IDR 161 trillion. The 
program also managed to absorb 1.65 million 
workers and contributed to the reduction of 
greenhouse gas emissions by 34.9 million tons 
of Co2 equivalent.
 
Following the success of the B35 Program, 
the government plans to increase the biodiesel 
blending rate to 40% (B40) starting on January 1, 
2025. The B40 program is expected to boost 
domestic palm oil consumption for biodiesel to 
13.9 million metric tons, up from about 11 million 
tons required for B35 implementation.

produk turunan minyak kelapa sawit lainnya 
seperti PFAD, RBDPO, Glycerin dan Fatty 
Mater yang kesemuanya menjadi bahan 
baku industri makanan dan consumer goods 
yaitu produk yang dikonsumsi masyarakat 
sebagai bagian dari kebutuhan sehari-hari 
seperti sabun, produk kecantikan dan lain-
lain.
With the construction of a Palm Oil Mill that 
produces CPO as the main raw material for 
biodiesel; and cooking oil, the Company's 
business activities become an integrated 
downstream business, starting from 
Plantations producing FFB, processing FFB 
at PKS which produces CPO, and CPO will be 
processed into cooking oil at the Edible Oil 
Mill and can be processed into FAME at the 
Biodiesel plant as biofuel and in the biodiesel 
production process also produces other 
palm oil derivative products such as PFAD, 
RBDPO, Glycerin and Fatty Mater, all of 
which become raw materials for the food 
industry and consumer goods, namely 
products consumed by the public as part of 
their daily needs such as soap, beauty 
products and others.

sebesar Rp.1.400.000.000.000,00 (satu triliun empat ratus miliar rupiah).

Transaksi Benturan Kepentingan sebagaimana dimaksud pada POJK

dalam Peraturan Otoritas Jasa Keuangan POJK17/POJK.04/2020 tentang Transaksi 
Material dan perubahan kegiatan usaha. 
- Transaksi tersebut telah memperoleh persetujuan dari para pemegang saham pada 
saat Rapat Umum Pemegang Saham Luar Biasa tanggal 18 Oktober 
2024. 
- Jangka Waktu Kredit selama 96 Bulan
- On Thursday, October 24, 2024, the signing of the Investment Credit Agreement 
Deed between Bank Mandiri and the Company was held with a credit limit of IDR 
1,400,000,000,000.00 (one trillion four hundred billion rupiah).
- The value of the pledged assets is in the form of 12 (twelve) certificates of Right to 
Cultivate (HGU) for oil palm plantations, biodiesel factory buildings, cooking oil factory 
buildings and power plant buildings along with their facilities and infrastructure 
belonging to the Company with a guarantee value of IDR 1,400,000,000,000. 000,00 
(one trillion four hundred billion rupiah) and the Company's guarantee on behalf of PT 
Eshan Agro Sentosa.
- The transaction is not an Affiliated Transaction as referred to in the Financial Services 
Authority Regulation regarding Affiliated Transactions and Conflict of Interest 
Transactions as referred to in POJK 42/POJK.04/2020.
-The transaction is exempted from Material Transactions as referred to in the Financial 
Services Authority Regulation POJK17/POJK.04/2020 concerning Material Transactions 
and changes in business activities.

General Meeting of Shareholders on October 18, 2024.
