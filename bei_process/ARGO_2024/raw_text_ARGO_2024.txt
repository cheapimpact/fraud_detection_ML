Menurut Badan Pusat Statistik (BPS), pertumbuhan 
ekonomi sepanjang tahun 2024 tercatat sebesar 5,03% 
(c-to-c). Capaian ini lebih rendah dari pertumbuhan 
sepanjang tahun 2023 sebesar 5,05% (c-to-c). Di sisi 
lain, pertumbuhan ekonomi global pada tahun 2024 
menurut IMF World Economic Outlook Update (Jan-
2025) diproyeksikan mencapai 3,2%. Proyeksi ini lebih 
rendah dari capaian tahun 2023 sebesar 3,3%.
Pemerintah mampu menjaga tingkat pertumbuhan di 
atas 5%. Terjaganya konsumsi rumah tangga, investasi, 
dan ekspor nonmigas turut mendorong pertumbuhan 
perekonomian akhir tahun 2024, begitu pula dengan 
belanja pemerintah yang menopang permintaan 
domestik.  
Kendati perumbuhan relatif positif, namun secara 
keseluruhan masih terdapat banyak tantangan di 
dalam negeri. Tingginya gelombang PHK di sektor 
manufaktur dan masih tingginya angka kemiskinan 
menjadi hambatan bagi Indonesia untuk meningkatkan 
pertumbuhan ekonomi secara maksimal.
Perseroan bertransisi ke sektor logistik, yang saat ini 
masih mengandalkan jasa sewa properti yang dimiliki. 
Perseroan memiliki aset pergudangan yang terletak 
di lokasi strategis, yaitu Bekasi dan Tangerang. Kedua 
daerah tersebut dikenal sebagai penghubung antara 
wilayah industri dan wilayah urban yang berpusat di 
Jakarta. Walaupun demikian, dengan perkembangan 
yang pesat di kedua daerah tersebut, terutama dengan 
banyaknya komplek residensial dan properti komersial 
yang tumbuh, kehadiran gudang Perseroan menjadi 
semakin bernilai tinggi bagi mitra bisnis kami. 
Pada tahun 2024, khusus untuk lapangan usaha 
transportasi dan pergudangan, BPS mencatat 
pertumbuhan 8,69%. Pertumbuhan transportasi dan 
pergudangan menjadi yang tertinggi kedua sepanjang 
tahun setelah lapangan usaha “jasa lainnya”. 
Peningkatan jumlah penumpang dan barang pada 
berbagai moda transportasi, terutama saat libur Natal 
dan Tahun Baru (Nataru) mendorong pertumbuhan 
transportasi dan pergudangan menjelang akhir tahun. 
Selain itu, tingkat konsumsi masyarakat yang kuat juga 
turut andil dalam mendorong pertumbuhan transportasi 
dan pergudangan secara keseluruhan. Tercatat indeks 
penjualan eceran riil tumbuh 3,01% (c-to-c) dan nilai 
impor barang konsumsi tumbuh 5,37% (c-to-c).
Pandangan Ekonomi dan Industri
Economic and Industrial Outlook
According to the Central Statistics Agency (BPS), 
economic growth during 2024 was recorded at 5.03% 
(c-to-c). This achievement is lower than the growth 
recorded in 2023, which stood at 5.05% (c-to-c). 
Meanwhile, global economic growth in 2024, according 
to the IMF World Economic Outlook Update (Jan-2025), 
is projected to reach 3.2%. This projection is lower than 
the 2023 achievement of 3.3%.  
The government has managed to maintain a growth 
rate above 5%. Sustained household consumption, 
investment, and non-oil and gas exports have contributed 
to economic growth at the end of 2024, along with 
government spending, which has supported domestic 
demand.  
Although the growth remains relatively positive, there 
are still many challenges within the country. The high 
wave of layoffs in the manufacturing sector and the 
persistently high poverty rate continue to hinder 
Indonesia from maximizing its economic growth.
The Company has transitioned into the logistics sector, 
and currently it still relies on property leasing services. 
The Company owns warehouses in strategic areas in 
Bekasi and Tangerang. These two regions serve as key 
hub between industrial zones and urban areas centered 
around Jakarta. With the rapid development in these 
two areas, particularly with the increasing number of 
residential and commercial property complexes, the 
presence of the Company’s warehouses has become 
even more valuable for our business partners.  
In 2024, specifically for the transportation and 
warehousing sector, BPS recorded a growth rate of 
8.69%. This sector experienced the second-highest 
growth rate of the year, following the “other services” 
sector.  
The increase in passenger and cargo volumes across 
various transportation modes, especially during the 
Christmas and New Year (Nataru) holiday season, 
contributed to the growth of the transportation and 
warehousing sector towards the end of the year. 
Additionally, strong consumer spending also played a 
role in driving overall growth in this sector. The retail 
sales index grew by 3.01% (c-to-c), while the value of 
imported consumer goods increased by 5.37% (c-to-c).

Tinjauan Operasional
Operational Review
Since its establishment, the Company has been engaged 
in textile production, while the transition to logistics 
began in 2020 and has continued to develop until now. 
Currently, the majority of the Company’s revenue comes 
from logistics, generated from its warehouse areas in 
Tangerang and Bekasi, along with the facilities within 
them.  
Throughout 2024, the Company faced several challenges 
in running its logistics business:  
1.	 Growing Number of New Warehouse Developers: 
The emergence of several modern warehouse areas 
offering new options for tenants has increased 
competition. The Company must remain competitive 
in pricing, facilities, and location to meet market 
demands.  
2.	 Value Proposition: The Company is expected to offer 
distinct value to attract tenants, whether through the 
facilities of the leased land and buildings or other 
advantages.  
3.	 Fluctuating Demand & Customization Needs;  
a.	 The e-commerce, FMCG, and manufacturing 
industries have different warehousing 
requirements.  
b.	 Some clients require on-demand warehousing 
or multi-client warehouse solutions, demanding 
high flexibility in storage systems.  
c.	 The Company must adapt to seasonal demand 
spikes, such as during National Online Shopping 
Day (Harbolnas), Eid, and year-end, without 
compromising operational efficiency.  
To address these challenges, the Company has 
implemented the following business strategies:
1.	 Enhancing and improving the quality of its facilities. 
2.	 Strengthening partnerships with both international 
and local property marketing agents to position the 
Company’s facilities as a top choice for consumers. 
3.	 Implementing specialized/customizable systems and 
facilities tailored to tenants’ business sector needs. 
4.	 Offering flexible rental options—short-term, medium-
term, and long-term—at certain warehouse locations 
to accommodate fluctuating market demand.

segmen, yaitu penghasilan kerja sama dan pendapatan

dari penggunaan bahan baku untuk produksi tekstil.

maka porsi pendapatan dari tekstil juga berkurang,

yang dihasilkan dari aktivitas usaha.

consumption has also helped the Company lower its

Management Discussion and Analysis
Berdasarkan tinjauan operasional sebelumnya, 
pendapatan Perseroan terbesar saat ini berasal dari 
sektor logistik, terutama di sektor yang terakit erat 
dengan e-commerce (e.g. jasa kurir),  yang terlihat dari 
pendapatan sewa yang meningkat di sektor ini berasal 
dari sektor logistik. Kinerja pendapatan yang positif 
tidak terlepas dari peran aktivitas pemasaran yang lebih 
menitikberatkan aset-aset logistik dan pergudangan. 
Pada 2024, sesuai dengan informasi yang sudah 
diungkapkan dalam Kegiatan Usaha di Profil Perusahaan, 
Argo Pantes telah menjalin kerja sama dengan 25 
perusahaan dalam menyewakan lahan, bangunan, dan 
gudang yang dimiliki. Terjadi peningkatan dari tahun 
2023 yang tercatat 23 perusahaan. 
Perseroan memang melakukan penguatan pada 
bisnis logistik melalui digital transformation dengan 
pemantauan secara real-time yang memungkinkan 
proses analisa dan pengambilan keputusan lebih cepat. 
Perbaikan standar prosedur operasional bongkar muat 
juga terus dilakukan untuk menjaga tingkat efisiensi kerja 
dengan tetap memastikan produk terjaga dengan aman. 
Peningkatan dari sisi internal juga dilakukan seiring 
dengan permintaan pelanggan karena kami secara 
konsisten melakukan survei untuk mendapatkan 
masukan guna meningkatkan pelayanan dan mutu 
produk. Pelanggan yang puas akan meningkatan 
transaksi dengan Perseroan dan sekaligus menjadi 
promotor terpercaya akan kualitas layanan kami. 
Okupansi gudang Bekasi yang dimiliki Perseroan 
mencapai 26.145 m2 dan okupansi gudang Tangerang 
mencapai 54.268 m2. Estimasi luas gudang di 
Jabodetabek adalah 10,6 juta m2 dan berdasarkan data 
sebaran gudang modern di Jabodetabek,  luas gudang 
Bekasi mencakup 40% atau +/- 4.2 juta m2 dan luas 
gudang Tangerang 25% atau +/- 2.6 juta m2. Berdasarkan 
aktivitas pemasaran yang telah dijelaskan sebelumnya 
dan estimasi luas gudang, dapat kami sampaikan bahwa 
Perseroan saat ini memiliki pangsa pasar logistik di 
Tangerang dan Bekasi masing-masing mencapai sekitar 
0,6% dan 2% secara berturut-turut.
Aspek Pemasaran 
Marketing Aspect
Based on the previous operational review, the Company’s 
largest revenue currently comes from the logistics sector, 
particularly in industries closely related to e-commerce 
(e.g., courier services). This is evident from the increase 
in rental income within this sector. The positive revenue 
performance is strongly influenced by marketing 
activities that emphasize logistics and warehouse assets. 
In 2024, as disclosed in the Business Activities section of 
the Company Profile, Argo Pantes has collaborated with 
25 companies in leasing land, buildings, and warehouses. 
This marks an increase from 23 companies in 2023.  
The Company has strengthened its logistics business 
through digital transformation, enabling real-time 
monitoring for faster analysis and decision-making. 
Improvements in operational loading and unloading 
procedures continue to be made to maintain work 
efficiency while ensuring product safety.  
Internal improvements have also been carried out 
in response to customer demands, as the Company 
consistently conducts customer surveys to gather 
feedback for service and product quality enhancement. 
Satisfied customers tend to increase transactions with 
the Company and act as trusted promoters of our 
service quality.  
The occupancy of the Company’s Bekasi warehouse has 
reached 26,145 sqm, while the Tangerang warehouse 
has an occupancy of 54,268 sqm. The estimated total 
warehouse space in Greater Jakarta (Jabodetabek) is 
10.6 million sqm. Based on the distribution of modern 
warehouses in the region:  Bekasi warehouses account 
for 40% (approximately 4.2 million sqm). Tangerang 
warehouses account for 25% (approximately 2.6 million 
m²). Considering the marketing activities and estimated 
warehouse area, the Company currently holds a market 
share in the logistics sector of approximately 0.6% in 
Tangerang and 2% in Bekasi.

Prospek Usaha
Business Prospect
Sejumlah lembaga internasional dalam rilisan terbarunya 
meramalkan pertumbuhan ekonomi Indonesia cenderung 
stabil di kisaran 5%. Bank Dunia dalam World Bank East 
Asia and The Pacific Economic Update yang dirilis 8 
Oktober 2024 memperkirakan ekonomi Indonesia 
tumbuh sebesar 5,1% pada 2025. 
Bank Dunia dalam laporannya menunjukkan keyakinan 
terhadap kekuatan ekonomi Indonesia di tengah 
tantangan yang dihadapi negara-negara di kawasan Asia 
Timur dan Pasifik. Peningkatan konsumsi masyarakat, 
investasi, serta belanja pemerintah menjadi faktor 
pendorong utama pertumbuhan ekonomi Indonesia ke 
depan dan hanya Indonesia yang diperkirakan tumbuh 
pada atau di atas tingkat sebelum pandemi. 
Prospek ekonomi Indonesia pada 2025 diharapkan 
mencapai pertumbuhan yang stabil, didorong oleh 
kebijakan yang berfokus pada stabilitas, investasi, 
konsumsi domestik, dan program sosial, sementara 
kemungkinan besar akan menghindari ekspansi fiskal 
besar-besaran. Tantangan utama yang dihadapi Indonesia 
adalah fluktuasi harga komoditas dan pertumbuhan yang 
lebih lambat dari mitra dagang terbesar, yaitu China. 
Pasar konsumen Indonesia berpotensi mencatatkan 
pertumbuhan yang kuat jika langkah-langkah stimulus 
fiskal dapat dipertegas, yang akan mendorong konsumsi. 
Mengacu  pada data BPS, Supply Chain Indonesia 
(SCI) menyatakan bahwa subsektor transportasi dan 
pergudangan nasional berpeluang tumbuh 12,53% tahun 
2025, ditopang geliat di industri pengolahan hingga 
perdagangan. Di industri pengolahan nonmigas atau 
manufaktur, potensi logistik tahun 2025 akan didominasi 
industri makanan dan minuman. Industri kimia dan 
farmasi, industri barang logam dan elektronik, industri 
alat angkutan, industri logam dasar, serta industri tekstil 
dan pakaian jadi, semuanya akan mengikuti untuk 
mendorong peningkatan logistik. 
Peningkatan sektor logistik bukanlah sebuah keniscayaan 
karena potensi pertumbuhannya memang sangat besar 
dan berperan sentral terhadap pertumbuhan ekonomi 
nasional secara menyeluruh. 
Various international institutions in their latest release 
predict that Indonesia’s economic growth tends to 
remain stable in the range of 5%. The World Bank, in 
the World Bank East Asia and The Pacific Economic 
Update, released on October 8, 2024 estimates that 
Indonesia’s economy will grow by 5.1% in 2025. 
The World Bank’s report showed confidence in 
Indonesia’s economic strength amid challenges faced by 
countries in the East Asia and Pacific region. Increasing 
public consumption, investment, and government 
spending are the main driving factors behind Indonesia’s 
economic growth going forward and only Indonesia 
is expected to grow at or above pre-pandemic levels. 
Indonesia’s economic outlook for 2025 is expected 
to achieve stable growth, driven by policies focused 
on stability, investment, domestic consumption, and 
social programs, while most likely avoiding massive 
fiscal expansion. The main challenges facing Indonesia 
are fluctuations in commodity prices and slower growth 
from its largest trading partner, China. Indonesia’s 
consumer market has the potential to record strong 
growth if fiscal stimulus measures can be affirmed, which 
will encourage consumption. 
Referring to BPS data, Supply Chain Indonesia (SCI) 
stated that the national transportation and warehousing 
subsector has the opportunity to grow by 12.53% in 
2025, supported by the processing and trade industries. 
In the non-oil and gas processing or manufacturing 
industry, the logistics potential in 2025 will be 
dominated by the food and beverage industry. The 
chemical and pharmaceutical industries, the metal and 
electronic goods industry, the transportation equipment 
industry, the base metal industry, and the textile and 
apparel industry, will all follow suit in driving logistics 
improvement. 
Improving the logistics sector is not a necessity because 
its growth potential is indeed very large and plays a 
central role in overall national economic growth.

4.	 Perbaikan infrastruktur logistik dan penyewaan aset
