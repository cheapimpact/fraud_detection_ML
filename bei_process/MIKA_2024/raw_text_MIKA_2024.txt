Sektor kesehatan Indonesia telah mengalami transformasi 
yang signifikan dengan keberhasilan penerapan skema 
perlindungan kesehatannya, Jaringan Kesehatan Nasional 
(JKN). Dalam kurun waktu kurang dari satu dekade, program 
ini telah menjadi sistem asuransi kesehatan nasional yang 
terbesar di dunia, yang memberi perlindungan kesehatan 
ke sekitar 98,2% dari total populasi Indonesia.
The year 2024 was also marked with a peaceful and well-

democratic maturity. The incoming administration

continuity 
and

The year 2024 marked the first full year since the World 
Health Organization (WHO) declared the end of COVID-19 
as a Public Health Emergency of International Concern 
(PHEIC) on May 5, 2023. 
With the recovery of social mobility, health centers and 
hospitals have reported a rebound in patient visits over 
the past two years. This period has also highlighted the 
Government’s unwavering commitment to the health 
sector, reflected in the 2024 state health budget, which 
increased by 8.1% to Rp186.4 trillion compared to the 
2023 budget realization of Rp183.2 trillion.
Indonesia’s healthcare sector has been significantly 
transformed by the successful launch of the country’s 
universal health coverage scheme, or Jaringan Kesehatan 
Nasional (JKN). In less than a decade, the program has 
emerged as the world’s largest government-run national 
health insurance system, providing healthcare coverage 
to roughly 98.2% of the country’s total population.

KAJIAN USAHA
Berdiri di tahun 1989, Mitra Keluarga merupakan salah 
satu kelompok usaha rumah sakit swasta yang terbesar 
di Indonesia berdasarkan jumlah kunjungan pasien. 
Perseroan menjadi perusahaan publik melalui penawaran 
sahamnya di Bursa Efek Indonesia di bulan Maret 2015 
dengan kode saham MIKA.
Untuk memperluas jangkauan layanannya, pada bulan 
Oktober 2017 Mitra Keluarga telah melakukan akuisisi 
atas Rumah Kasih Indonesia (Kasih Group), sehingga kini 
ada dua kelompok jaringan rumah sakit: rumah sakit Mitra 
Keluarga dan rumah sakit Kasih Group.
Rumah-rumah sakit Mitra Keluarga merupakan rumah sakit 
komunitas dengan berbagai spesialisasi yang melayani 
wilayah paling urban di Indonesia: area Jabodetabek dan 
kota Surabaya. Sementara itu, Rumah-rumah sakit Kasih 
Group terutama melayani pasien dengan perlindungan 
JKN di kota-kota sekunder utama pilihan di Indonesia.
Dengan lokasinya yang strategis serta komunitas yang 
tumbuh dan profil demografis yang mendukung, seluruh 
rumah sakit menikmati kemudahan akses ke basis pasien 
kelas menengah yang terus berkembang serta tenaga 
medis yang berkualitas, agar dapat menawarkan layanan 
kesehatan yang komprehensif dan berkualitas.

BUSINESS REVIEW
Established in 1989, Mitra Keluarga is one of the largest 
private hospital groups in Indonesia by patient volume. 
The Company became publicly listed on the Indonesian 
Stock Exchange in March 2015 under the ticker code 
MIKA.
In October 2017, Mitra Keluarga expanded its reach by 
acquiring Rumah Kasih Indonesia (Kasih Group), creating 
two distinct hospital networks: Mitra Keluarga and Kasih 
Group hospitals.
Mitra Keluarga hospitals are multi-specialty, community-
focused hospitals in Indonesia’s most urbanized regions, 
the Greater Jakarta area and Surabaya. Meanwhile, the 
Kasih Group hospitals primarily serve JKN-covered 
patients in selected Indonesia’s key secondary cities.
Strategically located in thriving communities with 
favorable demographics, all hospitals benefit from 
access to a growing middle-class patient base and highly 
qualified medical professionals, ensuring comprehensive 
and quality healthcare services.

tahap renovasi ruang rawat inap dan ruang konsultasinya

serta menghadirkan lingkungan yang mendukung dan 
mempercepat proses pemulihan. Pekerjaan renovasi juga 
sedang berlangsung di beberapa rumah sakit Kasih Group 
yang ada.

Kasih Group.

PARIPURNA rating in 2024, the highest accreditation level 
granted by Indonesian authorities. 
186 hospital beds were added to the existing hospitals, 
bringing the total operational hospital beds to 4,054 beds 
by the end of 2024.

administration. Net revenue from drugs and medical 
supplies remained the largest contributor with 43.6% 
contribution, followed by net revenue from inpatient 
rooms and medical support services with 22.9% and 17.6% 
contributions respectively to net revenue from inpatient 
services in 2024.

ASET LANCAR
Aset lancar terdiri dari kas dan setara kas, investasi jangka 
pendek, piutang usaha, piutang lain-lain, aset keuangan 
lancar lainnya, persediaan, biaya dibayar di muka dan uang 
muka, pajak dibayar di muka, serta aset lancar lainnya.
Kas dan setara kas meningkat dari sebesar Rp842,8 miliar 
di tahun 2023 menjadi Rp1.067,6 miliar, seiring dengan 
pertumbuhan pendapatan bersih di tahun 2024. Kas 
dan setara kas terdiri dari kas, kas di bank, dan deposito 
berjangka. Per 31 Desember 2024, sebagian besar kas dan 
setara kas disimpan sebagai deposito pada bank-bank 
yang beroperasi di Indonesia.
Jangka waktu dari deposito berjangka berkisar antara 1 
minggu - 3 bulan. Tingkat suku bunga deposito berjangka
berkisar masing-masing antar 5,25%-6,65% dan 5,25%-
7,00% untuk tahun-tahun yang berakhir pada 31 Desember 
2024 dan 2023.
Per 31 Desember 2024, investasi jangka pendek tercatat 
sebesar Rp1.364,4 miliar.

masing-masing tercatat sebesar Rp723,2 miliar dan

Perusahaan.

customer, and requiring certain customers to provide 
guarantee deposits. In addition, receivable balances are 
monitored on an ongoing basis to reduce the exposure 
to bad debts.

RISIKO LIKUIDITAS
Risiko likuiditas adalah risiko dimana Perseroan akan 
mengalami kesulitan dalam rangka memperoleh dana 
untuk memenuhi komitmennya terkait dengan liabilitas 
keuangan.

the supply of all services and products to the customer 
in the event of late payment and/or default and applies 
the customer’s guarantee deposit as payment for such 
customer’s account in default.
The Company is also exposed to credit risk arising from 
the funds placed in banks under current accounts and 
time deposits also placement in mutual funds unit and 
corporate bond. To mitigate this risk, the Company 
has a policy to place its funds only in banks and asset 
management of mutual funds that have good reputation.
FOREIGN CURRENCY EXCHANGE RATE RISK
Foreign currency exchange rate risk is the risk that the fair 
value or future cash flows of a financial instrument will 
fluctuate because of changes in foreign exchange rates.
The Company manages the risk of foreign currency 
exchange rate by monitoring the fluctuations in foreign 
exchange rate continuously so as to perform approriate 
actions to reduce the risk of foreign currency exchange 
rate.
INTEREST RATE RISK
Interest rate risk is the risk that the fair value or contractual 
future cash flows of a financial instrument will be affected 
due to changes in market interest rates. The Company’s 
exposures to the interest rate risk relate to cash and cash 
equivalents which have floating interest rates.
The Company supervises the impact of interest rate 
movements to minimize the negative impact on 
Company.
LIQUIDITY RISK
Liquidity risk is the risk that the Company will encounter 
difficulty in obtaining funds to meet commitments 
associated with financial liabilities.
