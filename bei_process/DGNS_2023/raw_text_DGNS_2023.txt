Analisis Makro Ekonomi
Tahun 2023, perekonomian global masih mengalami 
perlambatan meski diperkirakan mengalami peningkatan. 
Beberapa risiko harus terus diwaspadai antara lain dampak 
dari perang Rusia-Ukraina, kondisi keuangan global yang 
ketat, inflasi yang persisten, risiko pemulihan Tiongkok, 
pengetatan kebijakan moneter, perkembangan sektor 
manufaktur global yang masih relatif lemah, suku bunga 
yang cenderung tinggi, debt distress, serta peningkatan 
kecenderungan fragmentasi politik yang memicu de-
globalisasi, termasuk volatilitas di sektor keuangan 
menciptakan tantangan bagi perekonomian dan sektor 
keuangan domestik.
International Monetary Fund (IMF) mencatatkan 
perekonomian global tahun 2023 tumbuh melemah 
dari 3,5% di 2022 menjadi 3,00% dan akan mengalami 
pelemahan pada angka 2,9% di 2024. IMF juga mencatatkan 
inflasi global mencapai ke level 5,1%, meski dibandingkan 
tingkat inflasi tahun 2022 yang sebesar 8,5%. Meski terjadi

ekonomi global ketiga di mana suku bunga di negara 
Macro Economic Analysis
In 2023, the global economy remains to experience a 
slowdown despite of its prediction as an increase. Numerous 
risks shall continue to be cautious, including the impact of 
the Russia-Ukraine war, tight global financial conditions, 
persistent inflation, the risk of China’s recovery, tightening 
monetary policy, development of the global manufacturing 
sector which is remain torelatively weak, interest rates 
that tend to be high, debt distress, as well as increasing 
tendencies of political fragmentation that trigger de-
globalization, including volatility in the financial sector 
creating challenges for the domestic economy and financial 
sector.

global economy in 2023 grew weaker from 3.5% in 2022 to 
3.00% and weakened to 2.9% in 2024. The IMF also recorded 
that global inflation reached the level of 5.1%, even compared 
to the 2022 inflation rate of 8.5%. Even though there was a 
decline from the previous year, high inflation this year shows 
the characteristics of the third global economic downturn 
where interest rates in developed countries tend to be 
high. This indicates that inflation will continue at least as of

Analisis Kinerja Sektor Kesehatan
Dampak pandemi COVID-19 dalam tiga tahun terakhir 
mengakibatkan pencapaian target-target pembangunan 
kesehatan sulit tercapai sehingga turut berdampak pada 
capaian pembangunan sumber daya manusia yang tidak 
optimal. Dalam hal ini pemerintah memegang peranan 
penting dalam pembangunan kesehatan untuk menciptakan 
derajat kesehatan masyarakat yang setinggi-tingginya 
melalui pelayanan kesehatan yang merata dan berkualitas. 
Kementerian Kesehatan berupaya membangun kembali 
sistem kesehatan Indonesia dari dampak pandemi COVID-19 
melalui 6 pilar program prioritas. Keenam pilar tersebut 
bermuara pada peningkatan pelayanan kesehatan bagi 
masyarakat. Pilar pertama adalah transformasi layanan 
primer dengan memperkuat upaya promotif dan preventif 
sekaligus mendekatkan akses layanan kesehatan yang 
berkualitas. Pilar kedua adalah transformasi layanan rujukan 
untuk memenuhi dan mendukung pemerataan fasilitas 
pelayanan kesehatan. Pilar ketiga yakni transformasi 
2025. IMF inflation projection figures also indicate that the 
global economy will remain in a high condition for longer.
In the midst of these conditions, the Indonesian economy 
on the other hand showed solid performance where in 
Quarter IV-2023 it again recorded growth of 5.04% (yoy) 
or higher than Quarter III-2023 which grew by 4.94% 
(yoy). So in full year terms, national economic growth in 
2023 recorded impressive growth of 5.05% (ctc). Resilient 
growth in the national economy is influenced by various 
current economic indicators, including consistent economic 
expansion, investment showing an upward trend, strong 
consumption in conformity with high consumer confidence, 
expansive national manufacture of Purchasing Managers 
Index (PMI), and a controlled inflation rate.
On the other hand, at the end of December 2023, the value 
of the Rupiah closed at the level (bid) of Rp15,399 per US 
dollar. Overall, the rupiah exchange rate against the United 
States (US) dollar in 2023 is quite surprising to market 
players. This is caused by the value of the Rupiah almost 
touching the psychological level, namely Rp16,000/US$ 
in October 2023. Meanwhile, the weakest position in the 
value of the Rupiah occurred on October 27 2023, namely 
at Rp15,935/US$ which is also the worst position since 3.5 
last year. On the other hand, the rupiah’s strongest position 
is Rp14,665/US$ on April 28 2023.
Analysis of Health Sector Performance
The impact of the COVID-19 pandemic in the last three 
years has made it difficult to achieve health development 
targets, which has also had an impact on human resource 
development achievements that are not optimal. In this 
case, the government plays an important role in health 
development to create the highest level of public health 
through equitable and quality health services.
The Ministry of Health is trying to rebuild Indonesia’s 
health system from the impact of the COVID-19 pandemic 
through 6 priority program pillars. These six pillars lead to 
improving health services for the community. The first pillar 
is the transformation of primary services by strengthening 
promotive and preventive efforts while bringing access 
to quality health services closer. The second pillar is the 
transformation of referral services to meet and support 
equitable distribution of health service facilities. The third 
pillar is the transformation of the national resilience system

In 2023, Indonesian people encountered a number of health 
complaints that are becoming the focus of attention. One 
of the main complaints is mental health problems which 
are increasingly rampant, especially due to the impact 
of the prolonged COVID-19 pandemic. Levels of stress, 
anxiety and depression are increasing, requiring serious 
attention from the health sector and government to provide 
adequate mental health services. Additionally, the problem

COVID-19 pandemic and its recovery period, the population 
allocated greater expenditures for health, especially the 
costs of preventive or preventive services.

residents is much higher or almost double that of rural 
residents. When compared with 2021, urban population 
health expenditure in 2023 decreased by around 13.09%, but 
on the contrary in rural areas it actually increased by 2.14%.

Strategi Perusahaan
Pada awal tahun 2023, Perusahaan telah menyusun 
strategi bisnis dengan memperhatikan perkembangan 
kondisi pasar yang ada. Selain itu, Perusahaan juga 
memastikan lingkungan bisnis tetap terjaga terutama 
dalam mempertahankan pertumbuhan dan profitabilitas 
dengan mengutamakan inisiatif dan inovatif secara cepat 
dan terarah. 
Adapun strategi bisnis yang diterapkan oleh Perusahaan 
mencakup:
•	
Mengembangkan layanan pemeriksaan genetik untuk 
mendukung terciptanya precision medicine Perusahaan 
berupaya memenuhi kebutuhan layanan kesehatan bagi 
masyarakat yang lebih presisi berdasarkan karakteristik 
setiap pasien melalui layanan pemeriksaan genetik 
yang fokus pada identifikasi kromosom, gen ataupun 
protein dengan mempelajari ciri khas, bawaan penyakit, 
karakterisitik dari tiap gen yang ada di dalam tubuh 
seseorang.
•	
Memperluas jaringan dan cakupan layanan yang 
bekerjasama dengan Fasilitas Kesehatan baik dari 
internal maupun pihak ketiga.
•	
Perusahaan bekerja sama dengan berbagai Fasilitas 
Kesehatan mulai dari Fasilitas Kesehatan tingkat 
pertama sampai dengan tingkat ketiga untuk 
mendapatkan captive market dengan cepat dan mampu 
menjadi perusahaan laboratorium klinik terbaik di masa 
depan. Kerja sama ini didukung oleh outlet dan cabang 
yang semula berjumlah 39 pada tahun 2022 menjadi 30 
pada tahun 2023 yang tersebar di wilayah Jabodetabek, 
Batam, Bandung, Sumatra, Sulawesi, dan Bali. 
•	
Menyediakan layanan pemeriksaan laboratorium klinik 
yang baru, dengan melakukan kerja sama dengan 
perusahaan penyedia layanan pemeriksaan laboratorium 
yang ada di dalam dan luar negeri.
•	
Perusahaan mencari partner atau rekan kerja sama 
dengan perusahaan dari dalam maupun luar negeri 
agar layanan pemeriksaan laboratorium lebih lengkap 
dari laboratorium lainnya. Sampai dengan saat ini, 
Perusahaan bekerja sama dengan Mayoclinic dan 
Prenetics serta menjalin hubungan dengan perusahaan 
dan universitas yang fokus pada layanan kesehatan. 
•	
Implementasi teknologi dan digitalisasi pada 
Laboratorium Klinik Perusahaan berencana melakukan 
digitalisasi layanan laboratorium dengan fokus pada

laboratorium sedang dikembangkan sebagai wujud 
inovasi Perusahaan untuk memberikan pengalaman 
yang lebih baik bagi pelanggan dalam mengakses 
layanan kesehatan yang disediakan oleh Perusahaan.
Company Strategies
At the beginning of 2023, the Company has developed a 
business strategy by taking into account developments in 
existing market conditions. Apart from that, the Company 
also ensures that the business environment is maintained, 
especially in maintaining growth and profitability by 
prioritizing initiative and innovation in a fast and directed 
manner.
The business strategies implemented by the Company 
contain:
•	
Developing genetic examination services to support the 
creation of precision medicine. The Company strives to 
meet the need for more precise health services for the 
community based on the characteristics of each patient 
through genetic examination services that focus on 
identifying chromosomes, genes or proteins by studying 
the characteristics, inherited diseases, characteristics of 
each gene that is in a person’s body.
•	
Expanding the network and scope of services in 
collaboration with Health Facilities, both internal and 
third parties.
•	
The Company collaborates with various Health Facilities 
ranging from first level to third level Health Facilities to 
quickly gain a captive market and be able to become 
the best clinical laboratory Company in the future. This 
collaboration is supported by outlets and branches 
which originally numbered 39 in 2022 to 30 in 2023 
spread across the Jabodetabek, Batam, Bandung, 
Sumatra, Sulawesi and Bali areas.
•	
Providing new clinical laboratory examination services, 
by collaborating with companies providing laboratory 
examination services at home and abroad.
•	
Companies are looking for partners or co-workers with 
companies from within and outside the country hence 
laboratory examination services are more complete than 
other laboratories. Thus far, the Company collaborates 
with Mayoclinic and Prenetics and maintains relationships 
with companies and universities that focus on health 
services.
•	
Implementation of technology and digitalization in the 
Clinical Laboratory. The Company plans to digitalize 
laboratory services with a focus on laboratory services 
that are easily accessible to anyone, anywhere and at any 
time. The laboratory architecture is being developed as 
an establish of Company innovation to provide a better 
experience for customers in accessing health services 
provided by the Company.

Kemampuan Membayar Utang
Sampai dengan akhir tahun 2023, posisi keuangan 
Perusahaan masih terjaga solid. Dari sisi likuiditas, rasio 
lancar dan rasio kas dapat terjaga dengan baik dengan 
tidak ada perubahan signifikan dari tahun sebelumnya. 
Perusahaan memiliki komitmen untuk mengelola utang 
dengan mengukur rasio-rasio yang mencerminkan 
kemampuan membayar utang seperti rasio likuiditas dan 
rasio solvabilitas. Dengan menggunakan rasio likuiditas, 
Perusahaan dapat mengukur kemampuan membayar 
kewajiban jangka pendek serta rasio solvabilitas untuk 
mengukur kemampuan membayar kewajiban jangka pendek 
dan jangka panjang.
Dengan pengukuran dan pengelolaan tersebut menunjukkan 
bahwa Perusahaan yakin dapat melunasi kewajiban lancar 
atau utang jangka pendeknya. 
Kemampuan Membayar Utang

Dalam pelaksanaannya, Diagnos membagikan dividen 
kepada para Pemegang Saham dengan rasio setinggi-

tahun berjalan, dengan mempertimbangkan antara lain 
ketersediaan dana cadangan, persyaratan belanja modal, 
hasil usaha dan kas serta keberhasilan dalam pelaksanaan 
strategi usaha, keuangan akibat adanya kompetisi dan

Perusahaan ataupun usaha Perusahaan

ditentukan dalam keputusan RUPS, dan dibayarkan 
maksimal 1 (satu) bulan setelah putusan RUPS ditetapkan.
Adapun faktor-faktor yang mempengaruhi besaran dividen 
untuk dibagikan:
1.	 Dilakukan pengukuran Dividend Payout Ratio 
Perusahaan;
2.	 Profitabilitas dan likuiditas secara keseluruhan untuk 
menentukan besaran kemampuan Perusahaan dalam 
memberikan dividen;
3.	 Akses ke pasar keuangan;
4.	 Pertumbuhan pendapatan Perusahaan; 
5.	 Preferensi pemegang saham dan keleluasaan untuk 
menyimpang dari maksimisasi kemakmuran;
6.	 Ketersediaan dan biaya alternatif sumber dana, apabila 
biaya modal tinggi, maka penggunaan laba ditahan akan 
semakin menarik; dan 
7.	 Pembatasan-pembatasan yang diberikan kreditur 
diiringi dengan stabilitas terhadap kondisi bisnis pada 
umumnya.
The Company distributes dividends based on the 
resolutionof the General Meeting of Shareholders, this is in 
conformity with Law Number 40 of 2007 concerning Limited 
Companies and the Company’s Articles of Association. The 
policy regarding dividend distribution implemented by the 
Company depends on several considerations such as the 
Company’s profit in the fiscal year concerned. Apart from 
that, the Company also considers the level of health of the 
Company and without prejudice to the rights of the GMS 
to stipulate otherwise.
In its implementation, Diagnos distributes dividends to 
Shareholders with a maximum ratio of 30% (thirty percent) 
of the current year’s net profit, taking into account, among 
other things, the availability of reserve funds, capital 
expenditure requirements, business and cash results as well 
as success in implementing business strategies, finances due 
to competition and regulation, general economic conditions 
and other matters that apply specifically to the condition 
of the Company or the Company’s business
Dividend payments to Shareholders are determined in the 
GMS decision, and paid a maximum of 1 (one) month after 
the GMS resolution is determined.
The factors that influence the amount of dividends to be 
distributed:
1.	 The Company’s Dividend Payout Ratio is measured;
2.	 Overall profitability and liquidity to stipulate the 
Company’s ability to provide dividends;
3.	 Access to financial markets;
4.	 Company revenue growth;
5.	 Shareholder preferences and freedom to deviate from 
prosperity maximization;
6.	 Availability and cost of alternative sources of funds, if 
capital costs are high, then the use of retained earnings 
will become more attractive; and
7.	 The restrictions imposed by creditors are accompanied 
by stability in general business conditions.

The Company assesses that the health industry is 
currently in the recovery stage and is getting better since 
the pandemic occurred. Public awareness of personal 
health guides people’s lifestyles towards preventive and 
predictive medicine from initially corrective medicine, and 
this encourages the health industry to develop even better. 
In conformity with the direction of development, Diagnos 
is currently capturing market momentum by providing 
predictive solutions, encouraging the development of MCU 
(Medical Check Up) panels, and continuing to encourage 
expansion of the outlet development business to reach a 
wider market.
Based on the Company’s performance in 2023, we are 
quite optimistic about Diagnos’ growth potential in 2024. 
This is also supported by the growth outlook for the health 
industry nationally. This optimism is also supported by 
the work program, namely an organic expansion plan, as 
well as encouraging the contribution of partnerships with 
clinicians and a variety of examination tests that is able to 
meet community needs.
