TINJAUAN MAKROEKONOMI  
Macroeconomics Overview
Tahun 2024 menjadi periode penuh dinamika bagi 
perekonomian global, yang terus dihadapkan pada 
ketidakpastian 
akibat 
meningkatnya 
ketegangan 
geopolitik, kebijakan moneter ketat di negara maju, serta 
volatilitas harga komoditas yang mengganggu rantai 
pasok dunia. Tekanan terhadap pertumbuhan ekonomi 
semakin terasa, sebagaimana tercermin dalam laporan 
yang dirilis oleh International Monetary Fund (IMF). IMF 
memperkirakan pertumbuhan ekonomi global tahun 
2024 sebesar 3,30%, lebih rendah dibandingkan rata-
rata historis 3,70% pada periode 2000-2019l[1].
Di 
tengah 
ketidakpastian 
global, 
perekonomian 
Indonesia tetap mampu menunjukkan ketahanan yang 
cukup baik. Berdasarkan data Badan Pusat Statistik 
(BPS), pertumbuhan ekonomi nasional tahun 2024 
tercatat sebesar 5,03% secara tahunan (C-to-C).
Dalam sektor perdagangan internasional, Indonesia 
mencatat surplus perdagangan sebesar 31,04 miliar 
dolar AS, yang merupakan angka terendah dalam empat 
tahun terakhir. Penurunan ini dipicu oleh meningkatnya 
impor bahan baku 5,29% dan barang modal sebesar 
5,34%, mengindikasikan tingginya permintaan industri 
domestik. Sementara itu, pelemahan ekspor di sektor 
pertambangan akibat fluktuasi harga komoditas global 
turut berkontribusi terhadap menurunnya surplus. 
Meskipun ekspor tetap tumbuh sebesar 2,29%, 
percepatan pertumbuhan impor mengindikasikan bahwa 
aktivitas industri dalam negeri masih terjaga, sekaligus 
mencerminkan daya beli masyarakat yang tetap kuat [3].
Inflasi pada tahun 2024 berada pada tingkat yang relatif 
terkendali di angka 1,57%, meskipun terdapat disparitas 
antarwilayah. Kenaikan harga barang kebutuhan pokok, 
khususnya makanan dan minuman, serta lonjakan biaya 
jasa seperti perawatan pribadi yang meningkat hingga 
7,02%, menjadi faktor utama yang mendorong inflasi. 
Pemerintah menanggapi kondisi ini dengan menerapkan 
kebijakan 
ekonomi 
yang 
lebih 
fleksibel, 
seperti 
penyesuaian subsidi energi di daerah dengan inflasi 
tinggi serta penguatan program bantuan sosial guna 
menjaga daya beli masyarakat yang paling terdampak. 
[4].
2024 has been a dynamic period for the global 
economy, which continues to face uncertainty due to 
rising geopolitical tensions, tight monetary policies in 
developed countries, and commodity price volatility 
disrupting global supply chains. The pressure on 
economic growth is becoming more evident, as reflected 
in the World Economic Outlook report released by the 
International Monetary Fund (IMF). The IMF forecasts 
global economic growth 2024 at 3.30%, lower than the 
historical average of 3.70% from 2000 to 2019[1].
Amid global uncertainty, Indonesia’s economy has 
managed to demonstrate a strong level of resilience. 
According to data from the Central Statistics Agency 
(BPS), national economic growth 2024 is recorded at 
5.03% year-on-year (C-to-C). 
In the international trade sector, Indonesia recorded 
a trade surplus of USD 31.04 billion, the lowest figure 
in the last four years. This decline was driven by a 
5.29% increase in raw material imports and a 5.34% 
rise in capital goods imports, reflecting high demand 
in the domestic industry. Meanwhile, weaker mining 
exports due to global commodity price fluctuations also 
contributed to the reduced surplus. While exports still 
grew by 2.29%, the faster growth of imports indicates that 
domestic industrial activity remains healthy, reflecting 
the continued purchasing power of the public [3].
In 2024, inflation remained relatively under control at 
1.57%, despite regional disparities. The rise in the prices 
of basic goods, particularly food and beverages, and a 
sharp increase in service costs, such as personal care, 
which rose by 7.02%, were the main factors driving 
inflation. The government responded by implementing 
more flexible economic policies, such as adjusting energy 
subsidies in regions with high inflation and strengthening 
social assistance programs to maintain the purchasing 
power of the most affected communities. [4].

in 2024, driven by government policies, price trends,

this industry path. Throughout 2024, nickel prices 
on the London Metal Exchange (LME) experienced 
significant fluctuations and eventually closed at their 
lowest level in several years. Further, the Indonesian 
government announced a plan for production quota 
adjustment to balance global supply and demand. 
 
Overall, the nickel industry in 2024 faced complex 
challenges and despite the increasing investment and 
downstreaming agenda, this industry’s sustainability 
depends largely on balancing economic growth, market 
stability, and environmental conservation.

Production Process:
1.	
Exploration
The exploration process is a crucial initial stage in 
operations, where the primary focus is on identifying 
and researching potential nickel resources in 
predetermined locations. The Company strictly 
adheres to JORC standards and relies on traditional 
drilling methods to obtain information on the 
quality of nickel ore. Geological, geophysical, and 
geochemical data are carefully collected to assess 
the mining potential in the area.
2.	
Mine Engineering
Following the exploration phase, the Company 
designs the mine based on exploration results, 
determines the most efficient mining method, and 
develops mining infrastructure, including road 
construction and operational facility installations. The 
Company also implements a strict and integrated 
work safety and environmental management system 
to ensure that mining operations run safely and with 
minimal impact on the surrounding environment.
3.	
Production
The Company operates open-pit mining with 
a systematic and efficient approach. After the 
extraction process, ore samples are sent to the 
laboratory for quality analysis before being stored 
and shipped.
4.	
QA & QC (Quality Assurance & Quality Control)
The Company’s laboratory is equipped with three 
XRF machines, ensuring accurate mineral content 
data. This system allows for effective ore stock 
management and guarantees that the ore prepared 
for shipment meets strict quality standards.
5.	
Shipment
After passing the QA & QC process, nickel ore is 
packaged and prepared for shipment. The Company 
works closely with logistics partners to coordinate 
the transportation of nickel ore to customers or 
designated markets, ensuring full compliance with 
export and environmental regulations.
As the Company continues optimizing operations in 
Block A and Block B, production capacity continues to 
increase. Additionally, the Company plans to implement 
mining technology upgrades and expand exploration to 
secure long-term nickel resource availability.

GCG principles socialized within the Company:
Transparansi / Transparancy
Prinsip keterbukaan diterapkan dalam proses pengambilan keputusan serta penyampaian keterbukaan informasi 
terkait agenda korporasi Perseroan secara tepat waktu dan akurat kepada otoritas maupun publik luas;
The principle of openness is applied in the decision-making process and the delivery of information disclosure related to 
the Company’s corporate agenda in a timely and accurate manner to the authorities and the wider public;
Kewajaran dan Kesetaraan / Fairness and Equality
Prinsip kewajaran antara lain diterapkan dengan memenuhi hak pemegang saham dan pemangku kepentingan 
secara adil dan setara serta dengan memastikan bahwa seluruh transaksi material yang dilakukan pada tahun 
buku telah memenuhi prinsip kewajaran dan sesuai peraturan perundang-undangan yang berlaku.
The principle of fairness, among others, is applied by fulfilling the rights of shareholders and stakeholders fairly and 
equally and by ensuring that all material transactions carried out in the financial year have met the principle of fairness 
and in accordance with applicable laws and regulations. authorities and the wider public;
Indenpendensi / Independence
Prinsip independensi antara lain diterapkan melalui pengelolaan bisnis yang profesional, di mana Perseroan 
memastikan tidak adanya benturan kepentingan ataupun tekanan dari pihak manapun yang bertentangan 
dengan peraturan perundang-undangan yang berlaku;
The principle of independence, among others, is applied through professional business management, where the 
Company ensures that there is no conflict of interest or pressure from any party that conflicts with applicable laws and 
regulations;
Tanggung Jawab / Respobility
Prinsip tanggung jawab diterapkan dengan komitmen Perseroan untuk menjalankan bisnisnya sesuai ketentuan 
perundang-undangan yang berlaku serta sesuai best practices;
The principle of responsibility is applied with the Company’s commitment to run its business in accordance with applicable 
laws and regulations and in accordance with best practices;
Akuntabilitas / Accountability
Prinsip akuntabilitas diterapkan dengan membangun kejelasan fungsi dan tanggung jawab di antara organ 
pendukung GCG untuk mendukung pengambilan keputusan yang efektif;
The principle of accountability is applied by building clarity of functions and responsibilities among GCG supporting 
organs to support effective decision making;
