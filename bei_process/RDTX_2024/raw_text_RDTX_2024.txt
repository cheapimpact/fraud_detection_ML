Dividend Policy
Based on Law no. 40 of 2007 regarding Limited Liability 
Companies, dividend payments are made based on 
the decision of the General Meeting of Shareholders. 
The Company always strives to pay attention to the 
rights of the shareholders while still paying attention 
to the financial condition and performance of the 
Company in the current year. The decision to distribute 
the Company’s dividends is related to several 
considerations, including net income for the fiscal year, 
the Company’s obligation to allocate reserve funds in 
accordance with applicable regulations and the need 
for funds for further business expansion plans, without 
prejudice to the rights of the Annual General Meeting of 
Shareholders (AGM). to determine in accordance with 
the provisions of the Company’s Articles of Association. 
The distribution of interim dividends is stipulated 
based on the resolution of the Board of Directors 
following approval by the Board of Commissioners, 
taking into account projection for profit and Company’s 
financial capabilities as well as Articles of Association. 
In the event that the Company suffers from loss after 
the financial year, the interim dividends that have been 
distributed must be returned back to the Company by 
the shareholders.
