PRODUCTION
The realization of OTC production compared to the 
predetermined target was achieved by 103.9% and 
compared to the previous year decreased by 44.83%. 
This decrease in production was mainly due to a 
decrease in the production of Tablets, Salute Tablets, 
Liquid Sachets, and Capsules.
The realization of ETHICAL production compared to 
the predetermined target was achieved by 98.12% and 
compared to the previous year decreased by 48.67%. 
This decrease in production was mainly due to a 
decrease in the production of Tablets, Salute Tablets, 
Capsules, Injection, Ointments, and Medical Devices. 
The realization of GENERIC production compared to 
the predetermined target was achieved by 97.95% and 
compared to the previous year decreased by 38.69%. 
This decrease in production was mainly due to a 
decrease in the production of Tablets, Salute Tablets, 
Amp Injection and Vials, Ointments and Mixtures.
The realization of TOLL IN production compared to the 
predetermined target was achieved by 95.94% and 
compared to the previous year experienced an increase 
of 6.63%. This increase in production was mainly due to 
an increase in the production of Ampoule Injection.

Segmen OTC
Divisi OTC adalah divisi yang menangani produk obat 
yang dijual bebas. Produk-produk OTC selain dijual 
apotek bisa di jual melalui toko kelontong, warung, 
atau minimart.
Perseroan saat ini mempunyai kurang lebih 15 (lima 
belas) item produk OTC dimana salah satunya adalah 
produk unggulan yang sudah menjadi legenda yaitu 
Antimo. Selain itu ada Antimo Anak, Livron B Plek, Noza, 
Hemorogard, Bioron dan lain lain.
SALES
OTC SEGMENT
The OTC division is the division that handles over-the-
counter medicinal products. OTC products, apart from 
being sold in pharmacies, can be sold through grocery 
stores, stalls, or minimarts.
The Company currently has approximately 15 (fifteen) 
OTC product items, one of which is the flagship product 
that has become a legend, Antimo. In addition there 
are Antimo Anak, Livron B Plek, Noza, Hemorogard, 
Bioron and others.

Kinerja Produk OTC

Margin mengalami penurunan dikarenakan menurunnya 
penjualan Antimo group yang memiliki margin cukup 
tinggi. Pandemi COVID-19 merupakan salah satu 
penyebab turunnya penjualan.
Pemasaran Produk OTC
Menjalankan program Promosi below the line ke 
pasar sasaran produk. Program Peningkatan jumlah 
outlet termasuk diantaranya ke modern outlet, serta 
memanfaatkan situs belanja dalam jaringan dan 
media sosial sebagai media promosi.

produk obat generik berlogo (OGB). Target market 
untuk produk ini adalah institusi pemerintah dan rumah 
sakit. Dengan adanya BPJS maka kebutuhan obat 
generik berlogo di pasar semakin besar.

OTC Product Performance
OTC sales achievement decreased compared to last 
year by 24.6%. Products that experienced a decrease in 
sales were Antimo Group Products with sales of Rp112.3 
billion, reaching 66.8% of its budget and decreasing 
compared to last year by 24.3% or Rp36.0 billion, Livron 
B Plex with sales of Rp10.2 billion, reaching 40.4% of 
the budget and decreasing compared to last year by 
20.4% or equivalent to Rp2.6 billion, and Becefort with 
sales of Rp6.7 billion, reaching 40.7% of the budget 
and decreasing compared to last year by 12.9% or 
equivalent to Rp985.0 million.
Profitability of OTC Products
Margins have decreased due to the decline in sales of 
the Antimo group which has a fairly high margin. The 
Covid-19 pandemic is one of the causes of the decline 
in sales.
	
OTC Product Marketing
Running a below the line promotion program to the 
product target market. Increasing the number of outlets 
including modern outlets, as well as utilizing online 
shopping sites and social media as promotional media.
