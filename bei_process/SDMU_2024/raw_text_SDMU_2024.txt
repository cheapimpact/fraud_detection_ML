Company Profile
The global economy faced significant challenges in 2024 due 
to prolonged geopolitical tensions in Europe and the Middle 
East. This situation put additional pressure on the world 
economy, which had only just begun to recover from the 
COVID-19 pandemic. The International Monetary Fund (IMF) 
projected global economic growth of 3.2% in 2024, while 
the World Bank was more pessimistic with a projection of 
2.6%.
Despite facing such challenging global conditions, Indonesia’s 
economy demonstrated a positive trend. In the fourth quarter 
of 2024, the domestic economy recorded growth of 5.02% 
(yoy) compared to the same period in the previous year. This 
growth was supported by several key sectors. In terms of 
production, the business field of Other Services experienced 
the highest growth of 11.36% while in terms of expenditure, 
the Export of Goods and Services component showed the 
highest increase with 7.63% growth. Overall, throughout the 
year, national economy grew by 5.03%.
Tinjauan Makroekonomi Global dan Nasional 
Global and National Macroeconomic Review
Pada tahun 2024, perekonomian global menghadapi 
tantangan signifikan akibat ketegangan geopolitik yang 
berkepanjangan di Eropa dan Timur Tengah. Situasi ini 
menambah tekanan pada ekonomi dunia yang baru saja mulai 
pulih pasca pandemi COVID-19. International Monetary Fund 
(IMF) memproyeksikan pertumbuhan ekonomi global sebesar 
3,2% selama 2024, sementara Bank Dunia lebih pesimis 
dengan angka 2,6%.
Di Indonesia, meskipun menghadapi kondisi global yang 
menantang, perekonomian nasional menunjukkan tren 
positif. Pada triwulan IV 2024, perekonomian Indonesia 
mencatat pertumbuhan sebesar 5,02% dibandingkan 
periode yang sama tahun sebelumnya (year-on-year). 
Pertumbuhan ini didukung oleh beberapa sektor utama. 
Dari sisi produksi, Lapangan Usaha Jasa Lainnya mengalami 
pertumbuhan tertinggi sebesar 11,36%. Sementara itu, 
dari sisi pengeluaran, Komponen Ekspor Barang dan Jasa 
menunjukkan peningkatan tertinggi dengan pertumbuhan 
sebesar 7,63%. Secara keseluruhan, sepanjang tahun 2024, 
ekonomi Indonesia tumbuh sebesar 5,03%.

Management Discussion and Analysis
Namun, krisis pasokan energi global telah mendorong inflasi 
tinggi, meningkatkan biaya produksi barang dan jasa. Negara-
negara dengan ketergantungan tinggi terhadap impor energi, 
seperti Indonesia, merasakan dampak signifikan. Kenaikan 
harga bahan bakar dan listrik memicu biaya hidup yang lebih 
tinggi, yang berdampak pada daya beli masyarakat.
Bagi PT Sidomulyo Selaras Tbk (SDMU), yang bergerak di 
bidang pengangkutan dan penyimpanan bahan berbahaya 
seperti bahan kimia, minyak, dan gas, kondisi ini menimbulkan 
tantangan tersendiri. Fluktuasi harga komoditas energi 
dapat mempengaruhi biaya operasional perusahaan. Selain 
itu, ketidakpastian geopolitik dan ekonomi global dapat 
berdampak pada permintaan jasa logistik dan transportasi 
yang disediakan oleh SDMU. Oleh karena itu, Perusahaan 
perlu mengadopsi strategi adaptif untuk menjaga stabilitas 
operasional dan keuangan di tengah dinamika pasar yang 
tidak menentu.
Tinjauan per Segmen Operasi 
Review of Each Operational Segment
Total pendapatan bersih tahun 2024 tercatat sebesar Rp94,95 
miliar, meningkat 3,40% dibandingkan tahun sebelumnya 
yang sebesar Rp91,83 miliar. Pertumbuhan ini terutama 
didorong oleh peningkatan pendapatan dari segmen jasa 
angkutan, yang mencatatkan kenaikan 5,76% dari Rp89,56 
miliar di tahun 2023 menjadi Rp94,72 miliar pada tahun 2024. 
Segmen ini menjadi kontributor utama dengan menyumbang 
99,76% dari total pendapatan bersih.
Di sisi lain, segmen penjualan ban mengalami penurunan 
signifikan dari Rp2,25 miliar menjadi Rp203,3 juta, 
berkontribusi sebesar 0,21% terhadap total pendapatan 
bersih perusahaan di tahun 2024. Penurunan ini menunjukkan 
adanya 
perubahan 
strategi 
bisnis 
atau 
menurunnya 
permintaan terhadap produk ban.
Sementara itu, pendapatan dari jasa inklaring mengalami 
kenaikan 50%, dari Rp17,5 juta di tahun 2023 menjadi 
Rp26,25 juta pada tahun 2024, menyumbang 0,03% 
terhadap total pendapatan bersih. Kenaikan ini mencerminkan 
peningkatan aktivitas layanan kepabeanan dan pengurusan 
dokumen ekspor-impor.
Nonetheless, the global energy supply crisis had driven high 
inflation, increasing the cost of production of goods and 
services. Countries highly dependent on energy imports, such 
as Indonesia, were thus affected significantly from this crisis. 
Increases in fuel and electricity prices led to higher living costs, 
which had an impact on people’s purchasing power.
As the Company is engaged in the transportation and storage 
of hazardous materials such as chemicals, oil, and gas, these 
conditions posed their own challenges. Fluctuations in energy 
commodity prices affected the Company’s operating costs. 
In addition, geopolitical uncertainty and the global economy 
had an impact on the demand for logistics and transportation 
services provided by SDMU. Therefore, the Company needed 
to adopt an adaptive strategy to maintain operational and 
financial stability amid erratic market dynamics. 
Total revenues–net in 2024 amounted to Rp94.95 billion, 
an increase of 3.40% from Rp91.83 billion recorded in the 
previous year. This growth was mainly driven by the increase 
of 5.76% in revenues from the transportation services 
segment, from Rp89.56 billion in 2023 to Rp94.72 billion 
in 2024. This segment represented the primary contributor, 
accounting for 99.76% of total revenues–net.
On the other hand, the tire sales segment declined significantly 
from Rp2.25 billion in 2023 to Rp203.3 million in 2024; 
contributing 0.21% to the Company’s total revenues–net this 
year. Such decline indicated a change in business strategy or 
slowing demand for tire products.
Meanwhile, revenues from customs clearance services 
increased by 50%, from Rp17.5 million in 2023 to Rp26.25 
million in 2024; accounting for 0.03% of total revenues–
net. This increase reflected the increase in customs service 
activities and export-import document processing.

STORAGE SERVICE
The Company provides storage tank rental services for 
customers or industry players of hazardous-and-toxic materials 
(B3) or isotonic materials that do not have adequate storage 
facilities. To support its operational activities, the Company 
has equipped itself with a number of facilities, including pools 
and vehicle maintenance and repair workshops in Cilegon,

center in Cilegon. The Company also has a transportation 
fleet covering trucks, trailers, isotanks, crane vehicles, and 
emergency response containers, in sufficient number.

Company cooperates with third parties to provide additional 
trucks and isotanks as a backup alternative, if needed.

and client testimonials and displayed on the Company’s 
official website and social media to enhance credibility 
and market trust.
