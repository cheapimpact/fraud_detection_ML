TINJAUAN INDUSTRI
INDUSTRY OVERVIEW 
Persoalan daya beli masyarakat juga terasa pada industri pangan. 
Hal ini tercermin dari harga jual sejumlah bahan pangan pada 
pertengahan tahun 2024 mengalami penurunan, termasuk 
daging sapi, daging ayam hingga telur ayam. Berdasarkan data 
Panel Harga Pangan dari Badan Pangan Nasional, harga daging 
sapi murni sejak awal tahun 2024 meningkat hingga puncaknya 
pada bulan April mencapai Rp 138.067/Kg. Namun, setelah itu 
harga daging sapi kembali turun sampai harga terendahnya  yaitu 
Rp 135.499/Kg pada bulan Oktober.  Fluktuasi harga daging sapi 
ini juga masih dipengaruhi oleh wabah penyakit sapi seperti LSD 
(Lumpy Skin Desease) yang merebak pada awal 2024 dan Penyakit 
Mulut dan Kuku (PMK) di akhir 2024. Wabah penyakit tersebut 
membuat banyak sapi peternak di dalam negeri terdampak yang 
membuat supply sapi menurun. Begitu juga dengan minat beli 
konsumen terhadap daging sapi yang ikut turun karena merasa 
khawatir.  Hal ini juga tercermin dari angka konsumsi daging 
sapi nasional yang tercatat mencapai  760.198 ton, menurut 
data BPS. Jumlah ini hanya tumbuh 1,8% YoY, lebih rendah dari 
pertumbuhan sebelumnya yang mencapai 4,8% YoY. 
Hal serupa juga terjadi pada industri perunggasan nasional. 
Sepanjang tahun 2024, harga telur dan daging ayam ras sempat 
memuncak hingga bulan Maret dan April, namun kembali 
melemah hingga mencapai harga terendahnya pada bulan 
September. Berdasarkan data Panel Harga Pangan Nasional dari 
Badan Pangan Nasional, harga telur pada bulan Maret 2024 
sempat menyentuh Rp 31.862/Kg, namun pada bulan September 
2024 hanya Rp 28.664/Kg. Sedangkan, harga daging ayam ras 
pada bulan April 2024 mencapai Rp 39.055/Kg, sedangkan pada 
bulan September 2024 hanya Rp 35.311/Kg. Fluktuasi harga 
ayam ini juga masih dipengaruhi oleh kondisi oversupply daging 
ayam. Berdasarkan data prognosis ketersediaan daging ayam ras 
dari Kementerian Pertanian yang diolah oleh tim riset internal 
Perseroan, total kebutuhan daging ayam sepanjang tahun 2024 
mencapai 3,7 juta ton, sedangkan total produksi mencapai 4 juta 
ton. Dengan demikian, masih ada surplus sekitar 300 ribu ton. 
Di tengah berbagai tantangan tersebut, potensi pertumbuhan 
industri pangan tanah air kedepannya masih cukup besar. 
Konsumsi daging sapi, daging ayam dan bahan pangan lainnya 
diprediksi akan terus meningkat kedepannya seiring dengan 
proyeksi pendapatan per kapita penduduk Indonesia yang 
semakin tinggi. Selain itu, program Makan Bergizi Gratis yang 
diusul pemerintah juga akan memberi dampak positif bagi 
permintaan protein hewani seperti daging sapi, daging ayam dan 
telur. 
The issue of people’s purchasing power is also felt in the food 
industry. This is reflected in the selling price of a number of food 
ingredients in mid-2024 which has decreased, including beef, 
chicken and chicken eggs. Based on data from the Food Price 
Panel from the National Food Agency, the price of pure beef has 
increased since the beginning of 2024 to its peak in April reaching 
IDR 138,067/Kg. However, after that the price of beef fell again to 
its lowest price of IDR 135,499/Kg in October. This fluctuation in 
beef prices is also still influenced by outbreaks of cattle diseases 
such as LSD (Lumpy Skin Disease) which broke out in early 2024 and 
Foot and Mouth Disease (FMD) at the end of 2024. The outbreak 
of this disease has affected many domestic cattle farmers, which 
has reduced the supply of cattle. Likewise, consumer interest in 
buying beef has also decreased because they are worried. This is 
also reflected in the national beef consumption figure which was 
recorded at 760,198 tons, according to BPS data. This number only 
grew by 1.8% YoY, lower than the previous growth of 4.8% YoY.
The similar situation also happen in the national poultry industry. 
Throughout 2024, the price of eggs and chicken meat peaked until 
March and April, but weakened again to reach its lowest price 
in September. Based on data from the National Food Price Panel 
from the National Food Agency, the price of eggs in March 2024 
touched IDR 31,862/Kg, but in September 2024 it was only IDR 
28,664/Kg. Meanwhile, the price of chicken meat in April 2024 
reached IDR 39,055/Kg, while in September 2024 it was only IDR 
35,311/Kg. This fluctuation in chicken prices is also still influenced 
by the oversupply of chicken meat. Based on the prognosis data 
on the availability of chicken meat from the Ministry of Agriculture 
processed by the Company’s internal research team, the total need 
for chicken meat throughout 2024 reached 3.7 million tons, while 
the total production reached 4 million tons. Thus, there is still a 
surplus of around 300 thousand tons.
Amidst these challenges, the potential for growth in the country’s 
food industry in the future is still quite large. Consumption of beef, 
chicken and other food ingredients is predicted to continue to 
increase in the future along with the projection of the increasingly 
high per capita income of the Indonesian population. In addition, 
the Free Nutritious Meal program proposed by the government 
will also have a positive impact on the demand for animal protein 
such as beef, chicken and eggs.
 Pelemahan daya beli ini juga tercermin dari data inflasi Indonesia 
sepanjang 2024 yang tercatat sebesar 1,57% (YoY) atau terendah 
sepanjang masa. Selain itu, Indonesia juga sempat mengalami 
deflasi selama lima bulan beruntun. BPS telah mengumumkan, 
deflasi lima bulan beruntun terjadi sejak Mei 2024 yang sebesar 
0,03%, lalu berlanjut pada Juni 2024 sebesar 0,08%, dan Juli 2024 
sebesar 0,18%. Lalu, pada Agustus 2024 sebesar 0,03%, dan 
per September 2024 makin dalam menjadi 0,12%.  Hal ini tentu 
menjadi tantangan tersendiri bagi Perseroan yang bergerak di 
bidang Consumer Goods & Commodity Agriculture.
This weakening purchasing power is also reflected in Indonesia’s 
inflation data throughout 2024 which was recorded at 1.57% (YoY) 
or the lowest of all time. In addition, Indonesia also experienced 
deflation for five consecutive months. BPS has announced that 
five consecutive months of deflation have occurred since May 
2024 which was 0.03%, then continued in June 2024 at 0.08%, 
and July 2024 at 0.18%. Then, in August 2024 it was 0.03%, and as 
of September 2024 it had deepened to 0.12%. %. This is certainly 
a challenge for the Company which operates in the Consumer 
Goods & Commodity Agriculture sector.

Perseroan adalah salah satu perusahaan peternakan terintegrasi

rumah potong hewan (dengan fasilitas rumah potong hewan

The Company is one of the largest integrated livestock companies 
in Indonesia that focuses on cattle and poultry. The Company 
started its business in cattle farming in 1996, the Company has 
expanded into various other businesses including trading of 
agricultural and plantation commodities, and meat processing. The 
Company expanded its cattle farm and feed mill in 2006 and built 
the Company’s first abattoir to supply meat products in Indonesia 
in 2013. To date, the Company is vertically integrated (from 
rearing, slaughtering, and then to meat processing) and integrated 
horizontally (through trading of agricultural commodities and the 
Company’s plantation business). In particular, to support integrated 
cattle and poultry farming, and to offer a comprehensive range of 
processed foods, the Company continues to expand its downstream 
operations such as abattoirs (with modern slaughterhouse 
facilities). The Company’s net sales were mainly driven by sales in 
cattle, poultry and meat processing.
The Company formulates its business strategy through the 
implementation and improvement of cost efficiency in all business 
processes, including produces animal feed in- house and develops 
a self-produced Biofertilizer.
Currently, the Company is engaged in five main business lines 
through the ownership of various operating Subsidiaries, namely:
•	
integrated cattle farming (including feedlot, breeding, feed 
factory, and biofertilizer);
•	
integrated poultry farming (including poultry slaughterhouse, 
commercial farming, hatching, breeding, and feed factory, 
etc.);
•	
meat processing (including slaughterhouse, leather factory, 
and manufacturing processed meat products);
•	
trade in agricultural and plantation commodities;
•	
construction and energy.

PROSPEK USAHA
BUSINESS PROSPECT 
Bisnis Perseroan kedepannya masih memiliki prospek yang baik seiring 
dengan proyeksi nilai konsumsi beberapa produk pangan seperti sapi, 
ayam, telur, jagung, beras dan kedelai. Berdasarkan data dari BPS yang 
diolah tim riset internal Perseroan, total nilai pasar atas produk-produk 
pangan tersebut pada tahun 2025 mencapai Rp 993 triliun dan akan 
meningkat hingga 23% menjadi Rp 1.221 triliun pada tahun 2030.  Hal ini 
seiring dengan pertumbuhan populasi dan PDB per kapita masyarakat 
Indonesia. 
Permintaan daging sapi dan unggas di Indonesia juga menunjukkan 
tren pertumbuhan positif, karena penduduk Indonesia didominasi oleh 
populasi penduduk usia produktif dan pertumbuhan kelas menengah. 
Selain itu, sebagai negara dengan mayoritas penduduk muslim, daging 
sapi dan unggas juga menjadi sumber protein hewani utama yang 
dikonsumsi penduduknya. Meski demikian, jika dibandingkan dengan 
beberapa negara lain di Asia Tenggara, konsumsi daging sapi dan unggas 
di Indonesia masih tergolong rendah. 
Pada tahun 2024, menurut data OECD-FAO konsumsi daging sapi 
dan unggas di Indonesia tercatat sebesar 1,67 kg/kapita dan 4,66 kg/
kapita. Nilai ini masih lebih rendah dari Malaysia, Vietnam dan Filipina. 
Bahkan, negara tetangga Indonesia, Malaysia, konsumsi daging sapi 
dan unggasnya mencapai 5,59 kg/kapita dan 31,14 kg/kapita. Hal ini 
menunjukkan, masih ada potensi ruang pertumbuhan yang cukup besar 
untuk konsumsi daging sapi dan unggas di Indonesia yang didukung oleh 
pertumbuhan pendapatan per kapita di Indonesia. Berdasarkan proyeksi 
dari Susenas-BPS, konsumsi daging sapi dan unggas di Indonesia pada 
tahun 2029 dapat mencapai 2,89 kg/kapita dan 20,19 kg/kapita. 
Prospek positif industri pangan tanah air juga didukung oleh program 
prioritas pemerintah saat ini yaitu Makan Bergizi Gratis (MBG). 
Berdasarkan data dari BPS yang diolah tim riset internal Perseroan, 
konsumsi daging sapi dan kerbau pada tahun 2025 dengan adanya 
program makan bergizi gratis mencapai 863.704 ton. Jumlah ini naik 
dari konsumsi daging sapi dan kerbau pada tahun 2024 yang mencapai 
760.198 ton sebelum dijalankannya program makan bergizi gratis. 
Potensi peningkatan ini tentu juga terjadi pada konsumsi daging unggas 
dan telur. 
Selain kondisi pasar yang positif di Indonesia, Perseroan diuntungkan 
dari kehadirannya yang telah lama berdiri di pasar, skala besar dan 
operasinya di negara yang terintegrasi secara vertikal dan horizontal. Hal 
ini memungkinkan Perseroan untuk mencapai posisi yang kuat dalam 
industri peternakan terintegrasi di Indonesia dan untuk mendapatkan 
hasil yang lebih baik daripada pesaingnya yang lebih kecil dan tidak 
terintegrasi. Perseroan memiliki peluang besar untuk tumbuh lebih jauh 
dan memperluas kehadirannya di seluruh area lainnya.
The Company’s future business still has good prospects in line with 
the projected consumption value of several food products such as 
cattle, chicken, eggs, corn, rice and soybeans. Based on data from BPS 
processed by the Company’s internal research team, the total market 
value of these food products in 2025 reached Rp 993 trillion and will 
increase by 23% to Rp 1,221 trillion in 2030.  This is in line with the 
growth in population and GDP per capita of the Indonesian people. 
The demand for beef and poultry in Indonesia also shows a positive 
growth trend, as the Indonesian population is dominated by a 
population of productive age and a growing middle class. In addition, 
as a Muslim-majority country, beef and poultry are also the main 
sources of animal protein consumed by the population. However, 
compared to several other countries in Southeast Asia, beef and 
poultry consumption in Indonesia is still relatively low. 
In 2024, according to OECD-FAO data, beef and poultry consumption 
in Indonesia was recorded at 1.67 kg/capita and 4.66 kg/capita, 
respectively. These values are still lower than Malaysia, Vietnam 
and the Philippines. In fact, Indonesia’s neighbor Malaysia has beef 
and poultry consumption of 5.59 kg/capita and 31.14 kg/capita, 
respectively. This suggests that there is still considerable potential 
room for growth in beef and poultry consumption in Indonesia, 
supported by the growth of per capita income in Indonesia. Based 
on projections from Susenas-BPS, beef and poultry consumption in 
Indonesia in 2029 could reach 2.89 kg/capita and 20.19 kg/capita.
The positive outlook for the country’s food industry is also supported 
by the current government’s priority program, Makan Bergizi Gratis 
(MBG). Based on data from BPS processed by the Company’s internal 
research team, beef and buffalo consumption in 2025 with the free 
nutritious meal program will reach 863,704 tons. This is an increase 
from the consumption of beef and buffalo in 2024 which reached 
760,198 tons before the implementation of the free nutritious meal 
program. This potential increase would also occur in the consumption 
of poultry meat and eggs. 
In addition to positive market conditions in Indonesia, the Company 
benefits from its long-standing market presence, large scale and 
vertically and horizontally integrated country operations. This enables 
the Company to achieve a strong position in the integrated livestock 
industry in Indonesia and to earn better returns than its smaller, non-
integrated competitors. The Company has a great opportunity to grow 
further and expand its presence across other areas

Perseroan telah bekerja sama dengan para pelanggan utama di 
bidang usaha peternakan sapi selama 28 tahun dan selama lebih dari 
6 tahun dengan para pelanggan utama di bidang usaha peternakan 
ayam. Tidak terdapat ketentuan kontraktual yang spesifik dengan 
para pelanggan sapi Perseroan mengingat Perseroan bekerja sama 
dengan para pelanggan tersebut sebagai mitra rumah potong 
untuk penjualan lebih lanjut ke pasar tradisional atau pasar basah 
di seluruh Indonesia. Syarat pembayaran dengan pelanggan sapi 
Perseroan pada umumnya adalah Tunai sebelum Pengiriman (Cash 
before Delivery, CbD, terutama untuk pelanggan baru) dan sesuai 
tenggang pembayaran (Terms of Payment, TOP, pada umumnya 
untuk pelanggan lama) hingga 15 hari setelah penagihan.
Pelanggan Perseroan di bidang usaha peternakan ayam pada 
umumnya berdasarkan kontrak 1 tahun dengan salah satu dari 
kesepakatan berikut ini: (i) berdasarkan kuantitas tetap (yaitu 
pembelian minimum) dengan harga yang mengikuti harga pasar; 
atau (ii) tanpa ketentuan pembelian minimum, tetapi pembelian 
tunduk pada harga tetap yang akan dinegosiasikan ulang setiap 3 
bulan. Syarat pembayaran dengan pelanggansapi Perseroan pada 
umumnya adalah Tunai sebelum Pengiriman (Cash before Delivery, 
CbD), Tunai pada Pengiriman (Cash on Delivery, COD) dan sesuai 
tenggang pembayaran (Terms of Payment, TOP) hingga 15 hari 
setelah penagihan. Penundaan pembayaran hanya diizinkan dalam 
keadaan khusus bagi pelanggan tetap dengan hubungan yang telah 
berlangsung lama.
The Company has been working with major customers in the cattle 
farming business for 28 years and for more than 6 years with key 
customers in the chicken farming business. There are no specific 
contractual provisions with the Company’s cattle customers 
considering that the Company cooperates with these customers 
as slaughterhouse partners for further sales to traditional or wet 
markets throughout Indonesia. Payment terms with the Company’s 
cattle customers are generally Cash before Delivery (CbD, especially 
for new customers) and according to the payment grace period 
(Terms of Payment, TOP, generally for existing customers) up to 15 
days after billing.
The Company’s customers in the chicken farming business are 
generally based on a 1-year contract with one of the following 
agreements: (i) based on a fixed quantity (i.e. minimum purchase) 
at a price that follows the market price; or (ii) without a minimum 
purchase requirement, but purchases are subject to a fixed price 
that will be renegotiated every 3 months. Payment terms with the 
Company’s customers are generally Cash before Delivery (CbD), 
Cash on Delivery (COD) and according to the Payment Grace Period 
(Terms of Payment, TOP) up to 15 days after billing. Payment delays 
are only allowed in special circumstances for regular customers 
with longstanding relationships.
