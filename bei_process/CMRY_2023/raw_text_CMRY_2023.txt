Assets

primarily by higher current assets.

increase was primarily derived from the increase of 
investment in marketable securities.

In 2023, Cash and Cash Equivalents increased from 
Rp2,159.3 billion in 2022 to Rp2,197.4 billion in 2023.

were posted at Rp869.0 billion compared to 
Rp763.8 billion in 2022, along with the Company’s 
business growth.

Rp9.0 billion, primarly due to higher third parties 
other receivables.
Inventories
Inventories decreased by 3.4% from Rp908.6 billion 
in 2022 to Rp877.5 billion in 2023, driven 
primarily by lower raw and packaging materials of 
Rp489.9 billion in 2023 compared to Rp724.3 billion 
in 2022. 
Advances and Prepaid Expenses
Advances decreased by 71.5%, reached 
Rp35.6 billion in 2023 from Rp125 billion in 2022, 
on lower Inventories Advances of Rp32.5 billion in 
2023 compared to Rp118.6 billion in 2022. Prepaid 
Expenses reached Rp2.9 billion in 2023 on lower 
Rent Prepaid Expenses that reached Rp0.66 billion 
in 2023. 
Advances and Prepaid Expenses - others mainly 
represent payments of advances for repairs of 
buildings and equipment.
Non-Current Assets
In 2023, Non-Current Assets increased by 14.1%, 
from Rp2,198.0 billion in 2022 to Rp2,507.7 billion in 
2023, driven by higher Fixed Assets, Net that grew 
by 30.0% to Rp1,647.1 billion in 2023.

nvestment in marketable securities is investment
