2024 increased to USD 20.7 million or 16% up compared 
to 2023.
 
As a result, the Company recorded a net profit in 2024 of 
USD 8.9 million, increased of 43.5% compared to a net 
profit in 2023 of USD 6.2 million. The increment in net 
profit was mainly due to increasing of copper sales and 
cost efficiency.
