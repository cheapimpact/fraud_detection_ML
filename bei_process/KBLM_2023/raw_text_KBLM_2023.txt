1.	 Operational Review Per Business Segment
In 2023, the Company was engaged in 1 (one) business 
segment which was cable business.
The Company manufactures low voltage cable, building 
wire, control cable and instrument cable, as well as 
medium voltage cable. To produce these cable types, the 
Company utilizes production machines with nameplate 
capacity and implements several stages of production.
The manufacturing process generally consists of eight 
stages:
1.	 Drawing the main material (copper and aluminium) to 
reduce the size.
2.	 Combining several conduits.
3.	 Fitting isolation on conduits.
4.	 Applying protector on certain cables.
5.	 Combining cables.
6.	 Fitting isolation on certain cables.
7.	 Applying protector on cable such as steel tape or steel 
wire.
8.	 Fitting external isolation on certain cables.

Making The Connection
Diamond
Diamond Beach (DB), Nusa Penida, Bali dikenal akan keindahan pantainya dengan garis pasir 
putih yang halus dan lembut. Nama DB diambil dari batu karang yang berbentuk mirip 
intan yang menjulang di tengah pantai. Pengunjung bisa menikmati snorkeling, berenang, 
dan keindahan matahari terbenam. Saat matahari terbenam, cahaya matahari memancar 
dari balik batu karang dan pantai yang indah menciptakan pemandangan yang memukau.
Diamond Beach (DB), Nusa Penida, Bali is known for its beautiful beach lined with white, 
soft sand. The name DB was taken from the coral reef resembling a diamond that sits in 
the centre of the beach. Visitors can enjoy snorkelling, swimming and stunning sunsets. 
At sunset, sunlight shines from behind the reef and the beautiful beach creates a 
captivating view.
