PRODUK TERAK
Terak atau klinker merupakan produk setengah jadi untuk 
pembuatan semen yang berasal dari pembakaran campuran 
bahan baku, yang terdiri dari batu kapur, tanah liat, pasir besi, 
dan pasir silika. Selain produksi semen, Perusahaan memiliki 
lini bisnis produksi terak yang dihasilkan di pusat produksi 
yang terletak di Baturaja, yakni Pabrik Baturaja I dengan 
kapasitas 1.200.000 ton/tahun dan Pabrik Baturaja II dengan 
kapasitas 1.500.000 ton/tahun.
WHITE CLAY
White Clay atau tanah liat merupakan produk turunan yang 
dihasilkan Semen Baturaja. Bahan baku white clay diperoleh 
dari proses penambangan di area milik Perusahaan yang 
kemudian digiling dan dikeringkan. White Clay memiliki 
banyak kegunaan salah satunya sebagai bahan dalam proses 
pembuatan keramik dan pupuk NPK. 
GROUND LIMESTONE
Ground Limestone atau Batu Kapur pecah merupakan produk 
sampingan SMBR yang digunakan di Pembangkit Listrik 
Tenaga Uap (PLTU) untuk mengurangi emisi sulfur dioksida 
(SOx) dari hasil pembakaran batu bara, serta dapat juga 
digunakan untuk mengurangi keasaman dalam lingkungan 
dan air sebagai netralisasi limbah asam tambang dan air 
asam.
JASA PENGANGKUTAN DAN LAIN-LAIN
Semen Baturaja melalui PT Baturaja Multi Usaha (BMU) 
yang merupakan entitas anak Perusahaan, melaksanakan 
kegiatan usaha di bidang transportasi semen zak, curah, 
dan bigbag. Selain menjadi transportir semen, BMU juga 
menjadi transportir bahan-bahan material seperti, klinker 
dalam negeri, pozzolan, gypsum, dan lateritic ores, serta 
limbah bahan berbahaya dan beracun. Di samping itu, BMU 
juga bergerak sebagai distributor Semen Baturaja dengan 
menjual semen jenis zak, curah, dan bigbag.
CLINKER PRODUCTS
Clinker is an essential raw material for the production of 
cement. The process of making clinker involves burning a 
mixture of raw materials such as limestone, clay, iron sand, 
and silica sand. The Company operates two production 
centers for clinker, which are the Baturaja I Plant and the 
Baturaja II Plant, both located in Baturaja, Indonesia. The 
Baturaja I Plant has a capacity of 1,200,000 tons per year, 
while the Baturaja II Plant has a capacity of 1,500,000 tons 
per year.
WHITE CLAY
White Clay is a derivative produced by Semen Baturaja. 
The raw material for white clay is obtained from the mining 
process in the Company's area, which is then milled and dried. 
White Clay has many uses, one of which is as a material in the 
ceramics and NPK fertilizer production processes. 
GROUND LIMESTONE
Ground Limestone is a byproduct of SMBR used in Steam 
Power Plants (PLTU) to reduce sulfur dioxide (SOx) emissions 
from coal combustion, and it can also be used to decrease 
acidity in the environment and water as a neutralizer of acid 
mine waste and acid water.
FREIGHT SERVICES AND OTHERS
PT Baturaja Multi Usaha (BMU), a subsidiary of Semen 
Baturaja, is engaged in cement transportation services 
through cement sacks, bulk, and big bags. In addition 
to cement, BMU also transports other materials such as 
domestic clinker, pozzolan, gypsum, and lateritic ores, as 
well as hazardous and toxic waste materials. BMU also acts as 
a distributor of Semen Baturaja by selling cement in various 
types of sacks, bulk, and bigbags.

ABILITY TO PAY DEBT
The Company’s ability to meet all obligations, both short-
term and long-term, is measured through the liquidity ratio 
and solvency ratio. The liquidity ratio is used to measure the 
Company’s ability to pay off short-term obligations, while 
the solvency ratio is used to measure the Company’s ability 
to fulfill all of its obligations, including both short-term and 
long-term obligations. The Company is highly committed 
to fulfilling creditors’ rights by making timely payments of 
principal and interest.
ABILITY TO PAY SHORT-TERM DEBT BASED ON 
LIQUIDITY RATIO
The Company uses the liquidity ratio to determine its ability 
to fulfill short-term obligations that will mature. The liquidity 
ratio is measured based on the current ratio and cash ratio. 
The current ratio compares the Company’s current assets 
with its current liabilities, while the cash ratio compares

KEBIJAKAN MANAJEMEN ATAS STRUKTUR 
MODAL
Struktur modal merupakan perimbangan antara penggunaan 
modal sendiri dengan pinjaman/utang, yang terdiri dari utang

dengan minimum biaya penggunaan dana Weighted Average

nilai saham Perusahaan, tetapi tidak berarti mengakibatkan 
peningkatan laba bersih per saham (earning per share/
EPS). Leverage yang besar tidak hanya dapat meningkatkan 
EPS tetapi juga meningkatkan risiko. Dengan demikian, 
Perusahaan menetapkan kebijakan struktur permodalan 
sebagai upaya dalam memaksimalkan nilai bagi pemegang 
saham dan pemangku kepentingan.
Dalam implementasinya, berdasarkan Undang-Undang No. 
40 Tahun 2007 tentang Perusahaan Terbatas, Perusahaan 
mengalokasikan 
setidaknya 
20% 
dari 
modal 
saham 
diterbitkan dan dibayar penuh ke dalam dana cadangan 
yang tidak boleh didistribusikan. Persyaratan permodalan 
eksternal tersebut telah dipertimbangkan oleh Perusahaan 
serta telah diputuskan pada Rapat Umum Pemegang Saham 
(RUPS).
Selain itu, Perusahaan mengelola dan menyesuaikan struktur 
permodalannya berdasarkan perubahan kondisi ekonomi. 
Perusahaan dapat menyesuaikan pembayaran dividen 
kepada pemegang saham, menerbitkan saham baru, atau

MANAGEMENT POLICY ON CAPITAL STRUCTURE
The capital structure represents a balance between the use 
of equity and loans/debt, which includes short-term and long-
term debt. Capital structure with the minimum cost of using 
Weighted Average Cost of Capital (WACC) which increases 
the value of the Company’s shares, but does not necessarily 
increase net earnings per share (EPS). A high leverage can 
lead to an increase in EPS, but it also increases the risk. 
Therefore, the Company aims to determine an optimal capital 
structure policy that maximizes the value of the Company for 
stakeholders and shareholders.
In its implementation, based on Law No. 40 of 2007 on Limited 
Liability Companies, the Company allocates at least 20% of 
issued and fully paid-up share capital to a reserve fund that 
cannot be distributed. The Company has taken into account 
the external capital requirements and has made decisions at 
the General Meeting of Shareholders (GMS).
In addition, the Company manages and adjusts its capital 
structure in response to changes in economic conditions. 
The Company can adjust dividend payments to shareholders, 
issue new shares, or seek funding through loans.
Furthermore, the Company tracks its capital by dividing total 
interest-bearing loans by total equity attributable to owners 
of the parent entity through the gearing ratio. The Company’s 
policy is to maintain gearing ratios within the range of leading 
companies in Indonesia to ensure access to funding at a 
reasonable cost. Components included in total interest-
bearing loans include short-term bank loans, long-term bank 
loans, loans to the Government of the Republic of Indonesia, 
and finance lease liabilities.

HASIL ASSESSMENT TERHADAP HAL-HAL YANG 
MEMPENGARUHI KINERJA PERUSAHAAN
Melihat kinerja Perusahaan pada tahun 2024, manajemen 
Perusahaan menilai bahwa Perusahaan akan mampu 
melanjutkan kelangsungan usahanya dan berkeyakinan 
Perusahaan memilki sumber daya yang cukup untuk 
melanjutkan usahanya di masa mendatang. Program 
efisiensi SMBRGO45 yang telah dimulai sejak tahun 2020 
tetap dilakukan seperti mengoptimalkan dan menyesuaikan 
stamformasi kereta api, pengoptimalan pemanfaatan 
sludge oil, Spent Bleaching Earth (SBE), kantong pecah, oli 
bekas, majun bekas dan bad stock dari industri lain sebagai 
bahan bakar alternatif, penurunan faktor terak, sentralisasi 
produksi semen di Baturaja 2, melakukan tender bersama 
barang, jasa, bahan baku, bahan bakar serta jasa angkutan 
dan jasa umum lainnya. Perusahaan turut melakukan 
pelunasan dipercepat untuk menurunkan beban bunga 
sebagai langkah cost leadership di bidang finansial. Untuk 
meningkatkan pendapatan lainnya, Perusahaan tetap terus 
mengoptimalkan penjualan white clay dan ground limestone.  
DAMPAK PADA PERUSAHAAN
Penerapan inisiatif strategis pada tahun 2024 telah 
memberikan dampak positif bagi Perusahaan, di mana 
terlihat dari kinerja Perusahaan hingga akhir 2024 tetap 
menunjukkan pertumbuhan positif, di mana penjualan 
semen mencapai angka 2,23 juta ton atau tumbuh 3,43% 
terhadap periode yang sama tahun sebelumnya. Pencapaian 
tersebut berdampak terhadap meningkatnya pendapatan 
usaha yang mencapai 2,48% dari periode tahun lalu atau 
mencapai Rp2,09 triliun. Perolehan laba bersih Perusahaan 
positif di angka Rp129,25 miliar. Perusahaan juga mampu 
mempertahankan posisi market leader di wilayah Sumatera 
Selatan sebesar 33%.
ASSESSMENT RESULTS ON MATTERS 
IMPACTING COMPANY PERFORMANCE
Based on the Company's performance in 2024, the Company 
management assesses that the Company would be able 
to maintain its business continuity, and have sufficient 
resources to continue its operations in the future. The 
Company will continue its SMBRGO45 efficiency program, 
which began in 2020 and involves optimizing and adjusting 
train stamformation, utilizing alternative fuels such as sludge 
oil, Spent Bleaching Earth (SBE), broken bags, used oil, used 
waste, and bad stock from other industries, reducing the 
clinker factor, centralizing cement production in Baturaja 2 
Plant, joint tender for goods, services, raw materials, fuel, 
and transportation services, as well as other public services. 
The Company is also carrying out accelerated repayment to 
reduce interest expenses as a cost leadership step in the 
financial sector. To increase other income, the Company will 
continue to optimize revenues from white clay and ground 
limestone.
IMPACT ON THE COMPANY
The implementation of strategic initiatives in 2024 positively 
impacted the Company, as seen from the Company’s 
performance by the end of 2024 which continued to show 
positive growth, where cement sales reached 2.23 million 
tons or grew 3.43% compared to the same period the 
previous year. This achievement impacted on increasing 
business revenues which reached 2.48% from the previous 
year or reached Rp2.09 trillion. The Company’s net profit 
was positive at Rp129.25 billion. The Company managed to 
maintain its market leader position in the South Sumatera 
region by 33% as well.
