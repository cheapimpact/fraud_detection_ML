Management’s Review and Analysis
1. Tinjauan Operasi per Segmen Operasi
Pada tahun 2024, Perseroan menjalankan 1 segmen 
operasi yaitu usaha kabel. 
Perseroan memproduksi kabel tegangan rendah, 
building wire, kabel kontrol dan kabel instrumen, 
serta kabel tegangan menengah. Untuk menghasilkan 
kabel-kabel tersebut, Perseroan mendayagunakan 
kapasitas terpasang mesin-mesin produksi kabel dan 
menjalankan beberapa proses produksi.
Proses produksi yang dilakukan oleh Perseroan secara 
umum terdiri dari 8 proses, yaitu:
1. 
Penarikan bahan baku utama (tembaga dan 
aluminium) agar ukurannya menjadi lebih kecil. 
2. 
Menggabungkan beberapa penghantar.
3. 
Memberikan bahan isolasi pada penghantar. 
4. 
Memasang bahan pelindung pada suatu kabel 
jenis tertentu. 
5. 
Menggabungkan beberapa kabel.
6. 
Memberikan isolasi pada kabel jenis tertentu. 
7. 
Pemberian pelindung kabel seperti steel tape atau 
steel wire.
8. 
Memberikan isolasi luar pada kabel jenis tertentu.
Penjualan neto Perseroan tahun 2024 adalah Rp 1.988,6 
miliar dengan laba bruto sebesar Rp 138,1 miliar. 
Sebagai perbandingan, penjualan neto Perseroan 
tahun 2023 adalah Rp 1.648,5 miliar dengan laba bruto 
sebesar Rp 90,1 miliar. 
Pemakaian tembaga meningkat dari 7.570 metrik ton 
di tahun 2023 menjadi 9.488 metrik ton di tahun 2024 
atau naik 25,34%. Pemakaian aluminium mengalami 
penurunan dari 4.077 metrik ton di tahun 2023 menjadi 
2.110 metrik ton di tahun 2024 atau turun 48,25%.
Harga rata-rata tembaga selama tahun 2024 adalah 
sebesar USD 9.298 per metrik ton, naik sebesar 9,61% 
dibandingkan dengan tahun 2023 yang sebesar USD 
8.483 per metrik ton. Harga rata-rata aluminium selama 
tahun 2024 adalah sebesar USD 2.725 per metrik ton, 
naik sebesar 21,00% dibandingkan dengan tahun 2023 
yang sebesar USD 2.252 per metrik ton.
Produksi kabel Perseroan mengalami peningkatan di 
tahun 2024 dibandingkan dengan tahun 2023. Pada 
tahun 2024, Perseroan menggunakan 9.488,3 metrik ton 
tembaga atau 85,26% dari target dan 2.109,8 metrik ton 
aluminium atau 66,41% dari target.
1. Operational Review per Business Segment     
In 2024, the Company was engaged in 1 business segment 
which was cable business.
The Company manufactures low voltage cable, building 
wire, control cable and instrument cable, as well as 
medium voltage cable. To produce these cable types, the 
Company utilizes production machines with nameplate 
capacity and implements several stages of production.
The manufacturing process generally consists of eight 
stages:
1. 
Drawing the main material (copper and aluminium) 
to reduce the size.
2. 
Combining several conduits.
3. 
Fitting isolation on conduits.
4. 
Applying protector on certain cables.
5. 
Combining cables.
6. 
Fitting isolation on certain cables.
7. 
Applying protector on cable such as steel tape or 
steel wire.
8. 
Fitting external isolation on certain cables.
The Company’s sales figure in 2024 was Rp 1,988.6 billion, 
with a gross profit of Rp 138.1 billion. For comparison, the 
Company’s net sales in 2023 stood at Rp 1,648.5 billion, 
with a gross profit of Rp 90.1 billion. 
Copper consumption rose from 7,570 metric tons in 
2023 to 9,488 metric tons in 2024, an increase of 25.34%. 
Aluminum consumption fell by 48.25% from 4,077 metric 
tons in 2023 to 2,110 metric tons in 2024.
Copper prices in 2024 averaged USD 9,298 per metric 
ton, up 9.61% from the 2023 average of USD 8,483 per 
metric ton. Aluminum averaged USD 2,725 per metric ton 
in 2024, 21.00% higher in comparison to USD 2,252 per 
metric ton in 2023.
The Company’s cable production grew in 2024 compared 
to 2023. In 2024, the Company used 9,488.3 metric tons of 
copper, or 85.26% of the target, and 2,109.8 metric tons of 
aluminum, or 66.41% of the target.

lancar berdampak pada meningkatnya likuiditas dan 
memperkuat kemampuan operasional Perseroan.

tahun 2023 menjadi Rp 426,8 miliar di akhir tahun 
2024. Meningkatnya total liabilitas jangka pendek ini

liabilitas jangka panjang tidak berdampak signifikan 
terhadap total liabilitas Perseroan.

Statement of Financial Position
Total assets of the Company at the end of 2024 were valued 
at Rp 1.9 trillion, an increase of 18.39% in comparison to 
that of the previous year. This increase was caused by the 
rise of current assets by Rp 155.5 billion and non current 
assets by Rp 138.2 billion. The change in total assets had 
a positive impact on the Company’s financial position.
Current assets increased from IDR 554.8 billion in 2023 
to IDR 710.3 billion in 2024 or an increase of 28.04%, 
mainly due to the increase in trade receivables. Trade 
receivables increased from Rp 311.8 billion in 2023 to Rp 
494.3 billion in 2024. The increase in total current assets 
has positively impacted liquidity and enhanced the 
Company’s operational capabilities.
There was an increase of 13.25% in non-current assets 
from Rp 1,042.7 billion at the end of 2023 to Rp 1,180.8 
billion at the end of 2024, which was caused by a 13.25% 
or Rp 138.2 billion rose in fixed assets. The change to this 
total non-current assets had a positive impact on the 
Company’s total assets.
Total liabilities were up by Rp 136.7  billion, or 41.07%, 
when compared to the previous year. This rise was mainly 
due to an increase of Rp 134.4 billion in current liabilities. 
The change in the total liabilities resulted in an incline of 
the ratio of total liabilities to total equity, from 26.31% to 
33.01%.
Total current liabilities rose by 43.08% from Rp 312.1 
billion in 2023 to Rp 446.5 billion in 2024. This was mainly 
due to a rise in trade payables. Trade payables increased 
by 44.95%, from Rp 294.4 billion in 2023 to Rp 426.8 billion 
in 2024. The increase in total current liabilities had an 
impact on the decline in the current ratio.
Non-current liabilities comprise deferred tax liabilities 
and post-employment benefits. As of the end of 2024, 
total non-current liabilities stood at Rp 22.9 billion, 
representing an increase of Rp 2.2 billion, or  10.69% from 
the end of 2023. This change in non-current liabilities did 
not significantly impact the total liabilities.
