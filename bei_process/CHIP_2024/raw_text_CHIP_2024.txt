ANALISA EKONOMI DAN INDUSTRI
ECONOMIC AND INDUSTRIAL ANALYSIS
Pada 
tahun 
2024, 
perkembangan 
geopolitik 
dan 
perekonomian global memberikan dampak signifikan 
terhadap kebijakan moneter dan dinamika pasar 
keuangan internasional. Penurunan inflasi global pada 
triwulan III 2024 mendorong bank sentral, termasuk 
The Federal Reserve (The Fed), untuk menurunkan suku 
bunga, meski ketegangan geopolitik dan ketidakpastian 
pasar global masih tinggi. Fragmentasi perdagangan 
global juga memperlambat pertumbuhan ekonomi dunia 
yang diproyeksikan hanya 3,2%.
Sebaliknya, ekonomi Indonesia menunjukkan ketahanan 
yang kuat, dengan proyeksi pertumbuhan ekonomi 
4,7%-5,5% pada 2024. Pendorong utama pertumbuhan 
ini adalah konsumsi rumah tangga, investasi terkendali, 
dan Proyek Strategis Nasional (PSN). Inflasi nasional dapat 
ditekan dalam rentang 2,5±1%, dengan Indeks Harga 
Konsumen (IHK) yang terjaga stabil pada 1,57% YoY pada 
Desember 2024.
Pertumbuhan ekonomi Indonesia juga didukung oleh 
sektor-sektor 
utama, 
seperti 
industri 
pengolahan, 
pertanian, perdagangan, konstruksi, serta informasi dan 
komunikasi. Sektor informasi dan komunikasi mengalami 
kenaikan signifikan sebesar 6,86%, dipengaruhi oleh 
penguatan infrastruktur digital, peningkatan ekonomi 
digital, dan inovasi yang terus berkembang. Pemerintah 
juga berperan penting dalam mendukung perkembangan 
sektor ini melalui kebijakan dan Proyek Strategis Nasional 
(PSN).
In 2024, geopolitical and global economic developments 
have a significant impact on monetary policy and 
international financial market dynamics. The decline 
in global inflation in the third quarter of 2024 pushed 
central banks, including The Federal Reserve (The Fed), 
to lower interest rates, although geopolitical tensions 
and global market uncertainty are still high. Global trade 
fragmentation also slows down the projected growth of 
the world economy by only 3.2%.
On the other hand, Indonesia’s economy shows strong 
resilience, with a projected economic growth of 4,7%-
5,5% in 2024. The main drivers of this growth are 
household consumption, controlled investment, and 
National Strategic Projects (PSN). National inflation can 
be suppressed in the range of 2.5±1%, with the Consumer 
Price Index (IHK) which is maintained stable at 1,57% YoY 
in December 2024.
Indonesia’s economic growth is also supported by major 
sectors, such as the processing industry, agriculture, trade, 
construction, as well as information and communication. 
The information and communication sector experienced 
a significant increase of 6.86%, influenced by the 
strengthening of digital infrastructure, the improvement 
of the digital economy, and innovation that continues 
to grow. The government also plays an important role 
in supporting the development of this sector through 
policies and National Strategic Projects (PSN).

dikarenakan peningkatan aset tetap sebesar 42,45%.
Total Assets
The Company’s total assets increased by 4.82%, equivalent 
to Rp5.49 billion, from Rp113.91 billion in 2023 to Rp119.40 
billion in 2024. This condition was driven by an increase in 
non-current assets.
Total Current Assets
The Company recorded current assets of Rp77.87 billion 
in 2024. This amount decreased by 6.72% or equivalent to 
Rp5.61 billion compared to the previous year, which was 
recorded at Rp83.49 billion. This change was influenced by 
a 50.75% decline in cash and bank balances.

In 2024, the Company’s non-current assets amounted to 
Rp41.52 billion, an increase of 36.47% or equivalent to 
Rp11.10 billion compared to the previous year, which was 
recorded at Rp30.43 billion. This increase was mainly due 
to a 42.45% rise in fixed assets.
