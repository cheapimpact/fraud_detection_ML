TINJAUAN OPERASIONAL
OPERATIONAL REVIEW
The global economy in 2023 was characterized by a 
slowdown in the momentum of recovery. The deceleration 
in growth rates was evident, particularly in the third 
quarter, with most of it attributed to complex external 
factors, including tight financial conditions, sluggish 
trade growth, reduced optimism among businesses 
and consumers, and heightened geopolitical tensions 
stemming from the Russia-Ukraine conflict, coupled with 
the ongoing Palestine-Israel conflict. Additionally, the 
impact of tight monetary policies and extreme weather 
conditions also played significant roles.
Economic growth in several advanced economies 
remained weak. The United States achieved a growth 
rate of 3.3%, China 5.2%, and Japan experienced a 
contraction of 0.4% year-on-year in the fourth quarter of 
2023. In response to these conditions, central banks in 
most countries opted to maintain their interest rates in 
line with gradual inflation easing. Meanwhile, the energy 
commodities market showed a positive trend, although 
lower compared to 2022. Agricultural commodity prices 
exhibited an increasing trend, base metal prices varied, 
and precious metal values strengthened.
In contrast to the global economic slowdown, Indonesia 
demonstrated reasonable growth in line with targets. 
According to data from the Central Statistics Agency, 
Indonesia’s economy grew by 5.05% in 2023. This growth 
was partly due to a decrease in inflation rates and driven 
by strong domestic demand. Household consumption 
grew by 4.82%, supported by increasing mobility, stable 
purchasing power, and sustained consumer confidence 
following the COVID-19 pandemic.
In addition to geopolitical conflicts, the world also faced 
challenges of climate crisis. Environmental changes 
directly impacted the economy, particularly in the spike of 
rice prices by more than 20% due to prolonged droughts 
and reduced production caused by El Nino.
In the bottled drinking water (AMDK) sector, there are 
dynamics that need attention. Increased consumer 
demand, changing sustainability trends, and innovation 
in packaging are driving changes in this industry.

Perseroan 
menerapkan 
strategi 
pengembangan 
distribusi melalui PT Sentralsari Primasentosa (SPS), 
perusahaan afiliasi yang kemudian diakuisisi menjadi 
anak perusahaan pada akhir tahun 2023. SPS berhasil 
menambah 52 titik distribusi, meningkat dari 290 titik 
distribusi menjadi 342 titik distribusi pada akhir tahun 
2023. Perseroan juga terus memperluas jangkauan 
pemasaran ke seluruh Indonesia dengan menambah 
jumlah pabrik dan distribusi logistik, sehingga produk 
Perseroan dapat lebih dekat dengan konsumen. Pada 
tahun 2023, Perseroan telah berhasil menambah jumlah 
pabrik dari 28 pabrik menjadi 32 pabrik di seluruh 
Indonesia.
Di 
tahun 
2023, 
Perseroan 
mengusung 
program 
pemasaran dengan tema “Two Decades Of Innovation 
for Future Growth”, yang mencatat berbagai pencapaian 
baru. Tahun ini ditandai dengan peluncuran dua 
produk baru, yakni Cleo Platine Sparkling dan Cleo 
Galon Nusantara, yang ditujukan untuk memenuhi 
kebutuhan segmen pasar di Special Outlet dan Rumah 
Tangga. Langkah ini akan mengembangkan pemasaran 
Perseroan ke seluruh wilayah Indonesia. Penambahan 
fasilitas pabrik dan perluasan jaringan distribusi logistik 
menjadi salah satu strategi untuk memperluas kehadiran 
Tanobel, sehingga produk-produk Tanobel semakin 
mudah dijangkau oleh konsumen.
Efisiensi biaya operasional juga diperoleh dengan 
perbaikan proses dan otomatisasi proses produksi 
dengan tehnologi mesin baru, serta pengelolaan rantai 
suplai dan logistik yang lebih efisien dengan tetap 
menjaga kualitas produk yang dihasilkan.
Demi mendukung kegiatan pemasaran, Perseroan juga 
giat menggelar berbagai acara pemasaran seperti Cleo 
Festival Kuliner, Festival Cleo 20th di Surabaya, dan 
kampanye #LangkahMurni Cleo.
Ditopang oleh strategi yang efektif dan kerja keras dari 
seluruh pihak, pada tahun 2023, Perseroan berhasil 
mencatatkan penjualan bersih sebesar Rp2,09 triliun, 
atau naik sebesar 25% dibanding penjualan bersih tahun 
2022 sebesar Rp1,67 triliun, dimana segmen kemasan 
botol mencatat pertumbuhan sebesar 37% dengan 
realisasi penjualan sebesar Rp1,11 triliun, dan kemasan 
bukan botol mencatat realisasi pertumbuhan sebesar 
12,8% dengan realisasi penjualan sebesar Rp944 miliar. 
Dengan realisasi kinerja tersebut dan didukung dengan 
efisiensi biaya, Perseroan berhasil membukukan laba 
bersih pada tahun 2023 sebesar Rp324 miliar yang 
berarti menunjukkan kenaikan signifikan sebesar 66% 
dari laba bersih pada tahun 2022 yakni sebesar Rp195 
miliar.
The Company implemented a distribution development 
strategy through PT Sentralsari Primasentosa (SPS), 
an affiliated company which was later acquired as a 
subsidiary by the end of 2023. SPS successfully added 
52 distribution points, increasing from 290 distribution 
points to 342 distribution points by the end of 2023. 
The Company also continued to expand its marketing 
reach throughout Indonesia by increasing the number 
of factories and logistics distribution, allowing the 
Company’s products to be closer to consumers. In 2023, 
the Company successfully increased the number of 
factories from 28 to 32 across Indonesia.
In 2023, the Company launched a marketing program 
with the theme “Two Decades Of Innovation for Future 
Growth,” which recorded various new achievements. This 
year was marked by the launch of two new products, 
Cleo Platine Sparkling and Cleo Galon Nusantara, aimed 
at meeting the needs of market segments in Special 
Outlets and Households. This step will expand the 
Company’s marketing to all regions of Indonesia. The 
addition of factory facilities and the expansion of the 
logistics distribution network are among the strategies 
to expand Tanobel’s presence, making Tanobel products 
more accessible to consumers.
Operational cost efficiency was also achieved by 
improving 
processes 
and 
automating 
production 
processes with new machine technologies, as well as 
more efficient supply chain and logistics management 
while maintaining the quality of our products. 
To support marketing activities, the Company also 
actively organizes various marketing events such as the 
Cleo Culinary Festival, the Cleo 20th Festival in Surabaya, 
and the #LangkahMurni Cleo campaign.
Supported by effective strategies and hard work from all 
parties, in 2023, the Company successfully recorded net 
sales of Rp2.09 trillion, representing an increase of 25% 
compared to the net sales in 2022 of Rp1.67 trillion. The 
bottle packaging segment saw growth of 37%, with sales 
realization reaching Rp1.11 trillion, while the non-bottle 
packaging segment recorded growth of 12.8%, with 
sales realization reaching Rp944 billion.
With this performance realization and supported by cost 
efficiency measures, the Company achieved a net profit 
of Rp324 billion in 2023, indicating a significant increase 
of 66% from the net profit in 2022, which was Rp195 
billion.

REVENUES
The Company’s net sales in 2023 amounted to Rp2.09 
trillion, representing an increase of 24.85% compared 
to the net sales in 2022 of Rp1.67 trillion. The bottle 
segment contributed 53.17% to the net sales, while the 
non-bottle segment contributed 45.17%, and others 
contributed 1.66%. Sales in the bottle segment increased 
by 37.08% to Rp1.11 trillion. In the non-bottle segment, 
sales increased by 12.74% to Rp944 billion. Additionally, 
sales in the others segment increased by 33.47% to Rp34 
billion.
BOTTLE AND NON-BOTTLE PRODUCTS
The net sales in the bottle segment were mainly due 
to an increase in bottle sales, as evidenced by the rise 
in sales of mini bottle packaging that has been well-
received in the market. On the other hand, the increase in 
non-bottle sales was primarily driven by the rise in sales 
of packaged gallon AMDK products.
OTHER PRODUCTS 
The sales of other products achieved in 2023 amounted 
to Rp34 billion, indicating an increase compared to Rp26 
billion in 2022. This increase was attributed to the higher 
sales of other products conducted by the Company’s 
subsidiary entities.
EXPENSES 
The increase in cost of goods sold by 9%, which is lower 
than the increase in sales, is attributed to several factors. 
There was a decrease in the ratio of raw material costs 
to sales, as well as production cost efficiency due to 
increased production volume. Additionally, the increase 
in retail selling prices also contributed to this increase.

Perseroan, tetapi tidak meningkatkan laba bersih per
