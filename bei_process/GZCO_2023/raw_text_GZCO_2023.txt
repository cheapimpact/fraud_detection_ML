Operational Segment Analysis
The Company carries out business activities in developing, 
operating plantations, trading and processing palm oil 
and vegetable oil (crude palm oil) through its subsidiaries. 
Significant plantation areas for subsidiaries are located in 
the province of South Sumatra, and less so in the province 
of Central Kalimantan. No other business activities and 
significant assets outside the business activities and the 
two Provinces that are mentioned.
The company does not need to review operations by 
segments required for transparent information as long 
as it is operating within the scope of business activities, 
performance, and asset management as explained above.

Lahan TM per akhir tahun seluas 12.452 ha turun 248 ha,

Perkembangan hasil panen buah tandan segar (TBS), 
pasokan TBS dari luar dan jumlah TBS diproses selama 3 
(tiga) tahun terakhir sebagai berikut : 
Cultivated land is classified as "Immature Palms" or TBM, 
namely land with plants up to 4 years old, and "Productive 
palms” or TM, namely land with palms over 4 years old.
The total planted area as of the end of 2023 was 13,114 
Ha, up from 12,917 Ha or up 1.5% compared to the position 
at the end of last year due to the addition of 197 Ha of new 
plantings and 331 Ha of replanting (TM to TBM mutation).
TM's land area of 12,452 hectares at the end of the year 
decreased by 248 hectares, or an decrease of around 2% 
compared to the position at the end of last year which 
amounted to 12,700 hectares. This portion of TM land 
constitutes 98% of the total planted area.
This TM land area is 2,446 ha, around 20% of which are 
TM aged 9 years and under whose results are not yet fully 
commercial, and still need subsidies in operating cash 
flow.
The TM group aged 9 years and under decreased by 472 
Ha or decreased by 16% compared to the previous year. 
TM's land aged 10 years and over which supports the 
Company's cash flow, amounted to 10,005 Ha, or around 
76% of the Company's total plantations, an increase of 2%.
The immature area increased by 445 Ha due to replanting 
of 331 Ha and new plantings of 197 Ha and also due to 
charge in status from immature to mature of 83 Ha.
The increase in the status of the plantings to a more 
commercial direction, although the amount is not that 
significant, will have a positive impact on the Company's 
ability to generate cash.

Perkembangan hasil produksi minyak sawit mentah (CPO) 
dan kernel (PK) selama 3 (tiga) tahun terakhir sebagai 
berikut:
Fresh Fruit Bunches or FFB from own plantations, or 
nucleus estates in 2023 amounted to 208.937 tons net 
weight after sorting, an increase of 19.217 tons or an 
increase of around 10% compared to last year's harvest.
The yield ratio for the nucleus plantation harvest in 2023 
will increase to 16.78 tons per ha TM. up 12% compared to 
last year's harvest yield of 14.94 Tons per Ha TM
The increase in harvest yield was achieved as a result of 
efforts to effectively fertilize mature crops which have 
been implemented since last year. 
The supply of processed FFB came from the harvest of 
198.404 tons of nucleus plantations and from the supply 
(purchase) of FFB from plantations of 28.621 tons or a 
total of 227,025 tons
The amount of FFB processed in 2023 will increase by 
16,895 tonnes, or an increase of 8% compared to last year's 
realization. The increase in FFB supply also occurred in 
FFB supply from inti plantations.
The supply of FFB in third parties  in 2023, compared 
to that achieved last year, decreased by 5,447 tons, or 
decreased by 16%.
The harvested yield from the nucleus plantation sold and 
stock amounted to 10,533 tons, consisting of 10,017 tons 
of Fresh Fruit Bunches (net 9,816 tons) sold, mainly from 
the TSP nucleus plantation in Kalimantan which does not 
have a CPO processing plant there, with the remaining 515 
tons stock.
With the capacity of PKS SA 90 T/Hour, and assuming a 
normal effectiveness of 75%, then the PKS occupancy rate 
is still 60%.

Analisa biaya produksi per produk CPO dan Kernel dihitung 
berdasar metode relatif harga jual. Dalam penerapan 
metode ini, nilai penjualan TBS diperlakukan sebagai 
pengurang biaya produksi CPO dan Kernel. 
Setelah dikurangi nilai penjualan TBS, netto biaya produksi 
tahun 2023 sebesar Rp.533.28 miliar, didistribusi sebagai 
biaya produksi CPO sebesar Rp.487.35 miliar dan sebagai 
biaya produksi kernel sebesar Rp.45,93 miliar. 
Biaya produksi CPO per ton tahun 2023 sebesar Rp.9,69 
miliar naik 6% menjadi lebih tinggi dibanding realisasi 
tahun lalu, karena total biaya produksinya naik 10% 
sedangkan tonase hasil produksinya naik lebih kecil 
hanya sekitar 3%.
Biaya produksi kernel per Ton tahun 2023 sebesar Rp.4,36 
miliar turun 33% menjadi lebih rendah dibanding realisasi 
tahun lalu, karena total kenaikan hasil produksi yang 
dapat direalisasi sebesar 11% dapat direalisasi dengan 
penurunan total biaya produksi yang lebih besar yaitu 
sekitar 26%.
Analysis of production costs per CPO and Kernel product 
is calculated based on the selling price relative method. In 
applying this method, the sales value of FFB is treated as 
a deduction from the CPO and Kernel production costs.
After deducting the sales value of FFB, net production 
costs in 2023 amounted to IDR 533.28 billion, distributed 
as CPO production costs of IDR 487.35 billion and as 
kernel production costs of IDR 45.93 billion
The production cost of CPO per ton in 2023 amounted to 
Rp.9.69 billion, increasing by 6% compared to the previous 
year's realization. This increase is attributed to a 10% rise 
in total production costs, while the production volume 
increased by a smaller margin of only around 3%
The production cost of kernel per ton in 2023 amounted 
to Rp. 4.24 billion, a decrease of 35% compared to the 
previous year's realization. This decrease occurred 
because the total increase in achievable production yield 
was 11%, which was realized alongside a larger decrease 
in total production costs, approximately around 26%.

Laba (Rugi) kotor dan laba sebelum bunga dan pajak 
(LSBP) selama 3 (tiga) tahun terakhir sebagai berikut:
The company sells its products in the form of CPO and 
palm kernel to buyers at the general price that applies at 
the time of the transaction. To maximize sales results, the 
Company monitors developments in world CPO prices. 
It could happen, the Company withholds inventory not 
marketed Buyers because the CPO price prediction is 
trending up. Of course this is done by taking into account 
the strength of liquidity. The opposite condition can also 
occur, the Company releases its supplies to Buyers for 
liquidity reasons even though at that time the trend of world 
CPO prices was rising. All CPO and kernel sales are carried 
out in rupiah units and are based on delivery transactions 
to Buyers from the Company's tanks or bulking silo.
The sales value in 2023 amounted to Rp. 744.27 billion, 
increasing by approximately 34%. About 89% of the total 
sales, or Rp. 660.73 billion, was obtained from CPO sales. 
The increase in sales value was achieved due to an increase 
in the volume tonnage of CPO products sold.
Compared to the achievement of the previous year, CPO 
sales increased by Rp. 198,26 billion or 43% due to the 
increase in CPO tonnage sold by 20,736 tons or 54%, despite 
the decrease in achievable selling price by 6.9%.
Kernel sales decreased by Rp. 2.18 billion or 3%, despite 
the increase in kernel tonnage sold by 2,930 tons or 34%. 
However, the selling price decreased by 28.1%.
The sales of Fresh Fruit Bunches (TBS) decreased by Rp. 
6.53 billion or 22% due to the decrease in TBS tonnage sold 
by 2,924 tons or 22%, as well as a decrease in the selling 
price by 4.13%.

Work Plan & Realization
The company is classified as small-scale in the palm oil 
plantation and CPO business nationally. The total land 
managed by the company in 2023 was 13,114 hectares, which 
is less than 0.07% of the total national oil palm plantation 
area of around 17 million hectares (data from the Ministry of 
Agriculture, 2023). With such a scale, the company must base 
its annual work plan on its internal potential.
With a solid grasp of palm oil cultivation techniques and 
governance, management has developed the Company's 
annual performance plan. This plan begins with an inventory 
of inti palm oil plantation land, plasma land, and land owned by 
farmers around the factory. Management sets targets for fruit 
tonnage availability sourced from harvests in inti plantations, 
purchases from plasma plantations, and purchases from third 
party small holders.
Taking into account the quality and age of the plantings, the 
fruit yield is predicted, and targets for CPO production tonnage 
and CPO sales are established. A budget for the necessary 
activities is then formulated.
Furthermore, considering the forecasted CPO selling price, 
sales value targets are established. Then, by considering the 
budgeted costs, targets for profit/loss and the target cash 
surplus that can be generated (EBITDA) are determined.
The target capital expenditure investment for additional costs 
for immature plantings and for the acquisition/replacement 
of fixed assets and other investments is set to be funded as 
much as possible from internal sources.
The annual operational and financial performance plan 
or targets are established and periodically monitored for 
achievement by the Board of Directors and the Board of 
Commissioners.
The capital structure is a concern for management in financial 
management, prioritizing exploration and funding from 
internal sources. However, in certain situations, financing 
with borrowed funds cannot be avoided, along with all its 
consequences (interest, etc.). Therefore, the capital structure 
is not included in the annual target setting, due to the potential 
consequences from an avoidable external sources.
