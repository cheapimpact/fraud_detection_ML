In line with the ratification of the Indonesian 
Financial Reporting Standards Framework, the

affect the contents of each PSAK and ISAK.

	 The amendment affect only the presentation

amount or timing of recognition of any asset,

if covenants are complied with at the end of the 
reporting period, and introduce a definition of 
‘settlement’ to make clear that settlement refers 
to the transfer to the counterparty of cash, 
equity instruments, other assets or services.
