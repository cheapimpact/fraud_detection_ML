General Economic Overview  
Perekonomian 
Indonesia 
di 
tahun 
2024
berjalan penuh dinamika. Mengutip released
Bank 
Indonesia, 
pertumbuhan 
ekonomi
Indonesia pada tahun 2024 relatif stabil,
tumbuh diangka 5,03%. Kondisi mendasar
perekonomian ini berlanjut di tahun 2025.
Indonesia diperkirakan akan tumbuh 5,2%
dengan target inflasi yang terkendali pada
kisaran 2,5%.
Konflik geopolitik di Timur Tengah dan Ukraina,
kemudian terjadinya pergantian kepemimpinan
melalui proses politik di Indonesia dan di
negara lainnya seperti USA, serta masih
lemahnya 
pertumbuhan 
ekonomi 
global,
kondisi tersebut mewarnai dan mempengaruhi
pertumbuhan ekonomi Indonesia. Struktur
perekonomian yang resilient, masih bertumpu
signifikan 
pada 
pengeluaran 
masyarakat,
termasuk 
pengeluaran 
masyarakat 
kelas
menengah yang banyak mengkonsumsi produk
FMCG (Fast Moving Consumer Goods).
Pertumbuhan ekonomi global tahun 2024
tumbuh 
melambat 
dibanding 
tahun
sebelumnya. 
IMF 
memperkirakan
perekonomian dunia di tahun 2024 tumbuh
3,2%. Produk domestik bruto (PDB) Amerika
Serikat (AS) di tahun 2024 meningkat akibat
kebijakan inward looking Trump 2.0. Dinamika
kebijakan Trump 2.0 turut memicu volatilitas
pasar keuangan global, baik di pasar saham,
surat utang, maupun pasar nilai tukar, dengan
disertai outflow dari mayoritas pasar keuangan
emerging market, termasuk pasar keuangan
Indonesia. Di Negara maju Eropa terjadi
penurunan pertumbuhan, khususnya Jerman
dan 
Prancis 
karena 
penurunan 
sektor
manufaktur 
dan 
ekspor 
barang, 
serta
terjadinya ketidakstabilan politik dalam negeri
mereka. China tumbuh sekitar 4,7% dibawah
ekspektasi serta China menghadapi kebijakan
inward looking Trump 2.0.
Indonesia’s economy in 2024 is in dynamics
mode. Quoting released by Bank Indonesia,
Indonesia’s 
economic 
growth 
in 
2024 
is
relatively stable, growing by 5.03%. This basic
economic 
condition 
continues 
in 
2025.
Indonesia is expected to grow 5.2% with a
controlled inflation target of around 2.5%.
Geopolitical conflicts in the Middle East and
Ukraine, then the change of leadership through
the political process in Indonesia and in other
countries such as the USA, and the weak global
economic 
growth, 
those 
conditions 
have
influenced Indonesia’s economic growth. The
resilient 
economic 
structure 
still 
relies
significantly 
on 
community 
expenditure,
including the expenditure of the middle class
who consume a lot of FMCG (Fast Moving
Consumer Goods) products.
Global economic growth in 2024 grow slow
compared to the previous year. The IMF
estimates that the world economy in 2024 was
grow by 3.2%. The United States (US) gross
domestic product (GDP) in 2024 increased due
to Trump 2.0 inward-looking policy. The
dynamics of Trump 2.0 policy also triggered
volatility in the global financial market, both in
the stock market, bonds, and exchange rate
market, followed by outflows from the majority
of 
emerging 
market 
financial 
markets,
including the Indonesian financial market. In
Europe, the developed countries, there was a
decline in growth, especially Germany and
France due to a decline in the manufacturing
sector and exports of goods, as well as
domestic political instability. China grew by
approximately 4.7%, falling below expectations,
and is facing the inward-looking policies of
Trump 2.0.

packaging
