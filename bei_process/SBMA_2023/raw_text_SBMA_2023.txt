tahun 2022 yaitu sebesar Rp113 Miliar dari Rp103

Perseroan tahun 2023 sebesar Rp4,7 miliar. Penurunan 
signifikan ini telah dijelaskan pada bagian profitabilitas.
Perseroan kemudian melakukan mitigasi dan strategi 
untuk proyeksi keuangan di tahun 2024 diantaranya 
dengan menganalisis dengan metode root cause 
analysis. Diketahui bahwa penurunan ini disebabkan 
oleh biaya distribusi yang meningkat drastis akibat 
kenaikan BBM sehingga Perseroan mempertimbangkan 
kembali harga ongkos kirim kepada pelanggan. 
Untuk memitigasi kenaikan biaya pokok, Perseroan 
memang sudah merencanakan untuk pembaharuan 
utilitas pabrik agar lebih efisien salah satunya melalui 
pembelian unit Air Separation Plant (ASP) yang akan 
beroperasi di tahun 2024. Pada biaya pengangkutan 
Limbah B3, Perseroan juga dalam tahap kajian 
pemanfaatan LB3 agar limbah yang diangkut dapat 
dikelola mandiri dan menghasilkan nilai manfaat lebih.
Adapun target pendapatan Perseroan di tahun 
2024 adalah sebesar Rp136 miliar dengan laba 
yang ditargetkan 11.04% dari pendapatan atau 
sekitar Rp15 miliar dengan catatan Pabrik baru telah 
beroperasi sepenuhnya. Sampai laporan ini diterbitkan, 
Perseroan belum memiliki rencana perubahan struktur 
permodalan. 
In 2023, the Company’s sales exceeded the previous 
year’s sales, which was Rp 113 billion from previously 
Rp103 billion; however, the Company’s profit decreased 
from the target of 10.19% from the revenue or Rp12.5 
billion. In 2023, the Company’s profit realization 
amounted to Rp4.7 billion. This significant decline 
has been explained in the profitability section.
The Company then carried out mitigation and 
strategies for financial projections in 2024, including 
analyzing the root cause using the root cause analysis 
method. The Company discovered that this decline 
was caused by distribution costs that increased 
dramatically due to the increase in fuel, so the 
Company reconsidered the price of shipping costs 
to customers. To mitigate the increase in cost, the 
Company has planned to renew the factory utilities 
to be more efficient, one of which is through the 
purchase of an air separation plant (“ASP”) unit, 
which will be operational in 2024. Regarding the 
cost of transporting hazardous waste, the Company 
is also in the stage of studying the utilization of LB3 
so that the transported waste can be managed 
independently and produce more value.
The Company’s revenue target in 2024 is Rp 136 
billion, with a profit target of 11.04% of the revenue or 
around Rp 15 billion, provided that the new factory 
is fully operational. Until this report was published, 
the Company had no plans to change its capital 
structure.

Perusahaan yang Baik

pengelolaan Perseroan berlandaskan kepatuhan

the Company’s organizational mechanisms and 
structures to improve the Company’s performance 
in running a sustainable business.”

strengthening good governance in all aspects. By

to navigate the risks and challenges encountered 
both now and in the future. This commitment serves 
an essential role in maintaining investor, potential 
investor, customer, and general public confidence 
and optimizing the Company’s value for shareholders 
and other stakeholders over the long term.

For the company, good corporate governance 
(GCG) is the principle that constitutes the process 
and mechanism of the company’s management 
based on compliance with the applicable laws 
and regulations and is a reflection of an ethical 
business world. The Company is fully committed to 
implementing GCG principles to create sustainable 
benefits for shareholders, society in general, and other 
stakeholders (employees, customers, regulators, 
partners/suppliers, etc.) both in the short and long 
term.
