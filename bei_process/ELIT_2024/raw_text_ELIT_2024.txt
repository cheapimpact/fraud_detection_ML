TINJAUAN EKONOMI DAN INDUSTRI
ECONOMIC AND INDUSTRIAL OVERVIEW
Situasi 
ekonomi 
global 
masih 
dipenuhi 
dengan 
ketidakpastian tinggi akibat geopolitik, proteksionisme, 
dan perlambatan ekonomi Tiongkok. Meskipun tren 
inflasi global menunjukkan penurunan, beberapa negara 
masih mencatatkan kenaikan inflasi.  Terpilih kembalinya 
Donald Trump sebagai Presiden AS memberikan sentimen 
positif pada pasar saham AS, namun meningkatkan 
risiko bagi negara berkembang akibat potensi kebijakan 
proteksionisme dan perang dagang. Ekspektasi pasar 
tentang kondisi high for longer yang sempat mereda 
kembali muncul akibat kebijakan Trump yang dinilai 
akan berdampak pada progress penurunan inflasi di AS. 
Tensi geopolitik dan dinamika politik berbagai negara 
juga menambah risiko bagi stabilitas ekonomi global. 
Potensi perang dagang dan peningkatan proteksionisme 
memberikan dampak negatif bagi arus investasi dan 
perdagangan global. 
IMF memprediksi pertumbuhan ekonomi global stagnan 
di 3,2% pada 2024 dan 2025, dengan tekanan utama 
dari perubahan iklim, tensi geopolitik, dan utang negara 
berkembang. Sementara itu, ekonomi Indonesia tetap 
tangguh dengan pertumbuhan 5,03% hingga triwulan 
III 2024, didorong oleh konsumsi domestik, peningkatan 
investasi, dan ekspor yang membaik.
Pemerintah berhasil menjaga stabilitas ekonomi melalui 
pengendalian inflasi, optimalisasi belanja APBN, dan 
dukungan terhadap daya beli masyarakat. Langkah 
strategis yang dilakukan seperti penurunan suku bunga 
acuan dan penguatan program perlindungan sosial 
menjadi kunci dalam momentum pertumbuhan ini. 
Ekonomi digital Indonesia mengalami pertumbuhan dua 
digit, dengan kontribusi 13% terhadap PDB nasional. 
Berdasarkan laporan e-Conomy SEA 2024 dari Google, 
Temasek, dan Bain & Company, nilai ekonomi digital 
Indonesia diperkirakan mencapai USD 90 miliar (Rp 1.440 
triliun) pada akhir 2024. Industri AI dan gaming menjadi 
sektor 
dengan 
pertumbuhan 
tercepat, 
sementara 
ekonomi digital Indonesia tetap menjadi yang terbesar di 
Asia Tenggara. Dengan tren ini, Gross Merchandise Value 
(GMV) Indonesia diproyeksikan mencapai USD 200-360 
miliar pada 2030.
The global economic situation is still filled with high 
uncertainty due to geopolitics, protectionism, and China’s 
economic slowdown. Although global inflation has started 
to decline, some countries are still experiencing increases. 
The re-election of Donald Trump as US President gave 
positive sentiment to the US stock market, but increased 
risks  for  emerging  economies  due  to  potential 
protectionist policies and trade wars. Market expectation 
of high for longer interest rate condition that had subsided 
re-emerged due to Trump’s policy which are considered 
to impact the progress of inflation decline in the US. 
Geopolitical tension and political dynamics of various 
countries also add risks to global economic stability. 
Potential trade wars and increased protectionism have a 
negative impact on global investment and trade flows. 
The IMF predicts global economic growth to stagnate at 
3.2% in 2024 and 2025, with major pressures from climate 
change, geopolitical tensions, and emerging market debt. 
Meanwhile, Indonesia’s economy remains resilient with 
growth of 5.03% as of the third quarter of 2024, driven 
by domestic consumption, increased investment, and 
improved exports.
The government managed to maintain economic stability 
through inflation control, optimization of state budget 
spending, and support for people’s purchasing power. 
Strategic measures such as lowering the benchmark 
interest rate and strengthening social protection programs 
are key to this growth momentum. 
Indonesia’s digital economy experienced double-digit 
growth, contributing 13% to national GDP. Based on the 
e-Conomy SEA 2024 report from Google, Temasek, and 
Bain & Company, the value of Indonesia’s digital economy 
is estimated to reach USD 90 billion (IDR 1,440 trillion) by 
the end of 2024. The AI and gaming industries are the 
fastest growing sectors, while Indonesia’s digital economy 
remains the largest in Southeast Asia. With this trend, 
Indonesia’s Gross Merchandise Value (GMV) is projected to 
reach USD 200-360 billion by 2030.

managing digital governance in Indonesia.

INNOVATION AND PRODUCT DEVELOPMENT [F.26]
To meet customer needs and expectations while ensuring 
business sustainability, the Company continuously drives 
innovation and development across both products and 
services. Elitery is highly committed to developing and 
delivering high-quality products to enhance customer 
satisfaction.
In 2024, the Company focused its innovation efforts 
on two main areas. First, the development of Artificial 
Intelligence (AI)-based applications designed to support 
both internal and external needs. These applications were 
developed with careful consideration of ongoing business 
processes, current industry trends, and customer 
expectations. The goal is to create solutions that not only 
enhance operational efficiency but also enable Elitery to 
remain agile in responding to market dynamics.
Second, the advancement of Elitery’s proprietary 
products, which are widely utilized within the organization 
and offered as external solutions to clients. This initiative 
includes improvements in internal management systems, 
operational efficiency, and service quality. Through this 
approach, Elitery empowers its business partners to 
concentrate more on their core business development.
