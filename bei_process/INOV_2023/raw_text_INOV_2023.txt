OPERATION REVIEW PER OPERATING SEGMENT 
The Company recorded Sales in 2023 of IDR 600.2

which was IDR 691.5 billion. Sales for each business 
segment can be seen in the following presentaion:

is produced in 3 (three) types, namely Hollow 
Conjugate Fiber, Special Function Fiber and Solid 
Fiber. Re-PSF is produced with the main material 
PET plastic bottle waste which is cleaned and 
crushed into flakes. Then the flakes are processed

factories with annual production capacities of

decreased by 9.31% from the sales of Re-PSF in 
2022 of IDR 486.2 billion. Re-PSF’s sales contributed 
73.47% in 2023 and 70.31% in 2022
2. Non Woven
Non-Woven is a derivative product of the 
Company, which is produced using the main 
material Re-PSF. The Company produces various 
types of Non-Woven products including Padding, 
Needle Punch, Geotextile, and Thermal Bonding.
