Global And National Economic Review

Seperti diketahui, Perekonomian global mengalami 
tantangan dari perlambatan ekonomi hingga ancaman 
akan tumbuh stagnan di tahun Depan. Menurut, 
Lembaga Keuangan Internasional atau Dana Moneter 
Internasional (IMF) per Oktober 2024, outlook 
perekonomian global untuk 2024 diproyeksikan akan 
tumbuh pada level 3,2 persen dan tumbuh stagnan di 
level 3,2 persen pada 2025. Bagi negara-negara maju, 
pertumbuhan ekonomi tahun 2025 diproyeksikan

We have high expectations for the government to be 
more concentrated on encouraging the acceleration of 
inclusive and sustainable economic growth amidst the 
uncertainty of the world economy. As is known, the 
global economy is experiencing challenges from 
economic slowdown to the threat of stagnant growth in 
the next year. According to the International Financial 
Institution or International Monetary Fund (IMF) as of 
October 2024, the global economic outlook for 2024 is 
projected to grow at a level of 3.2 percent and grow 
stagnant at a level of 3.2 percent in 2025. For developed 
countries, economic growth in 2025 is projected to be 
at a level of 1.8 percent, while developing countries are 
predicted to experience growth of 4.2 percent.

dibandingkan tahun 2023 yang mencapai 5,05%. 
Namun meskipun demikian, IMF mencatat Indonesia 
berada posisi ke 7 ekonomi terbesar di dunia 
berdasarkan Produk Domestik Bruto (PDB) yang 
disesuaikan dengan paritas daya beli (PPP) pada tahun 
2024. 
The impact of the global economy will always have an 
impact on the country's economy. In 2024 Indonesia's 
economic growth also experienced a slight slowdown 
compared to the achievements of the previous year. 
The country's economic growth in 2024 is 5.03%, lower 
than in 2023 which reached 5.05%. However, despite 
this, the IMF recorded Indonesia as the 7th largest 
economy in the world based on Gross Domestic 
Product (GDP) adjusted for purchasing power parity 
(PPP) in 2024.

Kemampuan Membayar Utang 
Debt Service Ability 
Kemampuan Perseroan untuk memenuhi seluruh 
kewajiban yang telah jatuh tempo dapat diukur dengan 
menggunakan perhitungan rasio-rasio keuangan, 
seperti rasio likuiditas dan rasio solvabilitas. Rasio 
likuiditas mengukur kemampuan dalam memenuhi 
kewajiban jangka pendek, sedangkan rasio solvabilitas 
mengukur kemampuan dalam memenuhi kewajiban 
jangka pendek dan panjang. Untuk mengukur rasio 
solvabilitas, Perseroan menggunakan instrumen rasio 
utang terhadap ekuitas (Debt to Equity Ratio/DER) dan 
rasio utang terhadap aset (Debt to Asset Ratio/DAR). 
Pada tahun 2024, DER Perseroan tercatat sebesar 0,15x 
dibandingkan 
tahun 2023 yang tercatat 0,13x, 
sedangkan DAR tahun 2024 Perseroan tercatat sebesar 
0,13x dibandingkan pada tahun 2023 sebesar 0,12x. 
The Company's ability to meet all obligations that have 
matured can be measured by using the calculation of 
financial ratios, such as liquidity ratio and solvency ratio. 
The liquidity ratio measures the ability to meet short-
term obligations, while the solvency ratio measures the 
ability to meet both short-term and long-term 
obligations. To measure the solvency ratio, the 
Company uses the Debt-to-Equity Ratio (DER) and Debt 
to Asset Ratio (DAR) instruments. In 2024, the 
Company's DER was recorded at 0,15x compared to 
2023 which was recorded at 0,13x, while the Company's 
DAR in 2024 was recorded at 0,13x compared to 0,12x 
in 2023.
