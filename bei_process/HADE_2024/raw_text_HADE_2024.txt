SEGMEN JASA PENGISIAN LPG
LPG Filling Service Segment
Jasa pengisian LPG merupakan jasa Fill­
ing Fee dari dari PT Pertamina Patra Nia­
ga kepada Entitas Anak Opdaka, Proses 
pengisian gas elpiji di Opdaka adalah se­
bagai berikut:
1.	 Proses LPG diangkut menggunakan 
skid truk dari PT Pertamina Patra Niaga 
ke lokasi SPBE. Setelah sampai di SPBE, 
LPG tersebut akan dimasukkan ke tan­
gki timbun yang berkapasitas 50 ton.
LPG filling service is a Filling Fee service 
from PT Pertamina Patra Niaga to Opda­
ka’s subsidiary. The LPG filling process at 
Opdaka is as follows:
1.	 The LPG process is transported using 
skid trucks from PT Pertamina Patra Ni­
aga to the SPBE location. After arriving 
at the SPBE, the LPG will be put into 
a storage tank with a capacity of 50 
tons.

2.	 Untuk proses pengisian LPG, truk-truk 
agen yang ditunjuk oleh PT Pertam­
ina Patra Niaga akan mengangkut 
tabung LPG 3 kg yang kosong ma­
suk ke area Filling Hall SPBE. Tabung 
kosong tersebut sebelum digunakan

ahan antara kondisi yang layak dan

agen-agen yang ditunjuk oleh PT Per­
tamina Patra Niaga.

Dengan kapasitas 10 mesin filling mas­
ing-masing sebesar 3 ton/hari dan 2 me­

dapat penurunan volume dibandingkan 
tahun 2023 yaitu sebesar 9.685 ton.
2.  For the LPG filling process, agent trucks 
appointed by PT Pertamina Patra Nia­
ga will transport empty 3 kg LPG cylin­
ders into the SPBE Filling Hall area. The 
empty tube before being used is car­
ried out a sorting process, separating 
the conditions that are feasible and 
not feasible. Unfit cylinders will be ex­
changed to BPT (Tube Maintenance 
Workshop), and suitable cylinders filled 
with LPG gas using a filling machine to 
be handed over to agents.
3.	 After the canisters are filled, then put 
back into the agent truck and data 
collection is carried out on the name 
of the agent and the number of tubes. 
LPG cylinders are ready to be market­
ed to the public through agents ap­
pointed by PT Pertamina Patra Niaga.

2024 are as many as 12 machines. With a

ment is quite a decrease, where there is 
a decrease in volume compared to 2023,
