On the expenditure side, household

Current Assets 
In 2024, the Company’s total current

0.8%, or Rp3.01 billion, from Rp387.21

Company’s strategy of utilizing opera-

on ﬁxed asset purchases, in light of the

well as optimizing working capital usage.
