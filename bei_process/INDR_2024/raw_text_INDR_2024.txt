capacity starting up from the expansions made in second half of

in the product prices by 3% as compared to the previous year 
and increase in sales volumes as explained above. Operating loss 
during the year was US$ 18.1 million as compared to Operating 
loss of US$ 29.5 million in 2023 due to slower than expected 
recovery in demand and margins on account of geopolitical issues 
and slower growth in consumer spending and unfair dumping of 
products from China (which has significant overcapacities). Spun 
Yarns are manufactured through a spinning process using natural 
and man-made fibers as the raw materials. The production 
capacity and actual production for 2024 were 168,000 MT and 
162,124 MT respectively.
Polyester production and sales volumes increased by 4% and 
12% respectively over previous year, mainly on account of better 
demand as compared to the previous year. Sales Values were 
higher by 9% compared to previous year, almost mainly due 
to increase in sales volume as compared to the previous year 
as explained above and partly offset by decrease in product 
prices by 3%. Operating loss was US$ 5.1 million as compared 
to Operating loss of US$ 13.8 million in 2023 due to slower than 
expected recovery in profit margins due to unfair dumping 
of products from China (which has significant overcapacities). 
Polyester is manufactured through a chemical reaction process 
using two basic raw materials i.e., PTA and MEG. The production 
capacity and actual production for 2024 were 334,000 MT and 
356,094 MT respectively.
