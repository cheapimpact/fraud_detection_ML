tersebut masih lebih tinggi sebesar 8,43% dibanding harga 
pada bulan Desember 2022 yaitu US$281,48. Kenaikan harga 
pada bulan Januari 2023 disebabkan karena tingginya curah 
hujan yang berakibat pada terkendalanya distribusi batu 
bara Australia di pelabuhan muat, sehingga menghambat 
pasokan batu bara Australia ke negara importir. Harga batu 
bara tertinggi terjadi pada bulan Juni 2022 dan Oktober 
2022 yaitu di level US$323,91 dan US$330,97 yang terutama 
disebabkan oleh krisis energi global, terutama lonjakan harga 
gas alam, serta kondisi cuaca buruk di Australia, pemasok 
utama internasional. Secara umum ketika Harga Batu 
bara Acuan (HBA) mengalami kenaikan, Perusahaan batu 
bara akan berusaha meningkatkan produksi dan berusaha 
menjual lebih banyak batu bara.
Tingginya permintaan batu bara global mendorong harga 
batu bara hingga mencapai level tertinggi. Menurut 
International Energy Agency (IEA) terdapat beberapa faktor 
utama yang mendorong meningkatnya permintaan batu 
bara global. Pertama, pasokan gas alam yang terbatas dan 
harga gas yang tinggi menyebabkan beberapa negara dan 
perusahaan beralih ke batu bara yang relatif lebih murah. 
Kedua, gelombang panas dan kekeringan di beberapa wilayah 
di dunia meningkatkan permintaan listrik dan mengurangi 
pembangkit listrik tenaga air, menciptakan celah yang harus 
diisi oleh sebagian besar pembangkit listrik tenaga panas 
yang dapat dikirim. Terakhir, pembangkit tenaga nuklir 
sangat lemah pada tahun 2022, terutama di Eropa, di mana 
Prancis harus menutup sebagian besar kapasitas nuklirnya 
untuk pemeliharaan. 
2023 BUSINESS PROSPECT AND STRATEGIES
Over the last two years the Reference Coal Price (HBA) has 
experienced a very significant increase from US$75.84 in 
January 2021 to US$305.21 in January 2023 or 302.44%. The 
price in January 2023 is still 8.43% higher than the price in 
December 2022, namely US$281.48. The price increase in 
January 2023 was caused by high rainfall which resulted 
in problems with the distribution of Australian coal at 
loading ports, thus hampering the supply of Australian coal 
to importing countries. The highest coal prices occurred 
in June 2022 and October 2022, namely at US$323.91 and 
US$330.97, which was mainly caused by the global energy 
crisis, especially the spike in natural gas prices, as well as 
bad weather conditions in Australia, the main international 
supplier. In general, when the Reference Coal Price (HBA) 
increases, coal companies will try to increase production and 
try to sell more coal.
The high global demand for coal pushed coal prices to their 
highest levels. According to the International Energy Agency 
(IEA), there are several main factors driving the increase 
in global coal demand. First, limited natural gas supplies 
and high gas prices have caused several countries and 
companies to switch to relatively cheaper coal. Second, 
heat waves and droughts in some regions of the world are 
increasing electricity demand and reducing hydro power plant 
generation, creating a gap that most dispatchable thermal 
power plants must fill. Finally, nuclear power plant is very 
weak in 2022, especially in Europe, where France had to shut 
down most of its nuclear capacity for maintenance.

PSAK 16 (Amendment), “Fixed Assets”: Results Before 
Intended Use.
The amendments prohibit entities from deducting from 
the cost of fixed assets the proceeds received from the 
sale of items produced by those fixed assets before they 
are ready for use for their intended purpose. Instead, the 
entity recognizes the proceeds from the sale of the item, 
and the costs of producing the item, in profit or loss.

in Accounting Estimates, and Errors”: Definition of 
Accounting Estimates.
The amendment introduces the definition of ‘accounting 
estimates’ and clarifies the difference between changes 
in accounting estimates and changes in accounting 
policies and corrections of error. The amendment also 
clarifies how entities use measurement techniques and 
inputs to develop accounting estimates.
•	
PSAK 46 (Amendment), “Income Taxes”: Deferred Taxes 
related to Assets and Liabilities Arising from a Single 
Transaction.
This amendment proposes that entities recognize both 
deferred tax assets and liabilities upon initial recognition, 
for example lease transactions, to eliminate differences 
in current practice for such transactions and other similar 
transactions.

Reform Provisions of Pillar Two Model.
The amendment stipulates temporary exception to

international tax reform (Pillar Two Model), and disclosures 
for better comprehension to an entity’s exposure to Pillar 
Two income taxes.

checks the expense costs during the operations. The Company 
implements several strategies to minimize costs affected by external 
conditions, such as fuel and others by prioritizing the efficiency 
of the operations. Moreover, the Company has implemented price 
adjustments due to the rise of solar fuel price to curb disruption to the 
Company’s operations.
Risiko Sumber Daya Manusia /  
Human Resources Risk
Dalam mengelola risiko sumber daya manusia, Perseroan telah 
menawarkan gaji dan tunjangan yang mampu bersaing dengan 
kompetitor Perseroan. Selain itu, Perseroan juga mengedepankan 
keselamatan kerja karyawan sehingga potensi kecelakaan dalam 
pekerjaan menjadi rendah. /
In managing human resources risk, the Company offers a competitive 
salary and allowance compared to the competitors. In addition, the 
Company prioritizes employee’s occupational safety, thereby lowering 
occupational accident potentials.
Risiko Perubahan Teknologi / 
Technology-Related Risk
Dalam mengelola risiko perubahan teknologi yang semakin mengarah 
kepada penggunaan energi baru terbarukan (renewable energy), 
Perseroan melakukan langkah-langkah penyesuaian jika terdapat 
perubahan kebijakan Pemerintah dan institusi lainnya terkait energi 
baru terbarukan maupun peraturan perundang-undangan yang berlaku 
dalam industri pertambangan batubara dan bidang lingkungan hidup. /
In managing technology-related risk with leaning preference to 
renewable energy, the Company takes adaptive measures when there 
are amendments to the Governmental or other institutional policies 
relating to new renewable energy or laws and regulations prevailing in 
the coal mining industry and biodiversity.
Risiko Kerusakan Peralatan Penunjang Operasional /  
Damages to Operations Supporting Equipment Risk
Dalam mengelola risiko kerusakan peralatan penunjang operasional, 
Perseroan melalui divisi maintenance memiliki kebijakan perawatan 
dan pengawasan terhadap peralatan dan alat pengangkutan Perseroan 
secara berkala sehingga dapat menjaga umur pakai peralatan 
tersebut. / 
In managing damages to operations supporting equipment risk, 
the Company through the maintenance division has the policy to 
periodically maintain and supervise the Company’s equipment and 
transportation, thereby preserving the age of the equipment.
