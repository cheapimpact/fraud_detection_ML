Proses Produksi
Production Process
Proses produksi Perseroan terdiri dari 3 tahap, yaitu 
Pasteurized Crabmeat, Frozen Seafood, dan Canned Sterilized 
Seafood, dengan rincian sebagai berikut.
The Company's production process consists of 3 stages:

Proses Produksi Pasteurized Crabmeat 
Pasteurized Crabmeat Production Process
Proses produksi produk Pasteurized Crabmeat diawali 
dengan proses pengadaan daging rajungan yang telah 
dikemas dalam toples dan disimpan pada suhu dingin 
dengan es curah. Daging kemudian dikirim menggunakan 
mobil ke pabrik dengan suhu maksimal 4,4 derajat celsius. 
Setibanya di pabrik, karyawan akan mengecek mutu 
organoleptik daging seperti penampakan, aroma, dan 
tekstur. Jika lulus standar, daging rajungan akan diproses 
lebih lanjut, sedangkan yang tidak sesuai standard akan 
ditolak dan dikembalikan ke pengepul yang bersangkutan.
The production process of Pasteurized Crabmeat begins 
with the procurement of crab meat, which is packed in jars 
and stored at a cold temperature with bulk ice. The meat is 
then transported to the factory using refrigerated trucks 
at a maximum temperature of 4.4 degrees Celsius. Upon 
arrival at the factory, employees conduct an organoleptic 
quality check, assessing the appearance, aroma, and texture 
of the meat. If it meets the standards, the crab meat will be 
processed further, while those that do not meet the standards 
will be rejected and returned to the relevant collector.  
Daging rajungan yang telah lulus pengecekan organoleptik 
akan disortir hingga bersih dari cangkang halus dan benda 
benda asing lainnya. Daging hasil sortir akan disortir ulang 
untuk memastikan bahwa daging rajungan telah benar-
benar bersih dari kulit atau benda asing lainnya.
Crabmeat that has passed organoleptic checks will be 
sorted until it is clean of fine shells and other foreign 
objects. The sorted meat will be resorted to ensure that 
the crab meat is completely clean of skin or other foreign 
objects.

Setelah disortir ulang, daging rajungan melewati mesin 
metal detector guna memastikan tidak ada serpihan 
logam. Kemudian dilanjutkan ke mesin pencampur bumbu 
sesuai standar yang diminta pelanggan. Daging yang 
sudah tercampur rata kemudian dikemas dalam kaleng,

After sorting, the crab meat passes through a metal 
detector machine to ensure there are no metal fragments. 
It then proceeds to the seasoning mixer, where spices are 
blended according to customer specifications. The evenly 
mixed meat is then packaged in cans, pouches, or tubs. 
A preservative, Sodium Acid Pyrophosphate (SAPP), is 
added to maintain its color. Each product is labeled with 
a production code for traceability. The packaging is then 
sealed using a can seamer machine.

proses 
pengemasan, 
produk 
Pasteurized 
Crabmeat 
kemudian

pasteurisasi

dikemas dalam master karton sebelum didistribusikan ke 
pelanggan dengan menjaga suhu penyimpanan 1-3°C agar 
kadaluwarsa terjaga. Perseroan menerapkan pembelian 
daging rajungan sebagai bahan baku, dibandingkan 
rajungan matang, guna menjaga kualitas dan konsistensi 
produk Pasteurized Crabmeat.
Following the packaging process, the Pasteurized 
Crabmeat undergoes pasteurization at a temperature of 
85-90°C for 140 minutes. It is then rapidly cooled to below 
temperature 3°C for 120 minutes to maintain the meat's 
texture and freshness. The finished products are packed 
into master cartons before being distributed to customers, 
ensuring a storage temperature of 1-3°C to maintain shelf 
life. The Company implements the purchase of crab meat 
as raw material, rather than cooked crab meat, in order 
to maintain the quality and consistency of Pasteurized 
Crabmeat products.
Proses Produksi Frozen Cephalopods (Squid)
Frozen Cephalopods (Squid) Production Process
Cephalopods diperoleh dalam kemasan toples yang telah 
didinginkan dengan es curah. Selama pengiriman menuju 
pabrik menggunakan truk, suhu senantiasa dipantau 
pada level maksimal 4,4°C. Setibanya di pabrik, mutu 
organoleptik cephalopods seperti suhu, warna, tekstur, 
dan aroma akan diperiksa. Bahan baku yang memenuhi 
standar kualitas dan kesegaran akan diproses lebih lanjut. 
Sementara cephalopods yang tidak sesuai standar akan 
dikembalikan ke pemasoknya.
Cephalopods are received in jars that have been chilled 
with bulk ice. During transportation to the factory by truck, 
the temperature is continuously monitored, maintaining 
a maximum level of 4.4°C. Upon arrival at the factory, 
the organoleptic quality of the cephalopods—including 
temperature, color, texture, and aroma—is inspected. 
Only raw materials that meet the required quality and 
freshness standards proceed to further processing, while 
those that do not comply are returned to the supplier.  
Cephalopods yang lolos pemeriksaan organoleptik 
kemudian dikupas untuk membuang bagian yang tidak 
terpakai seperti kulit, tulang, dan mata. Kegiatan ini 
dilakukan di bawah air mengalir agar bersih. Daging 
cephalopods yang sudah bersih kemudian dipisah 
berdasarkan ukuran sesuai standar yang ditetapkan. 
Daging yang terpilah kemudian dibekukan pada suhu 
-18°C hingga -41°C selama 2-4 jam agar tetap segar. 
Produk akhir dikemas dalam plastik dengan berat tertentu 
sesuai pesanan pembeli. Kemasan ditempatkan di dalam 
master karton sebelum didistribusikan ke pelanggan 
dengan suhu penyimpanan minimal -18°C guna menjaga 
kadaluwarsa.
Cephalopods that pass the organoleptic inspection are 
then peeled to remove unwanted parts such as skin, 
bones, and eyes. This process is carried out under running 
water to ensure cleanliness. The cleaned cephalopod meat 
is then sorted according to size based on predetermined 
standards. The selected meat is frozen at a temperature 
of -18°C to -41°C for 2-4 hours to maintain freshness. The 
final product is packaged in plastic with specific weights 
according to customer orders. The packaged products are 
then placed in master cartons before being distributed to 
customers, ensuring a minimum storage temperature of 
-18°C to maintain shelf life.

Proses Produksi Pengalengan Udang
Canned Shrimp Production Process
Daging udang yang diperoleh oleh Perseroan disimpan 
dengan suhu terkontrol di dalam fiber yang terinsulasi 
dan diangkut ke pabrik menggunakan mobil dengan suhu 
maksimum 4,4°C. Saat penerimaan, dilakukan pengecekan 
mutu organoleptik, dan hanya daging udang berkualitas 
tinggi yang memenuhi standar yang diproses, sementara 
yang tidak sesuai dikembalikan kepada pemasok. Langkah-
langkah ini memastikan hanya menggunakan bahan baku 
berkualitas dalam produksi.
The shrimp meat obtained by the Company is stored at a 
controlled temperature inside insulated fiber containers 
and transported to the factory using a car, maintaining 
a maximum temperature of 4.4°C. Upon receipt, an 
organoleptic quality inspection is conducted, and only 
high-quality shrimp meat that meets the standards is 
processed, while non-compliant products are returned to 
the supplier. These measures ensure that only premium 
raw materials are used in production.  
Daging udang direbus pada suhu 100°C selama 16 menit, 
kemudian didinginkan dengan air es bersuhu 50°C. Lalu 
dilakukan penyortiran untuk memisahkan cangkang halus 
dan benda asing lainnya. Daging hasil sortir kemudian 
diperiksa kembali untuk memastikan daging dalam 
keadaan bersih. Setelah itu melewati mesin metal detector 
untuk memastikan tidak mengandung serpihan logam 
yang dapat membahayakan konsumen dan agar daging 
dapat terjamin kualitas dan keamanannya.
The shrimp meat is boiled at 100°C for 16 minutes, then 
cooled with ice water at 50°C. A sorting process follows to 
remove fine shells and other foreign materials. The sorted 
shrimp meat undergoes another inspection to ensure 
cleanliness. It then passes through a metal detector to 
confirm the absence of metal fragments that could be 
hazardous to consumers, ensuring both quality and safety.
Daging udang dimasukkan ke dalam kaleng dan dicampur 
dengan larutan garam sesuai standar. Setiap kaleng 
diberi kode produksi untuk pelacakan. Kemasan ditutup 
menggunakan seamer, lalu produk disterilkan pada suhu 
116°C selama 55 menit dan didinginkan kembali pada 
suhu 45°C. Setelah itu, kemasan kaleng ditempatkan 
dalam master carton dan disimpan hingga distribusi 
pada suhu ruang. Produk udang kaleng menunggu masa 
inkubasi selama 2 minggu di gudang. Jika tidak ada 
kaleng yang mengembung, produk siap diekspor. Tahapan 
ini memastikan kualitas dan keamanan udang kaleng 
sebelum dijual ke konsumen.
The shrimp meat is placed into cans and mixed with a salt 
solution according to the required standards. Each can is 
assigned a production code for traceability. The cans are 
sealed using a seamer, then sterilized at 116°C for 55 
minutes before being cooled to 45°C. After sterilization, 
the canned products are placed in master cartons and 
stored at room temperature until distribution. The canned 
shrimp products undergo a two-week incubation period 
in the warehouse. If no cans exhibit swelling, the products 
are deemed safe for export. This process guarantees the 
quality and safety of canned shrimp before they reach 
consumers.
