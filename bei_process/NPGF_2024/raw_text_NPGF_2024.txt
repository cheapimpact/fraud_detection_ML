Tinjauan Makroekonomi Global dan Nasional
Global and Domestic Macroeconomic Review
Tinjauan Makro Ekonomi Global
Sepanjang tahun 2024, dunia menghadapi berbagai 
tantangan yang kompleks dan beragam. Pertama, konflik 
geopolitik. Kedua, perubahan kepemimpinan politik di 
banyak negara. Ketiga, proyeksi ekonomi global dan negara-
negara besar di dunia yang masih lemah. Saat ini dunia 
berada dalam situasi global yang tidak pasti. Efek perang 
Ukraina-Rusia masih terasa. Konflik geopolitik di Timur 
Tengah dan di Laut Cina Selatan juga masih belum usai, 
bahkan ada kecenderungan kian memanas.
Dana Moneter Internasional (International Monetary Fund/
IMF) memproyeksikan pertumbuhan ekonomi global stagnan 
di level 3,2% secara tahunan (year on year) untuk tahun ini. 
Sedangkan perekonomian Amerika Serikat tumbuh 2,5% yoy 
seiring solidnya kinerja ekonomi domestik. Pertumbuhan 
ekonomi global tetap stabil dan diperkirakan berkisar 2,5%, 
peningkatan sebesar 0,2% dari proyeksi IMF pada Oktober 
2024. Perkiraan pertumbuhan ekonomi global terus membaik 
hingga 3,2% di 2024. IMF memperkirakan pertumbuhan 
ekonomi yang lebih baik di AS, di mana kebijakan moneter 
ketat masih bekerja dan menunggu untuk diturunkan.
Cina yang selama beberapa tahun terakhir dapat tumbuh 
di atas 10%, kini mengalami perlambatan laju ekonomi dan 
tumbuh di bawah 5%. Berbagai dinamika situasi global ini 
sedikit banyak memberi dampak terhadap perekonomian 
Indonesia.
Tinjauan Makro Ekonomi Nasional
Ditengah ketidakpastian ekonomi global, kinerja ekonomi 
Indonesia masih tetap solid dan lebih baik dibandingkan 
beberapa negara maju maupun berkembang lainnya. Pada 
Q4-2024, Indonesia mencatatkan pertumbuhan ekonomi 
sebesar 5,02% (yoy), lebih tinggi dibandingkan peer 
countries seperti Singapura (4,3%), Arab Saudi (4,4%), dan 
Malaysia (4,8%). Sementara itu di sepanjang tahun 2024, 
pertumbuhan ekonomi Indonesia mampu membukukan 
capaian sebesar 5,03%.
Stabilitas ekonomi ini juga didukung oleh upaya Pemerintah 
dalam menjaga inflasi untuk tetap rendah dan terkendali, 
dimana inflasi pada Desember tahun 2024 tercatat sebesar 
1,57% (yoy) atau tetap dalam rentang sasaran 2,5±1%. Hal 
ini mencerminkan kebijakan fiskal yang diselenggarakan 
dengan hati-hati dan memberikan ruang yang cukup untuk 
investasi publik. 
Global Macroeconomic Review
Throughout 2024, the world faced various complex and 
diverse challenges. First, geopolitical conflicts. Second, 
changes in political leadership in many countries. Third, 
weak global economic projections, especially among major 
world economies. The world was in an uncertain global 
situation. The effects of the Ukraine-Russia war were still 
being felt. Geopolitical conflicts in the Middle East and the 
South China Sea had yet to be resolved, with indications of 
increasing tensions.
The International Monetary Fund (IMF) projected global 
economic growth to stagnate at 3.2% year-on-year for this 
year. Meanwhile, the United States economy grew by 2.5% 
year-on-year, supported by strong domestic economic 
performance. Global economic growth remained stable and 
was estimated to be around 2.5%, an increase of 0.2% from 
the IMF’s October 2024 projection. The forecast for global 
economic growth continued to improve, reaching 3.2% in 
2024. The IMF anticipated stronger economic growth in 
the U.S., where tight monetary policies remained in effect, 
awaiting adjustments.
China, which in recent years has achieved growth above 10%, 
was experiencing an economic slowdown, growing below 
5%. Various global dynamics certainly had some impact on 
Indonesia’s economy. 
Domestic Macroeconomic Review
Amid global economic uncertainty, Indonesia’s economic 
performance remained solid and better than several other 
developed and developing countries. In Q4-2024, Indonesia 
recorded economic growth of 5.02% (yoy), higher than peer 
countries such as Singapore (4.3%), Saudi Arabia (4.4%), and 
Malaysia (4.8%). Meanwhile, throughout 2024, Indonesia’s 
economic growth was able to post an achievement of 5.03%.
This economic stability was also supported by the 
Government’s efforts in keeping inflation low and under 
control, where inflation in December 2024 was recorded at 
1.57% (yoy) or remained within the target range of 2.5±1%. 
This reflects a prudent fiscal policy that provides sufficient 
room for public investment.

2024 Business Strategy
Di sisi lain, Produk Domestik Bruto (PDB) Indonesia juga 
mengalami peningkatan. Di tahun 2024 PDB Indonesia 
mencapai Rp22.139 triliun, meningkat dari tahun sebelumnya 
yang sebesar Rp20.892 triliun. Lebih jauh, PDB per kapita 
Indonesia saat ini telah mencapai Rp78,62 juta atau 
setara dengan USD4.960,33. Selain itu, indikator sektor 
riil juga menunjukkan ketahanan ekonomi dan optimisme 
masyarakat, tecermin dari Purchasing Managers’ Index (PMI) 
Manufaktur Indonesia pada yang berhasil rebound dan 
kembali mencatatkan level ekspansif di angka 51,2, setelah 
sebelumnya sempat berada di level kontraktif. Peningkatan 
ini didorong oleh kenaikan pesanan baru, baik domestik 
maupun ekspor, serta peningkatan aktivitas pembelian 
bahan baku oleh Perusahaan.
On the other hand, Indonesia’s Gross Domestic Product (GDP) 
also increased. In 2024, Indonesia’s GDP reached Rp22,139 
trillion, an increase from the previous year of Rp20,892 
trillion. Furthermore, Indonesia’s GDP per capita has now 
reached Rp78.62 million or equivalent to USD4,960.33. 
In addition, real sector indicators also showed economic 
resilience and public optimism, reflected in the Indonesian 
Manufacturing Purchasing Managers’ Index (PMI) which 
managed to rebound and again recorded an expansionary 
level at 51.2, after previously being at a contractionary level. 
This increase was driven by an increase in new orders, both 
domestic and export, as well as an increase in raw material 
purchasing activity by the Company.
Manajemen Perseroan telah merumuskan strategi unggulan, 
namun tetap menggarap segmen market utama yaitu sektor 
perkebunan kelapa sawit untuk meningkatkan pertumbuhan 
dan perkembangan bisnis perusahaan. Manajemen 
perseroan selalu berupaya untuk menjaga kepuasan 
pelanggan melalui produk unggulan dengan formulasi 
khusus sesuai kebutuhan dan permintaan pelanggan. Tidak 
adanya ijin pembukaan area perkebunan kelapa sawit baru 
serta kenaikan harga CPO yang tinggi semakin mendorong 
intensifikasi perkebunan kelapa sawit. Upaya tersebut sangat 
membutuhkan pasokan pupuk dengan formulasi khusus.
Pakar agronomi perkebunan kelapa sawit berupaya untuk 
merumuskan spesifikasi pupuk yang sesuai untuk setiap 
lokasi perkebunan. Sehingga spesifikasi pupuk yang 
didatangkan dapat berbeda antar kebun yang satu dengan 
lainnya. Manajemen perseroan sangat terbuka untuk 
menerima pesanan pupuk dengan spesifikasi khusus tersebut. 
Jika pada umumnya, perkebunan yang statusnya sudah TM 
(Tanaman Menghasilkan) membutuhkan pupuk NPK 13-6-
27-4 maka dalam program intensifikasi perkebunan tersebut 
maka spesifikasinya bisa berbeda. Sebagai komitmen untuk 
mendukung kesuksesan bisnis konsumen maka perseroan 
siap menerima permintaan pupuk dengan spesifikasi khusus. 
Sehingga perseroan menerima permintaan pupuk dengan 
spesifikasi NPK 14-6-26-2, NPK 17-8-24-2, NPK 15-6-25-2 
dan sebagainya. Teknologi produksi yang dimiliki perseroan 
sangat memungkinkan dan memiliki fleksibilitas tinggi dalam 
memenuhi permintaan pupuk spesifikasi khusus tersebut. 
The Company's management has formulated a superior 
strategy, but continued to work on the main market segment, 
namely the oil palm plantation sector to increase the growth 
and development of the Company's business. The Company's 
management endeavored to maintain customer satisfaction 
through superior products with special formulations 
according to customer needs and demands. The absence of 
permits to open new oil palm plantation areas and the high 
increase in CPO prices further encouraged the intensification 
of oil palm plantations. This effort required the supply of 
fertilizers with special formulations.
Oil palm plantation agronomists were trying to formulate 
fertilizer specifications that were suitable for each plantation 
location. Therefore, the specifications of the fertilizers 
imported differed from one plantation to another. The 
Company's management was open to receiving fertilizer 
orders with these special specifications. If in general, 
plantations with TM (Producing Plants) status required 
NPK 13-6-27-4 fertilizer, prompting different specifications 
in the plantation intensification program. As a commitment 
to support customers' business success, the Company 
was ready to accept requests for fertilizers with special 
specifications that enabled the Company to receive requests 
for fertilizers with specifications of NPK 14-6-26-2, NPK 17-8-
24-2, NPK 15-6-25-2 and so on. The production technology 
of the Company allows such customization and has high 
flexibility in meeting the demand for special specification 
fertilizers.

Business Prospects
Tahun 2024, dunia menghadapi berbagai tantangan 
yang kompleks dan beragam. Adapun tantangan global 
yang tengah terjadi saat ini dibagi ke dalam tiga hal 
besar. Pertama, konflik geopolitik. Kedua, perubahan 
kepemimpinan politik di banyak negara. Ketiga, proyeksi 
ekonomi global dan negara-negara besar di dunia yang 
masih lemah.Efek perang Ukraina-Rusia masih terasa. 
Konflik geopolitik di Timur Tengah dan di Laut Cina Selatan 
juga masih belum usai, bahkan ada kecenderungan kian 
memanas. Selain itu, banyak negara melakukan pemilihan 
umum sehingga muncul pimpinan negara baru, baik di 
negara maju maupun berkembang. Lebih dari 60 negara 
melakukan pemilihan umum dan melibatkan empat miliar 
orang di dunia. Perubahan kepemimpinan ini dipastikan 
akan menimbulkan perbedaan arah kebijakan. Pemilihan 
umum juga berlangsung di Indonesia dan menciptakan 
pergantian pimpinan dari Presiden Joko Widodo ke Presiden 
Prabowo Subianto.
Tantangan lain yang masih membayangi situasi global 
adalah kondisi perekonomian global yang belum sepenuhnya 
pulih dari dampak pandemi Covid-19. Pada 2024, aktivitas 
ekonomi global cenderung lemah dengan kinerja yang 
bervariasi antarkawasan. Kinerja ekonomi negara-negara 
maju masih cukup resilien meski masih lebih rendah 
dibanding periode sebelum pandemi. Pada kuartal III-2024, 
ekonomi Amerika Serikat tumbuh 2,7% yoy. Di Eropa, laju 
ekonomi masih stagnan dan tumbuh di kisaran 0,9% pada 
kuartal III-2024. Ekonomi Tiongkok menunjukkan tanda-
tanda perlambatan. Sementara itu, ekonomi di kawasan 
ASEAN masih resilien meskipun permintaan global turun. 
Cina yang selama beberapa tahun terakhir dapat tumbuh 
di atas 10%, kini mengalami perlambatan laju ekonomi dan 
tumbuh di bawah 5%. Berbagai dinamika situasi global ini 
sedikit banyak memberi dampak terhadap perekonomian 
Indonesia.

Untuk menjaga kelangsungan usaha di masa depan, 
Perseroan akan selalu berusaha memanfaatkan momentum 
pemulihan ekonomi nasional dan mengambil peran strategis

In 2024, the world faced a variety of complex and diverse 
challenges. The global challenges included three major 
events. First, geopolitical conflicts. Second, changes in 
political leadership in many countries. Third, global 
economic projections and major countries in the world that 
remained weakened. The effects of the Ukraine-Russia war 
were still being felt. Geopolitical conflicts in the Middle East 
and in the South China Sea were not over, and there was 
even a tendency to heat up. In addition, many countries 
held general elections and new leaders emerged, both in 
developed and developing countries. More than 60 countries 
held general elections, involving four billion people in the 
world. This change in leadership certainly led to differences 
in policy direction. General elections also took place in 
Indonesia and created a change of leadership from President 
Joko Widodo to President Prabowo Subianto.
Another challenge that still loomed over the global situation 
was the global economy which had not fully recovered 
from the impact of the Covid-19 pandemic. In 2024, 
global economic activity tended to be weak with varying 
performance between regions. The economic performance 
of developed countries was relatively resilient despite still 
being lower than the period before the pandemic. In the third 
quarter of 2024, the United States economy grew 2.7% yoy. 
In Europe, the economic pace remained stagnant and grew 
at around 0.9% in the third quarter of 2024. China’s economy 
was showing signs of slowing down. Meanwhile, economies 
in the ASEAN region were resilient despite falling global 
demand. China, which in recent years had been able to grow 
above 10%, was experiencing a slowdown in economic growth 
and was growing below 5%. These various dynamics of the 
global situation had more or less impacted the Indonesian 
economy.
Nevertheless, the Company is optimistic about the prospects 
for future business growth by implementing various 
strategies and policies that are directed and sustainable. 
To maintain business continuity in the future, the Company 
endeavors to capitalize on the momentum of national 
economic recovery and take a strategic role to support the 
improvement of market performance in Indonesia.

Strategi Pemasaran
Guna membangun jaringan pemasaran, kedekatan dengan 
konsumen dan meningkatkan kegiatan pemasaran, 
Perseroan melakukan kegiatan pemasaran di 3 (tiga) kota 
besar di seluruh Indonesia, yaitu Surabaya, Jakarta, dan 
Medan. Perseroan juga bekerja sama dengan distributor 
untuk memperluas jangkauan area pemasaran pupuk. Agar 
lebih kompetitif, perseroan selalu berusaha memberikan 
pelayanan yang terbaik kepada konsumen dari segi harga, 
kualitas produk, dan ketepatan waktu dalam pengiriman. 
Pengembangan dan inovasi produk pupuk juga dilakukan 
untuk membuka peluang pasar-pasar baru.
Pangsa Pasar
Perkebunan kelapa sawit di Indonesia merupakan 
target pasar utama yang dibidik oleh perseroan. Saat ini 
potensi kebutuhan pupuk untuk perkebunan kelapa sawit 
diperkirakan mencapai 15 juta ton per tahun. Potensi pasar 
perkebunan yang begitu besar ini baru dapat terpenuhi 
sekitar 3% dari pupuk NPK non subsidi. Adapun pangsa pasar 
pupuk NPK non subsidi diperkirakan sebesar 3,9 juta ton per 
tahun. Perseroan saat ini hanya mampu menyuplai sekitar 
5% dari total pangsa pasar pupuk NPK non subsidi yang ada.
Conventional Channel (Offline)
Optimizing marketing, communication and promotion 
activities conducted at the customer and business partner 
level with the aim of engaging business partners and 
customers and increasing awareness of the Company 
including its products, services and offers, both through 
below the line and above the line channels. These include 
the following:
	•
Partnering with distributors to expand marketing 
network
	•
Conducting a demo plot program
	•
Participating in exhibition activities. 
Marketing Strategy
In order to build a marketing network, proximity to consumers 
and improve marketing activities, the Company conducted 
marketing activities in 3 (three) major cities throughout 
Indonesia, namely Surabaya, Jakarta and Medan. The 
Company also cooperated with distributors to expand the 
reach of fertilizer marketing areas. To be more competitive, 
the Company endeavored to provide the best service to 
consumers in terms of price, product quality, and timeliness 
in delivery. Fertilizer product development and innovation 
were also carried out to open new market opportunities.
Market Share
Oil palm plantations in Indonesia are the main target market 
targeted by the company. Currently, the potential need for 
fertilizer for oil palm plantations is estimated at 15 million 
tons per year. This huge plantation market potential can only 
be met by around 3% of non-subsidized NPK fertilizers. The 
market share of non-subsidized NPK fertilizers is estimated 
at 3.9 million tons per year. The Company is currently only 
able to supply around 5% of the total market share of non-
subsidized NPK fertilizers.
