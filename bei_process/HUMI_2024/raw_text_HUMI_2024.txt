Economic and Industry 
Overview
Ekonomi global masih dihadapkan pada 
sejumlah tantangan pada tahun 2024. 
Memanasnya konflik geopolitik di Eropa dan 
Timur Tengah telah memberikan pengaruh 
yang sangat besar terhadap perekonomian 
global, salah satunya adalah terganggunya 
rantai pasok perdagangan global. Selain 
itu, perang tarif antara Amerika Serikat dan 
China juga semakin memperburuk kondisi 
perdagangan global, karena juga berimbas 
pada negara-negara lain yang memiliki 
hubungan dagang dengan kedua negara 
tersebut.
The global economy was still encountering 
a number of challenges in 2024. The 
escalating geopolitical conflicts in Europe 
and the Middle East have had a huge impact 
on the global economy, one of which was the 
disruption of the global trade supply chain. 
In addition, the tariff war between the United 
States and China has also worsened global 
trade conditions, as it has also affected other 
countries that have trade relations with the 
two countries.

Analisa dan Pembahasan Manajemen
Management Discussion and Analysis
Perekonomian global 2024 masih dalam posisi yang lemah, 
di mana meskipun inflasi mengalami moderasi, namun 
belum serta merta menurunkan suku bunga yang melonjak 
cukup tinggi. Menurut Bank Dunia pertumbuhan global 
berpotensi stagnan di kisaran 2,6% pada 2024, sebelum 
meningkat sedikit menjadi rata-rata 2,7% pada 2025 dan 
2026. Perkiraan itu lebih rendah dari pertumbuhan 2023 
yang diproyeksikan Bank Dunia sebesar 2,9%. Di sisi lain, 
pertumbuhan ekonomi negara-negara berkembang hanya 
akan tumbuh rata-rata di kisaran 4% pada 2024 hingga 
2025, juga sedikit lebih lambat dibandingkan perkiraan 
pertumbuhan ekonomi global pada 2023 sebesar 4,1%.
Berbagai permasalahan yang terjadi di tingkat global akan 
semakin membuat 108 negara sulit keluar dari jebakan negara 
berpendapatan menengah atau middle income trap, karena 
juga dihadapkan pada menuanya masyarakat produktif 
sebelum mampu menjadi kaya atau berpendapatan tinggi.
Di tengah berbagai tantangan yang terjadi di tingkat global, 
ekonomi Indonesia masih tumbuh cukup solid kendati sedikit 
melambat dibandingkan tahun sebelumnya. Berdasarkan 
data Badan Pusat Statistik (BPS), ekonomi Indonesia tahun 
2024 tumbuh sebesar 5,03%, sedikit lebih rendah dari tahun 
sebelumnya 5,05%.
Pertumbuhan ekonomi Indonesia utamanya ditunjang 
oleh konsumsi rumah tangga, belanja pemerintah dan 
kinerja ekspor yang cukup baik. Sepanjang tahun 2024, 
nilai ekspor Indonesia mencapai USD264,70 miliar dari 
tahun sebelumnya sebesar USD258,77 miliar. Hal tersebut 
membuat neraca perdagangan Indonesia tahun 2024 
membukukan surplus sebesar USD31,04 miliar.
Tingkat inflasi nasional juga dapat dijaga pada level yang 
rendah, yaitu 2,13%, sehingga tidak terjadi gejolak yang berarti 
di tingkat masyarakat. Hal ini tak lepas dari peran pemerintah 
dan Bank Indonesia melalui penerapan kebijakan moneter 
dan fiskal yang tepat.
The global economy in 2024 was still in a weak position, 
where even with the moderation in inflation, it has not 
immediately reduced interest rates which have soared 
quite high. According to the World Bank, global growth has 
the potential to stagnate at around 2.6% in 2024, before 
increasing slightly to an average of 2.7% in 2025 and 2026. 
This estimate was lower than the 2023 growth projected 
by the World Bank of 2.9%. On the other hand, economic 
growth in developing countries will only grow on average in 
the range of 4% in 2024 to 2025, also slightly slower than 
the estimated global economic growth in 2023 of 4.1%.
Various occurring global issues will make it even tougher for 
108 countries to escape the middle-income trap, because 
they are also faced with the aging of their productive society 
before they are able to become rich or have high incomes.
Amidst various global challenges that occur, the Indonesian 
economy was still growing quite solidly, although it was 
slightly slower than the previous year. Based on data from 
the BPS-Statistics Indonesia (BPS), the Indonesian economy 
in 2024 grew by 5.03%, slightly lower than the previous 
year's 5.05%.
Indonesia's economic growth was mainly supported by fairly 
good household consumption, government spending and 
export performances. Throughout 2024, Indonesia's export 
value reached USD264.70 billion from the previous year of 
USD258.77 billion. This made Indonesia's trade balance in 
2024 recorded a surplus of USD31.04 billion.
The national inflation rate can also be maintained at a low 
level, which was 2.13%, so that there was no significant 
turmoil at the community level. This was inseparable from 
the role of the government and Bank Indonesia through the 
implementation of appropriate monetary and fiscal policies.

comply with international and local shipping regulations.

Pada tahun 2024, PT Humpuss Maritim Internasional Tbk. 
(HUMI) menerapkan beberapa strategi pemasaran untuk 
memperkuat posisinya di industri maritim Indonesia, melalui

HUMI melalui anak usahanya PCSI menambah 4 kapal 
oil dan chemical dan HTC menambahkan 6 tugboat 
dengan kapasitas 2.000 HP dan 1.600 HP, serta 1 barge 
berukuran 300 feet. Langkah ini bertujuan memenuhi 
permintaan di pasar Indonesia yang terus berkembang, 
khususnya di sektor logistik maritim.

perluasan pangsa pasar di segmen bahan kimia dan 
peningkatan pendapatan dari segmen bisnis transportasi 
bahan kimia. 
In 2024, PT Humpuss Maritim Internasional Tbk. (HUMI) 
implemented several marketing strategies to strengthen 
its position in the Indonesian maritime industry, through the 
following initiatives:
1.	 Addition of Vessel Fleets
HUMI through its subsidiary PCSI added 4 oil and 
chemical vessels and HTC added 6 tugboats with a 
capacity of 2,000 HP and 1,600 HP, as well as 1 barge 
measuring 300 feet. This step aims to meet the growing 
demand in the Indonesian market, especially in the 
maritime logistics sector.
2.	 Significant Capital Expenditure
The Company allocated capital expenditure in 2024, 
these funds were used to add its fleet throughout 2024.
3.	 Income Growth Target
HUMI targets net income growth of 20.5%. This increase 
is expected to be driven by the expansion of market 
share in the chemical segment and increased revenue 
from the chemical transportation business segment.

and Provisions
Direksi menyatakan bahwa berbagai transaksi afiliasi telah 
melalui prosedur yang memadai untuk memastikan bahwa 
transaksi afiliasi dilaksanakan sesuai dengan praktik bisnis 
yang berlaku umum dan dilakukan dengan memenuhi 
prinsip transaksi yang adil dan wajar (fair and arms’ length 
principle). Demikian juga dengan Dewan Komisaris dan 
Komite Audit telah menelaah transaksi afiliasi di atas, dan 
telah memastikan bahwa kedua transaksi afiliasi di atas 
telah melalui prosedur yang memadai, dilaksanakan sesuai 
dengan praktik bisnis yang berlaku umum, dan telah 
memenuhi prinsip transaksi yang adil dan wajar (fair and 
arms’ length principle).
Untuk transaksi afiliasi atau transaksi material yang 
merupakan kegiatan usaha yang dijalankan dalam rangka 
menghasilkan pendapatan usaha dan dijalankan secara 
rutin, berulang, dan/atau berkelanjutan, Perseroan telah 
mengungkapkannya sebagaimana penjelasan saldo dan 
transaksi di atas sejalan dengan informasi yang tertuang 
pada Catatan 37 pada Laporan Keuangan Konsolidasian 
Perseroan (sebagaimana terlampir).
The Board of Directors stated that various affiliated 
transactions have gone through adequate procedures 
to ensure that affiliated transactions are carried out in 
accordance with generally accepted business practices and 
are carried out in accordance with the fair and arms’ length 
principle. Likewise, the Board of Commissioners and the Audit 
Committee have reviewed the above affiliated transactions, 
and have ensured that both affiliated transactions have 
gone through adequate procedures, have been carried out 
in accordance with generally accepted business practices, 
and have met the fair and arms’ length principle.
For affiliated transactions or material transactions which 
are business activities carried out in order to generate 
business income and are carried out routinely, repeatedly 
and/or continuously, the Company has disclosed them as 
explained in the balance and transactions above in line 
with the information contained in Note 37 to the Company’s 
Consolidated Financial Statements (as attached).

Ketentuan dalam Undang-undang Republik Indonesia No. 
66 tahun 2024 tentang perubahan ketiga atas Undang-
undang No. 17 tahun 2008 tentang pelayaran mengatur 
bahwa perusahaan usaha patungan dengan perusahaan 
asing wajib memiliki kapal berbendera Indonesia dengan 
ukuran paling rendah 50.000 GT.
The provisions under the Law of the Republic of Indonesia 
No. 66 of 2024, which is the third amendment to Law No. 17 
of 2008 on Shipping, stipulate that joint venture companies 
with foreign partners are required to operate Indonesian-
flagged vessels with a minimum size of 50,000 GT.
