Kinerja Ekonomi Domestik
Domestic Economic Performance
Pada tahun 2024, perekonomian Indonesia mencatat 
pertumbuhan sebesar 5%, sama dengan kondisi pada tahun 
sebelumnya. Meskipun angka ini menunjukkan stabilitas, 
namun menjadi laju pertumbuhan paling lambat dalam tiga 
tahun terakhir. Faktor-faktor yang berkontribusi meliputi 
pengeluaran kampanye politik, peningkatan investasi, dan 
percepatan proyek infrastruktur menjelang pergantian 
pemerintahan pada Oktober 2024.
Pada tahun 2024, Indonesia mengalami penurunan daya 
beli masyarakat yang berdampak signifikan pada sektor 
distribusi dan perdagangan, termasuk bisnis Perseroan. 
Data menunjukkan bahwa penjualan ritel hanya tumbuh 
sebesar 3,7% secara year-on-year sepanjang semester I 
2024, mengindikasikan lemahnya konsumsi domestik dan 
kepercayaan konsumen. Penurunan daya beli ini terutama 
disebabkan oleh tekanan ekonomi yang dialami masyarakat, 
meskipun nilai tukar rupiah tetap stabil. Kondisi ini 
menyebabkan harga barang terasa lebih mahal bagi konsumen, 
sehingga mereka cenderung menahan pengeluaran dan 
mengurangi konsumsi.
Selain itu, terjadi penyusutan kelas menengah Indonesia, yang 
turun dari 21,5% menjadi 17,1% dari total populasi antara tahun 
2019 dan 2024. Penurunan ini mengakibatkan berkurangnya 
basis konsumen utama bagi sektor distribusi dan perdagangan, 
yang merupakan sektor bisnis Perseroan.
In 2024, Indonesia’s economy recorded growth of 5%, same as 
the condition in the previous year. While this figure showed 
stability, it was the slowest growth rate in the last three years. 
Contributing factors include political campaign spending, 
increased investment, and accelerated infrastructure projects 
ahead of the change of government in October 2024.
In 2024, Indonesia experienced a decline in purchasing power 
which had a significant impact on the distribution and trade 
sectors, including the Company’s business. Data shows that 
retail sales only grew by 3.7% year-on-year during the first 
semester of 2024, indicating weak domestic consumption 
and consumer confidence. This decline in purchasing power 
was mainly due to the economic pressure experienced by the 
public, despite the rupiah exchange rate remaining stable. This 
condition made goods more expensive for consumers, so they 
tended to hold back on spending and reduce consumption.
In addition, there was a shrinking of Indonesia’s middle class, 
which fell from 21.5% to 17.1% of the total population between 
2019 and 2024. This decline has resulted in the reduction 
of the main consumer base for the distribution and trading 
sector, which is the Company’s business sector.

KINERJA OPERASIONAL
Pada tahun 2024, berdasarkan Laporan Keuangan yang telah 
diaudit, Perseroan mencatatkan Pendapatan Konsolidasian 
sebesar Rp13.363,66 miliar, yang mencerminkan penurunan 
sebesar 5,96% dibandingkan tahun sebelumnya yang 
mencapai Rp14.210,14 miliar. Kondisi ini terjadi seiring dengan 
dinamika pasar serta strategi optimalisasi portofolio bisnis 
yang dijalankan Perseroan.
Kontribusi Unit Usaha Consumer Products (CP) tetap 
mendominasi dengan peningkatan proporsi terhadap total 
pendapatan menjadi 95,98% dibandingkan 95,49% pada 
tahun sebelumnya. Pendapatan Tira Cipta Logistik (TCL) 
tumbuh signifikan dari Rp7,54 miliar di tahun 2023 menjadi 
Rp40,92 miliar di tahun 2024, mencatatkan pertumbuhan 
sebesar 442,85%. Tira Cipta Transportasi (TCT) mencatatkan 
pendapatan 2024 sebesar  Rp11,00 miliar atau bertumbuh 
543,37% dibanding tahun 2023 yang mencatatkan pendapatan 
sejumlah Rp1,71 miliar. Sementara itu, beberapa unit usaha 
lainnya, seperti Manufacturing Service (MS), Tira Satria Niaga 
(TSN), dan Blue Gas Indonesia (BGI) mengalami tantangan 
dalam menghadapi perubahan pasar. 
Perseroan terus beradaptasi dengan lanskap industri yang 
dinamis melalui strategi penguatan bisnis inti dan eksplorasi 
peluang pertumbuhan baru. Dengan kapasitas pendanaan 
yang solid baik dari sumber internal maupun eksternal, 
Perseroan memiliki peluang besar untuk mengakselerasi 
pertumbuhan melalui penerapan teknologi digital serta 
pengembangan inisiatif berkelanjutan.

OPERATIONAL PERFORMANCE
In 2024, based on the audited Financial Statements, the 
Company recorded Consolidated Revenue of Rp13,363.66 
billion, reflecting a change of 5.96% compared to the previous 
year which reached Rp14,210.14 billion. This condition was 
in line with market dynamics and the Company’s business 
portfolio optimization strategy.
The contribution of the Consumer Products (CP) Business 
Unit continued to dominate with an increase in proportion to 
total revenue to 95.98% compared to 95.49% in the previous

other business units, such as Manufacturing Service (MS), Tira 
Satria Niaga (TSN), and Blue Gas Indonesia (BGI) experienced 
challenges in facing market changes.
The Company continues to adapt to the dynamic industry 
landscape through a strategy of strengthening its core 
business and exploring new growth opportunities. With solid 
funding capacity from both internal and external sources, 
the Company has a great opportunity to accelerate growth 
through the application of digital technology and the 
development of sustainable initiatives.

PROSPEK USAHA
Prospek Ekonomi Global 
Prospek ekonomi secara global di masa yang akan datang 
akan dihadapkan oleh beberapa tantangan yang masih perlu 
diwaspadai, dan diantisipasi secara tepat dengan penguatan 
sinergi kebijakan ekonomi nasional. World Bank & IMF memprediksi 
bahwa pada tahun 2025 ekonomi global akan tumbuh sekitar 
3,3%, sehingga akan berdampak positif pada proses pemulihan 
kondisi ekonomi global, seiring juga dengan meningkatnya daya 
beli masyarakat. Namun demikian, kebijakan suku bunga yang 
tinggi yang bertujuan untuk mengatasi inflasi diperkirakan akan 
memperlambat konsumsi dan investasi, sehingga membatasi 
laju pertumbuhan ekonomi di berbagai negara maju. Banyak 
negara yang akan menghadapi tantangan dalam menjaga tingkat 
pengangguran yang rendah di tengah kebijakan moneter yang 
lebih ketat. Ketidakpastian ekonomi dan keuangan global makin 
tinggi seiring dengan meningkatnya ketegangan geopolitik, 
sehingga memerlukan penguatan respons kebijakan untuk 
memitigasi dampak negatif rambatan global terhadap ketahanan 
ekonomi domestik di negara-negara EMDEs, termasuk Indonesia.
Dengan harapan meredanya ketegangan geopolitik, ekonomi 
dunia diperkirakan akan kembali membaik pada 2025. 
Keberhasilan dalam mengelola inflasi, ketegangan geopolitik 
dan adaptasi terhadap berbagai tantangan akan menjadi 
salah satu faktor yang dapat menentukan kecepatan dan 
keberlanjutan pemulihan ekonomi global.  
Prospek Ekonomi Indonesia 
Prospek perbaikan ekonomi Indonesia diperkirakan berlanjut 
ditopang oleh kebijakan moneter yang bersinergi dengan 
kebijakan ekonomi nasional sehingga mendukung kebangkitan 
ekonomi Indonesia. 
Dengan perkembangan tersebut, pertumbuhan ekonomi 
diperkirakan dapat mencapai 4,7% sampai dengan 5,3% pada 
2025. Berlanjutnya perbaikan ekonomi pada 2025 terutama 
didorong oleh meningkatnya konsumsi rumah tangga. 
Peningkatan daya beli masyarakat serta stabilitas sosial yang 
relatif lebih baik setelah tahun politik. Infrastruktur akan tetap 
menjadi salah satu pilar dalam mendorong pertumbuhan 
ekonomi Indonesia. Pembangunan infrastruktur tidak hanya 
menciptakan lapangan kerja, tetapi juga meningkatkan 
konektivitas yang akan mendukung kegiatan ekonomi. 
Selain itu, upaya untuk menarik lebih banyak investasi asing 
melalui kebijakan-kebijakan yang dibentuk untuk menaikkan 
perekonomian 2025.  
BUSINESS OUTLOOK
Global Economic Outlook 
The global economic outlook in the future will be faced with 
several challenges that still need to be watched out for, and 
anticipated appropriately by strengthening the synergy of 
national economic policies. The World Bank and IMF predict 
that the global economy will grow by approximately 3.3% in 
2025, which will have a positive impact on the recovery of 
global economic conditions, alongside an increase in consumer 
purchasing power. However, high interest rate policies aimed 
at tackling inflation are expected to slow consumption and 
investment, limiting the pace of economic growth in many 
developed countries. Many countries will face challenges 
in maintaining low unemployment amid tighter monetary 
policies. Global economic and financial uncertainties are 
heightened as geopolitical tensions escalate, necessitating a 
strengthened policy response to mitigate the negative impact 
of the global slowdown on domestic economic resilience in 
EMDEs, including Indonesia.
With the hope of easing geopolitical tensions, the world 
economy is expected to rebound in 2025. Success in managing 
inflation, geopolitical tensions and adaptation to various 
challenges will be some of the factors that can determine the 
speed and sustainability of the global economic recovery.  
Indonesia’s Economic Outlook 
The outlook for Indonesia’s economic improvement is 
expected to continue supported by monetary policy that 
synergizes with national economic policy so as to support 
Indonesia’s economic revival.
With these developments, economic growth is expected 
to reach approximately 4.7% up to 5.3% in 2025. Continued 
economic improvement in 2025 is mainly driven by 
increased household consumption. Increased purchasing 
power and relatively better social stability after the political 
year. Infrastructure will remain one of the pillars in driving 
Indonesia’s economic growth. Infrastructure development 
not only creates jobs, but also improves connectivity that 
will support economic activities. In addition, efforts to attract 
more foreign investment through policies are set to boost the 
economy by 2025.

mengenai harga saham Perseroan dan dapat meningkatkan

DIVIDEND POLICY
Since listed in Jakarta Stock Exchange (now Indonesia Stock 
Exchange) well ahead until 2005, the formulation of dividend 
calculation was based on a certain percentage of Net Profit 
After Tax which is common for many other companies. As 
a norm, the Company determined that the payment of 
dividends would be at least 35% of the Company’s Net Profits 
unless there was corporate actions or other extraordinary 
circumstances.
However, since 2006, the formula of calculation was changed 
into a formula based on Cost of Equity plus a certain 
percentage from Economic Profit. Nevertheless, the form of 
payment, whether in cash or shares, is always determined 
by considering the Company’s cash availability and funding 
requirements for future financing needs of the Company’s 
operations and investments.
We expect that with the employment of the formula for dividend 
payment based on Cost of Equity amount plus a certain 
percentage of Economic Profit, shareholders or investors 
will have assurance on the return of their investments, and 
the Company’s shares will be more attractive for investors to 
trade. A more active share tradings hopefully will realistically 
reflect the Company’s share price, and could possibly increase 
the number of shares traded in the market.
The Economic Profit is not only distributed to the shareholders, 
but is also enjoyed by the Directors and employees in the 
form of cash performance bonus. In an effort to increase its 
Economic Profit, the Company has adopted the use of Activity 
Based Costing/Management tool. If productivity is high, we 
may expect that the distribution costs will decrease, and 
the Company will become more competitive thus satisfying 
principals, the Company’s main customer.
