TINJAUAN OPERASIONAL
OPERATIONAL HIGHLIGHT
PT Alkindo Naratama Tbk (Perseroan) adalah perusahaan terbuka 
nasional yang berlokasi di Kabupaten Bandung Barat, Jawa Barat. 
Perseroan memiliki 2 sektor bisnis yaitu kertas dan bahan kimia. 
Sektor Kertas
Perseroan bergerak dalam bidang produksi kertas konversi. Produk 
yang dihasilkan adalah paper tube, paper core, honeycomb, edge 
protector, paper box dan beberapa varian lainnya, dengan 
pemasaran di seluruh Indonesia.  
PT Eco Paper Indonesia (EPI), entitas anak, bergerak dalam 
produksi kertas coklat seperti core board, kraftliner, greyboard, 
dan duplex dengan daerah pemasaran di seluruh Indonesia.  
Sektor Kimia
PT Swisstex Naratama Indonesia (SNI) adalah agen dan distributor 
pewarna tekstil dan bahan kimia yang ditunjuk oleh Achroma, 
perusahaan multinasional, yang wilayah pemasarannya di seluruh 
Indonesia, dengan produk Novacron, Terasil, Novasol, Lyoprint, 
Albatex, Eriopon, Uvitex dan Stiffener. 
PT Alfa Polimer Indonesia (API) adalah mengkhususkan diri dalam 
pembuatan polimer berbasis air untuk aplikasi industri yang 
beragam seperti Alfachem, Alfabond, dan Alfacoat. Produk API 
banyak digunakan oleh industri kayu, cat, dan atap bangunan.
PT Alkindo Naratama Tbk (the Company) is a national public 
company located in West Bandung Regency, West Java. The 
company has 2 business sectors, they are paper and chemicals.
Paper Sector
The Company is engaged in converting paper production. The 
products produced are paper tube, paper core, honeycomb, edge 
protector, paper box and several other variants. 
PT Eco Paper Indonesia (EPI), a subsidiary, is engaged in the 
production of industrial paper such as core board, kraftliner, 
greyboard, and duplex with marketing areas throughout Indonesia.
Chemical Sector
PT Swisstex Naratama Indonesia (SNI) is an agent and distributors 
of textile dyes and chemicals appointed by Achroma, a global 
company, whose marketing areas all over Indonesia, with the 
products are Novacron, Terasil, Novasol, Lyoprint, Albatex, 
Eriopon, Uvitex and Stiffener.
PT Alfa Polimer Indonesia (API) is a specializes in manufacturing 
water-based polymers for a diverse industrial application such as 
Alfachem, Alfabond, and Alfacoat. API’s products products are 
used by wood, paint, and roof coating industries.

Production process of papertube similiar to production of 
papercore. Papercore is longer and thicker compare to papertube. 
The production starts with slitting of raw materials in the form of 
jumbo roll paper into small size (slitted). Then proceed with the 
process of winding slitted paper into a long tube. In this process 
parchment is added in accordance with customer demand (only for 
paper tube). Special glue is used as an addhesive. Long tubes are 
then dried (oven). After that, dried long tubes are cut to the size 
ordered by the customer. Cutted tubes are ready to be packaged in 
the box and delivered to customers.
Productions of honeycomb begun by inserting Jumbo Roll Paper 
into machine to form paper into honeycomb layer. This layer is then 
cut vertically by cutting machine. Then the pieces are connected 
together horizontally with glue. The process followed by laminating 
layer of honeycomb become honeycomb board. After passed 
drying process, honeycomb board is ready to be packaged and 
delivered to customers.

Produksi edge protector dimulai dengan memotong bahan baku

Paper Box terbuat dari bahan baku kertas yang sudah dilaminasi 
Polyethylene. Kertas ini kemudian dimasukan ke dalam mesin yang 
bisa memotong sesuai dengan pola yang sudah dirancang 
sebelumnya. Selesai proses dalam mesin, dilakukan pemisahan sisa 
kertas dari pola yang sudah ditentukan. Paper Box selesai 
diproduksi, kemudian dikemas sedemikian rupa dan siap dikirim ke 
pelanggan.
Production of Edge protector begun by slitting raw materials 
Jumbo Roll Paper into smaller pieces. Then, these pieces are 
processed into layering and bending machine. This machine 
presses slitted paper to form edge protectors which are then dried. 
Once dried, edge protectors are ready to be packaged and 
delivered to customers.
Paper Box is made from paper raw materials that have been 
laminated by Polyethylene. This paper is then put into a machine to 
be cut according to designed patterns. After the process in the 
machine, separating the remaining paper from pattern was carried 
out. Paper box is produced then packaged in such a way and ready 
to be sent to the customer.

Paper Bag terbuat dari bahan baku kertas. Kertas ini kemudian

Hexa Wrap merupakan pelindung barang dari benturan yang 
terbuat dari kertas coklat sebagai alternatif pengganti bubble 
wrap. Produksi dimulai dari penempatan bahan baku kertas ke 
dalam mesin, kemudian mesin dengan pisaunya membentuk

Paper Bag is made from paper as raw material. This paper is then 
fed into a machine which can cut according to a pre-designed 
pattern. The next stage is to attach the paper rope handle to the 
Paper Bag. Paper bag that is ready with handle is then packaged 
and ready to be sent to customers.
Hexa Wrap is a protective item from impact made of brown paper 
as an alternative to bubble wrap. Production starts from placing the 
paper raw material into the machine, then the machine uses a knife 
to form certain cuts so that when the paper is stretched it forms a 
honeycomb-like texture. Hexa Wrap paper is prepared in the form 
of rolls and ready to be sent to customers.

Perseroan memiliki 2 sub sektor yaitu: sub sektor kertas dan sub 
sektor kimia. Sub Sektor kertas terdiri dari kertas konversi yang 
diproduksi oleh PT Alkindo Naratama Tbk (ALDO), Entitas Induk, 
dan kertas coklat yang diproduksi oleh PT Eco Paper Indonesia 
(EPI), Entitas Anak. Sedangkan sub sektor kimia terdiri dari

Aset 
Aset konsolidasi meningkat sebesar 12% atau sebesar Rp182,9 
miliar dari tahun 2022 sebesar Rp1,569 triliun menjadi Rp1,752 
triliun tahun 2023. 
Aset Lancar
Aset lancar konsolidasi meningkat sebesar Rp162,7 miliar dari 
tahun 2022 sebesar Rp714,1 miliar menjadi Rp876,8 miliar di tahun 
2023 atau bertumbuh sebesar 23%. 
Kenaikan Aset lancar konsolidasi disebabkan oleh penambahan kas 
dan setara kas juga peningkatan piutang dagang yang dilakukan 
secara kredit. Dengan beroperasinya Paper Machine 2 (PM2) di 
Entitas Anak, pembelian bahan baku semakin bertambah sehingga 
persediaan dan uang muka pembelian mengalami kenaikan. 
Environmentally Friendly Products
Focus on developing environmentally friendly products manufac-
tured through environmentally friendly processes.
The Company has 2 sub-sectors, namely: paper sub-sector and 
chemical sub-sector. The paper sub-sector consists of converting 
paper produced by PT Alkindo Naratama Tbk (ALDO), the Parent 
Entity, and brown paper produced by PT Eco Paper Indonesia 
(EPI), a Subsidiary. Meanwhile, the chemical sub-sector consists of 
the distribution of textile chemicals carried out by PT Swisstex 
Naratama Indonesia (SNI), a Subsidiary, and polymers produced by 
PT Alfa Polimer Indonesia (API), a Subsidiary.
Assets
Consolidated Assets increased by 12% or by Rp182.9 billion from 
Rp1.569 trillion in 2022 to Rp1.752 trillion in 2023.
Current Assets 
Consolidated current assets increased by Rp162.7 billion from 
Rp714.1 billion in 2022 to Rp876.8 billion in 2023, or grew by 23%. 
The increase in consolidated current assets was caused by 
additional cash and cash equivalents as well as an increase in trade 
receivables carried on credit. With the operation of Paper Machine 
2 (PM2) at the Subsidiary, purchases of raw materials have 
increased so that inventory and down payments for purchases have 
increased.

The Company and Subsidiaries monitor their capital using gearing

funds at a reasonable cost.

payables and accrued expenses less cash on hand and in banks.

maintains healthy capital ratios in order to support its business and
