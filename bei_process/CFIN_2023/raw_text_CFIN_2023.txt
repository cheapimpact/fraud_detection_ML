TINJAUAN MAKROEKONOMI
GLOBAL DAN NASIONAL
Global and National Macroeconomic Review
Kinerja ekonomi Indonesia tetap kuat di tengah dinamika 
perekonomian global. Pertumbuhan ekonomi tercatat naik sebesar 
5,05 persen (yoy). Capaian tersebut didukung oleh permintaan 
domestik, sejalan dengan kenaikan konsumsi rumah tangga 
dan pemerintah, serta peningkatan investasi. Namun, dinamika 
perekonomian global seperti tingkat inflasi yang masih persisten 
tinggi, suku bunga yang cenderung tinggi, intensifikasi perang 
Rusia-Ukraina, 
geoeconomics 
fragmentation, 
debt 
distress, 
termasuk volatilitas di sektor keuangan menciptakan tantangan 
bagi perekonomian dan sektor keuangan domestik.
Indonesia’s economic performance remains strong amidst global 
economic dynamics. Economic growth was recorded grew at 
5.05 percent (yoy). This achievement was supported by domestic 
demand, in line with the increase in household and government 
consumption, as well as increased investment. However, global 
economic dynamics such as persistently high inflation rates, 
interest rates that tend to be high, intensification of the war Russia-
Ukraine, geoeconomic fragmentation, debt distress, including 
volatility in the financial sector, create challenges for the domestic 
economy and financial sector.

(revised 2014) concerning “Related Party Disclosures”.
