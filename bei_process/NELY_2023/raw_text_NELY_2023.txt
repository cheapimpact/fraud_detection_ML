Operational Review per Business Segment
Perseroan mempunyai 3 segmen yaitu Jasa 
Penyewaan Kapal, Jasa Perbaikan dan 
Konstruksi Kapal, dan Jasa Lainnya.
Pada segmen Penyewaan Kapal, pendapatan 
meningkat sebesar Rp 170 miliar atau setara 
68%.  Hasil segmen meningkat sebesar 
Rp 121juta atau setara 84%. Aset segmen 
dan liabilitas segmen meningkat masing-
masing sebesar Rp 184 miliar atau 29% dan 
Rp 38 miliar atau 51%. Laba tahun berjalan 
mengalami peningkatan sebesar Rp 102 miliar 
atau setara 81%.
Pada segmen jasa perbaikan dan pembangunan 
kapal, 
pendapatan 
meningkat 
sebesar 
Rp 34,1 miliar atau setara 58,2%. Hasil 
segmen menurun sebesar Rp 7,7 miliar atau 
setara 64,9%. Aset segmen menurun sebesar 
Rp 8,1 miliar atau setara 8,7% dan Liabilitas 
segmen menurun sebesar Rp 18,8 miliar atau 
setara 56,2%. Laba tahun berjalan meningkat 
sebesar Rp 5,4 miliar atau setara 103,5%.
Pada segmen jasa lainnya, pendapatan 
meningkat sebesar Rp 8 miliar, hasil segmen 
juga menurun Rp 1,2 miliar, aset menurun 
sebesar Rp 7,7 miliar atau setara 33%, 
liabilitas meningkat sebesar Rp 2,9miliar atau 
setara 41% dan keuntungan tahun ini menurun 
sebesar Rp 8,8 miliar. 
The Company has 3 segments, namely Ship 
Rental Services, Ship Repair and Construction 
Services, and Other Services.
In the Ship Rental segment, revenue increased 
by IDR 170 billion or the equivalent of 68%. 
Segment results increased by IDR 121 million 
or the equivalent of 84%. Segment assets 
and segment liabilities increased by IDR 184 
billion or 29% and IDR 38 billion or 51%, 
respectively. Profit for the year increased by 
IDR 102 billion or the equivalent of 81%.
In the ship repair and construction services 
segment, revenue increased by IDR 34,1 
billion or the equivalent of 58,2%. Segment 
results decreased by Rp 7.7 billion or 
the equivalent of 64,9%. Segment assets 
decreased by Rp 8 billion or the equivalent of 
8,1% and segment liabilities decreased by Rp 
18.8 billion or the equivalent of 56,2%. Profit 
for the year increased by Rp 5.4 billion or the 
equivalent of 103.5%.
In the other services segment, revenue 
increased by IDR 8 billion, segment results 
also decreased by IDR 1.2 billion, assets 
decreased by IDR. 7.7 billion or the equivalent 
of 33%, liabilities increased by IDR 2.9 billion 
or the equivalent of 41% and profits this year 
decreased by IDR 8.8 billion.
KONDISI PEREKONOMIAN DAN INDUSTRI
Economy and Industry Climates
Penanaman Modal Asing (FDI) di Indonesia 
naik 13,7% dibandingkan tahun sebelumnya 
menjadi 744 triliun rupiah, khususnya pada 
industri pengolahan nikel karena semakin 
besarnya minat terhadap kendaraan listrik 
secara global. Biaya logistik Indonesia juga 
berkontribusi terhadap 23% PDB Indonesia 
karena posisi geografis alaminya sebagai 
negara kepulauan. Terakhir, ekspor juga 
naik tipis sebesar 1,3% dibandingkan tahun 
sebelumnya, sementara impor turun 1,6%.
Di tengah fluktuasi harga dan permintaan 
komoditas global, permintaan bahan baku di 
Indonesia relatif stabil.
Foreign direct Investments (FDI) in Indonesia 
rose 13.7% compared to previous year to 
744 Trillion rupiah, particularly in the nickel 
processing industry due to greater interest in 
electric vehicles globally. Indonesia’s logistics 
costs has also contributed to 23% of Indonesia’s 
GDP due to its natural geographical position 
as an archipelago. Lastly, exports we also 
up marginally at 1.3% compared to the year 
before, while imports declined 1.6%.
Amidst globally fluctuating commodity prices 
and demand, the demand for raw materials in 
Indonesia is relatively stable.
