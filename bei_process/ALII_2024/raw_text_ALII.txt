Analisis Perekonomian Global
Perekonomian global saat ini berada dalam tahap perubahan 
yang cepat dan penuh ketidakpastian. Proyeksi untuk 
tahun 2024 menunjukkan bahwa pertumbuhan ekonomi 
dunia diperkirakan sedikit melambat, dengan angka yang 
diperkirakan mencapai 3,2%, dibandingkan dengan tahun 
sebelumnya yang berada di angka 3,3%. Amerika Serikat, 
sebagai salah satu kekuatan ekonomi utama, diperkirakan 
akan tetap menunjukkan kekuatan, meskipun ada risiko 
pemanasan ekonomi yang akan membayangi. Peningkatan 
defisit fiskal dan beban utang pemerintah menjadi ancaman 
yang semakin nyata, menambah kompleksitas perekonomian 
global. Sementara itu, di belahan dunia lain, Tiongkok dan 
kawasan Eropa diperkirakan akan menghadapi tantangan 
dengan penurunan permintaan domestik. Selain itu, dampak 
dari kebijakan tarif dagang yang tinggi dari Amerika Serikat 
masih akan memberi tekanan pada kedua kawasan ini, 
sehingga memperlambat pemulihan ekonomi mereka.
Namun, di tengah situasi yang penuh tantangan ini, beberapa 
negara berkembang justru menunjukkan kinerja ekonomi 
yang cukup baik. Negara-negara seperti India, Indonesia, dan 
beberapa negara di kawasan Emerging Markets Economies 
(EMEs) mampu menjaga pertumbuhan ekonomi yang stabil, 
yang mana hal ini didorong oleh permintaan domestik yang 
kuat meski ada hambatan dalam ekspor akibat perlambatan 
ekonomi global. Dari sisi inflasi, terdapat perkembangan 
positif yang tercatat pada tahun 2024, dengan tingkat inflasi 
global yang diperkirakan turun menjadi 5,2%, lebih rendah 
dibandingkan dengan 6,2% pada tahun 2023. Kondisi ini 
memberikan angin segar bagi perekonomian dunia, meskipun 
tantangan tetap ada, dan ketidakpastian masih membayangi 
prospek pertumbuhan ekonomi jangka panjang.

Global Economic Analysis
The global economy is currently undergoing rapid changes 
xamid uncertainty. Projections for 2024 indicated a slight 
slowdown in global economic growth, with an estimated growth 
of 3.2%, compared to 3.3% in the previous year. The United 
States, as one of the world’s major economic powerhouses, 
is expected to maintain its strength, although concerns over 
economic overheating remain. Rising fiscal deficits and 
government debt burdens pose increasing risks, adding to 
the complexity of the global economic landscape. Meanwhile, 
in other regions, China and Europe are expected to face 
challenges due to weakening domestic demand. Additionally, 
the impact of high U.S. trade tariffs will continue to pressure 
these regions, further slowing their economic recovery.
However, amidst this challenging situation, several emerging 
economies have demonstrated strong economic performance. 
Countries such as India, Indonesia, and select nations in 
the Emerging Market Economies (EMEs) have maintained stable 
economic growth, which is driven by robust domestic demand, 
despite export challenges resulting from the global slowdown. 
On the inflation front, there were positive developments 
recorded in 2024, with global inflation expected to decrease to 
5.2%, down from 6.2% in 2023. This brings some relief to the 
global economy, even as challenges persist and uncertainties 
continue to overshadow long-term growth prospects.

Domestic Economic Analysis
Indonesia’s economy has demonstrated solid resilience 
amid various global challenges throughout 2024, achieving 
a growth rate of 5.03%, only slightly slower than 5.05% 
in the previous year. This minor slowdown still reflects 
Indonesia’s ability to maintain economic stability and 
sustain positive growth momentum. This performance was 
the result of a strong domestic consumption, continuous 
investment growth, as well as fiscal and monetary policies 
that, fortunately, did not face substantial challenges in 
maintaining macroeconomic stability. Although exports 
faced pressure due to weakening global demand, domestic 
economic strengthening, as well as enduring creativity across 
different community sectors, in a structural manner, becomes 
a key pillar of national resilience.
From a sectoral perspective, Indonesia’s economic growth 
exhibited diverse yet impressive patterns. The fastest-growing
sector was other services sector, which recorded an increase 
of 9.80%, followed by transportation and warehousing, which 
grew by 8.69%, and accommodation and food & beverage 
services, which expanded by 8.56%. These three sectors 
performed exceptionally well, highlighting the robustness of 
domestic-driven industries. Despite global challenges, these 
sectors continued to thrive, supported by stable domestic 
demand and the recovery of industries previously affected 
by the pandemic. As a result, even as the global economy 
faces various risks, Indonesia has been able to sustain its 
solid growth through economic diversification and enduring 
creativity across different community sectors that enhance 
national resilience.

highperformance diesel engines, typically ranging

entered into time charter for an additional 2 assist 
tugs. Assist tugs are used to assist and support 
180 feet and 300 feet barges during docking at jetties 
or ports.

used to move the transported coal from 180 feet barges

dedicated berthing facilities for coal loading and unloading, 
heavy equipment such as excavators, grab cranes, dozers, 
wheel loaders, and conveyor belts with a capacity of 1,500 
MT per hour, stockpiles with a capacity of 230,000 MT, 
as well as other facilities.

Kebijakan dan Pembagian Dividen
Kebijakan Dividen
Sesuai dengan kebijakan Perseroan dan peraturan perundang-
undangan, 
pembagian 
dividen 
dilakukan 
berdasarkan 
keputusan pemegang saham dalam RUPS Tahunan, yang 
diambil berdasarkan rekomendasi dari Direksi. Perseroan 
akan membagikan dividen hanya apabila mencatatkan 
laba bersih, dengan mempertimbangkan sejumlah faktor, 
seperti hasil operasional, arus kas yang tersedia, dan 
kondisi keuangan Perseroan secara keseluruhan. Selain 
itu, keputusan pembagian dividen juga akan disesuaikan 
dengan rencana pengembangan usaha Perseroan di masa 
depan, untuk memastikan bahwa distribusi dividen tidak 
mengganggu kelangsungan dan pertumbuhan Perseroan 
jangka panjang. Dengan demikian, kebijakan dividen ini 
bertujuan untuk menjaga keseimbangan antara memberikan 
imbal hasil kepada pemegang saham dan memastikan 
ketersediaan dana untuk pengembangan usaha.
Sesuai 
dengan 
ketentuan 
tersebut, 
Perseroan 
dapat 
membagikan dividen tunai sebanyak-banyaknya hingga 
80% dari laba tahun berjalan, setelah menyisihkan cadangan 
wajib dan dana untuk belanja modal dan kebutuhan modal 
kerja. Besarnya pembagian dividen akan bergantung pada 
hasil kegiatan usaha dan arus kas yang tersedia, serta 
mempertimbangkan kebutuhan investasi dan pengembangan 
usaha di masa depan. Keputusan ini diambil untuk memastikan 
bahwa meskipun dividen dibagikan dalam jumlah yang optimal 
kepada pemegang saham, Perseroan tetap memiliki dana 
yang cukup untuk mendukung operasional dan pertumbuhan 
berkelanjutan.
Pembagian Dividen
Perseroan tidak melakukan pembagian dividen pada tahun 
2024 maupun 2023. Sisa laba bersih Perseroan dibukukan 
sebagai saldo laba (retained earnings) untuk memperkuat struktur 
permodalan Perseroan.
Program Kepemilikan Saham oleh Karyawan 
dan/atau Manajemen
Pada tahun 2024, Perseroan mengimplementasikan program 
kepemilikan saham oleh karyawan sebagai bagian dari upaya 
untuk meningkatkan keterlibatan dan motivasi karyawan serta 
manajemen dalam pertumbuhan Perseroan yang dialokasikan 
dalam program ESA sebanyak 8.002.000 saham. Program ini 
memberikan kesempatan kepada karyawan untuk memiliki 
saham Perseroan, yang diharapkan dapat memperkuat rasa 
kepemilikan dan mendorong kinerja yang lebih baik seiring 
dengan pencapaian tujuan jangka panjang Perseroan.
Dividend Policy and Distribution
Dividend Policy
In accordance with the Company’s policy and applicable laws 
and regulations, dividend distribution is carried out based on 
the shareholders decisions at the Annual General Meeting of 
Shareholders (GMS), following recommendations from the 
Board of Directors. The Company will distribute dividends only 
if it records a net profit, taking into account several factors 
such as the Company’s operational performance, available 
cash flow, and the overall financial condition. Additionally, 
the decision on dividend distribution will be aligned with the 
Company’s future business development plans to ensure that 
the distribution does not disrupt the Company’s long-term 
sustainability and growth. Thus, the dividend policy has the 
purpose to balance providing returns to the shareholders while 
ensuring sufficient funds for business development.
In line with this policy, the Company can distribute cash 
dividends of up to 80% of profit for the current year, after setting 
aside mandatory reserves and funds for capital expenditures 
and working capital needs. The amount of dividend distribution 
will depend on business performance and available cash flows 
while considering investment needs and future business 
expansion. This decision is made to ensure that while 
the shareholders receive an optimal dividend payout, the 
Company retains sufficient funds to support operations and 
sustainable growth.
Dividend Distribution
The Company decided not to distribute any dividends in 
2024 or 2023. The remaining Company’s net profit was 
recorded as retained earnings to strengthen the Company’s 
capital structure.
Share Ownership Program by Employees 
and/or Management
In 2024, the Company implemented an employee stock 
ownership program as part of efforts to improve employee 
engagement and motivation in the Company’s growth 
allocated to ESA program amounting to 8.002.000 shares. 
This program provides employees with the opportunity 
to own shares in the Company, which is expected to 
strengthen their sense of ownership and drive better 
performance in line with the Company’s long-term goals.

as of 31 March 2024.
