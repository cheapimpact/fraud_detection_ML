Company.

Infrastruktur Terus Dikembangkan
Infrastructure Continues to Develop
The government in the Draft State Budget (RAPBN) has 
set a budget for infrastructure of Rp 423.4 trillion for 2024. 
The budget has increased by 8.01% or Rp31.4 trillion when 
compared to the budget realization for infrastructure for 
2023 which was reported at Rp392 trillion. In 2024, the 
government will still prioritize development in several 
sectors, especially in sectors that have a significant 
impact in contributing to national economic growth such 
as connectivity and transportation aspects in the form 
of regional road and toll road construction, energy and 
electricity in order to ensure a sustainable and equitable 
supply of electricity to all regions of Indonesia, as well as 
basic service infrastructure such as the provision of clean 
water and quality sanitation for a decent life. Furthermore, 
there is also a budget for the Capital City of the Archipelago 
(IKN) in the 2024 infrastructure budget, where some 
important infrastructure such as airports and toll roads have 
begun to be built in 2023.
Pemerintah dalam Rancangan Anggaran Pendapatan 
dan Belanja Negara (RAPBN) telah menetapkan anggaran 
untuk infrastruktur sebesar Rp 423,4 triliun untuk tahun 
2024. Anggaran tersebut mengalami peningkatan sebesar 
8.01% atau Rp31,4 triliun jika dibandingkan dengan 
realisasi anggaran untuk infrastruktur untuk tahun 2023 
yang dilaporkan sebesar Rp392 triliun. Di tahun 2024, 
pemerintah masih akan memprioritaskan pembangunan 
dalam beberapa sektor, terutama dalam sektor yang 
berdampak signifikan dalam kontribusinya terhadap 
pertumbuhan ekonomi nasional seperti aspek konektivitas 
dan transportasi yakni berupa pembangunan jalan daerah 
dan jalan tol, energi dan ketenagalistrikan agar dapat 
menjamin pasokan listrik yang bersifat berkelanjutan dan 
merata ke seluruh wilayah Indonesia, serta infrastruktur 
layanan dasar seperti penyediaan air bersih dan sanitasi 
yang berkualitas untuk kehidupan yang layak. Lebih lanjut, 
terdapat juga anggaran untuk Ibu Kota Nusantara (IKN) 
dalam anggaran infrastruktur 2024, dimana beberapa 
infrastruktur penting seperti bandara dan jalan tol telah 
mulai di bangun pada tahun 2023.

Prinsip Tata Kelola Perusahaan yang Baik 
(GCG)
Principles of Good Corporate Governance (GCG)
Prosedur GCG mewajibkan Perseroan menerapkan 
prinsip-prinsip Tata Kelola dengan berlandaskan pada 
pedoman dan praktek yang berlaku universal, disamping 
memenuhi peraturan perundang-undangan. Penerapan 
GCG dipandang tidak hanya untuk memenuhi kaidah 
dan etika bisnis, namun juga merupakan salah satu 
persyaratan agar usaha Perseroan dapat tumbuh 
berkelanjutan 
berdampingan 
dengan 
meningkatnya 
kepercayaan pemangku kepentingan termasuk elemen 
masyarakat. Dalam menerapkan prinsip-prisip GCG, 
manajemen dan karyawan Perseroan mengacu pada 
5 (lima) prinsip dasar yaitu: transparansi, akuntabilitas, 
responsibilitas, independensi, dan kewajaran. 
GCG procedures require the Company to apply Good 
Corporate Governance principles based on universally 
applicable guidelines and practices, in addition to 
complying with laws and regulations. The implementation 
of GCG is seen not only to fulfill business rules and 
ethics, but is also one of the requirements so that the 
Company’s business can grow sustainably side by side 
with increasing stakeholder trust including elements of 
the community. In applying the principles of GCG, the 
Company’s management and employees refer to 5 (five) 
basic principles, namely: transparency, accountability, 
responsibility, independence, and fairness.

Prinsip transparansi dilakukan dengan cara

Transparency
The principle of transparency is carried out by 
providing information quickly, precisely, and 
accurately through intensive communication 
media that is managed professionally, so 
that Shareholders, creditors, the public 
and all stakeholders can clearly know the 
performance and management activities of 
the Company and can provide suggestions. 
for the progress of the Company, but still 
pay attention to the applicable information 
management rules to protect the interests 
of the Company by establishing information 
confidentiality rules that limit access to 
information by interested parties.
