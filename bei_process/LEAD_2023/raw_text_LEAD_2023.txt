Pertumbuhan ekonomi Indonesia tetap kuat di tengah 
perlambatan ekonomi global. Data Badan Pusat Statistik 
(BPS) menunjukkan pertumbuhan ekonomi triwulan IV 
2023 tumbuh sebesar 5,04% (yoy), meningkat dibandingkan 
dengan pertumbuhan pada triwulan sebelumnya sebesar 
4,94% (yoy). Dengan perkembangan tersebut, pertumbuhan 
ekonomi Indonesia secara keseluruhan tahun 2023 tercatat 
tumbuh kuat sebesar 5,05% (yoy). 
Ketidakpastian global masih terus membayangi dunia 
persewaan kapal offshore, terutama karena perubahan atau 
peralihan dari sumber energi minyak dan gas bumi menjadi 
sumber – sumber energy terbarukan dan harga minyak 
yang sudah mulai menurun dibandingkan tahun 2022 
dimana saat itu perang Rusia dan Ukrainia dimulai sehingga 
melambungkan harga minyak di atas USD 100 per barrel. 
Di tahun 2023 ini, harga minyak mulai terkendali tetapi masih 
cukup tinggi dan menjadi alasan meningkatnya aktivitas 
eksplorasi minyak dan gas bumi, terutama di luar Indonesia. 
Dengan meningkatnya aktivitas eksplorasi minyak dan gas 
bumi secara global, permintaan atas kapal – kapal offshore 
yang perseroan miliki juga meningkat dan juga terlihat 
peningkatan harga sewa kapal terutama harga sewa kapal di 
luar Indonesia.
Indonesia’s economic growth remains strong amid the 
global economic slowdown. Data from the Central Statistics 
Agency (BPS) showed that economic growth in the fourth 
quarter of 2023 grew by 5.04% (yoy), an increase compared 
to growth in the previous quarter of 4.94% (yoy). With these 
developments, Indonesia’s overall economic growth in 2023 
was recorded to grow strongly by 5.05% (yoy).
Global uncertainty continues to overshadow the world 
of offshore vessel charters, mainly due to the change or 
transition from oil and gas energy sources to renewable 
energy sources and oil prices that have begun to decline 
compared to 2022 when the war between Russia and Ukraine 
began, catapulting oil prices above USD 100 per barrel. 
In 2023, oil prices began to be controlled but were still 
quite high and became the reason for increased oil and gas 
exploration activities, especially outside Indonesia. 
With the increase in oil and gas exploration activities globally, 
the demand for the company’s offshore vessels has also 
increased and also seen an increase in vessel charter rates, 
especially vessel charter rates outside Indonesia.

Financial Review
Diskusi dan analisa keuangan Informasi keuangan diambil 
dari Laporan Keuangan PT Logindo Samudramakmur Tbk 
tanggal 31 Desember 2023 dan untuk tahun yang berakhir 
pada tanggal tersebut yang disusun oleh manajemen 
Perusahaan sesuai dengan Standar Akuntansi Keuangan di 
Indonesia, yang telah diaudit oleh Kantor Akuntan Publik 
Purwantono, Sungkoro & Surja, berdasarkan Standar Audit 
yang ditetapkan oleh Institut Akuntan Publik Indonesia, 
dengan opini audit  dengan modifikasian sebagaimana 
tercantum dalam laporannya tanggal 31 Mei 2024 yang 
tercantum dalam publikasi ini. Informasi tersebut bukan 
merupakan penyajian yang lengkap dari laporan keuangan.
Aset
Pada tahun 2023, Perseroan mencatatkan aset sebesar 
US$129,52 juta. Angka ini mengalami penurunan sebesar 
3,17% dibandingkan dengan tahun 2022 yang tercatat 
sebesar US$133,76 juta. Penurunan ini terutama disebabkan 
karena akumulasi penyusutan kapal dan adanya tambahan 
penyisihan penurunan nilai kapal.
Aset Lancar
Aset Lancar pada tahun 2023 mengalami peningkatan 
sebesar 8,10% atau sebesar US$2,06 juta menjadi US$27,46 
juta dari tahun 2022 yang tercatat sebesar US$25,40 juta. 
Peningkatan ini disebabkan oleh peningkatan uang muka 
pihak ketiga sehubungan dengan kegiatan operasional 
Perseroan sebesar 559,92% atau sekitar US$2,28 juta.
Financial discussion and analysis of Financial information 
is taken from the Financial Report of PT Logindo 
Samudramakmur Tbk dated December 31, 2023 and for 
the year ended on that date which was prepared by the 
Company’s management in accordance with Financial 
Accounting Standards in Indonesia, which has been audited 
by the Purwantono, Sungkoro & Surja Public Accounting Firm, 
based on the Audit Standards established by the Indonesian 
Institute of Public Accountants, with a qualified opinion as 
stated in its report dated May 31, 2024, which is not included 
in this publication. This information does not constitute a 
complete presentation of the financial statements.
Asset
In 2023, the Company will record assets of US$129.52 million. 
This figure has decreased by 3.17% compared to 2022 which 
was recorded at US$133.76 million. This decrease was mainly 
due to accumulated depreciation of ships and additional 
provisions for impairment of ship values.
Current Asset
Current Assets in 2023 will increase by 8.10% or US$2.06 
million to US$27.46 million from 2022 which was recorded 
at US$25.40 million. This increase was caused by an increase 
in third party advances in connection with the Company’s 
operational activities amounting to 559.92% or around 
US$2.28 million.

Sepanjang tahun 2023, Liabilitas Jangka Panjang Perseroan 
tercatat sebesar US$94,08 juta, menurun 2,09% atau sekitar 
US$2,01 juta dibandingkan dengan tahun 2022 yang tercatat 
sebesar US$96,09 juta. Penurunan ini disebabkan oleh 
pinjaman bank jangka panjang yang telah menjadi kategori 
lancar pada tahun 2023.
Ekuitas
Sementara Ekuitas Perusahaan di tahun 2023 mencatatkan 
kinerja sebesar US$23,82 juta, menurun 18,29% atau sekitar 
US$5,33 juta dibandingkan dengan tahun 2022 yang tercatat 
sebesar US$29,15 juta. Penurunan ini disebabkan oleh 
kerugian yang dialami Perseroan sebesar US$5,41 juta
Non-Current Assets
The Company’s Non-Current Assets in 2023 was US$102.06 
million, decreased 5.81% or US$6.30 million compared 
to the US$108.36 million in 2022. This was mainly due 
to accumulated depreciation of vessels and additional 
impairment of vessel values.
Liabilities
Meanwhile in Liabilities, it increased 1.04% or US$1.09 million 
to US$105.70 million from the US$104.61 million in 2022. This 
was due to the rising accrued interest expense on long-term 
bank loans from US$13.24 million to US$19.24 million.
Current Liabilities
In 2023, the Company’s Current Liabilities reached US$11.62 
million, increasing 36.40% or US$3.10 million compared to 
the US$8.52 million in 2022. This was mainly due to long-
term bank loans turning current in 2023, reaching US$6.60 
million or increasing 60.81% to US$2.50 million compared to 
the US$4.10 million in 2022.
Non-Current Liabilities
In 2023, the Company’s Non-Current Liabilities were US$94.08 
million, decreasing 2.09% or US$2.01 million compared to 
the US$96.09 million in 2022. This was caused by long-term 
bank loans turning current in 2023.
Non-Current Liabilities
Meanwhile, the Company’s Equity in 2023 reached US$23.82 
million, decreasing 18.29% or US$5.33 million compared 
to the US$29.15 million in 2022. This was caused by the 
Company’s losses during the year for the amount of US$ 5.41 
million.

sebesar 14,44%.

Sementara pada Laba Usaha, Perusahaan mencatatkan

31,85% dan menurunnya Beban Operasi Lainnya sebesar 
96,57%.
Rugi Bersih Tahun Berjalan

peningkatan Laba Bruto Perusahaan.
Cost of Revenues
The Company’s Cost of Revenues in 2023 was US$28.04 
million, increasing 8.22% or US$2.13 million compared to the 
US$25.91 million in 2022. This was mainly due to the vessel 
fuel, increasing 97.07% to US$3.78 million in 2023 from 
US$1.92 million in 2022 as a result of increased mobilization.
Gross Profits

General and Administrative Expenses

compared to the US$4.56 million in 2022. This was caused by

138.28% or US$2.68 million to US$0.74 million Profit from

Loss before Income Tax Expenses

Expense of US$5.05 million, a decrease of 10.65% or around

in Gross Profit of 31.85% and a decrease in Other Operating 
Expenses of 96.57%.
Net Loss for the Year

year was US$5.41 million, decreasing 9.70% compared to the 
US$5.99 million in 2022. This was influenced by the increasing 
Company Revenue. The Company’s Net Loss Margin in 2023 
was 16.50%.
Comprehensive Loss for the Year
The Company’s Comprehensive Loss in 2023 was US$5.35 
million, decreasing 11.48% or US$0.69 million compared 
to the US$6.04 million in 2022. This was mainly due to the 
increasing Company’s Gross Profit.
