Gambaran Umum Ekonomi dan Industri
Economic and Industry Overview
Pertumbuhan ekonomi global pada tahun 2024 menurut IMF World 
Economic Outlook Update (Jan-2025) diproyeksikan mencapai 3,2%. 
Proyeksi ini lebih rendah dari capaian tahun 2023 sebesar 3,3%. Situasi 
global masih dapat terus bertumbuh walaupun dihadapkan sejumlah 
tantangan seperti konflik geopolitik dan tantangan komoditas global, 
yang terutama dipengaruhi oleh perubahan iklim. 
Di tengah tantangan ekonomi global, ekonomi Indonesia masih 
tercatat bertumbuh resilien. Sepanjang tahun 2024, pertumbuhan 
ekonomi Indonesia menurut Badan Pusat Statistik (BPS) tercatat 
sebesar 5,03% sepanjang tahun (c-to-c). Capaian pertumbuhan pada 
tahun 2024 tercatat lebih rendah dari pertumbuhan sepanjang tahun 
2023 sebesar 5,05% (c-to-c). 
 
Resiliensi pertumbuhan ekonomi terlihat dari tingkat pertumbuhan 
yang tetap terjaga di atas 5% sejak tahun kuartal IV tahun 2021. 
Terjaganya konsumsi rumah tangga, investasi, dan ekspor nonmigas 
turut mendorong pertumbuhan perekonomian, terutama pada akhir 
tahun 2024, begitu pula dengan belanja pemerintah yang menopang 
permintaan domestik. 
PT Gunanusa Era Mandiri Tbk sebagai entitas induk memiliki aktivitas 
usaha utama di bidang produksi dan pengolahan produk kacang-
kacangan. Bila dilihat dari lapangan usaha sesuai dengan data BPS, 
maka lapangan usaha yang erat kaitannya dengan Perseroan adalah 
“Penyediaan Akomodasi & Makan Minum” dan “Transportasi & 
Pergudangan”. 
According to the IMF World Economic Outlook Update (Jan-2025), 
global economic growth in 2024 is projected to reach 3.2%, slightly 
lower than the 3.3% recorded in 2023. Despite facing challenges 
such as geopolitical conflicts and global commodity issues, primarily 
influenced by climate change, the global economy continues to 
expand.  
Amid these global economic challenges, Indonesia’s economy has 
remained resilient. Throughout 2024, Indonesia's economy grew by 
5.03% (c-to-c), according to Statistics Indonesia (BPS). This growth is 
slightly lower than the 5.05% (c-to-c) recorded in 2023.  
The resilience of Indonesia’s economic growth is reflected in its ability 
to maintain a growth rate above 5% since Q4 2021. Key contributors 
to this sustained growth include household consumption, investment, 
and non-oil and gas exports, particularly in late 2024. Additionally, 
government spending played a crucial role in supporting domestic 
demand.  
As a holding entity, PT Gunanusa Era Mandiri Tbk operates primarily in 
the production and processing of nuts. Based on BPS sectoral data, the 
business sectors closely related to the Company are “Accommodation 
& Food and Beverage Services” and “Transportation & Warehousing”.

In 2024, the 3 (three) fastest-growing business sectors were “Other 
Services”, “Transportation & Warehousing”, “Accommodation & Food 
and Beverage Services”. The “Accommodation & Food and Beverage 
Services” sector grew by 8.56% (c-to-c), driven by increased public 
mobility and activities in both the public and private sectors. Public 
mobility growth was evident in rising passenger numbers across all 
transportation modes, including rail transport by 17.81% (c-to-c), 
sea transport by 20.46% (c-to-c), air transport by 5.70% (c-to-c). 
On the other side, the tourism sector also experienced significant 
growth in 2024, with domestic tourist trips increasing by 21.61% 
(c-to-c) and international tourist arrivals rising by 19.05% (c-to-c). 
Household consumption remained stable throughout 2024, supported 
by real retail sales index growth of 3.01% (c-to-c) and consumer 
goods import value increase of 5.37% (c-to-c). The government's 
inflation control measures also played a role in maintaining consumer 
purchasing power, with December 2024 inflation recorded at 1.57% 
(y-on-y).  
The positive economic growth and strong performance in key business 
sectors, supported by stable consumer spending, have had a favorable 
impact on the overall business development of the Company.

AKTIVITAS USAHA
Kontrak Manufaktur dan Jasa Maklon
Aktivitas kontrak manufaktur dan jasa maklon berasal dari perjanjian 
kontrak manufaktur dengan pelanggan. Perseroan memproduksi 
produk sesuai dengan spesifikasi pelanggan dan bertanggung jawab 
terhadap pengadaan bahan baku, bahan pendukung, dan menutupi 
biaya konversi. Berdasarkan kontrak manufaktur, Perseroan melakukan 
aktivitas jasa maklon, mengubah bahan baku pelanggan dan bahan 
pendukung menjadi barang jadi.
Sepanjang tahun 2024, aktivitas kontrak manufaktur dan jasa maklon 
Perseroan berjalan dengan normal walaupun ada penurunan penjualan 
sebesar 7% dibandingkan tahun 2023 akibat adanya aksi boikot 
terhadap produk-produk Unilever.
Pendapatan bersih yang diterima Perseroan dari aktivitas kontrak 
manufaktur dan jasa maklon tahun 2024 tercatat sebesar Rp801,35 
miliar. Terjadi penurunan sebesar 7,07% dibandingkan pendapatan 
tahun sebelumnya sebesar Rp862,27 miliar. Perbedaan tersebut 
terjadi karena adanya aksi boikot produk Unilever sebagai akibat 
konflik geopolitik di Timur Tengah.
Penjualan Produk Kacang
Aktivitas penjualan produk kacang mengalami kenaikan sebesar 
21%. Pada tahun 2024, total kuantitas produk kacang yang dijual 
Perseroan mencapai 8.314.403 kg, lebih tinggi 4,29% dari tahun 
2023 sebesar 7.972.336 kg. 
Sepanjang tahun 2024, aktivitas penjualan produk kacang Perseroan 
berjalan dengan sangat baik, bahkan permintaan pasar luar negeri 
meningkat sebesar 50% dibandingkan tahun 2023.
Pendapatan bersih yang diterima Perseroan dari aktivitas penjualan 
produk kacang tahun 2024 tercatat sebesar Rp571,96 miliar. Terjadi 
peningkatan sebesar 18,90% dibandingkan pendapatan tahun 
sebelumnya sebesar Rp481,07 miliar.
Lain-Lain
Perseroan juga mencatatkan pendapatan lain-lain yang bersumber

pengecatan dan cubic printing industri otomotif.

BUSINESS ACTIVITIES
Contract Manufacturing and Toll Services 
The contract manufacturing and toll services activities stem from 
manufacturing agreements with customers. The Company produces 
products according to customer specifications and is responsible 
for procuring raw materials, supporting materials, and covering 
conversion costs. Under the contract manufacturing agreements, the 
Company provides toll services by transforming customer-supplied 
raw materials and supporting materials into finished goods.  
Throughout 2024, the Company's contract manufacturing and 
toll services operations ran normally despite a 7% sales decrease 
compared to 2023 due to the boycott action of Unilever products.
The net revenue generated from contract manufacturing and toll 
services in 2024 amounted to Rp801.35 billion. This represents a 
decrease of 7.07% compared to the previous year's revenue of 
Rp862,27 billion. This difference occurred due to boycott action 
against Unilever products due to the geopolitics conflict in Middle 
East.  
Nut Product Sales 
The Company’s nut product sales activities increased by 21%. In 2024, 
the total quantity of nut products sold by the Company reached 
8,314,403 kg, representing an increase of 4.29% compared to 2023, 
which recorded 7,972,336 kg.  
Throughout 2024, the Company's nut product sales operations ran 
extremely well, which is evident in the increase of international market 
demand by 50% compared to 2023. 
The net revenue from nut product sales in 2024 amounted to 
Rp571.96 billion. This represents an increase of 18.90% compared 
to the previous year's revenue of Rp481,07 billion.
Others 
The Company also recorded other income derived from printing 
and coating services for plastic, wood, metal, and ceramic products. 
The Company generated revenue from painting services and cubic 
printing of automotive industry. 
Throughout 2024, other business activities operated well, and even 
experienced an increase in sales by 23.4% compared to the year 2023.

Penjualan
Pada 31 Desember 2024, Perseroan mencatatkan penjualan sejumlah 
Rp1,44 triliun. Nilai penjualan mengalami peningkatan sebesar 3,10%, 
jika dibandingkan dengan perolehan pada tahun 2023 sejumlah 
Rp1,40 triliun. Perubahan nilai tersebut disebabkan oleh peningkatan 
penjualan segmen kacang-kacangan dan juga segmen pendapatan 
lain-lain.
Harga Pokok Penjualan 
Pada 31 Desember 2024, Perseroan mencatatkan harga pokok 
penjualan sejumlah Rp1,25 triliun. Nilai tersebut mengalami 
peningkatan sebesar 4,95%, jika dibandingkan dengan perolehan 
pada tahun 2023 sejumlah Rp1,19 triliun. Perubahan nilai terjadi 
karena kenaikan harga bahan baku kacang-kacangan dan biaya 
produksi karena kenaikan UMR.
Laba Kotor
Pada 31 Desember 2024, Perseroan mencatatkan laba kotor sejumlah 
Rp185,83 miliar. Nilai tersebut mengalami penurunan sebesar 7,86%, 
jika dibandingkan dengan perolehan pada tahun 2023 sejumlah 
Rp201,69 miliar. Perubahan nilai terjadi karena kenaikan harga pokok 
penjualan yang lebih tinggi dari pada kenaikan penjualan.
Laba Operasi
Pada 31 Desember 2024, Perseroan mencatatkan laba operasi sejumlah 
Rp108,67 miliar. Nilai tersebut mengalami penurunan sebesar 15,49%, 
jika dibandingkan dengan perolehan pada tahun 2023 sejumlah 
Rp128,59 miliar. Perubahan nilai terjadi karena kenaikan beban umum 
dan admistrasi akibat dari kenaikan dari gaji, upah, dan tunjangan.
Laba Neto Tahun Berjalan
Pada 31 Desember 2024, Perseroan mencatatkan laba neto tahun 
berjalan sejumlah Rp78,59 miliar. Nilai tersebut mengalami penurunan 
sebesar 17,10%, jika dibandingkan dengan perolehan pada tahun 
2023 sejumlah Rp94,81 miliar. Perubahan nilai terjadi karena 
penurunan laba kotor dan kenaikan biaya operasional.
Penghasilan Komprehensif Lain
Pada 31 Desember 2024, Perseroan mencatatkan penghasilan 
komprehensif lain setelah pajak sejumlah Rp511 juta. Nilai tersebut 
mengalami penurunan sebesar 99,62%, jika dibandingkan dengan 
perolehan pada tahun 2023 sejumlah Rp135 miliar. Perubahan nilai 
terjadi karena pada tahun 2023 terdapat keuntungan akibat dari 
revaluasi tanah sebesar Rp135 miliar sedangkan pada tahun 2024 
tanah tersebut tidak direvaluasi kembali.
Laba Komprehensif Tahun Berjalan
Pada 31 Desember 2024, Perseroan mencatatkan total laba 
komprehensif tahun berjalan sejumlah Rp79,10 miliar. Nilai tersebut 
mengalami penurunan sebesar 65,60%, jika dibandingkan dengan 
perolehan pada tahun 2023 sejumlah Rp229,97 miliar. Perubahan 
nilai terjadi karena tidak dilakukan revaluasi tanah pada tahun 2024, 
sedangkan tahun 2023 terdapat keuntungan dari revaluasi tanah.
Revenues
As of December 31, 2024, the Company recorded sales of Rp1.44 
trillion. The value represents an increase of 3.10% compared to Rp1.40 
trillion in 2023. The change in this value was due to an increase in 
sales from the nuts segment as well as from other income segments.
Cost of Revenues
As of December 31, 2024, the Company recorded a cost of revenues 
of Rp1.25 trillion.  The value represents an increase of 4.95% compared 
to Rp1.19 trillion in 2023. The change in this value was due to an 
increase in the price of nut-based raw materials and production costs 
due to the rise in the minimum wage (UMR).
Gross Profit
As of December 31, 2024, the Company recorded a gross profit of 
Rp185.83 billion. The value represents a decrease of 7.86% compared 
to Rp201.69 billion in 2023. The change in this value was due to an 
increase in the cost of revenues that was higher than the increase 
in sales.
Operating Profit
As of December 31, 2024, the Company recorded an operating 
profit of Rp108.67 billion. The value represents a decrease of 15.49% 
compared to Rp128.59 billion in 2023. The change in this value was 
due to an increase in general and administrative expenses due to 
higher salaries, wages, and allowances.
Net Profit for the Year
As of December 31, 2024, the Company recorded a net profit for the 
year of Rp78.59 billion. The value represents a decrease of 17.10% 
compared to Rp94.81 billion in 2023. The change in this value was due 
to a decrease in gross profit and an increase in operating expenses.
Other Comprehensive Income 
As of December 31, 2024, the Company recorded other comprehensive 
income, net of tax of Rp511 million. The value represents a decrease 
of 99.62% compared to Rp135 billion in 2023. The change in this 
value was due to a gain from land revaluation amounting to Rp135 
billion in 2023, whereas in 2024, the land was not revalued again.
Comprehensive Income for the Year
As of December 31, 2024, the Company recorded a total 
comprehensive income for the year of Rp79.10 billion. The value 
represents a decrease of 65.60% compared to Rp229.97 billion in 
2023. The change in this value was due to no land revaluation in 2024, 
whereas in 2023, there was a gain from land revaluation.

agricultural machinery, post-harvest facilities and infrastructure,
