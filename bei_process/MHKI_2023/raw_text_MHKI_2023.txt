Company Business Activities, the Company has a One 
Package Service which is a unique selling that includes 
transportation (transporter), collection, processing, and 
utilization of B3 and non-B3 waste and is by the 3R 
Reuse, Recycle, Recovery concept and the Cadle to 
Grave principle. The Company provides services to 
various industries, each designed to reduce risk for 
clients and grouped into several sections.
• Transport (transporter) of B3, Non-B3 and Medical 
Waste.
• Collection of B3, Non-B3 and Medical Waste.
• Processing of B3, Non-B3 and Medical Waste.
• Utilization of B3, Non-B3 and Medical Waste.
Company Production Facilities Processing and Utilization 
Facilities for B3 and Non-B3 Waste.
• The medical waste processing facility uses an 
incinerator with a capacity of 4,320 tonnes/year.
• Liquid B3 processing facility (WWTP) with physical, 
chemical, and electrocoagulation processes with a 
processing capacity of 19,800 tons/year with the results 
obtained in the form of sludge and eﬄuent which are 
released into water bodies by quality standards.
• Alternative material utilization facility for B3 waste using
