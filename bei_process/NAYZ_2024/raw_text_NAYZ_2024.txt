Business Opportunities
The baby food industry in Indonesia exhibits promising growth
potential, as parents increasingly recognize the significance of
nutrition in their children's development. Shifts in the lifestyles of
young urban families, the expansion of the middle class, and the
rise of digital engagement have further fueled the demand for
healthy and organic baby food products.
Market opportunities are further bolstered by the increasing trend
of MPASI (Complementary Food for Breast Milk), which is
supported by education from health professionals, parenting
communities, and nutrition campaigns initiated by both the
government and the private sector. Products that assert being
free of additives, rich in nutrition, and composed of natural
ingredients have become the primary preference for millennial
and Gen Z parents. Given this substantial market potential, the
Company is optimistic about 2025 and is committed to
innovating products that align with market demands.
Entering 2025, the Company aims for sustainable growth with an
emphasis on brand enhancement, market expansion, and product
innovation. The primary objectives to be accomplished include:
Expansion of distribution into strategic regions, encompassing
tier 2 and 3 cities, through partnerships with local distributors
and contemporary retail collaborators.
