GLOBAL AND NATIONAL ECONOMIC REVIEW 
Perekonomian global 2024 yang diliputi oleh sejumlah 
tantangan ekonomi global mulai dari konflik geopolitik, 
perubahan kepemimpinan politik di banyak negara 
serta proyeksi ekonomi global dan negara-negara 
besar di dunia yang masih lemah. Tahun ini banyak 
negara melakukan pemilihan umum sehingga muncul 
pimpinan negara baru, baik di negara maju maupun 
berkembang. Lebih dari 60 negara melakukan 
pemilihan umum dan melibatkan empat miliar orang di 
dunia. Perubahan kepemimpinan dipastikan akan 
menimbulkan perbedaan arah kebijakan. Pemilihan 
umum juga berlangsung di Indonesia dan menciptakan 
pergantian pimpinan dari Presiden Joko Widodo ke 
Presiden Prabowo Subianto. 
 
The 2024 global economy is plagued by a number of 
global economic challenges ranging from geopolitical 
conflicts, changes in political leadership in many 
countries and projections of a weak global economy 
and major countries in the world. This year, many 
countries held general elections and new leaders 
emerged, both in developed and developing countries. 
More than 60 countries held elections and involved four 
billion people in the world. Changes in leadership will 
certainly lead to differences in policy direction. General 
elections also took place in Indonesia and created a 
change of leadership from President Joko Widodo to 
President Prabowo Subianto. 
Pemerintah juga memandang tantangan lain yang 
membayangi

adalah

bervariasi antarkawasan. Kinerja ekonomi negara-
negara maju masih cukup resilien meski masih lebih 
rendah dibanding periode sebelum pandemi. Pada 
kuartal III-2024, ekonomi Amerika Serikat tumbuh 2,7% 
yoy. Di Eropa, laju ekonomi masih stagnan dan tumbuh 
di kisaran 0,9% pada kuartal III-2024. Ekonomi 
Tiongkok menunjukkan tanda-tanda perlambatan. 
Sementara itu, ekonomi di kawasan ASEAN masih 
resilien meskipun permintaan global turun. 
 
The government also sees another challenge looming 
over the global situation is the condition of the global 
economy which has not fully recovered from the impact 
of the Covid-19 pandemic. In 2024, global economic 
activity tends to be weak with varying performance 
between regions. The economic performance of 
developed countries is still quite resilient although it is 
still lower than the period before the pandemic. In the 
third quarter of 2024, the United States economy grew 
2.7% yoy. In Europe, the economic pace remained 
stagnant and grew at around 0.9% in the third quarter 
of 2024. China's economy is showing signs of slowing 
down. Meanwhile, economies in the ASEAN region are 
still resilient despite falling global demand.

In the midst of uncertainty and various global 
challenges that could potentially affect economic 
stability, Indonesia was able to maintain positive 
economic growth. Throughout 2024 Indonesia's 
economic growth was 5.03%, although it slowed down 
compared to 2023 which reached 5.05%. Nevertheless, 
Indonesia's economic performance also remains solid, 
even better than the economic growth of several 
countries such as Singapore which was recorded at 
4.1%, Saudi Arabia at 2.8%, and Mexico at 1.5%.

DEBT SERVICE ABILITY 
Kemampuan Perseroan untuk memenuhi seluruh 
kewajiban yang telah jatuh tempo dapat diukur dengan 
menggunakan perhitungan rasio-rasio keuangan, 
seperti rasio likuiditas dan rasio solvabilitas. Rasio 
likuiditas mengukur kemampuan dalam memenuhi 
kewajiban jangka pendek, sedangkan rasio solvabilitas 
mengukur kemampuan dalam memenuhi kewajiban 
jangka pendek dan panjang. Untuk mengukur rasio 
solvabilitas, Perseroan menggunakan instrumen rasio 
utang terhadap ekuitas (Debt to Equity Ratio/DER) dan 
rasio utang terhadap aset (Debt to Asset Ratio/DAR). 
Pada tahun 2024, DER Perseroan tercatat sebesar 
0,094x dibandingkan tahun 2023 yang tercatat 0,089x, 
sedangkan DAR tahun 2024 Perseroan tercatat sebesar 
0,086x dibandingkan pada tahun 2023 sebesar 0,089x. 
The Company's ability to meet all obligations that have 
matured can be measured by using the calculation of 
financial ratios, such as liquidity ratio and solvency ratio. 
The liquidity ratio measures the ability to meet short-
term obligations, while the solvency ratio measures the 
ability to meet both short-term and long-term 
obligations. To measure the solvency ratio, the 
Company uses the Debt-to-Equity Ratio (DER) and Debt 
to Asset Ratio (DAR) instruments. In 2024, the 
Company's DER was recorded at 0.094x compared to 
2023 which was recorded at 0.089x, while the 
Company's DAR in 2024 was recorded at 0.086x 
compared to 0.089x in 2023.
