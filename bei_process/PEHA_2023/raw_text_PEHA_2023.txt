Production
The realization of OTC production compared to 
the predetermined target was achieved by 99.2% 
and compared to the previous year experienced 
a maturity of 8.0%. This increase in production was 
mainly due to the increase in production of Tablets,

the set target was achieved at 90.8%, and compared 
to the previous year, there was a decrease of 8.3%. 
This decrease in production was mainly due to 
a decrease in the production of coated tablets, 
capsules, injections, syrup, and medical devices.
The realization of GENERIK production compared to 
the set target was achieved at 98.2%, and compared 
to the previous year, there was a decrease of 29.4%. 
This decrease in production was mainly due to 
a decrease in the production of tablets, coated 
tablets, capsules, injection vials, ointments, liquid 
syrups, and mixtures.

SEGMEN OTC
Divisi OTC adalah divisi yang menangani produk 
obat yang dijual bebas. Produk-produk OTC selain 
dijual apotek bisa di jual melalui toko kelontong, 
warung, atau minimart.
Perseroan saat ini mempunyai kurang lebih 15 (lima 
belas) item produk OTC dimana salah satunya 
adalah produk unggulan yang sudah menjadi 
legenda yaitu Antimo. Selain itu ada Antimo Anak, 
Livron B Plek, Noza, Hemorogard, Bioron dan lain lain.
Kinerja Produk OTC
Pencapaian penjualan OTC mengalami penurunan 
terhadap tahun lalu sebesar 22.7%. Produk yang 
mengalami penurunan penjualan adalah Produk 
Antimo Group penjualannya sebesar Rp148.4 miliar, 
tercapai 79.8% dari anggarannya dan mengalami 
penurunan sebesar 11.9% atau Rp20.1 miliar, serta 
Becefort 
penjualannya 
sebesar 
Rp7.6 
miliar, 
tercapai 29.8% dari anggaran dan mengalami 
penurunan 48.7% atau Rp7.2 miliar dari tahun lalu.
Profitabilitas Produk OTC
Margin 
mengalami 
penurunan 
dikarenakan 
menurunnya penjualan Antimo group yang memiliki 
margin cukup tinggi. Pandemi COVID-19 merupakan 
salah satu penyebab turunnya penjualan.
	
Pemasaran Produk OTC
Menjalankan program Promosi below the line 
ke pasar sasaran produk. Program Peningkatan 
jumlah outlet termasuk diantaranya ke modern 
outlet, serta memanfaatkan situs belanja dalam 
jaringan dan media sosial sebagai media promosi. 
OTC SEGMENTS
The OTC division is the division that handles over-
the-counter medicinal products. Apart from being 
sold by pharmacies, OTC products can be sold 
through grocery stores, food stalls, or minimarts.
The company currently has approximately 15 
(fifteen) OTC product items, one of which is a superior 
product that has become a legend, namely Antimo. 
Apart from that, there are Antimo Anak, Livron B. Plek, 
Noza, Hemorogard, Bioron, and others.
OTC Product Performance
OTC sales achievement decreased compared to 
last year by 22.7%. Products that experienced a 
decline in sales were Antimo Group products, sales 
of Rp148.4 billion, reaching 79.8% of the budget and 
experiencing a decrease of 11.9%, or Rp20.1 billion, 
and Becefort sales of Rp7.6 billion, reaching 29.8% of 
the budget and experiencing a decrease of 48.7%, 
or Rp7.2 billion, from last year.
Profitabilitas Produk OTC
Margins experienced a decline due to declining 
sales of the Antimo group, which has quite high 
margins. The COVID-19 pandemic is one of the 
causes of the decline in sales.
	
OTC Product Marketing
Running a below-the-line promotion program for 
the product target market. The program to increase 
the number of outlets includes modern outlets as 
well as utilizing online shopping sites and social 
media as promotional media.

produk obat generik berlogo (OGB). Target market

handles 
generic

products 
bearing

government institutions and hospitals. With the 
existence of BPJS, the need for generic drugs with

Generic product performance
OGB team sales decreased compared to last year 
by 11.3%. The products that experienced a decline 
were Blood Additive Tablets, sales of Rp95.3 billion, 
reaching 181.0% of budget and last year down 16.6% 
or Rp19.0 billion; Omeprazole sales of Rp58.6 billion, 
reaching 131.3% of budget and last year down 17.4% 
or Rp12.3 billion; and Pantoprazole sales of Rp25.5 
billion, reaching 69.9% of the budget and decreasing 
48.5% or Rp24.0 billion compared to last year.

of Rp76.0 billion reached 80.3% of the budget and 
experienced a decrease of 19.8% or Rp18.8 billion 
compared to last year.

Ethical's profitability has increased from last year; 
this is due to an increase in sales.

dari perlambatan pertumbuhan ekonomi tersebut 
adalah risiko krisis real estate di Cina dan perang

Meskipun demikian, ekonomi Indonesia diprediksi

domestik. 
Industri Farmasi diprediksi akan tetap tumbuh pada 
tahun 2024. Salah satu kebijakan yang diperkirakan 
dapat mendorong pertumbuhan industri farmasi 
nasional adalah kebijakan pemerintah melalui 
Kementerian Perindustrian mendorong industri alat 
kesehatan dalam negeri agar dapat menghasilkan 
produk berbasis teknologi tinggi seperti electromedic 
devices, implan orthopedic, dan perangkat radiologi. 
Hal ini diwujudkan pemerintah dengan menambahkan 
sektor alat kesehatan dan farmasi ke dalam sektor 
prioritas dalam peta jalan Making Indonesia 4.0 
yang 
dipimpin 
oleh 
Kementerian 
Perindustrian 
(Kemenperin).
Peta jalan tersebut mengakselerasi perkembangan 
industri farmasi dan alat kesehatan, mencakup 
langkah yang harus dilalui, target perkembangan 
produk, serta jangka waktu. Target dari peta 
jalan tersebut adalah kemajuan industri untuk 
menghasilkan produk bahan baku yang berteknologi 
tinggi.
The October 2023 edition of the World Economic 
Outlook predicts that global economic growth will 
slow down again in 2024 to 2.9%. This prediction 
is lower than the July prediction of 3.0%. The main 
factor in slowing economic growth is the risk of a 
real estate crisis in China and the Palestine-Israel 
war, which is predicted to trigger a banking crisis 
and a spike in world energy prices.
Despite this, the Indonesian economy is predicted 
to continue to grow steadily. In the 2024 APBN, the 
government predicts that the national economy 
will grow by 5.2%, driven by domestic consumption.
The pharmaceutical industry is predicted to continue 
to grow in 2024. One of the policies that is expected to 
encourage the growth of the national pharmaceutical 
industry is the government's policy through the Ministry 
of Industry to encourage the domestic medical 
equipment industry to be able to produce high-
technology-based products such as electromedic 
devices, orthopedic implants, and radiology devices. .
The government realized this by adding the 
medical equipment and pharmaceutical sectors to 
the priority sectors in the Making Indonesia 4.0 road 
map led by the Ministry of Industry (Kemenperin).
The road map accelerates the development of the 
pharmaceutical and medical device industries, 
including the steps that must be taken, product 
development targets, and time periods. The target 
of the roadmap is industrial progress to produce 
high-tech raw material products.

Amendment to PSAK 1: Presentation of Financial

DSAK-IAI amends PSAK 1, ‘Presentation of

"Accounting policy information is material

does not need to be disclosed. However, if 
disclosed, the information must not obscure 
material accounting policy information.

Amendemen PSAK 25 – Kebijakan Akuntansi, 
Perubahan Estimasi Akuntansi dan Kesalahan: 
Definisi Estimasi Akuntansi.
	
Amendemen PSAK 25, ‘Kebijakan Akuntansi, 
Perubahan Estimasi Akuntansi dan Kesalahan’, 
mengklarifikasi bagaimana perusahaan harus 
membedakan perubahan kebijakan akuntansi 
dari perubahan estimasi akuntansi. Estimasi 
akuntansi didefinisikan sebagai jumlah moneter 
dalam laporan keuangan yang tunduk pada 
ketidakpastian pengukuran. Pembedaan ini 
penting, karena perubahan estimasi akuntansi 
diterapkan secara prospektif untuk transaksi 
masa depan dan peristiwa masa depan lainnya, 
sedangkan perubahan kebijakan akuntansi 
umumnya diterapkan secara retrospektif untuk 
transaksi masa lalu dan peristiwa masa lalu 
lainnya serta periode berjalan.
•	
Amendemen PSAK 16 - Aset tetap: tentang hasil 
sebelum penggunaan yang dimaksudkan; PSAK 
16, ‘Aset Tetap’, mensyaratkan bahwa hasil 
yang diterima dari penjualan hasil produksi 
yang dihasilkan sebelum aset tersebut siap 
untuk 
digunakan 
sesuai 
dengan 
maksud 
penggunaannya, diakui sebagai pendapatan 
dalam laba rugi. Biaya produksi yang terkait 
diukur dengan menggunakan panduan dalam 
PSAK 14, ‘Persediaan’, dan diakui sebagai beban 
dalam laba rugi pada saat penjualan.
	
Jika barang yang dijual merupakan hasil dari 
aktivitas normal entitas, maka pendapatan dan 
biaya diungkapkan sesuai dengan persyaratan 
PSAK 72, “Pendapatan dari Kontrak dengan 
Pelanggan”, dan PSAK 14. Jika barang yang 
dijual bukan merupakan bagian dari aktivitas 
normal entitas, maka amendemen PSAK 16 
mensyaratkan pengungkapan jumlah dan pos 
dalam laporan laba rugi komprehensif dimana 
pendapatan dan biaya tersebut dimasukkan.
•	
Amendment to PSAK 25: Accounting Policies, 
Changes in Accounting Estimates, and Errors: 
Definition of Accounting Estimates.
	
Amendments to PSAK 25, ‘Accounting Policies, 
Changes in Accounting Estimates, and Errors’, 
clarify 
how 
companies 
must 
distinguish 
changes in accounting policies from changes 
in accounting estimates. Accounting estimates 
are defined as monetary amounts in financial 
statements that are subject to measurement 
uncertainty. 
This 
distinction 
is 
important 
because changes in accounting estimates are 
applied prospectively to future transactions 
and other future events, whereas changes 
in accounting policies are generally applied 
retrospectively to past transactions and other 
past events as well as the current period.
•	
Amendment to PSAK 16: Fixed Assets: Regarding 
results before intended use, PSAK 16, 'Fixed 
Assets', requires that proceeds received from 
the sale of production results produced before 
the asset is ready to be used for its intended 
use be recognized as income in profit or loss. 
The related production costs are measured 
using the guidance in PSAK 14, ‘Inventories’, and 
recognized as an expense in profit or loss at the 
time of sale.
	
If the goods sold are the result of the entity's 
normal activities, then income and costs are 
disclosed in accordance with the requirements 
of PSAK 72, “Revenue from Contracts with 
Customers,"  and PSAK 14. If the goods sold are 
not part of the entity's normal activities, then 
amend PSAK 16 requires disclosure of amounts 
and items in the statement of comprehensive 
income where such income and expenses are 
included.

Amendment to PSAK 46: Income tax regarding 
deferred tax related to assets and liabilities 
arising from single transactions;
	
DSAK-IAI has amended PSAK 46, ‘Income Taxes’, 
to require the group to recognize deferred 
tax on certain transactions that, upon initial 
recognition, give rise to an amount equal to 
taxable temporary differences and deductible 
temporary differences. These amendments 
will typically apply to transactions such as 
leases to lessees and termination of operations 
obligations.
	
Paragraphs 15 and 24 of PSAK 46 have been 
amended to include additional conditions 
where the initial recognition exception does not 
apply. According to the amended guidance, 
temporary differences that arise upon initial 
recognition of an asset or liability are not 
subject to the initial recognition exception if the 
transaction gives rise to an amount equal to the 
taxable amount and the deductible amount of 
the temporary difference.
