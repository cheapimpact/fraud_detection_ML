In 2024, the Company recorded a decline in coal 
production and sales performance compared to the 
previous year. Production volume decreased significantly 
from 514,969 MT in 2023 to 325,670 MT in 2024, 
representing a decline of 36.78%. Similarly, coal sales 
volume fell from 565,947 MT to 336,225 MT, or a 
decrease of 40.59%. This decline reflects challenges 
stemming from both operational factors and market 
demand. 
Berdasarkan tren Harga Batubara Acuan (HBA) sepanjang 
tahun 2024, harga batubara relatif bergerak fluktuatif 
namun tidak menunjukkan lonjakan signifikan yang dapat 
mendorong peningkatan aktivitas produksi maupun 
penjualan. Harga HBA 1 (bituminous coal) berada di 
kisaran USD 90–100 per ton, HBA 2 (sub-bituminous) 
cenderung stagnan di bawah USD 70, dan HBA 3 (lignite) 
konsisten di bawah USD 50. Ketiadaan tren kenaikan 
harga yang kuat sepanjang tahun 2024 diduga turut 
mempengaruhi keputusan produksi dan permintaan 
pasar, sehingga turut menekan kinerja volume penjualan 
dan produksi Perseroan. 
Based on the trend of the Coal Reference Price (HBA) 
throughout 2024, coal prices moved relatively fluctuated 
but did not exhibit any significant surge that could drive 
an increase in production or sales activities. HBA 1 
(bituminous coal) hovered in the range of USD 90–100 
per ton, HBA 2 (sub-bituminous) remained relatively 
stagnant below USD 70, and HBA 3 (lignite) consistently 
stayed under USD 50. The absence of a strong upward 
price trend throughout 2024 is presumed to have 
affected both production decisions and market demand, 
thereby contributing to the pressure on the Company's 
production and sales volumes.
