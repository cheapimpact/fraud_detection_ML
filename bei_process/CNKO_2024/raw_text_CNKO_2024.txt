Analisis & Pembahasan Manajemen
Tinjauan Operasional
Sepanjang 2024, CNKO mencatat kinerja yang solid, di
tengah berbagai tantangan. Perubahan dari posisi rugi
menjadi laba mencerminkan keberhasilan Perseroan
dalam memperbaiki struktur biaya dan mengoptimalkan
proses operasional secara menyeluruh.
Capaian ini menjadi indikasi positif dari efektivitas strategi
penyesuaian dan penguatan internal yang dijalankan
sepanjang tahun. CNKO memfokuskan upaya operasional
pada pemenuhan komitmen pasokan batubara kepada
PLN, perbaikan kinerja PLTU serta penguatan efisiensi di
berbagai lini. Langkah-langkah ini tidak hanya menjaga
kelangsungan operasional, tetapi juga memperkuat
posisi Perseroan di tengah tantangan industri yang terus
berkembang.

Pendapatan Usaha
Pada 2024, CNKO membukukan pendapatan usaha
sebesar Rp1.731,5 miliar, turun tipis 0,6% dibandingkan
tahun sebelumnya yang mencapai Rp1.742,0 miliar. Hal
ini terutama disebabkan oleh berkurangnya pendapatan
dari PLTU Pangkalan Bun.
Operational Review
Throughout 2024, CNKO recorded solid performance in
the midst of various challenges. The shift from a loss to a
profit reflects the Company's success in improving its cost
structure and optimizing overall operational processes.
This achievement is a positive indication of the
effectiveness of the internal strengthening and adjustment
strategies implemented throughout the year. CNKO
focused its operational efforts on fulfilling coal supply
commitments to PLN, improving the power plant (PLTU)
performance as well as enhancing efficiency across
various areas. These measures not only ensured
operational continuity but also strengthened the
Company's position amid the ongoing challenges in the
industry.

Pendapatan Perseroan pada 2024 tetap didominasi oleh
segmen penjualan batubara sebesar Rp1.711,1 miliar.
Angka ini sedikit lebih rendah dibandingkan tahun
sebelumnya yang mencapai Rp1.715,6 miliar. Mayoritas
penjualan berasal dari PT PLN Indonesia Power dan PT
PLN Nusantara Power yang merupakan sub-holding dari
PT PLN (Persero), dimana masing-masing menyumbang
Rp983,4 miliar (56,8%) dan Rp727,7 miliar (42,0%)
terhadap total pendapatan usaha.
Sementara itu, pendapatan dari PLTU Pangkalan Bun
tercatat sebesar Rp20,4 miliar, menurun 22,7% dari Rp26,4
miliar pada tahun sebelumnya. Hal ini disebabkan oleh
adanya kendala teknis yang sempat menyebabkan PLTU
tidak beroperasi selama beberapa bulan.
Secara keseluruhan, kontribusi penjualan batubara
terhadap total pendapatan usaha mencapai 98,8%,
meningkat dibandingkan tahun sebelumnya yang 98,5%.
Sementara pada periode yang sama, kontribusi PLTU
Pangkalan Bun turun menjadi 1,2%, dari 1,5%.
Laba Bruto
Selama tahun berjalan, beban pokok pendapatan tercatat
sebesar Rp1.486,6 miliar, turun 3,7% dibandingkan tahun
sebelumnya yang mencapai Rp1.543,5 miliar. Penurunan
ini terutama disebabkan oleh berkurangnya biaya
pengangkutan dari Rp436,2 miliar pada 2023 menjadi
Rp394,0 miliar pada 2024. Kondisi ini mencerminkan
keberhasilan Perseroan dalam mengoptimalkan efisiensi
biaya, terutama melalui negosiasi tarif pengangkutan.
Keberhasilan efisiensi juga ditunjukkan oleh berkurangnya
denda keterlambatan yang signifikan dari Rp6,6 miliar
menjadi Rp0,4 miliar.
Sebagai hasil dari efisiensi biaya ini, laba bruto CNKO
tumbuh 23,4% menjadi Rp244,9 miliar dari Rp198,5 miliar.
Oleh Karena itu,  marjin laba bruto juga mengalami
perbaikan, naik menjadi 14,1% dari 11,4% pada tahun
sebelumnya.
EBITDA
CNKO mencatatkan EBITDA sebesar Rp200,3 miliar di
tahun 2024, meningkat sekitar 23,8% dibandingkan tahun
sebelumnya yang sebesar Rp161,7 miliar.
Seiring dengan peningkatan tersebut, marjin EBITDA
Perseroan juga mengalami perbaikan, naik menjadi 11,4%
dibandingkan 9,3% pada tahun sebelumnya.
Laba Operasional
Beban operasional CNKO di 2024 tercatat sebesar
Rp64,5 miliar, naik 12,2% dibandingkan tahun
sebelumnya yang sebesar Rp57,5 miliar. Peningkatan ini
terutama berasal dari peningkatan beban umum dan
administrasi, termasuk biaya tenaga kerja.
In 2024, the Company’s revenue continued to be
dominated by the coal sales segment amounting
Rp1,711.1 billion. This figure was slightly lower than the
previous year’s Rp1,715.6 billion. The majority of sales
came from PT PLN Indonesia Power and PT PLN
Nusantara Power, both of which are sub-holdings of PT
PLN (Persero), contributing Rp983.4 billion (56.8%) and
Rp727.7 billion (42.0%) respectively to total operating
revenue.
Meanwhile, revenue from the PLTU Pangkalan Bun was
recorded at Rp20.4 billion, down 22.7% from Rp26.4
billion in the previous year. This was caused by technical
constraints which caused the PLTU to not operate for
several months.
Overall, coal sales contributed 98.8% to total operating
revenue, an increase from 98.5% the previous year. While
during the same period, contribution from the PLTU
Pangkalan Bun dropped to 1.2%, from 1.5% in the same
period.
Gross Profit
Throughout the year, the cost of revenues was recorded
at Rp1,486.6 billion, down 3.7% from the previous year’s
Rp1,543.5 billion. This decrease was primarily due to a
reduction in transportation costs, from Rp436.2 billion in
2023 to Rp394.0 billion in 2024. This condition reflects
the Company’s success in optimizing cost efficiency,
particularly through the renegotiation of transportation
tariffs. Efficiency gains were also evident in the significant
decrease in late penalty charges, from Rp6.6 billion to
Rp0.4 billion.
As a result of these cost efficiencies, CNKO’s gross profit
grew by 23.4%, reaching Rp244.9 billion compared to
Rp198.5 billion. Therefore, the gross profit margin also
improved, rising to 14.1% from 11.4% in the previous
year.
EBITDA
CNKO recorded an EBITDA of Rp200.3 billion in 2024,
increased approximately 23.8% compared to Rp161.7
billion in the previous year.
Along with this improvement, the Company’s EBITDA
margin also strengthened, rising to 11.4% from 9.3% in
the prior year.
Income from Operations
CNKO’s operating expenses in 2024 were recorded at
Rp64.5 billion, rose 12.2% compared to Rp57.5 billion
in the previous year. This increase was mainly came from
higher general and administrative expenses, including
labor costs

In 2024, the Company successfully recorded a profit
before income tax of Rp96.7 billion, a positive turnaround

year before.
This shift was primarily supported by a substantial
decrease in other expenses – net, which stood at Rp18.4
billion in 2024, much lower when compared to the
previous year  of Rp104.0 billion. In addition, interest
and other financial charges during the same period also
down from Rp71.4 billion in 2023 to Rp65.8 billion.
Comprehensive Profit Attributable to Owners of
the Parent Entity
CNKO recorded a comprehensive profit for the year
attributable to owners of the parent entity of Rp71.1 billion
at the end of 2024. This is an extraordinary achievement
because in the last four years, the Company still had to
post losses.

Assets
CNKO recorded total assets of Rp909.8 billion at the end
of 2024, representing a decrease of 6.8% compared to
Rp976.5 billion at the end of the previous year. This decline
was primarily driven by a reduction in non-current assets,
while current assets remained relatively stable.

in trade receivables from third parties, which declined by
38.8% to Rp124.4 billion from Rp203.2 billion.
Non-current assets were recorded at Rp431.8 billion at
the end of 2024, down 13.3% from Rp498.3 billion in the
previous year. This was mainly due to a substantial
decrease in financial advances, which fell by 65.1% to
Rp27.4 billion from Rp78.5 billion.

Kas neto yang digunakan untuk aktivitas pendanaan
selama tahun berjalan adalah Rp47,9 miliar atau

penambahan pembayaran utang bank sebesar 11,6%,
dari Rp44,0 miliar menjadi Rp49,1 miliar.
Liabilities
The Company has a total liabilities of Rp2,165.5 billion
in 2024. This position is 6.0% lower compared to the
previous year's of Rp2,303.4 billion. This was mainly due
to a decrease in the non-current liabilities, while non-
current liabilities recorded a slight increase.
The Company's current liabilities increased by 2.2%, from
Rp1,728.0 to Rp1,765.9 billion during the year. This
increase was mainly contributed by an increase in tax
payables as well as current maturity of long-term bank
loan portion.
On the other hand, non-current liabilities decreased
significantly by 30.6%, from Rp575.4 billion to Rp399.6
billion, following a Rp175.9 billion bank loan repayment.
Equity
At the end of 2024, the Company still recorded a capital
deficiency of Rp1,255.7 billion. This figure shows an
improvement from the position of Rp1,327.0 billion in
the year before.
Consolidated Statement of Cash Flows
Cash Flow from Operating Activities
Throughout 2024, CNKO recorded net cash obtained from
operating activities of Rp73.9 billion, down 26.0%
compared to the previous year of Rp99.8 billion. This
decrease was mainly due to a quite significant increase
in payments to suppliers, which was up by 21.1%, from
Rp1,324.0 billion to Rp1,603.0 billion.
Cash Flow from Investing Activities
Net cash obtained from investment activities reached
Rp587.3 million at the end of 2024, up almost three times
compared to Rp196.1 million obtained during the year
before. This was mainly due to a 126.8% jump in interest
income from Rp222.8 million to Rp505.3 million.
Cash Flows for Financing Activities
Net cash used in financing activities during the year was
Rp47.9 billion or increased 8.8% from the previous year
of Rp44.0 billion. This increase was mainly driven by the
addition payment of bank loans by 11.6%, from Rp44.0
billion to Rp49.1 billion.

Association, CNKO has a dividend distribution policy that
is adjusted based on the company’s annual net profit
and the resolution of the General Meeting of Shareholders
(GMS). The Company has established the following
dividend distribution scheme:
•
30% of net profit if the current year’s net profit is less
than Rp50 billion.
•
40% of net profit if the current year’s net profit is equal
to or greater than Rp50 billion.
In 2024, the Company did not distribute dividends to
shareholders. This decision was made in consideration
of its financial condition.
