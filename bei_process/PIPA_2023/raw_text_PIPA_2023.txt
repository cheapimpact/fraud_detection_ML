Tinjauan Ekonomi dan Industri 
Economy and Industry Review 
Pada tahun 2023, Kondisi ekonomi diwarnai dengan 
ketidakpastian yang disebabkan oleh krisis politik dan 
ekonomi disetiap belahan dunia. Dari krisis Rusia dan 
Ukraina, Gaza dan Pemblokadean Houthi di Yemen 
membuat pertumbuhan global mengalami kejatuhan ke 
2,9 persen. Lalu diwarnai dengan kenaikan suku bungan 
yang terjadi di berbagai negara hingga masa masa 
PEMILU di Indonesia yang membuat Industri konstruksi 
mengalami stangnansi. 
In 2023, economic conditions will be characterized by 
uncertainty caused by political and economic crises 
in every part of the world. The crises in Russia and 
Ukraine, Gaza and the Houthi blockade of Yemen 
caused global growth to fall to 2.9 percent. Then it 
was colored by the increase in interest rates that 
occurred in various countries until the election period 
in Indonesia which caused the construction industry 
to experience stagnation.
 
TINJAUAN OPERASIONAL 
Perseroan menjalankan kegiatan usaha produksi dan 
distribusi pembuatan pipa dengan bahan dasar 
Polymervinyl Chloride (PVC) beserta dengan produk 
turunan 
dan 
produk 
bahan 
bangunan 
lainnya. 
Perseroan memasarkan produk material bangunan, 
berupa Pipa PVC, Fitting PVC, Ember Cor dan Bening, 
Talang, Lem Pipa PVC & Lem Kayu, serta produk 
lainnya. Selain itu, Perseroan menyediakan kebutuhan 
bahan bangunan di beberapa sektor dan industri 
properti, 
manufaktur, 
agrikultur, 
dan 
beberapa 
proyekproyek besar infrastuktur termasuk pemenuhan 
pipa PVC untuk keperluan jalan tol pemerintah di Pulau 
Jawa maupun Sumatera. 
The Company is carrying out business in the production 
and distribution of pipes made of Polymervinyl Chloride 
(PVC) and its accessories as well as other building 
material products. The Company is marketing its 
building material products, such as PVC pipes, PVC 
fittings, Cast and Clear Buckets, Gutters, PVC Pipe 
Glue & Wood Glue, as well as various range of 
products. Also, the Company is providing building 
materials for various sectors as well as for property, 
manufacturing, agriculture, industries, and several 
major infrastructure projects, including supplying the 
PVC pipes used in government-owned tollroad in Java 
and Sumatra.
