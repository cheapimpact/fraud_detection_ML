Marketing Aspects
The Company markets and distributes its 
products in the form of motor vehicle spare 
parts, electronics and sanitation to various 
companies in Indonesia through a Business-
to-Business (B2B) tender scheme. In order to 
increase revenue and increase penetration of 
customer expansion in the future.
The Company marketing strategies include 
active participation in industry exhibition events 
and utilising referrals from Indonesian spareparts 
associations as a significant marketing channel. 
Through a combination of these marketing 
strategies. The Company is committed to 
optimising its operations and ensuring that 
its products can be found, accessed and 
selected by customers who require high quality 
spareparts solutions.

inovasi sejak pendiriannya. Perseroan telah 
tumbuh menjadi salah satu pemain kunci

Perseroan memainkan peran penting dalam 
memenuhi kebutuhan pelanggan di sektor 
otomotif, elektronik, dan sanitasi. Dalam

memiliki fondasi yang kuat dalam produksi

berkualitas

Business Prospect
With dedication to quality and innovation since 
its establishment. The Company has grown to 
become one of the key players in the supply of 
spare parts with a special focus on stamping 
and assembling parts. The Company plays an 
important role in fulfilling the needs of customers 
in the automotive, electronics and sanitary 
sectors. In its endeavour to meet the evolving 
challenges and opportunities. The Company 
has a strong foundation in the production of 
high quality spare parts. With the increasing 
demand in these key sectors, as well as a 
commitment to innovation and sustainability. 
The Company has the potential to expand its 
market share and strengthen its position as an 
industry leader.
