TINJAUAN INDUSTRI
Industry Review
Berdasarkan data yang didapatkan dari 
Kementerian Keuangan Direktorat Jenderal 
Anggaran, indikator industri logistik di 
Indonesia memperlihatkan kinerja yang 
positif di tahun 2023 meskipun mengalami 
penurunan. Setelah perekonomian dunia 
telah berangsur-angsur membaik dari masa 
pandemi, perubahan pola kerja baru dan 
kompetisi semakin ketat. Apabila meninjau 
secara global, Indonesia memiliki skor 
Logistic Performance Indeks (LPI) yang lebih 
rendah yaitu dengan skor 3,0 dibandingkan 
dengan negara-negara lain di Asia seperti 
Malaysia, Cina, dan India. Komponen ini 
mengukur kecepatan pengiriman, kualitas 
infrastruktur transportasi, layanan logistik, 
dan kemudahan administrasi. Indonesia 
dinilai masih belum mumpuni dalam 
memenuhi komponen waktu pengiriman, 
pemrosesan dokumen, dan kepatuhan 
terhadap jadwal yang ditentukan.
Meskipun demikian, pemerintah tengah 
melakukan langkah strategis yang berpotensi 
menjadi solusi dan mendukung entitas bisnis 
dalam industri logistik. Pemerintah telah 
menerbitkan Instruksi Presiden Nomor 3 
tahun 2023 tentang Percepatan Peningkatan 
Konektivitas Jalan Daerah. Kebijakan 
ini meliputi kelanjutan pengembangan 
infrastruktur untuk konektivitas seperti 
pembangunan jaringan jalan tol, pelabuhan, 
kereta api, dan bandara yang modern dan 
eﬁsien.
Dengan legalisasi pengembangan 
infrastruktur, pengiriman komoditas di arus 
produksi dan distribusi yang menjadi bagian 
dari aktivitas bisnis Perseroan dapat lebih 
produktif.
Based on data obtained from the Ministry 
of Finance, Directorate General of Budget, 
logistics industry indicators in Indonesia 
showed positive performance in 2023 despite 
experiencing a decline. After the global 
economy has gradually recovered from the 
pandemic period, new work patterns and 
increased competition have emerged. Globally, 
Indonesia has a lower Logistics Performance 
Index (LPI) score of 3.0 compared to other 
countries in Asia such as Malaysia, China, 
and India. This component measures delivery 
speed, quality of transportation infrastructure, 
logistics services, and administrative ease. 
Indonesia is considered to still be lacking in 
meeting the components of delivery time, 
document processing, and compliance with 
speciﬁed schedules.
Nevertheless, the government is taking 
strategic steps that have the potential to 
become solutions and support business 
entities in the logistics industry. The 
government has issued Presidential Instruction 
Number 3 of 2023 regarding the Acceleration 
of Regional Road Connectivity Improvement. 
This policy includes the continuation of 
infrastructure development for connectivity 
such as the construction of toll roads, ports, 
railways, and modern and efﬁcient airports. 
With the legalization of infrastructure 
development, the delivery of commodities in 
the production and distribution ﬂow, which is 
part of the Company's business activities, can 
become more productive.

Tinjauan Operasi Per Segmen Operasi
Operational Review per Operating Segment
Kegiatan operasional Perseroan berpusat 
pada sektor integrated supply chain yaitu 
segmen downstream (pengiriman dari 
sumber ke tempat pengolahan bahan baku), 
processing (pengambilan dan pengiriman 
bahan baku manufaktur atau industri), 
inventory (pengiriman penjualan hasil 
pengolahan bahan baku yang terhubung 
rantai pasok), dan distribusi (pengiriman 
barang jadi atau setengah jadi ke gudang 
produsen dan/atau distributor). Untuk 
melancarkan kegiatan operasional, 
Perseroan melakukan pengiriman dengan  
menggunakan truk dan multimoda (truk dan 
kereta) serta kontainer (dry container dan 
iso tank). Kapasitas pengiriman Perseroan 
selama tahun 2023 sebesar 1.712.788 ton 
ton. Selama 2023, Perseroan membagi porsi 
segmen kegiatan operasional dalam cakupan 
yang berbeda yaitu segmen downstream 
sebesar 15%, distribusi sebesar 25%, 
processing sebesar 35%, dan inventory 
sebesar 25%. 
The Company's operational activities are 
focused on the integrated supply chain sector, 
namely the downstream segment (delivery 
from source to raw material processing 
sites), processing (collection and delivery of 
manufacturing or industrial raw materials), 
inventory (delivery of sales from processed 
raw materials linked to the supply chain), 
and distribution (delivery of ﬁnished or 
semi-ﬁnished goods to manufacturer and/
or distributor warehouses). To facilitate 
operational activities, the Company conducts 
deliveries using trucks and multimodal 
transportation (trucks and trains) as well as 
containers (dry containers and iso tanks). 
The Company's delivery capacity during 
2023 amounted to 1.712.788 tons. Throughout 
2023, the Company allocated the operational 
activity segments in different scopes, with 
the downstream segment at 15%, distribution 
at  25%, processing at 35%, and inventory at 
25%.
