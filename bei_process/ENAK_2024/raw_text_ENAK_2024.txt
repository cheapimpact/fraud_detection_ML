Indonesia merupakan yang terbaik di Emerging Market 
Economies (EMEs), dengan stabilitas yang terjaga dan 
pertumbuhan ekonomi yang cukup tinggi. Inflasi menurun 
dan terjaga dalam kisaran sasaran 2,5±1% serta stabilitas 
nilai tukar Rupiah terjaga sejalan komitmen Bank Indonesia. 
Stabilitas sistem keuangan juga tetap baik dengan kecukupan 
permodalan perbankan dan pertumbuhan kredit yang tinggi 
sehingga berkontribusi penting dalam pembiayaan ekonomi. 
Digitalisasi sistem pembayaran meningkat pesat sehingga 
menopang ekosistem ekonomi-keuangan digital nasional 
dan secara keseluruhan mampu mendukung pertumbuhan 
ekonomi Indonesia tetap baik pada tahun 2024.1
Berdasarkan data Badan Pusat Statistik, ekonomi Indonesia 
tahun 2024 tumbuh sebesar 5,03%. Pertumbuhan terjadi 
pada seluruh lapangan usaha. Lapangan usaha yang 
mengalami pertumbuhan tertinggi adalah Jasa Lainnya 
sebesar 9,80%, diikuti Transportasi dan Pergudangan 
sebesar 8,69%, dan Penyediaan Akomodasi dan Makan 
Minum sebesar 8,56%. Sementara itu, Industri Pengolahan 
yang memiliki peran dominan tumbuh 4,43%. Sedangkan 
Perdagangan Besar dan Eceran, Reparasi Mobil dan Sepeda 
Motor serta Pertanian, Kehutanan, dan Perikanan masing-
masing tumbuh sebesar 4,86% dan 0,67%.2
Dari data di atas dapat terlihat bahwa industri makanan dan 
minuman merupakan kontributor ketiga terbesar terhadap 
PDB nasional pada tahun 2024. Hal ini menggarisbawahi 
peran penting sektor makanan dan minuman dalam 
perekonomian Indonesia.
Namun demikian, kondisi geopolitik dunia tidak dapat 
dipungkiri berdampak terhadap peningkatan harga jual 
bahan baku dimana hal tersebut menjadi tantangan tersendiri 
bagi Perseroan yang menjalankan kegiatan bisnis di industri 
restoran. Kenaikan harga bahan baku mengakibatkan 
penurunan daya beli masyarakat pada banyak sektor di 
Indonesia. Kondisi ini membuat manajemen menerapkan 
berbagai strategi yang tepat sasaran untuk tetap tumbuh di 
tengah ketidakstabilan.
Indonesia is one of the best performing countries in Emerging 
Market Economies (EMEs), with maintained stability and 
fairly high economic growth. In line with Bank Indonesia’s 
commitment, inflation has decreased and maintained 
within the target range of 2.5±1% and the stability of the 
Rupiah exchange rate remains strong. The financial system 
also remained stable with adequate bank capital and high 
credit growth, which contributes significantly to economic 
financing. The rapid digitalization of the payment system 
has also supported the national digital economic-financial 
ecosystem and contributed to Indonesia’s continued 
economic growth in 2024.1
Based on data from Central Statistics Agency, the Indonesian 
economy grew by 5.03% in 2024. This growth ocurred 
across all business sectors, with the highest growth recorded 
by Other Services at 9.80%, followed by Transportation and 
Warehousing at 8.69%, and also the Accommodation and 
food Services at 8.56%. Meanwhile, the Manufacturing 
Industry, as a dominant contributor, grew by 4.43%. On the 
other hand, Wholesale and Retail Trade, Car and Motorcycle 
Repair, as well as Agriculture, Forestry, and Fisheries grew 
by 4.86% and 0.67%, respectively.2
The above-mentioned data shows that food and beverage 
industry is the third largest contributor to national GDP 
in 2024. This underlines the important role of food and 
beverage sector in the Indonesian economy.
However, the global geopolitical situation has inevitably led 
to an increase in the selling price of raw materials, which 
was a challenge for the Company, operating in the restaurant 
industry. The increase of raw material prices has lowered 
people’s purchasing power in many sectors in Indonesia. 
This situation has prompted the management to implement 
various targeted strategies to ensure continued growth amid 
the instability.

Perseroan terus memperkuat eksistensinya di industri 
makanan dan minuman yang dinamis di Indonesia dengan 
mengelola portofolio merek-merek restoran yang beragam, 
termasuk Raa-Cha, Gokana Ramen & Teppan, Monsieur 
Spoon, Platinum Resto & Café, Baso Malang Karapitan 
(BMK), Chopstix, dan Grillman. Dengan total 331 gerai 
yang tersebar secara strategis di seluruh Indonesia, 
Perseroan berkomitmen untuk memberikan pengalaman 
bersantap berkualitas tinggi dan harga yang terjangkau 
yang disesuaikan dengan selera konsumen Indonesia yang 
beragam.
Tulang punggung operasi Perseroan adalah proses bisnis 
yang terstruktur dengan cermat yang mencakup beberapa 
tahapan utama: Production Planning and Inventory Control 
(PPIC), Produksi dan Persiapan, serta Quality Control and 
Assurance (QA). Fungsi-fungsi ini diawasi oleh divisi khusus, 
yaitu Divisi Produksi, Divisi Logistik, dan Divisi Pengendalian 
Kualitas, yang memastikan bahwa setiap aspek rantai 
pasokan beroperasi secara efisien dan memenuhi standar 
tertinggi.
Menyadari evolusi yang cepat dari preferensi konsumen, 
Perseroan mengandalkan Tim Pengembangan Produk 
untuk mendorong inovasi dan adaptasi yang berkelanjutan. 
Seiring dengan berkembangnya bisnis dengan pembukaan 
gerai-gerai baru, kapasitas produksi ditingkatkan untuk 
mengakomodasi 
permintaan 
yang

diatur secara cermat ke dalam berbagai bagian berdasarkan 
jenis dan fungsi makanan guna mengoptimalkan efisiensi 
dan konsistensi.
Selama periode puncak seperti Idul Fitri, liburan sekolah, dan 
perayaan akhir tahun, ketika lalu lintas pelanggan melonjak, 
Perseroan menerapkan penyesuaian kapasitas strategis

Cirebon, Surabaya, Bali, Makassar, dan Medan. Fasilitas-
fasilitas ini berperan penting dalam menjaga kesegaran

The Company continues to strengthen its presence in 
Indonesia’s dynamic food and beverage industry by managing 
a diverse portfolio of restaurant brands, including Raa-Cha, 
Gokana Ramen & Teppan, Monsieur Spoon, Platinum Resto & 
Café, Baso Malang Karapitan (BMK), Chopstix, and Grillman. 
With a total of 331 strategically located outlets throughout 
Indonesia, the Company is committed to providing a high-
quality dining experience at affordable prices tailored to the 
diverse appetites of Indonesian consumers.
The backbone of the Company’s operations is a thorough 
structured business process that includes several main 
stages: 
Production 
Planning 
and 
Inventory 
Control 
(PPIC), Production and Preparation, and Quality Control 
and Assurance (QA). These functions are supervised by 
specialized divisions, which are the Production Division, the 
Logistics Division, and the Quality Control Division, which 
ensure that every aspect of the supply chain operates 
efficiently and meets the highest standards.
Considering the rapid evolution of consumers’ preferences, 
the Company relies on its Product Development Team to 
drive continuous innovation and adaptation. As the business 
grows with the establishment of new outlets, production 
capacity is increased to accommodate the ever-increasing 
demand. The Company proactively ensures the smooth 
supply chain operations by centralizing production flows 
in the Main Kitchen. This facility is carefully organized 
into sections based on food type and function to optimize 
efficiency and consistency.
During peak seasons such as Eid, school holidays, and year-
end celebrations, when customer traffic spikes, the Company 
implements strategic capacity adjustments to maintain its 
service excellence. In order to support its extensive network, 
the Company operates two warehouses located in Bekasi 
and Bandung, as well as seven Support Kitchens spread 
across Bekasi, Bandung, Cirebon, Surabaya, Bali, Makassar, 
and Medan. These facilities enable the Company to maintain 
the freshness of raw ingredients from the Main Kitchen 
before they are distributed to outlets, ensuring that every 
meal served is of the highest quality.

Development of New Menu 
In order to continuously improve the dining experience 
and strengthen the appeal of each of the Company’s 
brands, the Product Development Team actively creates 
innovative menu offerings. The Product Development 
Team’s efforts extend beyond introducing new dishes as the 
team is strategically designed to enhance the overall value 
proposition of each restaurant and attract new customers on 
a broader scale as well as increase the loyalty of existing 
customers. This process involves an in-depth analysis of 
the constantly evolving consumer preferences, industry 
trends, and competitive landscape. In addition, the team also 
carefully assesses the availability of raw materials, explores 
partnerships with potential suppliers, and considers other 
important factors that influence the feasibility of the menu 
and long-term success. Throughout 2024, the Product 
Development Team has released several new menus for 
almost all of the Company’s restaurants.

brand, enabling the appropriate strategy for expansion and 
performance improvement. The Company monitors the

performance assessments are consistently aligned with the 
financial statements.

Penjualan Bersih
Perseroan berhasil meraih peningkatan penjualan bersih 
sebesar 8,04% pada 2024, dari Rp1.425,95 miliar pada 
2023 menjadi Rp1.540,58 miliar. Peningkatan ini terutama 
disebabkan oleh penambahan gerai yang merupakan 
strategi ekspansi Perseroan sepanjang tahun 2024.
Beban Pokok Penjualan
Perseroan mencatat adanya peningkatan beban pokok 
penjualan sebesar 1,19% pada 2024, dari Rp540,49 miliar 
pada 2023 menjadi Rp546,93 miliar pada 2024. Peningkatan 
tersebut terutama disebabkan peningkatan penggunaan 
persediaan, biaya gaji dan biaya overhead lainnya yang 
sejalan dengan kenaikan penjualan bersih Perseroan pada 
tahun berjalan.
Laba Kotor
Laba kotor Perseroan pada 2024 tercatat sebesar Rp993,65 
miliar, meningkat sebesar 12,22% dari Rp885,46 miliar 
pada 2023. Peningkatan tersebut sejalan dengan kenaikan 
penjualan bersih Perseroan pada tahun berjalan.
Beban Usaha
Beban usaha Perseroan tercatat sebesar Rp937,97 miliar 
pada 2024, meningkat sebesar 13,53% dari Rp826,20 miliar 
pada 2023. Peningkatan tersebut terutama disebabkan 
adanya peningkatan beban penjualan serta peningkatan 
beban umum dan administrasi akibat dari peningkatan biaya 
gaji, penyusutan aset hak guna dan aset tetap yang sejalan 
dengan pertumbuhan jumlah gerai di tahun berjalan.
Laba Usaha
Per 31 Desember 2024, Perseroan membukukan laba 
usaha sebesar Rp55,68 miliar, menurun sebesar 6,04% dari 
Rp59,26 miliar pada 2023. Penurunan tersebut disebabkan 
oleh peningkatan persentase beban usaha terhadap 
penjualan bersih tahun 2024 dibandingkan tahun 2023.
Penghasilan Komprehensif Lain
Pada 2024, Perseroan mencatat adanya penghasilan 
komprehensif sebesar Rp1,45 miliar, meningkat sebesar 
492,58% dari Rp0,25 miliar pada 2023. Penurunan ini 
disebabkan oleh adanya penurunan atas pengukuran 
kembali liabilitas imbalan pasca kerja karyawan.
Penghasilan Komprehensif Tahun Berjalan
Perseroan membukukan penghasilan komprehensif sebesar 
Rp19,31 miliar per 31 Desember 2024, menurun sebesar 10,61% 
dari Rp21,60 miliar pada 2023. Penurunan tersebut disebabkan 
oleh peningkatan persentase beban usaha terhadap penjualan 
bersih tahun 2024 dibandingkan tahun 2023.
Net Sales
The Company managed to achieve an increase in net sales of 
8.04% in 2024, from Rp1,425.95 billion in 2023 to Rp1,540.58 
billion. The increase was mainly due to the establishment of 
new outlets, as part of the Company’s expansion strategy 
throughout 2024.
Cost of Goods Sold
The Company recorded an increase in cost of goods sold of 
1.19% in 2024, from Rp540.49 billion in 2023 to Rp546.93 
billion in 2024. It was mainly due to an increase in use of 
inventory, salary costs and other overhead costs, which are 
in line with the increase in the Company’s net sales for the 
year.
Gross Profit
The Company’s gross profit in 2024 was recorded at 
Rp993.65 billion, an increase of 12.22% from Rp885.46 
billion in 2023. This increase is in line with the increase in 
the Company’s net sales for the year.
Operating Expenses
The Company’s operating expenses were recorded at 
Rp937.97 billion in 2024, an increase of 13.53% from 
Rp826.20 billion in 2023. It was mainly due to an increase 
in selling expenses and an increase in general and 
administrative expenses due to an increase in salary costs, 
depreciation of right-to-use assets and fixed assets in line 
with the growth in the number of outlets in the current year.
