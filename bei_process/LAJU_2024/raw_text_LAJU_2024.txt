TINJAUAN PEREKONOMIAN GLOBAL
Perekonomian dunia tumbuh stabil pada tahun 2024 
di tengah meningkatnya risiko ketidakpastian yang 
disebabkan beragam faktor. Ketegangan politik yang 
meluas seperti di kawasan Timur Tengah dan Rusia-
Ukraina serta meningkatnya fragmentasi perdagangan 
memicu risiko ketidakpastian di pasar keuangan yang 
berdampak terhadap perekonomian global.
Inflasi global dalam tren menurun karena belum 
meratanya pertumbuhan ekonomi di setiap negara. 
Hal ini mendorong konvergensi pelonggaran 
kebijakan moneter terutama di negara maju seperti 
Amerika Serikat (AS).
Namun demikian, dinamika politik AS menjelang 
akhir tahun akan memengaruhi perekonomian di 
seluruh dunia termasuk negara-negara emerging 
market seperti Indonesia.  Terpilihnya Presiden baru 
AS diprakirakan akan diikuti dengan arah kebijakan 
fiskal lebih ekspansif dan strategi ekonomi 
berorientasi domestik, termasuk penerapan tarif 
perdagangan tinggi dan kebijakan imigrasi ketat. 
Perubahan kebijakan ekonomi dan perdagangan 
AS tersebut ini akan berdampak pada risiko 
melambatnya pertumbuhan ekonomi dan kembali 
meningkatnya inflasi global. Risiko modal keluar 
dari negara berkembang menuju negara maju juga 
diprediksi akan meningkat seiring dengan perbaikan 
ekonomi di negara maju. 
TINJAUAN PEREKONOMIAN INDONESIA
Perekonomian Indonesia menunjukkan resiliensi 
di tengah meningkatnya risiko ketidakpastian 
global. Sektor konsumsi masih menjadi penopang 
utama perekonomian nasional diikuti dengan 
Indeks Keyakinan Konsumen yang terjaga. Selain 
itu, Indeks Penjualan Eceran Riil tetap tumbuh di 
tengah  meningkatnya risiko pemutusan hubungan 
kerja di sektor manufaktur.
GLOBAL ECONOMIC REVIEW
The global economy is growing steadily in 2024 
amid increasing uncertainty risks caused by various 
factors. Widespread political tensions, such as 
those in the Middle East and the Russia-Ukraine 
region, as well as increasing trade fragmentation, 
trigger uncertainty risks in financial markets that 
impact the global economy.
Global inflation is on a downward trend due to 
uneven economic growth across countries. This 
encourages the convergence of monetary policy 
easing, especially in developed countries like the 
United States (US).
However, US political dynamics towards the end of 
the year will affect the global economy, including 
emerging markets like Indonesia. The election of 
a new US President is expected to be followed 
by a more expansive fiscal policy direction and a 
domestically-oriented economic strategy, including 
the implementation of high trade tariffs and strict 
immigration policies. 
The changes in US economic and trade policies will 
impact the risk of slowing economic growth and the 
resurgence of global inflation. The risk of capital 
outflow from developing countries to developed 
countries is also predicted to increase along with 
economic improvements in developed countries. 
OVERVIEW OF INDONESIA'S ECONOMY
Indonesia's economy demonstrates resilience 
amidst increasing global uncertainty risks. The 
consumption sector remains the main pillar of the 
national economy, supported by a stable Consumer 
Confidence Index. Additionally, the Real Retail Sales 
Index continues to grow despite the rising risk of 
layoffs in the manufacturing sector.

The Company operates in the field of Integrated 
Logistics Services, which includes Motorized 
Transportation for General Goods, Warehousing, and 
Cold Storage as part of its business development 
strategy.
The Company's main advantage lies in its Dedicated 
& Integrated Logistic Support services, which are 
comprehensive logistics solutions dedicated to B2B 
customers. In this concept, all supporting facilities 
including the fleet, drivers, delivery management, 
and warehousing are provided exclusively for one 
customer at one location to ensure operational 
efficiency and customer satisfaction.
This approach minimizes the risk of overlapping 
or waiting times for vehicles and drivers, thereby 
maintaining optimal utilization of operational 
vehicles and human resources. This service model 
also allows for smoother distribution, increases 
productivity, and ensures customers receive reliable 
and timely services.
The Company continues to strive to enhance 
operational standards by implementing a more 
effective logistics management system, supported 
by experienced employees and driver partners in 
performing their respective functions. This step 
aligns with the Company's vision to become a 
competitive, innovative, and adaptive provider of 
integrated logistics services in response to industry 
dynamics.

Kemampuan Perseroan dalam memenuhi kewajiban 
utang jangka pendek tercermin dari rasio likuiditas 
sedangkan kemampuan membayar utang jangka 
panjang tercermin dari rasio likuiditas dan rasio 
solvabilitas. 
RASIO LIKUIDITAS 
Rasio likuiditas dapat diukur dari rasio lancar yang 
merupakan perbandingan antara aset lancar dan 
liabilitas lancar. Pada tahun 2024, rasio lancar 
sebesar 1,77 kali.
RASIO SOLVABILITAS 
Rasio solvabilitas merupakan rasio untuk menilai 
kemampuan perusahaan dalam melunasi semua 
kewajibannya, baik jangka pendek maupun jangka 
panjang, dengan jaminan aktiva atau kekayaan yang 
dimiliki perusahaan hingga perusahaan tutup atau 
dilikuidasi. 
Rasio solvabilitas dapat diperoleh dari perbandingan 
antara total liabilitas dengan jumlah ekuitas yang 
dimiliki.  Pada tahun 2024, rasio solvabilitas sebesar 
0,45 kali dan sebesar 0,29 kali di tahun 2023.
Berdasarkan 
rasio-rasio 
tersebut, 
Perseroan 
memiliki kemampuan membayar seluruh utang 
yang dimiliki baik dalam jangka pendek maupun 
jangka panjang.
The Company's ability to meet short-term debt 
obligations is reflected in the liquidity ratio, while 
the ability to repay long-term debt is reflected in 
both the liquidity ratio and the solvency ratio. 
LIQUIDITY RATIO 
The liquidity ratio can be measured by the current 
ratio, which is the comparison between current 
assets and current liabilities. In 2024, the current 
ratio was 1.77 times.
SOLVENCY RATIO 
The solvency ratio is used to assess the company's 
ability to settle all its obligations, both short-term 
and long-term, with the guarantee of assets or 
wealth owned by the company until it closes or is 
liquidated.
The solvency ratio is derived from the comparison 
between total liabilities and the amount of equity 
owned. In 2024, the solvency ratio is 0.45 times, and 
in 2023, it is 0.29 times.
Based on these ratios, The Company has the ability 
to pay all its debts, both short-term and long-term.
Kolektibilitas 
piutang 
merupakan 
ukuran 
kemampuan Perseroan dalam mengubah tagihan 
menjadi kas. Pada tahun 2024, tingkat Kolektibilitas 
piutang Perseroan tercatat selama 36 hari.

Perseroan, serta prospek usaha di masa depan. 
Pada tahun 2024, dividen  yang dibagikan sebesar 
Rp1,913,449,247.
According to Law No. 40 of 2007 concerning 
Limited Liability Companies, dividend distribution 
will be conducted in accordance with the decision 
of the General Meeting of Shareholders (GMS). 
The Company will distribute dividends if retained 
earnings are positive. This decision is made by 
considering the recommendation of the Board of 
Directors, taking into account the positive retained 
earnings from business and financial results, the 
Company's liquidity condition, and future business 
prospects. In 2024, the dividends distributed 
amounted to Rp1,913,449,247.
