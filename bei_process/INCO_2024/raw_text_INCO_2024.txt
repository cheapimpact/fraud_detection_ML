Internasional 
(IMF) 
tumbuh 
3,2%.

tersebut 
dipengaruhi berbagai faktor termasuk ketegangan geopolitik

the global economy grew by 3.2% in 2024. Some of the 
determining factors include geopolitical tensions in a number 
of regions that triggered a decline in the global economy 
through conflicts and trade disputes, which impacted 
the confidence of market participants and investment. 
Fluctuating currency exchange rates was another factor 
which significantly affected the competitiveness of a 
country's exports and imports.
Domestically, the momentum of safe elections and other 
factors such as the Ramadan period and the accelerated 
completion of infrastructure projects, encouraged the 
national economy to continue growing. On the other hand, 
the relatively stable political dynamics were able to maintain 
investors' confidence in investing their capital in Indonesia. 
The publication of the Statistics Indonesia states that the 
national economic growth in 2024 was 5.03%. Meanwhile, 
according to the Investment Coordinating Board, investment 
in 2024 reached Rp1,714.2 trillion, exceeding the targeted 
Rp1,650 trillion. Of the total investment, IDR407.8 trillion 
or 23.8% was realized in the downstream sector, of which 
IDR153.2 trillion was used to finance nickel smelter.1

Tinjauan Industri Nikel
Nickel Industry Review
Nikel sejak lama dikenal sebagai logam serbaguna dan 
digunakan untuk berbagai produk. Penggunaan nikel kian 
masif seiring penerapan kebijakan transisi energi di dunia. 
Mengutip analisis yang disusun oleh Wood Mackenzie, 
Indonesian 
Mining 
Association 
(IMA) 
memaparkan 
permintaan nikel dunia masih didominasi oleh kebutuhan 
bahan baku baja nirkarat atau stainless steel yang mencapai 
1,9 juta ton pada 2040. Sebaliknya, permintaan nikel untuk 
bahan baku baterai kendaraan listrik atau electric vehicle 
(EV) diestimasikan meningkat signifikan dari 163.000 ton 
pada 2019 menjadi 1,22 juta ton pada 2040. Bertambahnya 
permintaan nikel ini didukung oleh sumber daya nikel yang 
tersebar di berbagai negeri termasuk Indonesia. 
Berdasarkan Kepmen ESDM RI, Nomor: 132.K/GL.01/
MEM.G/2024 tentang Neraca Sumber Daya dan Cadangan 
Mineral dan Batu Bara Nasional Tahun 2024 total sumber 
daya bijih nikel di Indonesia sebesar 18,55 miliar ton, total 
cadangannya 5,32 miliar ton.1  Kondisi ini menjadikan 
Indonesia memiliki posisi strategis dalam pasar nikel dunia, 
sehingga mengundang banyak pelaku industri nikel untuk 
menanamkan modal mereka. 
Sepanjang tahun 2024 terjadi pergeseran pasokan dan 
permintaan bijih nikel dunia. Kondisi ini berimbas pada 
fluktuasi harga nikel dunia sepanjang tahun 2024. Setelah 
sempat menembus level tertinggi senilai AS$21.275 per 
ton pada bulan Mei, harga nikel ditutup senilai AS$15.100 
per ton pada akhir tahun 2024. Demikian pula di Indonesia, 
berdasarkan Harga Mineral Acuan dari Kementerian ESDM, 
harga nikel pada Januari sejumlah AS$16.639 per ton dan 
ditutup AS$15.823 pada bulan Desember, dengan harga 
tertinggi mencapai nilai AS$18.962 per ton di bulan Juni.2
Nickel has long been known as a versatile metal used in various 
products. Its use is becoming increasingly massive as the world 
implements energy transition policies. Based on an analysis 
by Wood Mackenzie, the Indonesian Mining Association (IMA) 
highlighted that the global demand for nickel continues to be 
largely driven by the need for stainless steel raw materials, 
which is expected to reach 1.9 million tons by 2040. On the 
other hand, the demand for nickel used in electric vehicle (EV) 
batteries is projected to grow significantly from 163,000 tons 
in 2019 to 1.22 million tons by 2040. This growing demand for 
nickel is supported by resources available in various countries, 
including Indonesia.
Based on the Decree of the Minister of Energy and Mineral 
Resources of the Republic of Indonesia Number: 132.K/GL.01/
MEM.G/2024 concerning the National Mineral and Coal 
Resources and Reserves Balance in 2024, the total nickel ore 
resources in Indonesia amounted to 18.55 billion tons and the 
total reserves are 5.32 billion tons.1 Hence, Indonesia holds a 
strategic position in the world nickel market, attracting more 
investment from many nickel industry players.
Throughout 2024, there was a shift in the global supply 
and demand for nickel ore. This condition impacted the 
fluctuation of global nickel prices throughout the year. After 
briefly reaching its highest level of US$21,275 per ton in May, 
nickel prices closed at US$15,100 per ton at the end of 2024. 
Similarly, in Indonesia, based on the Reference Nickel Price 
from the Ministry of Energy and Mineral Resources, the price 
in January was US$16,369 per ton and closed at US$15,823 in 
December, with the highest price reaching US$18,962 per ton 
in June.2

PT Vale hanya beroperasi di Indonesia dengan segmen usaha 
penambangan dan pengolahan nikel. Perseroan melakukan 
kontrak penjualan jangka panjang dengan Vale Canada

sales contracts with Vale Canada Limited (VCL) and Sumitomo

produced.

Nickel Mining and Processing Business Segment 
Seluruh proses pertambangan dan pengolahan nikel 
berlangsung 
di

dengan

Produksi Operasi Pertambangan dan Produksi Nikel 
Dalam Matte
Penilaian kinerja operasi pertambangan dan produksi nikel 
dalam matte dilakukan berdasarkan volume realisasi produksi 
nikel dalam matte selama tahun buku, dibandingkan dengan 
periode sebelumnya. Realisasi total produksi nikel dalam 
matte tahun 2024 mencapai 71.311 ton atau 1% dari target 
70.805 ton, dan  meningkat 583 ton atau 1% dari tahun 2023 
sebesar 70.728 ton. Kenaikan ini menunjukkan kinerja positif 
Perseroan sebagai hasil pelaksanaan strategi pemeliharaan di 
sepanjang tahun, sehingga tidak ada perbaikan besar yang 
mempengaruhi kemampuan produksi. Realisasi tertinggi 
produksi nikel dalam matte berlangsung di triwulan keempat 
sebesar 18.528 ton, dan realisasi terendah berlangsung di 
triwulan kedua sebesar 16.576 ton. 
The entire nickel mining and processing process takes place 
in Indonesia, covering the production of nickel ore mining 
and the production of nickel in matte, as well as the sale of 
nickel matte.
Production of Mining Operations and Nickel In 
Matte Production
Performance assessment of mining operations and nickel 
in matte production is conducted based on the volume of 
nickel in matte production realized during the financial 
year, compared to the previous period. Total nickel in matte 
production in 2024 reached 71,311 tons or 1% of the target 
of 70,805 tons, and increased by 583 tons or 1% from 2023 
of 70,728 tons. This increase shows the Company's positive 
performance as a result of the implementation of the 
maintenance strategy throughout the year, so that there are 
no major improvements that affect production capability. 
The highest realization of nickel in matte production was at 
18,528 tons in the fourth quarter, and the lowest realization 
was at 16,576 tons in the second quarter.

Realisasi Penjualan Nikel Matte 
Penjualan yang dilakukan Perseroan menggunakan kontrak 
penjualan “harus ambil” jangka panjang dalam mata 
uang Dolar AS. Dalam menentukan harga jual, Perseroan 
menggunakan formula yang didasarkan atas harga tunai 
nikel di London Metal Exchange (LME), dan harga tunai kobalt 
di Fast Market. Pengukuran kinerja penjualan segmen usaha 
penambangan dan pengolahan nikel dilakukan berdasarkan 
pendapatan pada tahun buku, dari penjualan nikel matte 
kepada VCL dan SMM. 
Realisasi total volume penjualan nikel matte Perseroan 
tahun 2024 mencapai 72.625 ton atau 1% dari target 71.797 
ton, dan bertambah 1.517 ton atau 2% dari tahun 2023 
sebesar 71.108 ton, seiring bertambahnya volume produksi 
nikel dalam matte. Volume tertinggi penjualan nikel matte 
berlangsung pada triwulan keempat sebesar 19.196 ton, dan 
volume terendah berlangsung pada triwulan kedua sebesar 
17.505  ton. 
Realized Nickel Matte Sales 
The Company's sales are based on long-term “must-take” 
sales contracts denominated in US Dollars. In determining 
the selling price, the Company uses a formula based on the 
cash price of nickel on the London Metal Exchange (LME), and 
the cash price of cobalt on the Fast Market. Measurement 
of sales performance of the nickel mining and processing 
business segment is based on revenue in the financial year, 
from the sale of nickel matte to VCL and SMM.
The Company's total sales volume of nickel matte in 2024 
was realized at 72,625 tons or 1% of the target of 71,797 
tons, and increased by 1,517 tons or 2% from 71,108 tons in 
2023, as the production volume of nickel in matte increased. 
The highest volume of nickel matte sales was at 19,196 tons 
in the fourth quarter, and the lowest volume was at 17,505 
tons in the second quarter.

pada KNI menjadi 5,78%. Sebagai dampak dari penanaman 
modal selama 2024, Perseroan tidak lagi memiliki pengaruh 
signifikan karena tidak memiliki perwakilan Komisaris dan 
Direktur di KNI. Dengan demikian, pada tanggal 31 Desember 
2024, Perseroan mengakui kepemilikan atas KNI sebagai 
investasi pada saham sebesar AS$13,3 juta (nilai penuh).

Aset Derivatif
Nilai wajar aset derivatif terkait yang timbul dari hak 
tambahan partisipasi dalam investasi di KNI dan PT Huali 
Nickel Indonesia (HNI) dihitung berdasarkan estimasi atas 
proyeksi nilai ekuitas entitas-entitas pada dan setelah 
tanggal penyelesaian konstruksi smelter (penyelesaian 
mekanikal), serta proyeksi harga pelaksanaan hak tambahan 
partisipasi di masa depan. Nilai wajar atas hak tambahan 
partisipasi tersebut diakui oleh Perseroan sebagai aset 
derivatif sejumlah AS$4,75 juta pada 31 Desember 2024.

Aset Tetap Bersih
Sampai akhir tahun 2024, nilai Aset Tetap Bersih mengalami 
kenaikan sejumlah AS$279,56 juta atau 16,49% jika 
dibandingkan tahun 2023 sejumlah AS$1,70 miliar. Aset Tetap 
diasuransikan sebesar biaya pengganti untuk mencegah 
terjadinya risiko kerugian langsung yang disebabkan oleh 
semua risiko industri, termasuk namun tidak terbatas 
pada gempa bumi, kebakaran, kerusakan mekanis atau 
elektrik (termasuk gangguan usaha lainnya). Dengan nilai 
pertanggungan asuransi Perseroan berharap dapat menutup 
kerugian yang akan timbul karena risiko–risiko tersebut.

Employment Receivables in 2024 due to the repayment of 
loans by key management personnel.

Investment in Associated Entities 
On March 9, 2023, Huaqi (Singapore) Pte. Ltd invested in 
PT Kolaka Nickel Indonesia (KNI), changing the Company's 
ownership percentage in KNI from 100% to 20%. On December 
21, 2023, Ford Motor Company (FMC) invested in KNI which 
resulted in a change in the Company's ownership percentage 
to 18.3%. Therefore, as of December 31, 2023, the Company no 
longer controls KNI and recognized the Company's ownership 
as investment in associate.
•

invested in KNI, which resulted in a change in the Company's 
shareholding from 18.3% to 11.2%. On December 12, 2024, 
Huaqi (Singapore) Pte. Ltd and FMC made a reinvestment, 
decreasing the Company's ownership percentage to 5.78%. As 
a result of the reinvestment in 2024, the Company no longer 
has significant influence over KNI as it has no Commissioner 
and Director representatives in said company. Accordingly, as 
of December 31, 2024, the Company recognized its ownership 
in KNI as investment in shares amounting to US$13.3 million 
(full amount).

The fair value of the related derivative assets arising from 
the additional participation rights in investments in KNI (KNI)

estimated projections of the entities’ equity values on and 
after the completion of smelter construction (mechanical 
completion), as well as the projected future exercise price 
of the additional participation rights. The fair value of these 
additional participation rights is recognized by the Company 
as a derivative asset amounting to US$4.75 million as of 
December 31, 2024.

Net Fixed Assets

US$279.56 million, or 16.49%, compared to US$1.70 billion in

to prevent the risk of direct loss caused by all industrial risks, 
including but not limited to earthquake, fire, mechanical or 
electrical breakdown (including other business interruption). 
With the insurance coverage, the Company expects to cover 
losses arising from these risks.

EBITDA yang Disesuaikan
Adjusted EBITDA
Penggunaan istilah EBITDA yang Disesuaikan dilakukan 
untuk menggambarkan pengecualian terhadap biaya yang 
tidak berulang dan tidak dapat dianggap sebagai pengganti 
Laba Operasional atau sebagai acuan yang lebih baik dalam 
mengukur likuiditas dibanding Arus Kas Operasional yang 
dihitung sesuai dengan Standar Akuntansi Keuangan 
Indonesia. Perhitungan EBITDA dilakukan berdasarkan 
Laba Sebelum Pajak Penghasilan ditambah Biaya Keuangan 
Depresiasi serta menghilangkan dampak dari Laba/Rugi 
Selisih Kurs, Pendapatan Keuangan, Keuntungan/Kerugian 
atas Pengakuan Nilai Wajar Aset Derivatif, dan Keuntungan 
Pengakuan Nilai Wajar Investasi pada Saham.
Informasi mengenai kesanggupan Perseroan membayar 
utang, melakukan investasi, dan memenuhi kebutuhan 
modal kerja tercakup dalam EBITDA yang Disesuaikan. 
Selama tahun 2024, nilai EBITDA yang Disesuaikan lebih 
rendah sebesar 54,78% dibandingkan tahun sebelumnya. 
Hal ini dikarenakan Laba Tahun Berjalan yang lebih rendah 
dibandingkan tahun 2023 akibat biaya satu kali proses 
pemisahan dari Vale Base Metals pasca divestasi serta 
penurunan harga realisasi rata-rata nikel.
The use of the term Adjusted EBITDA is to illustrate the 
exclusion of non-recurring costs and should not be considered 
as a substitute for Operating Profit or as a better measure of 
liquidity than Operating Cash Flow calculated in accordance 
with Indonesian Financial Accounting Standards. The 
calculation of EBITDA is based on Profit Before Income Tax 
plus Depreciation Finance Costs while excluding the impact 
of Foreign Exchange Gains/Losses, Finance Income, Gains/
Losses from the Recognition of the Fair Value of Derivative 
Assets, and Gains from the Recognition of the Fair Value of 
Equity Investments.
Information on the Company's ability to service debt, make 
investments, and meet working capital requirements is

funds to be received by the Company in the framework of

of 8,233 Old Shares whose name is registered in the Company's 
Register of Shareholders (DPS) is entitled to 500 Pre-emptive 
Rights, where each one Preemptive Right entitles the holder 
to purchase as many as one New Share at the Exercise Price 
which must be paid in full at the time of submitting the 
Preemptive Rights exercise order.

Budget (RKAB) revision, allowing PT Vale to begin selling

the 2025 RKAB.

surrounding our operations. That is why we are committed 
to increasing local employee and contractor participation 
while actively investing in education, infrastructure, cultural 
programs, and community well-being to ensure long-term 
positive impact.

Social and Environmental Responsibility
Pada 24 Februari 2025, PT Vale kembali mencapai tonggak 
sejarah dengan menerima PROPER Emas dari Kementerian 
Lingkungan Hidup (KLH), yang merupakan pengakuan 
atas komitmen kami terhadap pengelolaan lingkungan 
yang melampaui kepatuhan terhadap peraturan. PT Vale 
merupakan satu-satunya perusahaan tambang nikel yang 
menerima penghargaan bergengsi ini dan satu dari hanya 
85 perusahaan—mewakili 2% dari semua peserta penilaian 
PROPER—yang memperoleh Peringkat Emas. 
 
Pada 2025, kami akan mengejar IRMA50, salah satu 
standar tertinggi untuk penambangan yang bertanggung 
jawab. Langkah-langkah ini akan semakin memantapkan 
posisi PT Vale sebagai pemimpin dalam operasi penambangan 
yang bertanggung jawab di panggung global. Khususnya, 
operasi Sorowako harus menjadi yang paling efisien, 
produktif, dan berkelanjutan. Prinsip "menghasilkan lebih 
banyak dengan lebih sedikit" harus menjadi inti dari kegiatan 
bisnis kami di masa mendatang.
Di 2025, Perseroan akan melanjutkan pengembangan proyek-
proyek strategis kami sebagai upaya untuk menciptakan nilai 
tambah bagi pemegang saham serta mendukung program 
hilirisasi pemerintah Indonesia.
Melanjutkan momentum yang sudah baik, Perseroan 
menargetkan 
pengembangan 
tambang 
Bahodopi 
di 
Kabupaten Morowali akan selesai tepat waktu pada triwulan 
ketiga 2025 dengan belanja modal yang lebih efisien, dan 
mulai beroperasi secara penuh.   
Pada proyek pengembangan tambang Pomalaa, Perseroan 
optimis bahwa capaian konstruksi di 2025 akan sesuai dengan 
target dan anggaran yang ada. Sementara itu, kami yakin 
bahwa kolaborasi kami dengan Zhejiang Huayou Cobalt Co., 
Ltd. dan Ford Motor Co. akan terus mengalami kemajuan 
yang pesat.
Dengan adanya izin lingkungan untuk proyek pengembangan 
tambang bijih limonit Sorowako yang kami terima pada akhir 
tahun lalu, maka kami yakin akan terus maju dengan rencana 
perluasan proyek pada 2025.
On February 24, 2025, PT Vale achieved again a historic 
milestone by receiving the Gold PROPER from the Ministry 
of Environment (MoE), recognizing our commitment to 
environmental management that goes beyond regulatory 
compliance. PT Vale is the only nickel mining company to 
receive this prestigious award and one of just 85 companies—
representing 2% of all participants in the PROPER 
assessment—to achieve Gold Rating. 
In 2025, we will pursue IRMA50 certification, one of the 
highest standards for responsible mining. These initiatives 
will further solidify PT Vale’s position as a global leader in 
responsible mining operations. Specifically, the Sorowako 
operations must become the most efficient, productive, and 
sustainable, with the principle of “producing more with less” 
at the core of our business activities going forward.
In 2025, the Company will continue developing strategic 
projects to create additional value for shareholders while 
supporting the Indonesian government’s downstream 
industrialization program.
Building on the positive momentum, the Company targets 
the completion of the Bahodopi mine development in 
Morowali Regency on schedule by the third quarter of 2025, 
with more efficient capital expenditures, and the mine will 
begin full operations.
For the Pomalaa mine development project, the Company 
is optimistic that 2025 construction progress will align with 
existing targets and budget. Additionally, we are confident 
that our collaboration with Zhejiang Huayou Cobalt Co., Ltd. 
and Ford Motor Co. will continue to make significant progress.
With the environmental permit for the Sorowako limonite 
ore development project received at the end of last year, 
we are confident that the expansion plans will continue to 
advance in 2025.
