Pembiayaan

Rp55.32 billion in 2022 to Rp90.95 billion in 2023. This segment 
contributed 6.46% to the total income.

Rp58.92 billion. This segment contributed 3.14% to the total 
income.

Rp14.76 billion in 2022 to Rp18.21 billion, Rp3.45 billion or 
23.38% increase. This segment contributed 2.02% to the total 
income.
