MANAGEMENT DISCUSSION & ANALYSIS
ANALISIS & PEMBAHASAN MANAJEMEN
Operational Review
Continuing the achievements from the previous year, in
2024 DWGL again showed positive performance, with
the comprehensive income for the current year attributable
to the owners of the parent entity soaring more than eight
times.
This achievement not only reflects DWGL's success in
continuously improving its operational performance but
also its success in implementing efficiency and
diversification throughout the year.
Consolidated Statement Of
Profit or Loss and Other
Comprehensive Income
Sales
In 2024, DWGL recorded sales of Rp3,337.3 billion, an
increase of 2.3% from Rp3,261.2 billion in the previous
year. This year's sales performance is consistent with last
year, where the increase in sales aligns with the rise in
coal delivery volumes.
The largest portion of sales was obtained from PT PLN
Nusantara Power at 35.1% or Rp1,170.4 billion. Followed
by two non-PLN clients, namely PT Sumber Segara
Primadaya at 34.2% or Rp1,141.9 billion and PT General
Energy Bali at 17.7% or Rp591.9 billion.
Tinjauan Operasional
Melanjutkan pencapaian dari tahun sebelumnya, pada
2024 DWGL kembali menunjukkan kinerja yang positif,
dimana laba komprehensif tahun berjalan yang dapat
diatribusikan kepada pemilik entitas induk melonjak lebih
dari delapan kali lipat.
Pencapaian ini tidak hanya menunjukkan keberhasilan
DWGL untuk terus meningkatkan kinerja operasionalnya
namun sekaligus keberhasilan dalam melakukan efisiensi
serta diversifikasi selama tahun berjalan.

Gross Profit
Despite the increase in sales, the cost of goods sold in
the current year only increased by 0.8% from Rp2,991.7
billion to Rp3,014.5 billion, following a decrease in
loading, unloading and other transportation costs from
Rp509.4 billion to Rp505.4 billion and late fines from Rp4.5
billion to Rp0.3 billion. This indicates the Company's
success in becoming more efficient in running its business.
With DWGL's success in maintaining sales performance
and controlling the cost of goods sold, the Company
managed to record a significant increase in gross profit
of 19.7%, from Rp269.5 billion to Rp322.7 billion for the
fiscal year. As a result, the gross profit margin increased
from 8.3% in the previous year to 9.7% in the current
year.
EBITDA
In 2024, DWGL recorded an EBITDA of Rp270.4 billion,
reflecting a 14.1% increase compared to the previous
year’s EBITDA of Rp237.0 billion.
In line with this growth, the Company’s EBITDA Margin
also improved, from 6.3% in 2023 to 8.1% in 2024.
Income from Operating
In 2024, the Company was also able to reduce operating
expenses, where despite the increase in sales, selling
expenses remained at the same level as in 2023, at Rp1.0
billion. Meanwhile, general and administrative expenses
during the same period decreased from Rp59.6 billion
to Rp51.9 billion. The most significant reduction came
from the rent expenses in the general and administrative
expenses, which decreased by Rp8.2 billion.
With this reduction in expenses, the Company's income
from operating grew by 29.2%, from Rp208.9 billion in
2023 to Rp269.8 billion in 2024. The income from
operating margin also increased from 6.41% in 2023 to
8.09% in 2024, reflecting the positive impact of the
changes in cost structure on the Company's profitability.
Profit before Income Tax
DWGL recorded a profit before income tax of Rp149.9
billion in 2024, a sharp increase of 537.9% compared to
Rp23.5 billion in the previous year.
This increase was primarily due to the reduction in other
expenses-net from Rp134.5 billion to Rp78.1 billion, with
Kontribusi klien non-PLN yang lebih besar daripada PLN
juga menunjukkan keberhasilan dari strategi diversifikasi
klien yang dijalankan.

which was Rp52.1 billion, was fully paid off in 2024, thus

Cash Flow from Operating Activities
DWGL recorded net cash generated from operating

customers, which rose by 32.9% from Rp2,794.9 billion
to Rp3,715.8 billion, in line with the increase in sales.

in net cash used for investing activities, increasing by
213.1% from Rp18.4 billion to Rp57.5 billion. The majority
of the investment funds were used for the purchase of
mutual funds, which amounted to Rp120.0 billion in 2024,
compared to only Rp20.0 billion in the previous year.
