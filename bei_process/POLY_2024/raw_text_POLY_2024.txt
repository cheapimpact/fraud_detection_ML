Net Sales/ Revenue 
 
Net sales revenue was USD190.15 million in 2024 
as compared to USD288.55 million in 2023, 
showing a significant drop of 34.1%. The export 
sales were USD31.54 million or 16.59% of net sales, 
and domestic sales were USD158.61 million or 
83.41% of the net sales. The decline in revenue 
occurred in both the domestic and export 
markets by 35.53% and 25.84%, respectively. The 
fall in revenue was primarily attributed to a 
significant reduction in volume 34.62% and a 
drop in selling prices.  
 
 
 
Demand in the domestic market fell due to the 
closure of downstream units stemming from 
sluggish trading conditions worldwide.  While the 
global economic downturn reduced demand for 
clothing and textile products, an influx of low-
cost imports also hindered the operations of 
Indonesian 
manufacturers. 
Amid 
growing 
uncertainties and an imbalanced market, prices 
remained volatile. Margins for many products in 
the value chain were compressed by market 
conditions, 
putting 
them 
under 
pressure 
throughout the year.  
 
The other operational revenue was USD1.85 
million, realized through the sale of recycled 
products, indirect materials, and waste/scrap.  
 
 
 
Cost of Goods Sold 
 
The cost of goods sold for the year 2024 was 
USD193.50 million, compared to USD288.98 
million in 2023, reflecting a decrease of 33.04% 
from last year, primarily due to a significant 
reduction in volume and a decline in raw material 
costs (which also led to lower sales prices). 
However, the conversion cost per unit of product 
increased because of higher energy costs and 
lower capacity utilization.
