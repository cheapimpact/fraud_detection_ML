Analisa Operasi per segmen
Perseroan melakukan kegiatan usaha pengembangan, 
pengoperasian perkebunan, perdagangan dan pengolahan 
kelapa sawit dan minyak nabati (crude palm oil) melalui 
entitas anaknya. Lahan perkebunan entitas anak signifikan 
berlokasi di provinsi Sumatera Selatan, Kalimantan Barat 
dan sedikit di provinsi Kalimantan Tengah. Tidak ada 
kegiatan usaha lain dan signifikan aset di luar kegiatan 
usaha dan ketiga provinsi yang disebutkan.

Operational Segment Analysis
The Company carries out business activities in developing, 
operating plantations, trading and processing palm oil 
and vegetable oil (crude palm oil) through its subsidiaries. 
Significant plantation areas for subsidiaries are located 
in the province of South Sumatra, West Kalimantan 
and less so in the province of Central Kalimantan. No 
other business activities and significant assets outside 
the business activities and the three Provinces that are 
mentioned.
The company does not need to review operations by 
segments required for transparent information as long 
as it is operating within the scope of business activities, 
performance, and asset management as explained above.

Age Profile of Planted Areas Dec 31, 2024
Dibanding lahan APL tahun sebelumnya seluas 17.188 ha, 
berarti ada tambahan sekitar 36% atau seluas 9.610 ha 
yang berasal dari lahan entitas anak SKM yang diakuisisi 
Perseroan melalui entitas anak Suryabumi per akhir tahun 
2023, pengesahan kehakiman per awal tahun 2024.
SKM punya total lahan seluas 9.610 ha terdiri atas lahan 
inti seluas 7.716 ha dan lahan plasma seluas 1.893 ha. 
Saat diakuisis, diatas lahannya sudah tertanam kelapa 
sawit seluas 3.046 ha, di lahan inti seluas 2.650 ha dan di 
lahan plasma seluas 396 ha.
Selain APL, perseroan juga punya lahan dengan status 
Hutan Produksi yang dapat Dikonversi (HPK) seluas 1.561 
ha atas nama TSP. Perijinan TSP seluas 2.573 ha terdiri 
atas lahan APL seluas 1.012 ha dan HPK seluas 1.561 
ha masih dalam proses pengurusan Ijin Pinjam Pakai 
Kawasan Hutan (IPPKH). Ijin Usaha Perkebunan, atau IUP 
yang diajukan melalui Sistim perizinan terintegrasi secara 
elektronik, atau sistim Online Single Submission, atau 
OSS masih dalam proses.
Untuk mempertahankan pencapaian panen, entitas anak 
Suryabumi telah melakukan replanting atas tanaman 
menghasilkan yang hasil panen buahnya tidak komersil 
lagi. Replanting direalisasi tahun 2024 seluas 895 ha. 
Ditambah realisasi sampai dengan tahun sebelumnya 
seluas 308 ha, maka sampai dengan tahun 2024 sudah 
direalisasi replanting seluas 1.203 ha.
Posisi lahan tertanam per akhir tahun 2024 dari Perseroan 
adalah sebagai berikut:
Compared to the previous year's APL land area of 17,188 
hectares, there has been an increase of approximately 
36% or 9,610 hectares, derived from the land owned by 
the subsidiary SKM, which was acquired by the Company 
through its subsidiary Suryabumi at the end of 2023, with 
judicial approval in early 2024.
SKM has a total land area of 9,610 hectares, consisting 
of 7,716 hectares of nucleus estate and 1,893 hectares of 
plasma estate. At the time of acquisition, 3,046 hectares 
were already planted with oil palm, including 2,650 
hectares in the nucleus estate and 396 hectares in the 
plasma estate.
Apart from land for other use (APL), the company also has 
one area of Productive Forest for conversion (HPK), an 
area of 1561 Ha under TSP. The TSP license covers 2,573 
ha and consists of  land for over use (APL) amounting to 
an area of 1,012 Ha, and productive forest for conversion 
(HPK) covering an area of 1,561 Ha, currently in progress 
the permit of permission to utilize and manage (IPPKH). 
The permit for plantation use (IUP) issued via Online 
Single Submission (OSS) is currently in process.  
To maintain harvest performance, Suryabumi’s subsidiary 
has carried out replanting on mature oil palm trees that 
are no longer commercially productive. In 2024, replanting 
was carried out on 895 hectares. Combined with the 308 
hectares replanted in previous year, the total replanting 
area reached 1,203 hectares by the end of 2024.

Lahan tertanam diklasifikasi atas lahan "Tanaman Belum 
Menghasilkan" atau TBM yaitu lahan dengan tanaman usia

Total lahan tertanam per akhir tahun 2024 seluas 15.596 
ha naik dari 13.114 ha atau naik sekitar 18,6% dibanding 
posisi per akhir tahun lalu karena tambahan tanaman baru 
seluas 2.483 ha dan replanting seluas 895 ha (mutasi TM 
ke TBM).
Lahan TM per akhir tahun seluas 13.938 ha bertambah 
1.187 ha, atau bertambah sekitar 12% dibanding posisi 
per akhir tahun lalu yang sebesar 12.452 ha. Porsi lahan 
TM ini merupakan 89% total lahan tertanam.
Tambahan TM berasal dari matangnya TBM dan dari TM 
entitas SKM. Berkurangnya TM (menjadi TBM) karena 
program replanting.
Didalam lahan TM, seluas 4.802 ha, sekitar 34% nya 
adalah TM dengan usia 9 tahun kebawah yang masih 
terikat kewajiban angsuran pokok dan bunga utang bank, 
dan perlu subsidi di arus kas operasinya.
Kelompok TM usia 9 tahun kebawah ini berkurang 2.356 
Ha atau turun 96% dibanding tahun sebelumnya. Lahan 
TM usia 10 tahun keatas yang menopang arus kas 
Perusahaan, menjadi sejumlah 9.137 ha, atau sekitar 59% 
total tanaman Perusahaan turun 9%.
Cultivated land is classified as "Immature Palms" or TBM, 
namely land with plants up to 4 years old, and "Productive 
palms” or TM, namely land with palms over 4 years old.
The total planted area as of the end of 2024 was 15,596 Ha, 
up from 13,114 Ha or up 18,6% compared to the position at 
the end of last year due to the addition of 2.483 Ha of new 
plantings and 895 Ha of replanting (TM to TBM mutation).
TM's land area of 13,938 hectares at the end of the year 
increased by 1.187 hectares, or an increase of around 12% 
compared to the position at the end of last year which 
amounted to 12,452 hectares. This portion of TM land 
constitutes 89% of the total planted area.
The increase in mature plantations (TM) comes from the 
maturation of immature plantations (TBM) and the addition 
of TM from SKM’s estate. Meanwhile, the decrease in TM 
(converted to TBM) is due to the replanting program.
This TM land area is 4,802 ha, around 34% of which are 
TM aged 9 years and under whose results are not yet fully 
commercial, and still requires financial support for the 
operating cash flow.
The TM group aged 9 years and under decreased by 2.356 
Ha or decreased by 96% compared to the previous year. 
TM's land aged 10 years and over which supports the 
Company's cash flow, amounted to 9.137 Ha, or around 
59% of the Company's total plantations, an decrease of 
9%.

Fresh Fruit Bunches or FFB from own plantations, or 
nucleus estates in 2024 amounted to 225.259 tons net 
weight after sorting, an increase of 16.323 tons or an 
increase of around 8% compared to last year's harvest.
SKM’s nucleus estate harvest amounted to 35,075 
tons, while the harvest from other sources outside SKM 
totaled 190,184 tons. Compared to the previous year, this 
represents a decrease of 18,752 tons, or approximately 
9%.
The yield ratio of the nucleus estate harvest in 2024 
declined by 0.15 points from the previous year's 16.78 
to 16.63 tons per hectare of mature plantation (TM), 
representing a decrease of approximately 1%.
The decline in harvest yield was influenced by the impact 
of El Niño or the prolonged drought in the previous year led 
to a drop in palm oil production.
The supply of fresh fruit bunches (TBS) processed at the 
mill came from nucleus estate harvests totaling 175,476 
tons and externally sourced (purchased) TBS from outside 
plantations amounting to 31,296 tons, bringing the total to 
206,772 tons, all sourced from Sumatra.

The total TBS processed in 2024 decreased by 20,253 
tons, or 9% compared to the previous year’s realization. 
This decline was due to the sale of harvested TBS from 
TSP and SKM plantations instead of processing them at 
the mill.
The supply of externally sourced TBS in 2024 increased 
by 2,675 tons, or 9%, compared to the previous year’s 
realization. This increase was driven by the contribution 
of harvested SKM Plasma, which needed to be purchased.
The sold TBS harvest amounted to 49,783 tons (net 
48,750 tons, with 1,033 tons allocated for stock and other 
purposes). This came from the nucleus estates of TSP 
and SKM, as well as SKM’s nucleus and plasma estates

processing mill in the region.

to the previous year. The percentage decrease in CPO 
production was higher than the 9% decline in processed 
TBS volume.

0.91 points, from 22.16% last year to 21.25% in 2024, a 
decrease of about 4% because of the impact of El Niño or 
the prolonged drought in the previous year led to a drop in 
palm oil production.
The kernel extraction rate in 2024 also declined by 0.23 
points, from 4.64% last year to 4.41%, representing a 
decrease of approximately 4.89%.

kepada pembeli bebas dengan harga umum yang berlaku

karena tonase TBS dijual naik 38.218 ton atau naik 363%. 
Harga jual yang bisa direalisasikan  naik lebih besar yaitu 
sebesar 28,83%.
Penjualan TBS direalisasi atas hasil panen kebun di 
Kalimantan (TSP & SKM), karena disana belum punya 
pabrik sendiri. Sedang pembelian TBS direalisasi atas hasil 
panen kebun plasma SKM, dan dari kebun luar di Sumatera 
(pembelian TBS yang dari kebun luar untuk diproses di pabrik 
di Suryabumi).
The company sells its products, namely CPO and kernel, to 
independent buyers at prevailing market prices at the time 
of the transaction. To maximize sales revenue, the company 
actively monitors global CPO price trends. At times, the 
company may hold back inventory from being sold if there 
is an upward price trend, based on price forecasts. However, 
such a decision is carefully balanced against liquidity 
considerations. Conversely, the company may release its 
inventory to buyers due to liquidity needs, even if global CPO 
prices are on an upward trend. All CPO and kernel sales are 
transacted in Indonesian Rupiah (IDR) and are based on 
deliveries to buyers from the company’s tanks or warehouses.
Total sales revenue in 2024 reached IDR 804.65 billion, 
marking an 8% increase compared to the previous year. 
Approximately 73% of total sales, or IDR 585.61 billion, came 
from CPO sales, while the remaining 27% was derived from 
kernel and FFB sales.
Compared to last year’s performance, CPO sales decreased by 
IDR 73.34 billion, or 11%, due to a decline in the volume of CPO 
sold by 14,752 tons, or 25%. However, the realized selling price 
increased by 18.24%.
Kernel sales increased by IDR 18.56 billion, or 30%, despite 
a 19% drop in kernel volume sold, which declined by 2,168 
tons. This was offset by a significant 60.29% increase in the 
realized selling price.
FFB sales rose sharply by IDR 115.17 billion, or 496%, driven 
by a substantial increase in FFB volume sold, which rose by 
38,218 tons, or 363%. This growth was further supported by a 
28.83% increase in the realized selling price.
The sale of FFB is realized from the harvest of plantations 
located in Kalimantan (TSP & SKM), considering that the area 
does not yet have its own processing plant. Meanwhile, the 
purchase of FFB is made from the harvest of SKM’s plasma 
plantations and from external plantations in Sumatra. The 
FFB purchased from external plantations is intended to be 
further processed at the plant located in Suryabumi.

Strategi Pemasaran
Menyadari skala operasinya kecil dan bukan penentu 
harga, Perseroan menerapkan strategi pemasarannya 
bertumpu pada potensi internal yang bisa diupayakan, 
yaitu “kualitas produk”. Kualitas CPO yang dihasilkan 
sesuai standar mutu CPO dalam SNI 01-2901-2006 yaitu 
kadar asam lemak bebas, air dan kotoran masing-masing 
maksimum 5%, 0,25% dan 0,25%. Produk Perseroan 
berupa CPO dengan kualitas yang sesuai standar, dijual 
kepada pembeli bebas dengan harga umum yang berlaku 
saat transaksi. Sejauh ini, tidak ada program promosi 
yang dilakukan. Perseroan cukup menjaga kepercayaan 
dan hubungan baik dengan pelanggan. 
Semua  penjualan CPO maupun kernel dilakukan dalam 
satuan rupiah dan berdasar transaksi penyerahan ke Pembeli 
dari tanki atau gudang Perseroan.
Excluding this interest expense from operating cash flow, 
the surplus from operating activities would increase to 
Rp201.38  billion.  
Investment activities showed a cash surplus of Rp118.76 
billion.
This surplus arose primarily due the liquidation of current 
investments amounting to Rp140.67 billion, minus the 
acquisition of fixed assets totaling Rp11.06 billion and 
investment in nucleus and plasma plantations amounting 
to Rp25.05 billion, offset by returns from plasma 
plantation investments of Rp14.22 billion, resulting in a 
net plantation investment of Rp10.83 billion.
Financing activities recorded a cash deficit of Rp489,26

amounting to Rp181.69 billion, repayment of non-bank 
financial institution loans totaling Rp292,38 billion, and 
repayment of lease obligations amounting to Rp15.18

At the end of the year, the company’s cash and cash 
equivalents stood at Rp154.53 billion, down from Rp355.36 
billion at the beginning of the year. This represents a 
decrease of Rp200.83 billion or a decline of approximately 
57%.
Despite the significant reduction, the year-end cash 
balance remains above the minimum threshold necessary 
to ensure smooth liquidity management.
Work Plan & Realization
Company scale
The company is classified as small-scale in the palm oil 
plantation and CPO business nationally. The total land 
managed by the company in 2024 was 15,596 hectares, 
which is less than 0.07% of the total national oil palm 
plantation area of around 17 million hectares (data from 
the Ministry of Agriculture, 2024). With such a scale, the 
company must base its annual work plan on its internal 
potential.
Marketing strategy
Recognizing that its scale of operations is small and that it 
is not a price setter, the Company implements its marketing 
strategy based on internal potential that can be pursued, 
namely 'product quality.' The quality of the CPO produced 
meets the quality standards for CPO as per SNI 01-2901-
2006, with free fatty acid content, moisture, and impurities 
not exceeding 5%, 0.25%, and 0.25%, respectively. The 
Company’s product, which is CPO of standard quality, is 
sold to buyers at the prevailing market price at the time 
of the transaction. So far, no promotional programs have 
been carried out. The Company focuses on maintaining 
trust and good relationships with its customers.
All sales of CPO and kernels are conducted in rupiah and 
based on transactions for delivery to the Buyer from the 
Company’s tank or warehouse

Target Struktur Modal
Struktur modal menjadi perhatian manajemen dalam 
mengelola keuangan, dengan utamakan penggalian dan 
pendanaan dari sumber internal. Walau, dalam kondisi 
tertentu tidak bisa dihindari pendanaan dengan dana 
pinjaman dengan segala konsekwensinya (bunga dan 
sebagainya).
Perseroan menetapkan target untuk struktur modal, yaitu 
modal kerja bersih positif atau rasio likuiditas sehat 
diatas 100%, dan target rasio solvabilitas sehat berupa 
rasio total pendanaan dengan utang maksimal 70% aset, 
atau rasio utang terhadap ekuitas maksimal sebesar 2,33 
kali. Pencapaian target struktur modal seperti dilaporkan 
di analisa likuiditas & solvabilitas 
The Company monitors the fluctuations in CPO prices 
and decides on product sales transactions by considering 
financial liquidity (working capital), production plans, 
and the storage tank capacity for the Company’s CPO 
products. It may happen that the Company holds its 
inventory back from selling to Buyers due to predictions 
of rising CPO prices. Conversely, the Company might also 
release its inventory to Buyers for liquidity reasons and/or 
because the storage tank capacity is full, even if the global 
CPO price trend is currently on the rise
Specifically for the sale of fruit by the subsidiaries TSP 
and SKM, both located on the island of Kalimantan, this 
is conducted because they do not yet have facilities for 
processing crude oil
Planning process
With a solid grasp of palm oil cultivation techniques and 
governance, management has developed the Company's 
annual performance plan. This plan begins with an 
inventory of inti palm oil plantation land, plasma land, and 
land owned by farmers around the factory. Management 
sets targets for fruit tonnage availability sourced from 
harvests in inti plantations, purchases from plasma 
plantations, and purchases from third party small holders.
Taking into account the quality and age of the plantings, 
the fruit yield is predicted, and targets for CPO production 
tonnage and CPO sales are established. A budget for the 
necessary activities is then formulated.
Furthermore, considering the forecasted CPO selling price, 
sales value targets are established. Then, by considering 
the budgeted costs, targets for profit/loss and the 
target cash surplus that can be generated (EBITDA) are 
determined.
The target capital expenditure investment for additional 
costs for immature plantings and for the acquisition/
replacement of fixed assets and other investments is set 
to be funded as much as possible from internal sources.
The annual operational and financial performance plan 
or targets are established and periodically monitored for 
achievement by the Board of Directors and the Board of 
Commissioners.
Target Capital Structure
The capital structure is a concern for management in 
financial management, prioritizing exploration and funding 
from internal sources. However, in certain situations, 
financing with borrowed funds cannot be avoided, along 
with all its consequences (interest, etc.).
The Company sets a target for its capital structure, 
which includes a positive net working capital or a healthy 
liquidity ratio above 100%, and a healthy solvency ratio 
where total funding through debt is a maximum of 70% 
of assets, or a debt-to-equity ratio of no more than 2.33 
times. Achievement of the capital structure targets is 
reported in the liquidity and solvency analysis
