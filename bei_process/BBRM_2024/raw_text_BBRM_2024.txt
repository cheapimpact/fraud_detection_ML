Tinjauan Makro Ekonomi dan Industri
Macro Economics and Industry Overview
Tahun 2024, perekonomian global dibayangi ketidakpastian 
akibat berbagai faktor seperti ketegangan geopolitik, risiko 
perang dagang, dan meningkatnya proteksionisme yang 
memberikan tekanan pada prospek pertumbuhan ekonomi 
dunia. 
In 2024, the global economy is overshadowed by 
uncertainty due to various factors such as geopolitical 
tensions, the risk of trade wars, and increasing 
protectionism which will put pressure on the prospects 
for global economic growth.
Menurut laporan Organisasi Kerja sama dan Pembangunan 
Ekonomi (OECD), pertumbuhan ekonomi global diproyeksi 
tetap stabil di angka 3,1% pada tahun 2024 dan sedikit 
meningkat menjadi 3,2% pada tahun 2025. Hal itu, 
lantaran masih banyaknya tantangan di berbagai sektor 
penopangnya.
 According to the report of the Organisation for Economic 
Co-operation and Development (OECD), global economic 
growth is projected to remain stable at 3.1% in 2024 and 
increase slightly to 3.2% in 2025. This is because there are 
still many challenges in various supporting sectors.
Di tengah tantangan global, perekonomian Indonesia 
menunjukkan 
ketahanannya 
dengan 
mencatat 
pertumbuhan sebesar 5,03 Persen, didorong oleh stabilnya 
konsumsi domestik, peningkatan investasi, serta perbaikan 
kinerja ekspor.
Amidst global challenges, the Indonesian economy 
demonstrated its resilience by recording growth of 5.03 
percent, driven by stable domestic consumption, increased 
investment, and improved export performance.
SKK Migas mencatat produksi rata-rata harian minyak 
sebesar 580.224 barel per hari sepanjang 2024, meleset 
dari target lifting minyak di APBN 2024 sebesar 653 ribu 
bph. Sedangkan pada tahun 2025, SKK Migas menetapkan 
target lifting sebesar 1.610 ribu barel setara minyak per 
hari (BOEPD). Kini, konsumsi minyak Indonesia mencapai 
sekitar 1,6 juta barel per hari (BOPD), sedangkan produksi 
minyak dalam negeri masih jauh di bawah angka tersebut.
SKK Migas recorded an average daily oil production of 
580,224 barrels per day throughout 2024, missing the oil 
lifting target in the 2024 State Budget of 653 thousand 
bpd. Meanwhile, in 2025, SKK Migas set a lifting target of 
1,610 thousand barrels of oil equivalent per day (BOEPD). 
Currently, Indonesia's oil consumption has reached around 
1.6 million barrels per day (BOPD), while domestic oil 
production is still far below that figure.
Permintaan minyak mentah dunia tahun 2025 menurut 
laporan IEA diproyeksikan akan meningkat sebesar 1,1 juta 
barel per hari menjadi 103,9 juta barel per hari. Tingginya 
kebutuhan konsumsi minyak di dalam negeri yang masih 
menjadi sumber energi vital maupun permintaan minyak 
dunia, memberikan dampak positif bagi industry kapal 
penunjang lepas Pantai terutama untuk pengeboran di laut
World crude oil demand in 2025 according to the IEA report 
is projected to increase by 1.1 million barrels per day to 
103.9 million barrels per day. The high demand for domestic 
oil consumption which is still a vital source of energy and 
world oil demand, has a positive impact on the offshore 
support vessel industry, especially for drilling at sea.

Hingga akhir tahun 2024, segmen Kapal Penunjang Lepas 
Pantai masih menjadi kontributor pendapatan terbesar 
bagi Perseroan, yaitu mencapai 88 % di tahun 2024. 
Pendapatan di segmen ini meningkat sebesar USD 2,95 juta 
dari sebesar USD 8,85 juta di tahun 2023 menjadi sebesar 
11,8 juta di tahun 2024. Faktor utama yang mendorong 
peningkatan ini adalah kenaikan tarif harian sewa kapal 
pada segment kapal penunjang lepas pantai. Selain itu 
Perseroan juga berhasil mempertahankan tingkat utilisasi 
kapal diatas 80 %, sehingga Pendapatan Perseroan tetap 
stabil dan meningkat.
Sementara itu, pada segmen Kapal Self Propelled Barge 
pendapatan Perseroan mengalami penurunan sebesar 
USD61 ribu atau sebesar 3,6%. Berbeda dengan segmen 
kapal Offshore Support Vessel, segmen kapal Self Propelled 
Barge mengalami penurunan pendapatan sepanjang 
tahun 2024. Faktor utama yang menyebabkan penurunan 
ini adalah kapal ini menjalani perawatan dan docking di 
awal tahun, sehingga mengurangi waktu operasional dan 
berdampak pada berkurangnya pendapatan sewa. 
Until the end of 2024, the Offshore Support Vessel segment 
is still the largest revenue contributor for the Company, 
reaching 88 % in 2024.  Revenue in this segment increased 
by USD2.95 million from USD8.85 million in 2023 to USD11.8 
million in 2024. The main factor driving this increase was 
the increase in vessel daily chartered rate in the offshore 
support vessel segment. In addition, the Company also 
managed to maintain the vessel utilization rate above 80%, 
so that the Company's revenue has increased and remain 
stable.
Meanwhile, in the Self Propelled Barge segment, the 
Company’s revenue decreased by USD61 thousand or 
3.6%. Unlike Offshore Support Vessel segment, the Self 
Propelled Barge segment experienced a decline in revenue 
throughout 2024. The main factor causing this decline was 
that these vessels underwent maintenance and docking at 
the beginning of the year, thus reducing operational time 
and and resulting in reduced rental income.
Dalam Operasi Penyewaan Kapal, Perseroan terus 
melakukan diversifikasi atau strategi baik dari segi cakupan 
geografis maupun segmen pasar. Saat ini Armada kapal 
Perseroan beroperasi di Indonesia, Malaysia dan Thailand.
In Ship chartering operations, the Company continues to 
diversify or strategize both in terms of geographic coverage 
and market segments. Currently the Company’s fleet of 
vessels operates in Indonesia, Malaysia and Thailand.

BEBAN pokok pendapatan
Beban Pokok Pendapatan Perseroan pada tahun 2024 
adalah sebesar USD6,93 juta meningkat sebesar USD1,05 
juta atau sebesar 18% dibandingkan tahun 2023 yang 
tercatat sebesar USD5,88juta. Peningkatan ini sejalan 
dengan peningkatan pendapatan. 
cost of Goods
The Company’s cost of revenues in 2024 were USD6.93 
million increase USD1.05 million or 18% compared to 2023 
which was recorded at USD5.88 million. This increase is in 
line with the increase in revenue.
LABA bruto
Perseroan membukukan laba kotor sebesar USD6,5 juta 
pada tahun 2024, mengalami kenaikan sebesar USD1,83 
juta atau sebesar 39% dibandingkan tahun 2023 yang 
tercatat sebesar USD4,67 juta. Peningkatan laba bruto 
disebabkan oleh meningkatnya pendapatan Perseroan 
pada segment kapal penunjang lepas pantai karena 
adanya kenaikan tarif harian sewa kapal. Tingkat utilisasi 
kapal Perseroan pada segmen ini mencapai 81,49% sedikit 
menurun dibandingkan tahun 2023 sebesar 84,67%.
GROSS PROFIT
The Company recorded a gross profit amounted USD6.5 
million in 2024, increase by USD1.83 million or 39% 
compare to 2023 which was recorded at USD4.67 million. 
The increase in gross profit  was due to the increase in the 
Company’s revenue in the offshore support vessel segment 
cause there is an increasing in daily charter rates. The 
Company’s vessel utilization rate in this segment reached 
81.49%, slightly decreasing compared to 2023 which was 
84.67%.
BEBAN USAHA
Beban usaha Perseroan pada tahun 2024 sebesar USD993 
ribu,  meningkat sebesar USD75 ribu atau sebesar 8% 
dibandingkan tahun 2023 yang tercatat sebesar USD918 
ribu. Peningkatan tersebut disebabkan karena adanya 
kenaikan biaya gaji karyawan   dan pemberian bonus 
kepada karyawan. 
OPERATING EXPENSES
The Company's operating expenses in 2024 amounted 
USD993 thousand, increased by USD75 thousand or 8% 
compare to 2023 which was recorded at USD918 thousand. 
This increase was due to an increased in employee salary 
expense and bonuses given to employees.
BEBAN LAINNYA
Beban lainnya Perseroan pada tahun 2024 sebesar 
USD969 menurun sebesar USD15 ribu atau sebesar 94% 
dibandingkan dengan tahun 2023 yang tercatat sebesar 
USD16 ribu. Beban lainnya Perseroan pada tahun 2024 
merupakan biaya administrasi yang dibebankan ke 
Perseroan atas pembayaran yang diterima, sedangkan 
beban lainnya Perseroan di tahun 2023 merupakan beban 
atas pajak penghasilan anak Perusahaan yang  tidak 
tercatat di tahun 2022.
OTHER EXPENSES
The Company's other expenses in 2024 amounted USD969 
decreased by USD15 thousand or 94% compare to 2023 
which was recorded at USD16 thousand.  The Company's 
other expenses in 2024 are administrative costs charged to 
the Company for payments received, while the Company's 
other expenses in 2023 are expenses for income tax of 
subsidiaries that were not recorded in 2022.
PENDAPATAN LAINNYA
Pendapatan lainnya Perseroan pada tahun 2024 sebesar 
USD2,71 juta mengalami peningkatan sebesar USD2,20 
juta atau sebesar 437% dibandingkan dengan tahun 2023 
yang tercatat sebesar USD505 ribu. Kenaikan pendapatan 
lainnya Perseroan pada tahun 2024 terutama disebabkan 
karena adanya penghapusan utang kepada pihak berelasi 
PT Marcopolo Shipyard sebesar USD2,46 juta. Selain itu 
kenaikan  pendapatan lainnya juga disebabkan karena 
adanya  penerimaan piutang yang telah dihapus bukukan 
sebesar USD31 ribu.
OTHER INCOME
The Company's other income in 2024  amounted USD2.71 
million increase by USD2.20 million or 437% compared 
to 2023 which was recorded at USD505 thousand. The 
increase in the Company's other income in 2024 was mainly 
due to the write off related party payable to PT Marcopolo 
Shipyard amounted USD2.46 million. In addition, the 
increase in other income was also due to the receipt of 
receivables that had been written off amounting to USD31 
thousand.

especially to meet domestic electricity needs. The Ministry 
of Energy and Mineral Resources (ESDM) stated that the 
target for domestic coal supply obligations or Domestic 
Market Obligation (DMO) in 2025 will be greater than in 
2024. The need for coal in Indonesia in 2025 is estimated to 
reach 239.7 million tons.
In order to meet electricity needs, 67% of power plants in 
Indonesia still use coal as fuel. This shows that Indonesia 
is still unable to fully free itself and  very dependent on 
the use of coal as an energy source. This will also have a 
positive impact for the prospects of Self Propelled Barge 
fleet chartered that  engaged in renting vessel to transport 
coal.
Seeing the prospect of increasing coal demand, the 
Company is taking the opportunity by adding to the SPB 
fleet which has been operating since October 2024.
