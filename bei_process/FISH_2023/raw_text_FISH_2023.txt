Ekonomi dan Industri
TINJAUAN DAN PROSPEK EKONOMI
Sampai akhir tahun 2023, situasi ekonomi global masih 
dipengaruhi oleh ketidakpastian yang disebabkan oleh 
faktor global. Inflasi yang tinggi di atas perkiraan telah 
mendorong kenaikan suku bunga untuk mengendalikan 
laju inflasi. Ketegangan geopolitik, seperti konflik Rusia-
Ukraina dan Palestina-Israel yang tak terelakkan, menjadi 
faktor yang turut mempengaruhi kondisi global. Kondisi 
ini telah meredam optimisme terhadap pertumbuhan 
ekonomi global.
Meskipun dalam konteks situasi ekonomi global yang 
melambat, Indonesia berhasil mencatatkan pertumbuhan 
ekonomi yang tinggi dibandingkan dengan negara-
negara di ASEAN dan G20. Pertumbuhan ekonomi 
Indonesia pada tahun 2023 sebesar 5,2% menunjukkan 
kinerja ekonomi yang kuat. Angka tersebut melampaui 
pertumbuhan ekonomi negara-negara ASEAN seperti 
Malaysia (3,77%) dan Korea (1,26%), serta bahkan 
lebih tinggi dari negara-negara G20 seperti Amerika 
Serikat (2,5%), Perancis (0,9%), bahkan Jerman yang 
mengalami kontraksi (-0,3%).
Pertumbuhan ekonomi Indonesia yang tinggi sebagian 
besar disumbang oleh konsumsi rumah tangga, investasi 
dalam pembentukan modal bruto, serta sektor-sektor 
ekonomi 
seperti 
transportasi, 
pergudangan, 
dan 
konstruksi yang aktif. Hal ini menunjukkan ketahanan 
ekonomi Indonesia di tengah dinamika ekonomi global 
yang tidak menentu.
Tahun 2024, pertumbuhan ekonomi di Indonesia tetap 
diperkirakan di atas 5% yang lebih didukung oleh 
konsumsi domestik dan pengeluaran pemerintah.
TINJAUAN INDUSTRI
Perdagangan dan Distribusi Bahan Pangan dan Pakan
Walaupun harga rata-rata tahun 2023 mulai lebih rendah 
dibanding tahun sebelumnya, perdagangan dan distribusi 
bahan pangan dan pakan masih dihadapkan dengan 
fluktuasi harga terutama sebagai akibat pasokan yang 
terbatas khususnya di periode saat terjadi perubahan 
iklim  El Nino serta akibat dari situasi geopolitik yang 
masih berlangsung sampai saat ini. Lonjakan harga 
saat keterbatasan pasokan umumnya mengakibatkan 
berkurangnya permintaan di pasar. Namun, kebutuhan 
konsumsi pangan dan pakan yang merupakan kebutuhan 
pokok tetap memberikan tingkat permintaan yang lebih 
tinggi di atas kebutuhan lainnya.
Economy and Industry
ECONOMIC REVIEW AND OUTLOOK
Until the end of 2023, the global economic situation is 
still influenced by uncertainties caused by global factors. 
Higher-than-expected inflation has led to an increase in 
interest rates to control inflation. Geopolitical tensions, 
such as the unavoidable conflicts between Russia and 
Ukraine and Palestine and Israel, are factors that also 
affect global conditions. This situation has undermined 
optimism about global economic growth.
Despite the context of a slowing global economy, 
Indonesia has managed to achieve high economic 
growth compared to other countries in ASEAN and the 
G20. Indonesia’s economic growth in 2023 at 5.2% 
demonstrates strong economic performance. This 
figure surpasses the economic growth of other ASEAN 
countries such as Malaysia (3.77%) and Korea (1.26%), 
and even exceeds that of G20 countries like the United 
States (2.5%), France (0.9%), and even Germany, which 
experienced a contraction (-0.3%).
Indonesia’s high economic growth is largely contributed 
by household consumption, investment in gross capital 
formation, as well as active sectors like transportation, 
warehousing, 
and 
construction. 
This 
indicates 
Indonesia’s economic resilience amidst the uncertain 
global economic dynamics.
In 2024, economic growth in Indonesia is still expected to 
be above 5%, mainly supported by domestic consumption 
and government spending.

compared to the previous year, the trade and distribution

Nino and due to the ongoing geopolitical situation up to 
now. Price spikes during supply shortages generally lead 
to lower demand in the market. However, the demand for 
food and feed consumption, which is a basic necessity, 
continues to show higher demand levels than other 
needs.

Logistik
Perseroan bergerak di bidang logistik terkait dengan 
pengadaan bahan pangan dan  bahan pakan curah dari 
mulai pelayanan di pelabuhan, pergudangan, sampai 
dengan pengiriman ke tempat pelanggan (“port to door”). 
Kondisi 2023 sangat dipengaruhi oleh pertumbuhan 
industri pangan dan bahan pakan ternak. Melalui portofolio 
beberapa jenis produk yang ditangani, industri logistik 
terkait bahan pangan dan pakan cukup berkembang. 
Perseroan yang memiliki keberadaan operasional di tiga 
pelabuhan curah kering di Indonesia menunjukkan kinerja 
yang cukup baik dan membuktikan peningkatan pangsa 
pasar.
Selain memusatkan pada pelayanan yang lebih luas 
untuk meningkatkan pangsa pasar, Perseroan juga terus 
melakukan proses transformasi digital untuk proses yang 
jauh lebih baik dan bersaing dengan pemain lainnya baik 
dari segi pelayanan di pelabuhan, pergudangan maupun 
transportasi.
Indonesia has a larger demographic structure of 
productive age groups (16-64 years) compared to 
non-productive groups (<15 years and 65 years and 
above). This condition demands that food supplies are 
available in sufficient, safe, and quality quantities for the 
population. Indonesia’s growing economy also increases 
the gross domestic product which continues to rise.
Currently, there is an increasing preference among the 
population for animal-based foods, especially poultry. 
One of the major components in livestock production is 
feed costs. The high fluctuations in feed prices in 2023, 
which elevated production costs, also affected the level 
of livestock feed production in 2023.
However, in 2024, the feed industry in Indonesia is 
projected to grow by around 5%. This is a national 
optimism because 2024 will be a political year that is 
expected to boost household consumption through 
various government assistance programs. Additionally, it 
is anticipated that El Nino will soon come to an end, and 
harvesting times will return to their regular schedules.
These various optimisms are still overshadowed by the 
uncertainty due to the transitional government conditions 
in 2024, which could potentially create investor doubts in 
boosting capital expenditures.
The Company sees the challenges of 2024 as 
opportunities. If managed correctly, they will provide the 
Company with the opportunity to further grow in the trade 
and distribution of food and feed ingredients.
Logistic
The Company operates in the logistics sector related 
to the procurement of food and bulk feed ingredients, 
providing services from port handling, warehousing, to 
delivery to customers (“port to door”). The conditions in 
2023 were significantly influenced by the growth of the 
food and feed ingredient industries. Through handling a 
portfolio of various products, the logistics industry related 
to food and feed ingredients has experienced significant 
growth. The Company, with operational presence in 
three dry bulk ports in Indonesia, has shown strong 
performance and ensured an increase in market share.
In addition to focusing on broader services to increase 
market share, the Company continues to undergo digital 
transformation processes to enhance operations and 
remain competitive with other players in terms of port 
logistics services, warehousing, and transportation.

Kontribusi segmen terbesar terhadap total pendapatan 
Perseroan pada tahun 2023 berasal dari segmen 
perdagangan dan distribusi dengan persentase 97,9% 
yang mana relatif sama dengan kondisi tahun 2022 
dengan persentase 98,3%.
Informasi mengenai kinerja masing-masing segmen 
dapat dilihat pada penjelasan berikutnya.
Perdagangan dan Distribusi
Aktivitas perdagangan dan distribusi Perseroan adalah 
pendistribusian bahan pangan dan bahan pakan melalui 
berbagai jaringan distribusi secara nasional. Bahan 
pangan termasuk di dalamnya bahan makanan pokok 
dan barang konsumen (FMCG) untuk keperluan masak, 
kelengkapan bahan utama di dapur dan snacking. Produk-
produk tersebut didistribusikan melalui jalur B2B dan juga 
B2C. 
Sepanjang tahun 2023, Perseroan dihadapkan pada 
tantangan fluktuasi harga bahan pangan sebagai dampak 
dari perubahan iklim yang mempengaruhi pasokan bahan 
pangan tertentu di periode-periode tertentu sepanjang 
tahun. Selain itu juga melemahnya Rupiah terhadap 
mata uang asing juga mengakibatkan meningkatnya 
harga pangan dan bahan pakan. Dengan adanya komite 
manajemen resiko yang mengelola resiko perubahan 
harga komoditas bahan pangan dan pakan serta resiko 
fluktuasi mata uang asing, Perseroan dapat menekan 
resiko kerugian sebagai akibat fluktuasi harga barang dan 
mata uang asing. Namun tidak dapat dipungkiri, kenaikan 
harga dengan tingkat daya beli yang menurun sebagai 
akibat perlambatan ekonomi mempengaruhi daya beli 
banyak pelanggan Perseroan. Untuk terus mengurangi 
resiko tersebut, Perseroan terus berupaya untuk melihat 
peluang yang ada untuk menambah portofolio produk-
produk yang dipasarkan sehingga mengurangi resiko 
saat produk-produk tertentu mengalami penurunan 
permintaan.
Pendapatan bersih yang diterima Perseroan dari aktivitas 
perdagangan dan distribusi tahun 2023 tercatat sebesar 
USD1,37 miliar. Terjadi penurunan sebesar 10,8% 
dibandingkan pendapatan tahun sebelumnya sebesar 
USD1,54 miliar. Perbedaan tersebut terjadi terutama 
disebabkan karena penurunan pada volume penjualan 
bahan pakan selama tahun 2023 dibandingkan tahun 
2022 sebagai akibat terpengaruhnya industri pakan atas 
fluktuasi harga bahan baku pakan khususnya selama 
harga meningkat sebagai akibat penurunan pasokan 
khususnya selama perubahan iklim yang cukup ekstrim.
The largest contribution to the Company’s total revenue 
in 2023 came from Trading and Distribution with 97.9% 
which is relatively the same as in 2022 with a percentage 
of 98.3%.
The performance of each segment is explained as 
follows.
Trading and Distribution
The Company’s trading and distribution activities are 
distributing food and feed ingredient through various 
distribution channel at national level. Food ingredients 
consists of staple foods and fast-moving consumer 
goods for cooking, additional ingredients in the kitchen, 
and snacks. Those products are distributed through B2B 
and B2C channels. 
Throughout 2023, the Company faced challenges 
stemming from fluctuations in food commodity prices 
due to climate change affecting the supply of certain food 
commodities at different periods throughout the year. 
Additionally, the weakening of the Rupiah against foreign 
currencies resulted in higher food and feed prices. With 
a risk management committee overseeing commodity 
price changes and foreign exchange fluctuations, the 
Company managed to mitigate losses due to fluctuating 
prices and currencies. However, it is undeniable that price 
increases coupled with reduced purchasing power due 
to economic slowdown affect the buying power of many 
of the Company’s customers. To further mitigate these 
risks, the Company continues to explore opportunities 
to diversify its product portfolio, reducing the risk when 
specific products experience decreased demand.
The Company’s net revenue from trading and distribution 
activities in 2023 amounted to USD1.37 billion, reflecting 
a 10.8% decrease compared to the previous year’s 
revenue of USD1.54 billion. This decline was mainly 
attributed to a decrease in feed sales volume during 
2023 compared to 2022, due to the impact of the feed 
industry being affected by fluctuations in raw material 
prices, especially during price increases resulting from 
decreased supply, particularly during extreme climate 
changes.

Logistics
The Company’s logistics services cover port services, 
warehousing, and transportation services. The revenues 
came from stevedoring and port handling services, 
warehousing (warehouse lease and management), and 
transportation services in Cilegon and Surabaya.
In 2023, port handling services volume experienced 
an increase of 2.80% compared to the previous year. 
Furthermore, in terms of loading and unloading services, 
there was a 39.95% increase in volume, mainly attributed 
to the overall increase in import volumes from some 
customers.
The Company’s net revenue from logistics activities in 
2023 amounted to USD33.43 million, reflecting a 3.2% 
increase compared to the previous year’s revenue of 
USD32.4 million. This increase was primarily due to the 
higher volume and tariffs in specific locations.
Manufacturing
The Company’s manufacturing activities involve the 
production of various processed fish products, including 
fish oil and fish meal. While this activity is the cornerstone 
of the business, it is not the primary focus of the Company 
at present. The revenue derived from processed fish 
products is highly dependent on the availability of fish.
The Company’s net revenue from manufacturing activities 
in 2023 amounted to USD2.42 million, showing a slight 
decrease of 2.31% compared to 2022. This decline was 
due to limited availability of specific fish types as raw 
materials during the year.

Pendapatan
Pada tahun 2023, pendapatan  bersih Perseroan tercatat 
sebesar USD1.401 juta, menurun sebesar 10,5% dari 
tahun 2022 senilai USD1.565 juta. Penurunan tersebut 
terjadi terutama karena penurunan sebesar USD166,6 
juta (10,8%) pada penjualan segmen perdagangan dan 
distribusi sebagian besar sebagai akibat penurunan 
volume. Sementara itu, pendapatan dari segmen logistik 
mengalami peningkatan sebesar USD2,6 juta (10,4%).
Beban Pokok Pendapatan
Pada tahun 2023, beban pokok pendapatan Perseroan 
turun sebesar 10,2% dari USD1.480,3 juta menjadi 
USD1.329,9 juta sejalan dengan penurunan pada 
pendapatan.
Laba Bruto
Laba bruto Perseroan menurun sebesar 16,2% dari 
USD84,8  juta di tahun 2022 menjadi USD71,1 juta namun 
marjin laba bruto masih berada di kisaran di atas 5%.
Revenues
In 2023, the Company recorded a net revenue of USD1,401 
million, a 10.5% decline compared to USD1,565 million 
in 2022. The decline mainly contributed by decrease 
of USD166.6 million (10.8%) in revenue in trading and 
distribution segment mainly attributable to lower volume 
decrease. Meanwhile, revenue from logistic segment 
increase by USD2.6 million (10.4%).
Cost of Revenues
The Company’s cost of revenue in 2023 decreased by 
10.2% from USD1,480.3 million to USD1,329.9 million in 
line with the decrease in revenue.
Gross Profit
The Company’s gross profit decreased by 16.2% from 
USD84.8 million in 2022 to USD71.1 million. However, 
the gross margin remains at the level of more than 5%.
Beban Operasional
Beban operasional terdiri dari beban penjualan dan 
distribusi, beban umum dan administrasi, beban operasi 
lain dan penghasilan operasi lain. Pada tahun 2023, 
jumlah beban operasional turun sebesar 18,7%, dari 
USD34,4 juta di tahun sebelumnya menjadi USD27,9 
juta. Penurunan ini dikontribusikan oleh penurunan pada 
beban umum dan administrasi serta beban penjualan 
dan distribusi masing-masing sebesar USD2,6 juta 
dan USD1,9 juta. Selain penurunan pada beban-beban 
sebagaimana disebutkan di atas, kenaikan penghasilan 
operasi lainnya sebesar USD1,7 juta juga mengurangi 
total biaya operasional secara keseluruhan.
Operating Expenses
Operating expenses consist of selling and  distribution 
expenses, general and administration expenses and 
other operating income/expenses. In 2023, the operating 
expenses decreased by 18.7% from USD34.4 million 
in previous year become USD27.9 million. The decline 
was attributable to decrease in selling and distribution 
expense and general & administration expenses by 
USD2.6 million and USD1.9 million, respectively. In 
addition to decreases in above mentioned expenses, an 
increase in other operating income by USD1.7 million 
also contributed to the overall decline in total operating 
expenses.
