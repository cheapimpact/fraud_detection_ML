TINJAUAN PEREKONOMIAN DAN INDUSTRI
Economic and Industrial Overview
Pada 
tahun 
2023, 
perekonomian
Indonesia mengalami berbagai dinamika
yang memengaruhi industri konstruksi
secara 
signifikan. 
Meskipun 
terdapat
tantangan, ada juga peluang yang dapat
dimanfaatkan untuk pertumbuhan dan
perkembangan sektor ini.
Sebagian besar tahun 2023 diwarnai oleh
dampak dari pandemi COVID-19 yang
masih berkecamuk di beberapa bagian
dunia. Namun, dengan adanya vaksinasi
massal dan langkah-langkah pemulihan
ekonomi yang diperkuat, Indonesia mulai
menunjukkan 
tanda-tanda 
pemulihan.
Pemerintah 
terus 
mendorong 
sektor
konstruksi 
sebagai 
salah 
satu 
motor
penggerak ekonomi nasional.
Salah satu aspek penting dari tinjauan
perekonomian 
adalah 
pertumbuhan
ekonomi makro. Tahun 2023 menyaksikan
pertumbuhan ekonomi Indonesia yang
stabil, walaupun mungkin tidak setinggi
yang diharapkan karena masih adanya
ketidakpastian global. Pertumbuhan ini
menjadi landasan penting bagi sektor
konstruksi, karena aktivitas pembangunan
infrastruktur dan properti bergantung
pada kondisi makroekonomi yang stabil.
Dalam 
konteks 
industri 
konstruksi,
sejumlah 
tren 
terlihat 
memengaruhi
dinamika sektor ini. Pertama, terdapat
peningkatan fokus pada pembangunan
infrastruktur. 
Pemerintah 
terus
mengalokasikan anggaran besar untuk
proyek-proyek infrastruktur seperti jalan,
jembatan, pelabuhan, dan bandara. Hal ini
menjadi 
peluang 
bagi 
perusahaan
konstruksi untuk mendapatkan proyek-
proyek 
yang 
menguntungkan 
dan
berkelanjutan.
In 2023, the Indonesian economy will
experience various dynamics that will
significantly 
impact 
the 
construction
industry. Although there are challenges,
there are also opportunities that can be
exploited 
for 
the 
growth 
and
development of this sector.
Much of 2023 is marked by the impact of
the COVID-19 pandemic which is still
raging in several parts of the world.
However, with mass vaccination and
strengthened 
economic 
recovery
measures, Indonesia is starting to show
signs 
of 
recovery. 
The 
government
continues to encourage the construction
sector as one of the driving forces of the
national economy.
One important aspect of the economic
review is macroeconomic growth. 2023
will see stable economic growth in
Indonesia, although perhaps not as high
as expected due to global uncertainty.
This growth is an important foundation
for the construction sector, because
infrastructure and property development
activities 
depend 
on 
stable
macroeconomic conditions.
In 
the 
context 
of 
the 
construction
industry, a number of trends appear to be
influencing the dynamics of the sector.
First, there is an increased focus on
infrastructure 
development. 
The
government continues to allocate large
budgets for infrastructure projects such
as roads, bridges, ports and airports. This
is 
an 
opportunity 
for 
construction
companies to obtain profitable and
sustainable projects.

by the construction industry. One of them

issues. Even though the government has
made efforts to simplify the licensing
process, there are still obstacles that
hinder 
the

of 
construction
projects. Additionally, sustainability is

In facing these various dynamics, the

in 
Indonesia
continues 
to 
adapt 
and

Construction companies are starting to

the Internet of Things (IoT) to increase
efficiency and productivity. Apart from

private sectors is also being increased to

infrastructure 
development
which is vital for economic growth.

from construction services amounting to
Rp343,374,489,792 which comprise 100%
of the Company’s total revenues. Revenue
from construction services is recognized
based 
on 
percentage 
of 
completion
method. Under this method, the revenue
recognized equals to the latest estimate
of the total value of the contract
multiplied by the actual completion rate
determined by reference to the physical
state of progress of the works (input
method).
