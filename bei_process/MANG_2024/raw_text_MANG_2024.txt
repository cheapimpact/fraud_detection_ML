Perekonomian global sepanjang tahun 2024 menghadapi 
tekanan yang cukup signifikan akibat ketidakpastian geopolitik 
yang masih berlangsung di berbagai kawasan dunia. Konflik 
antara Rusia dan Ukraina yang belum mereda, serta dinamika di 
Timur Tengah, terus memicu lonjakan harga energi global. Di sisi 
lain, Pemilu di Amerika Serikat turut menambah ketidakpastian 
pasar dan kebijakan fiskal global, sementara Tiongkok 
menghadapi perlambatan ekonomi akibat lemahnya konsumsi 
domestik.
Di tengah kondisi global yang bergejolak tersebut, Indonesia 
tetap mampu menunjukkan ketahanan ekonomi yang cukup baik. 
Berdasarkan data dari Badan Pusat Statistik (BPS), pertumbuhan 
ekonomi Indonesia pada tahun 2024 tercatat sebesar 5,03%, 
sedikit melambat dibandingkan tahun sebelumnya sebesar 
5,05%, namun tetap mencerminkan stabilitas dan ketahanan 
fundamental ekonomi nasional.
Salah satu pendorong utama dari ketahanan tersebut adalah 
sektor investasi yang tumbuh sebesar 4,61% year-on-year, 
tertinggi dalam enam tahun terakhir. Hal ini tidak terlepas dari 
percepatan penyelesaian proyek-proyek infrastruktur strategis 
yang telah direncanakan sebelumnya. Pemerintah juga terus 
mendorong peningkatan konektivitas dan pembangunan 
wilayah, termasuk pembangunan infrastruktur di Ibu Kota 
Negara (IKN) serta jalan tol Trans-Sumatra.
Sektor konstruksi kembali mencatatkan peran signifikan dalam 
struktur perekonomian nasional. Dengan dukungan anggaran 
infrastruktur sebesar Rp423,4 triliun, sektor ini tumbuh konsisten 
dan menyumbang 10,09% terhadap Produk Domestik Bruto 
(PDB). Angka ini menjadikan sektor konstruksi sebagai salah satu 
kontributor utama dalam lima besar penyumbang PDB nasional, 
bersama dengan industri pengolahan, perdagangan besar dan 
eceran, pertanian, serta pertambangan dan penggalian.
Meski menghadapi tantangan berupa fluktuasi rantai pasok 
global dan dinamika kebijakan investasi, sektor konstruksi 
tetap menjadi motor penting bagi pertumbuhan ekonomi 
berkelanjutan. Komitmen pemerintah dalam pembangunan 
strategis turut membuka peluang yang lebih luas bagi sektor 
ini untuk terus berkembang dan memberikan dampak nyata 
terhadap pembangunan nasional yang lebih inklusif.
Strategi Perseroan
Untuk memperkuat daya saing dan mendorong pertumbuhan 
bisnis yang berkelanjutan, Perseroan menerapkan strategi yang 
berfokus pada empat aspek utama, yaitu promosi, kualitas, 
harga, dan layanan purna jual:
• 
Strategi Promosi 
Perseroan aktif menyebarluaskan materi pemasaran seperti 
katalog dan brosur, serta mengundang calon pelanggan 
untuk meninjau langsung proyek-proyek yang telah 
diselesaikan. Kegiatan promosi juga diperluas ke wilayah 
Lampung dan sekitarnya guna meningkatkan penjualan 
The global economy throughout 2024 faced significant pressure 
due to ongoing geopolitical uncertainties across various regions 
of the world. The prolonged conflict between Russia and 
Ukraine, along with rising tensions in the Middle East, continued 
to trigger global energy price hikes. Meanwhile, the U.S. 
presidential election added further volatility to global markets 
and fiscal policy outlooks, while China experienced an economic 
slowdown due to weakened domestic consumption.
Amidst such global turbulence, Indonesia managed to 
demonstrate relatively strong economic resilience. According 
to data from the Central Statistics Agency (BPS), Indonesia’s 
economy grew by 5.03% in 2024, slightly down from 5.05% in 
the previous year, but still reflecting solid national economic 
fundamentals and stability.
One of the key drivers of this resilience was the investment 
sector, which grew by 4.61% year-on-year—its highest growth in 
the past six years. This was largely attributed to the accelerated 
completion of strategic infrastructure projects. The government 
remained focused on enhancing national connectivity and 
regional development, including infrastructure construction 
in the new capital city (IKN) and the Trans-Sumatra toll road 
network.
The construction sector once again played a vital role in the 
national economic structure. Supported by an infrastructure 
budget of IDR 423.4 trillion, the sector maintained steady growth 
and contributed 10.09% to the Gross Domestic Product (GDP). 
This solidified construction as one of the top five contributors 
to GDP, alongside manufacturing, wholesale and retail trade, 
agriculture, and mining and quarrying.
Despite challenges such as global supply chain fluctuations and 
shifting investment policies, the construction sector continues 
to be a crucial driver of sustainable economic growth. The 
government’s commitment to strategic development opens 
broader opportunities for this sector to expand and contribute 
meaningfully to inclusive national development
Company Strategies
To strengthen its competitiveness and support sustainable 
business growth, the Company has implemented a strategic 
focus on four key pillars: promotion, quality, pricing, and after-
sales service.
• 
Promotional Strategy
The Company actively distributes marketing materials such 
as catalogs and brochures, while also inviting potential 
clients to visit completed project sites. Promotional activities 
have been expanded to cover Lampung and surrounding 
areas to boost retail sales of asphalt and concrete products.

Strategi Kualitas 
Komitmen terhadap kualitas tetap menjadi prioritas

Strategi Pelayanan Purna Jual 
Hubungan jangka panjang dengan pelanggan dibangun 
melalui layanan purna jual yang responsif dan solutif, demi

Sebagai bagian dari penguatan strategi, Perseroan juga

Quality Strategy
Commitment to quality remains a top priority. The Company

through relevant ISO certifications.
 
• 
Pricing Strategy
The Company offers highly competitive pricing for 
construction services and the sale of ready-mix concrete 
and hot-mix asphalt, without compromising on the quality 
of work or service.
• 
After-Sales Service Strategy
Long-term customer relationships are built through 
responsive and solution-oriented after-sales services, aimed 
at maintaining customer satisfaction and loyalty.
As part of its strategic reinforcement, the Company has also 
expanded the scope of its projects, which now include office 
building construction, entertainment and sports facilities, land 
preparation works, and the development of irrigation and 
drainage networks.
To enhance project acquisition, the Company actively monitors 
and participates in online tenders through the LPSE platform. 
Strategic partnerships are also being broadened, including 
the establishment of Joint Operations (KSO) with state-owned 
enterprises (BUMN) in the construction sector to undertake 
large-scale road and building projects.
Supported by more than 30 years of experience, a competent 
workforce, and modern production facilities such as batching 
plants for ready-mix concrete and asphalt mixing plants (AMP) 
for hot-mix asphalt, the Company remains committed to being 
a reliable partner for both government and private clients in 
delivering high-quality infrastructure across Indonesia.
In 2024, the Company recorded a Revenue of Rp72.16 billion, 
reflecting an increase of 21.12% compared to the previous year’s 
figure of Rp59.60 billion. The Company’s revenue is divided into 
several segments, namely: Building and Construction, Road 
Infrastructure Development, and Hotmix Asphalt Production. 
Structure and Building Construction
In the building and construction segment, the Company 
recorded a 28% increase from the previous year’s revenue of 
Rp32.04 billion to Rp41.03 billion in 2024. This increase was 
driven by the acquisition of strategic projects in 2024, both from 
the private and public sectors, as well as the growing demand 
for commercial and office space.
Pada tahun 2024, Perseroan mencatatkan Pendapatan Usaha 
sebesar Rp72,16 miliar yang meningkat sebesar 21,12% 
dibandingkan tahun sebelumnya yang mencapai Rp59,60 
miliar. Pendapatan Usaha Perseroan terbagi ke dalam 
beberapa segmen, yaitu: Konstruksi Gedung dan Bangunan, 
Pembangunan Infrastruktur Jalan, dan Produksi Aspal Hotmix. 
Konstruksi Gedung dan Bangunan
Pada segmen konstruksi gedung dan bangunan, Perseroan 
mencatatkan peningkatan sebesar 28% dari tahun sebelumnya 
yang tercatat sebesar Rp32,04 miliar menjadi Rp41,03 miliar 
di tahun 2024. Peningkatan ini dipengaruhi oleh peningkatan 
proyek strategis yang diterima Perseroan selama tahun 2024, 
baik dari sektor swasta maupun sektor publik, serta meningkatnya 
kebutuhan akan ruang komersial dan perkantoran.

Operating Revenue
Operating revenue for the period ended December 31, 2024, 
amounted to Rp72.16 billion. Sales experienced an increase 
of 21.08%, or Rp12.56 billion, compared to Rp59.60 billion in 
2023. This growth was partly supported by an increase in the 
number of private projects secured by the Company in 2024.
Cost of Goods Sold
The Cost of Goods Sold for 2024 was recorded at Rp51.52 
billion, an increase of 14.33% compared to Rp45.06 billion in 
2023. This increase was in line with the higher volume of projects 
undertaken by the Company.
Gross Profit
Gross profit for 2024 increased by 42.00% from Rp14.53 billion 
in 2023 to Rp20.64 billion in 2024. The increase in gross profit in 
2024 was supported by the Company’s revenue growth and an 
improvement in gross profit margin compared to 2023, leading 
to a significant increase in the absolute gross profit figure.
Operating Profit
Operating profit in 2024 was recorded at Rp6.51 billion, 
compared to Rp5.55 billion in 2023. The 17.33% increase in 
operating profit in 2024 was primarily attributable to the growth 
in revenue.
Profit Before Income Tax
Profit before income tax in 2024 amounted to Rp1.46 billion, 
an increase of 12.54% or Rp163.06 million compared to the 
previous year’s Rp1.30 billion. This increase was mainly driven 
by higher revenue, which boosted gross profit, resulting in an 
increase in profit before income tax after deducting expenses.
Pendapatan Usaha 
Pendapatan Usaha untuk periode yang berakhir pada tanggal 
31 Desember 2024 adalah sebesar Rp72.16 miliar, Penjualan 
mengalami peningkatan sebesar 21.08%, atau sebesar Rp12.56 
miliar di mana pada tahun 2023 tercatat sebesar Rp59.60 miliar. 
Peningkatan ini sebagian ditopang oleh meningkatnya jumlah 
proyek swasta yang dimenangkan Perseroan pada tahun 2024.
Beban Pokok Pendapatan
Beban Pokok Pendapatan untuk tahun 2024 tercatat sebesar 
Rp51.52 miliar, naik sebesar 14.33% dibandingkan tahun 2023 
yang tercatat sebesar Rp45.06 miliar. Peningkatan ini terjadi 
seiring dengan meningkatnya jumlah proyek yang dikerjakan 
Perseroan.
Laba Bruto
Laba Bruto untuk tahun 2024 mengalami kenaikan sebesar 
42.00% dari sebelumnya Rp14.53 miliar di tahun 2023 menjadi 
Rp20.64 miliar di tahun 2024. Kenaikan laba bruto tahun 
2024 didukung oleh peningkatan pendapatan Perseroan dan 
kenaikan margin laba kotor dibandingkan tahun 2023 sehingga

Laba Usaha

Sementara pada tahun sebelumnya tercatat sebesar Rp5.55 
miliar di tahun 2023. Kenaikan Laba usaha sebesar 17,33% 
pada tahun 2024 ini utamanya disumbangkan oleh kenaikan

Laba Sebelum Pajak Penghasilan
Laba sebelum pajak penghasilan di tahun 2024 tercatat

Financial Review
Construction Of Road Infrastructure 
The Construction of road infrastructure segment recorded a 
revenue of Rp24.64 billion in 2024, reflecting a 193% increase 
compared to the previous year’s figure of Rp8.40 billion. This 
growth was driven by the Company’s performance in supporting 
various strategic projects from the central government, 
regional governments, and state-owned enterprises (SOEs), as 
well as increased investment in toll roads and transportation 
infrastructure development.
Production of  hotmix asphalt 
Production of  hotmix asphalt decreased by 56% from Rp14.81 
billion in the previous year to Rp6.45 billion in 2024. This decline 
reflects the increased competition within the industry and the 
decreased demand for hotmix asphalt due to a reduction in 
large-scale road construction projects and a slowdown in the 
procurement of infrastructure projects in several regions.

Company’s Initial Public Offering (IPO).

operational purposes.
