TINJAUAN EKONOMI MAKRO
Macroeconomics Review 
Melalui kacamata yang optimistis, perekonomian global 
tahun 2024 diharapkan menjadi bagian dari pemulihan 
setelah berbagai gangguan dan kendala global pada 
tahun-tahun sebelumnya. Namun nyatanya, mengutip rilis 
data International Monetary Fund (IMF) dan World Bank, 
perekonomian global melambat dengan pertumbuhan 2,6% 
(yoy). Angka ini lebih rendah dari tingkat pertumbuhan 
sebesar 2,7% di tahun 2023. 
Walaupun perekonomian global masih kurang bergairah, 
perekonomian Indonesia mencatat kinerja positif. Badan 
Pusat Statistik menyebutkan Produk Domestik Bruto (PDB) 
Indonesia tahun 2024 tumbuh sebesar 5,03% sedikit lebih 
rendah dibandingkan tahun 2023 yang sebesar 5,05%. 
Penurunan ini, menurut Bank Indonesia, dipicu oleh dinamika 
geopolitik, fragmentasi pola perdagangan dunia, bergesernya 
pola 
pertumbuhan 
ekonomi 
spasial 
dunia, 
tingginya 
suku bunga dan risiko utang, berubahnya pola investasi 
keuangan dunia, serta cepatnya digitalisasi antarnegara dan 
meningkatnya risiko operasional (siber).  Dengan mengacu 
pada data ini, perbaikan perekonomian nasional diprediksikan 
akan terus berlanjut.
Sementara itu, di level Jawa Barat, provinsi tempat  Perseroan 
berada, angka perekonomian tumbuh sebesar 5,02% (yoy) 
dengan angka PDRB-ADHB (Pendapatan Domestik Regional 
Bruto Atas Dasar Harga Berlaku) 2024 sebesar Rp2.799,61 
triliun  (Badan Pusat Statistik Provinsi Jawa Barat).
Berdasarkan data rilis Bank Indonesia per tanggal 15 Februari 
2024, Indeks Permintaan Properti Komersial untuk kategori 
sewa pada Triwulan IV/2024 tumbuh 2,84% (yoy). Sementara 
itu Indeks Permintaan Properti Komersial untuk kategori jual 
tumbuh pada  Triwulan IV/2024 tumbuh sebesar 0,35% (yoy).
Indeks Pasokan Properti Komersial kategori sewa pada 
Triwulan IV/2024 tumbuh sebesar 2,89% (yoy), dan Indeks 
Pasokan Komersial kategori jual tercatat tumbuh relatif stabil 
sebesar 0,11% (yoy).
From an optimistic viewpoint, the global economy in 2024 
is anticipated to be part of the recovery following various 
disruptions and challenges experienced in previous years. 
However, data from the International Monetary Fund (IMF) and 
the World Bank reveals that the global economy has actually 
slowed down, with a growth rate of 2.6% year-on-year. This 
figure is lower than the 2.7% growth rate recorded in 2023.
Despite the overall global economic slowdown, Indonesia’s 
economy has shown positive performance. The Central 
Statistics Agency reported that Indonesia’s Gross Domestic 
Product (GDP) in 2024 grew by 5.03%, a slight decline from 
5.05% in 2023. According to Bank Indonesia, this decrease 
can be attributed to geopolitical dynamics, fragmentation 
of world trade patterns, shifts in global economic growth, 
high interest rates and debt risks, changes in global financial 
investment patterns, rapid digitalization among countries, 
and increasing operational risks such as cyber threats. 
Nevertheless, based on this data, further improvement in the 
national economy is expected to continue.
At the provincial level in West Java, where the Company 
operates, the economic growth rate is reported at 5.02% 
year-on-year (yoy), with a projected Gross Regional Domestic 
Product for 2024 of Rp2,799.61 trillion, according to the West 
Java Provincial Statistics Agency.
Data from Bank Indonesia, released on February 15, 2024, 
indicates that the Commercial Property Demand Index for 
the lease category grew by 2.84% yoy in the fourth quarter 
of 2024. In contrast, the Demand Index for the sale category 
experienced a more modest growth of 0.35% (yoy) during 
the same period. Additionally, the Commercial Property 
Supply Index for the rental category increased by 2.89% yoy 
in Q4 2024, while the Supply Index for the sales category 
demonstrated stable growth at 0.11% (yoy).

LIABILITIES  
As of December 31, 2024, the Company’s total liabilities 
amounted to Rp53,606.38 million, representing a decrease 
of 27.96% from 2023, when they were Rp74,408.70 million. 
This decline was seen in both Long-Term Liabilities, which 
fell by 39.98%, and Short-Term Liabilities, which decreased 
by 22.74%. The primary reason for the reduction in liabilities 
this year was a decrease in down payments for the transfer of 
shophouse utilization, which dropped by Rp9,870.37 million, 
or 32.90%. Additionally, the Company recorded a reduction in 
Bank Debt and Lease Liabilities in 2024. Bank Debt decreased 
by Rp4,917.33 million, or 23.42%, from Rp20,999.05 million 
in the previous year. Meanwhile, Lease Liabilities fell by 
Rp4,428.45 million, or 52.33%, from the previous year’s 
Rp8,462.04 million. The decrease in Bank Loan was attributed 
to the Company fully repaying its debt to PT Bank Central Asia 
Tbk, amounting to Rp4,917.33 million. The reduction in Lease 
Liabilities was due to a decrease of Rp4,428.45 million related 
to the lease for 135,000 m³ of land in the city of Bandung to PT 
Kereta Api Indonesia (Persero).
EQUITY 
The Company’s equity position as of December 31, 2024, 
was recorded at Rp281 million, reflecting a growth of 24.10% 
compared to the previous year’s equity of Rp226 million. 
This increase was driven by a rise in the Company’s profit 
allocated for use, which increased by Rp2,756.29 million, 
or 11.31%. Additionally, profits not allocated for use rose by 
Rp51,716.61 million, or 93.69%. The Company also noted an 
increase in employee benefit liability profits by Rp124.05 
million, or 18.72%.

leaseback transactions.
Amendemen tentang liabilitas jangka panjang dengan kovenan. 
Amendemen ini mengatur bahwa hanya kovenan yang harus dipatuhi 
entitas pada atau sebelum tanggal pelaporan yang akan memengaruhi 
klasifikasi liabilitas jangka pendek atau jangka panjang serta 
pengungkapannya.
Amendments to long-term liabilities with covenants specify that only 
those covenants with which an entity must comply on or before the 
reporting date will influence the classification of liabilities as current 
or long-term, as well as their disclosure.

ini memperjelas pengungkapan terkait pengaturan pembiayaan 
pemasok.
Amendments to supplier financing arrangements. These amendments 
clarify disclosures related to supplier financing arrangements.
Revisi PSAK 409 terkait dengan pengukuran selanjutnya untuk aset 
zakat, infak, dan sedekah yang terpapar fluktuasi nilai wajar signifikan, 
serta menambahkan pengaturan baru seperti sedekah jasa dan diskon 
atau potongan atas pembelian aset atau jasa.
The revision of PSAK 409 pertains to the subsequent measurement 
of zakat, infaq, and alms assets that are subject to significant 
fluctuations in fair value. It introduces new provisions, including 
service alms and discounts or deductions on the purchase of assets or 
services. 
Revisi PSAK 401 menghilangkan penyajian laporan perubahan aset 
kelolaan sebagai salah satu komponen laporan keuangan.
Additionally, the revision of PSAK 401 removes the requirement to 
present the statement of changes in managed assets as part of the 
financial statements.
Amendemen tentang kekurangan ketertukaran. Amendemen ini 
memperjelas pengaturan terkait kondisi ketika suatu mata uang tidak 
tertukarkan serta pengungkapannya.
Amendments on non-convertibility. These amendments clarify the 
provisions regarding the conditions under which a currency is non-
convertible and the disclosure thereof.
