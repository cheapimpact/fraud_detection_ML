TINJAUAN MAKROEKONOMI
Macroeconomic Review
Sepanjang 2024, ketidakpastian global masih membayangi perekonomian 
dunia. Ketegangan geopolitik yang belum mereda, fragmentasi rantai 
pasok, perubahan iklim, serta ketidakjelasan arah kebijakan suku bunga 
global menimbulkan tekanan besar terhadap aktivitas ekonomi. IMF 
dalam laporan WEO Oktober 2024 memproyeksikan pertumbuhan 
global 2025 akan sedikit meningkat menjadi 3,2%, dibandingkan 
proyeksi 2024 sebesar 3,1%.
Amerika Serikat menutup kuartal keempat 2024 dengan pertumbuhan 
sebesar 2,5%, melambat dari 3,3% pada tahun sebelumnya. Sementara 
itu, pertumbuhan Tiongkok mencatatkan angka stabil di kisaran 5,4%. 
Di Eropa, tekanan harga energi dan penurunan permintaan ekspor 
menyebabkan stagnasi, dengan Jerman dan Perancis masing-masing 
mengalami kontraksi sebesar -0,2% dan -0,1% pada kuartal keempat 
2024.
Perekonomian Indonesia pada 2024 tetap mencatat pertumbuhan 
yang solid sebesar 5,03%, meskipun melambat tipis dibanding 2023 
(5,05%). Angka ini merupakan yang terendah dalam tiga tahun terakhir, 
dan menjadi refleksi dari perlambatan konsumsi masyarakat. Konsumsi 
rumah tangga hanya tumbuh sebesar 4,94%, di bawah pertumbuhan 
nasional dan belum sepenuhnya pulih ke potensi maksimal.
Salah satu indikator yang menonjol pada 2024 adalah tingkat inflasi yang 
sangat rendah. Inflasi tahun kalender per Desember 2024 tercatat hanya 
1,57%, yang merupakan level terendah dalam tiga tahun terakhir. 
Kendati menghadapi berbagai tantangan, perekonomian Indonesia 
menunjukkan ketahanan yang relatif kuat. Sepanjang tahun 2024, 
Purchasing Managers’ Index (PMI) Manufaktur Indonesia menunjukkan 
fluktuasi antara zona ekspansi dan kontraksi. Namun, pada Desember 
2024, PMI meningkat menjadi 51,2 dari 49,6 pada November, 
menandakan kembalinya sektor manufaktur ke fase ekspansi setelah 
lima bulan berada di zona kontraksi
Dengan berbagai kebijakan yang diarahkan untuk mendorong daya beli 
masyarakat, terdapat peluang pemulihan konsumsi dan pertumbuhan 
di tahun 2025. Hal ini memberikan sinyal positif bagi sektor-sektor 
strategis, termasuk Industri Barang Konsumsi (FMCG) seperti industri es 
krim, untuk terus berinovasi dan mengoptimalkan strategi pertumbuhan 
secara hati-hati namun tetap optimis.
Despite facing various challenges, Indonesia’s economy demonstrated 
relatively 
strong 
resilience. 
Throughout 
2024, 
the 
country’s 
Manufacturing Purchasing Managers’ Index (PMI) fluctuated between 
expansion and contraction zones. However, in December 2024, the 
PMI rose to 51.2 from 49.6 in November, signaling a return of the 
manufacturing sector to expansion territory after five consecutive 
months in contraction.
With a range of policies aimed at boosting consumer purchasing power, 
there are opportunities for a recovery in consumption and growth in 
2025. This presents a positive outlook for strategic sectors, including 
the Fast-Moving Consumer Goods (FMCG) industry—such as the ice 
cream sector—to continue innovating and cautiously yet optimistically 
optimizing their growth strategies.
Throughout 2024, global uncertainty continued to cast a shadow 
over the world economy. Ongoing geopolitical tensions, supply chain 
fragmentation, climate change, and unclear direction of global interest 
rate policies placed significant pressure on economic activity. In its 
October 2024 World Economic Outlook (WEO) report, the IMF projected 
that global growth in 2025 would slightly increase to 3.2%, compared to 
the 2024 projection of 3.1%.
The United States closed the fourth quarter of 2024 with a growth 
rate of 2.5%, slowing down from 3.3% the previous year. Meanwhile, 
China’s growth remained stable at around 5.4%. In Europe, energy price 
pressures and declining export demand led to stagnation, with Germany 
and France experiencing contractions of -0.2% and -0.1% respectively in 
the fourth quarter of 2024.
Indonesia’s economy in 2024 continued to record solid growth at 5.03%, 
although it slightly slowed compared to 2023 (5.05%). This figure marked 
the lowest growth in the past three years and reflected a deceleration 
in household consumption. Household consumption grew by only 
4.94%, below the national growth rate and not yet fully recovering to its 
maximum potential.
One notable indicator in 2024 was the exceptionally low inflation rate. 
As of December 2024, year-to-date inflation stood at just 1.57%—the 
lowest level in the past three years.

TINJAUAN INDUSTRI
Industry Review
Tahun 2024 diwarnai dengan berbagai tantangan terutama terkait 
biaya produksi. Gejolak geopolitik global dan fragmentasi rantai 
pasok berdampak pada fluktuasi harga bahan baku utama, termasuk 
susu, cokelat, dan energi. Hal ini menuntut solusi terkait efisiensi 
kegiatan operasional dan peninjauan kembali strategi sourcing guna 
menciptakan  ekosistem bisnis yang tetap kondusif dan kompetitif tanpa 
mengorbankan kualitas produk.
Di tengah angka konsumsi domestik yang belum sepenuhnya pulih, 
strategi untuk menciptakan nilai tambah bagi konsumen tentunya 
menjadi semakin krusial. Konsumen cenderung lebih selektif dalam 
belanja, namun tetap terbuka terhadap produk yang menghadirkan 
pengalaman unik, kenyamanan, dan nilai emosional. Sebagai respons 
terhadap dinamika tersebut, pelaku industri dituntut untuk terus 
berinovasi, baik dalam pengembangan produk baru, diversifikasi varian 
rasa, maupun melalui pendekatan yang lebih personal. Optimalisasi 
kanal digital untuk promosi dan penjualan, serta penguatan layanan 
home delivery dan kemitraan e-commerce, juga senantiasa menjadi 
bagian penting dari strategi distribusi di tengah perubahan pola perilaku 
konsumen.
Ke depan, industri es krim di Indonesia memiliki peluang untuk tetap 
tumbuh dengan pendekatan yang adaptif, kreatif, dan berfokus pada 
kebutuhan konsumen masa kini. Kombinasi antara inovasi produk, 
penguatan merek, efisiensi rantai pasok, serta pemanfaatan teknologi 
digital akan menjadi kunci dalam menjaga daya saing di tengah dinamika 
industri yang terus berkembang.
The year 2024 was marked by various challenges, particularly related 
to production costs. Global geopolitical tensions and supply chain 
fragmentation led to fluctuations in the prices of key raw materials, 
including dairy, chocolate, and energy. These conditions called for 
enhanced operational efficiency and a reevaluation of sourcing strategies 
to maintain a business ecosystem that remains both competitive and 
conducive, without compromising product quality.
Amidst a domestic consumption level that has yet to fully recover, 
strategies aimed at delivering added value to consumers have become 
increasingly critical. Consumers are becoming more selective in their 
spending but remain receptive to products that offer unique experiences, 
comfort, and emotional value. In response to these dynamics, industry 
players are expected to continuously innovate—not only through new 
product development and flavor diversification but also by adopting a 
more personalized approach. The optimization of digital channels for 
promotion and sales, along with the enhancement of home delivery 
services and e-commerce partnerships, continues to play a vital role in 
distribution strategies amid shifting consumer behavior.
Looking ahead, the ice cream industry in Indonesia holds strong potential 
for continued growth by adopting adaptive, creative, and consumer-
focused approaches. A strategic combination of product innovation, 
brand strengthening, supply chain efficiency, and digital technology 
utilization will be essential to maintaining competitiveness in an ever-
evolving industry landscape.

PENJUALAN DAN PEMASARAN
Sales and Marketing
Salah satu tonggak penting tahun ini adalah fokus Perseroan dalam 
mengembangkan strategi promosi yang lebih personal dan berbasis 
data, khususnya untuk menjangkau Generasi Z. Generasi ini dikenal 
dengan preferensi terhadap kecepatan, keautentikan, dan koneksi 
digital. Untuk itu, Perseroan mengadopsi pendekatan 5A customer path 
(Awareness, Appeal, Ask, Act, Advocate), yang diterjemahkan ke dalam 
konten singkat dengan visual cerah dan kuat dalam lima detik pertama 
(golden time strategy). Distribusi konten difokuskan pada TikTok dan 
Instagram Reels, serta diperkuat kolaborasi dengan mikro-influencer di 
bidang kuliner, gaya hidup, dan keberlanjutan. Pendekatan ini terbukti 
efektif dan mengantar Perseroan meraih penghargaan Indonesia WOW 
Brand Award 2024 dan Indonesia Digital Popular Brand Award (IDPB) 
2024.
Kampanye digital dengan menggunakan hashtag seperti #asliindonesia, 
#semangatbaru, #celebrategoodness, maupun #campinafuncreator 
menciptakan interaksi tinggi dengan konsumen serta meningkatkan 
brand recall. Campina juga mengedepankan nilai keberlanjutan yang 
penting bagi Gen Z, seperti penggunaan bahan alami dan nilai gizi yang 
terkandung di packaging es kriim.
Dalam eksekusinya, strategi omnichannel menjadi kunci utama. 
Campina menciptakan pengalaman konsisten antara kanal online dan 
offline. Situs web dan aplikasi icecreamstore.co.id ditingkatkan dengan 
pelacakan stok real-time, fitur personalisasi produk, reward points, 
dan gamifikasi seperti spin-to-win. Penjualan e-commerce menunjukan 
peningkatan dari tahun sebelumnya, didukung kolaborasi eksklusif 
dengan marketplace terkemuka. Di ranah offline, aktivasi seperti pop-up 
booth di berbagai event di kota-kota besar di Indonesia, baik di ranah 
e-sport (MPL ID season 13 dan 14), musik (Synchonize Fest, La La La Fest), 
maupun event lifestyle lainnya (Indofest dan Vegan Festival Indonesia) 
yang dikombinasikan dengan interaksi digital melalui QR code, AR filter, 
dan fitur-fitur lainnya yang terhubung langsung ke media sosial.
Dari sisi pengembangan produk, inovasi terus menjadi pilar utama. 
Perseroan meluncurkan berbagai varian baru yang inovatif di tahun 
2024, antara lain: Concerto icownic Blackmoon, Party Pack Mini 
Concerto, Tropicana Double Crunch, Strawberry Lime, Rainbow Pops, 
Happy Cow Hi-Calcium, Take Home Cookie Creamy ukuran 700ml dan 
350ml, Fruitzy, es krim Marvel, maupun Kid Series dengan rasa Fantazee 
Strawberry, Chocolate Milk, dan Choco Mix . Inovasi ini tidak hanya 
berfokus pada rasa dan varian, tetapi juga nilai gizi dan kemasan yang 
sejalan dengan tren keberlanjutan
One of the key milestones this year was the Company’s focus on 
developing a more personalized and data-driven promotional strategy, 
particularly to reach Generation Z. This generation was known for its 
preference for speed, authenticity, and digital connection. To address 
this, the Company adopted the 5A customer path approach (Awareness, 
Appeal, Ask, Act, Advocate), which was translated into short content 
with bright and strong visuals within the first five seconds (golden time 
strategy). Content distribution was focused on TikTok and Instagram Reels 
and was strengthened through collaborations with micro-influencers in 
the culinary, lifestyle, and sustainability sectors. This approach proved 
effective and led the Company to win the 2024 Indonesia WOW Brand 
Award and the 2024 Indonesia Digital Popular Brand Award (IDPB). 
 
Digital campaigns that used hashtags such as #asliindonesia, 
#semangatbaru, 
#celebrategoodness, 
and 
#campinafuncreator 
generated high engagement with consumers and increased brand recall. 
Campina also highlighted sustainability values that mattered to Gen Z, 
such as the use of natural ingredients and the nutritional value displayed 
on ice cream packaging.
 
In execution, the omnichannel strategy was a key factor. Campina 
created a consistent experience across online and offline channels. The 
website and app icecreamstore.co.id were upgraded with real-time 
stock tracking, product personalization features, reward points, and 
gamification features such as spin-to-win. E-commerce sales showed an 
increase from the previous year, supported by exclusive collaborations 
with leading marketplaces. In the offline space, activations such as pop-
up booths at various events in major cities across Indonesia whether 
in e-sports (MPL ID seasons 13 and 14), music (Synchronize Fest, 
La La La Fest), or other lifestyle events (Indofest and Vegan Festival 
Indonesia) were combined with digital interactions through QR codes, 
AR filters, and other features that connected directly to social media. 
 
From a product development standpoint, innovation remained a core 
pillar. The Company launched various innovative new variants in 2024, 
including: Concerto Icownic Blackmoon, Party Pack Mini Concerto, 
Tropicana Double Crunch, Strawberry Lime, Rainbow Pops, Happy 
Cow Hi-Calcium, Take Home Cookie Creamy in 700ml and 350ml sizes, 
Fruitzy, Marvel ice cream, and the Kid Series with Fantazee Strawberry, 
Chocolate Milk, and Choco Mix flavors. These innovations not only 
focused on flavor and variety but also on nutritional value and packaging 
aligned with sustainability trends.

Memasuki tahun 2024, Perseroan terus memperkuat proses bisnis yang 
unggul dengan menempatkan kualitas, efisiensi, dan keberlanjutan 
sebagai pilar utama dalam setiap lini operasional. Komitmen ini 
diwujudkan melalui peningkatan standar produksi, kepatuhan terhadap 
regulasi, serta adopsi teknologi dan sistem manajemen mutu yang lebih 
maju.
Sebagai bagian dari upaya untuk memastikan kualitas dan kepercayaan 
konsumen, seluruh lini produk Campina kini telah tersertifikasi halal 
dengan proses verifikasi yang diperbarui secara berkala. Sertifikasi ini 
menjadi landasan dalam menjawab permintaan pasar yang semakin 
sadar akan standar etika dan kehalalan produk.
Selain itu, pada tahun 2024, Perseroan berhasil meraih pemutakhiran 
sertifikasi ke  FSSC 22000, GMP, Sertifikasi Halal dari LPPOM MUI, 
SMETA, serta Nomor Kontrol Veteriner (NKV). Yang memperluas 
cakupan pengendalian risiko keamanan pangan dengan integrasi 
pendekatan berbasis risiko menyeluruh (risk-based thinking). Penerapan 
sistem ini memperkuat kendali terhadap potensi kontaminasi, sekaligus 
memastikan rantai pasokan dikelola secara proaktif, transparan, dan 
efisien.
Inisiatif efisiensi energi dan pengurangan limbah juga ditingkatkan 
di seluruh fasilitas produksi, sejalan dengan target pengurangan 
jejak karbon (carbon footprint) yang ditetapkan Perseroan. Teknologi 
pengolahan limbah cair terbaru, pengurangan plastik sekali pakai dalam 
kemasan, serta penerapan sistem monitoring energi secara real-time 
merupakan beberapa langkah konkret yang telah diimplementasikan 
sepanjang tahun 2024.
Dengan pendekatan menyeluruh terhadap efisiensi operasional, 
keberlanjutan, dan kualitas, Perseroan meyakini bahwa proses bisnis 
yang unggul bukan hanya menjadi keunggulan kompetitif, tetapi juga 
kontribusi nyata dalam membangun masa depan industri pangan yang 
lebih bertanggung jawab dan berdaya saing tinggi.
Entering 2024, the Company continued to strengthen its business 
processes by prioritizing quality, efficiency, and sustainability as the 
main pillars across all operational areas. This commitment was realized 
through enhanced production standards, adherence to regulations, and 
the adoption of advanced technology and quality management systems.
As part of the effort to ensure quality and consumer trust, all Campina 
product lines are now halal certified, with the verification process being 
updated regularly. This certification serves as a foundation to meet the 
growing market demand for products that comply with ethical standards 
and halal requirements.
Furthermore, in 2024, the Company successfully renewed its 
certifications for FSSC 22000, GMP, Halal Certification from LPPOM 
MUI, SMETA, and obtained the Veterinary Control Number (NKV). These 
certifications expanded the scope of food safety risk control through 
the integration of a comprehensive risk-based thinking approach. The 
implementation of this system strengthens the control over potential 
contamination, while ensuring that the supply chain is managed 
proactively, transparently, and efficiently.
Energy efficiency and waste reduction initiatives were also enhanced 
across all production facilities, in line with the Company’s target to 
reduce its carbon footprint. New wastewater treatment technologies, 
the reduction of single-use plastics in packaging, and the implementation 
of real-time energy monitoring systems are some of the concrete steps 
that were put into action throughout 2024.
With a comprehensive approach to operational efficiency, sustainability, 
and quality, the Company believes that excellent business processes not 
only serve as a competitive advantage but also contribute significantly 
to building a more responsible and competitive future for the food 
industry.

Struktur modal adalah keseimbangan atau perbandingan antara 
liabilitas dan ekuitas. Perusahaan berkeyakinan bahwa struktur modal

proyeksi laba tahun mendatang, proyeksi arus kas operasi, proyeksi 
investasi barang modal dan proyeksi peluang investasi yang strategis.
Capital Structure
Capital structure refers to the balance or proportion between liabilities 
and equity. The Company believes that an optimal capital structure 
will maximize its value. The Company manages its capital structure to 
maintain a healthy capital ratio in order to conduct business operations 
and provide benefits to stakeholders. The Company optimally manages 
its capital structure by taking into account current and projected earnings, 
projected operating cash flows, capital expenditure projections, and 
strategic investment opportunities.

Perseroan mengelola struktur permodalan dan melakukan penyesuaian 
berdasarkan kondisi ekonomi. Pengelolaan modal Perseroan dilakukan 
dengan penuh kehati-hatian dan secara berkala ditinjau untuk

economic conditions. Capital management is carried out prudently and 
reviewed periodically to ensure the maintenance of a healthy capital 
ratio that supports the Company’s sustainable business.
